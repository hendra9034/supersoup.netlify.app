##### Instructions to make Lentil Soup:

1. Add lentils and continue sauteeing 2 - 3 mins, stirring constantly.
1. Add broth, cayenne pepper, and soy sauce/marmite. Bring to a simmer.
1. Add carrots. Simmer 30 mins.
