##### The ingredients needed to make Corn Muffins:

1. Take 2 small boxes of corn muffin mixes
1. Take 2 large hen eggs
1. Prepare 250-300 ml pasteurised cow milk
1. Take  vegetable oil to grease muffin baking tray
