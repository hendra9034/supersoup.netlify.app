The ingredients needed to make Liquid chicken soup with noodles:
  1. Take 1 quart chicken broth
  2. Get 1 1/2 tbsp dehydrated onion flakes
  3. Get 1 tsp white pepper
  4. Get 1/2 tsp granulated garlic powder
  5. Take 4 oz Korean flat noodles or ramen noodles
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 2 large eggs
  8. Prepare 2/3 cup meat I used beef
  9. Take 1 tsp Herbes de Provence

Steps to make Liquid chicken soup with noodles:
  1. In a sauce pan add broth heat till boiling add noodles and onion flakes
  2. Add salt pepper and garlic
  3. Slice meat into small bite sized pieces add to soup
  4. Beat egg stir soup clockwise till a mini whirlpool is going slowly add beaten eggs
  5. Cook till eggs done add to a bowl now add the Herbes de Provence
  6. Let seep 5-7 minutes hope you enjoy
