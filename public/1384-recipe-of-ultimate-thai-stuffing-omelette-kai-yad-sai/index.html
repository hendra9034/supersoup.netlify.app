The ingredients needed to make Thai stuffing omelette (Kai Yad Sai ไข่ยัดใส้):
  1. Get For egg wrap
  2. Get 3 eggs
  3. Get 1 tsp fishsauce
  4. Get 1 tsp soysauce
  5. Prepare Pinch ground pepper
  6. Prepare Stuffing
  7. Prepare 150 g minced pork or chicken
  8. Prepare 3-4 baby corn, finely chopped
  9. Get 1/2 carrot, finely chopped
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped tomato
  11. Prepare 1/2 white onion, finely chopped
  12. Take 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup, bell peppers, finely chopped
  14. Take 1/2 cup tin of sweet corn
  15. Make ready 1/2 spring onions, finely chopped
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp each (oyster sauce, soysauce, fishsauce, sugar, tomato katchup, sriracha or hot chilli sauce)
  17. Take 2 tbsp vegetable cooking oil
Instructions to make Thai stuffing omelette (Kai Yad Sai ไข่ยัดใส้):
  1. Prepping all the vegetables ready to fry. Add vegetable cooking oil in a medium heat pan, add garlic and keep stirring until garlic lovely and golden.
  2. Add minced pork or chicken and breaking it up until the meat cooked about 80% the. Add the rest of your vegetable. Stir well.
  3. Seasoning your stuffing with all the sauces. Stir well. 1. To make omelette, beaten up eggs in a bowl, seasoning with sauces and ground pepper. On another non stick pan, medium heat, add 1 tbsp vegetable cooking oil on. Once your pan nice and hot add half of your beaten eggs in, spread it out thinly. 1. Turn your hop to low heat Add half of your stuffing in the middle of your egg. Use your spatular fold 4 corner of your omelette. Turn off the heat. 1. Cover your pan with a big plate, one hand secured your plate and one hand secured your pan then turn over and take your pan out. Cut across your omelette to reveal the yummy stuffing inside. Serve with Thai jasmine rice.
