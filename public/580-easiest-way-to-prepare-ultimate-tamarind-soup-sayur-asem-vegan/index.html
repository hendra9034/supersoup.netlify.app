The ingredients needed to make Tamarind Soup (Sayur Asem) *Vegan:
  1. Prepare Kangkung/water spinach (optional)
  2. Get carrots
  3. Make ready corn kernels
  4. Prepare red onion
  5. Take garlic cloves
  6. Prepare tamarind
  7. Get bayleaf
  8. Prepare galangal
  9. Prepare tomato
  10. Make ready salt
  11. Take sugar
  12. Get water
  13. Take You can use other vegetables such as:
  14. Get Cabbage
  15. Take Chokos
  16. Prepare Wombok
  17. Make ready Snake bean
Instructions to make Tamarind Soup (Sayur Asem) *Vegan:
  1. Boil the water, chop onion and garlic, cut up all vegetables.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlics into boiling water, stir it. Add tamarind, bayleaf, and galangal. Sprinkle salt and sugar stir it.
  3. Put the vegetables in (the vegetables that needed to be cooked longer should be added first like carrot and snake bean).
  4. Cover the pot until all the vegetables cooked well.
  5. Perfect to serve while its hot in winter or rainy weather. Also can be served with steamed rice, chili sauce, and garlic or prawn crackers.
