The ingredients needed to make Puy Lentil Bolognese:
  1. Get 1 white onion
  2. Take 1 large carrot
  3. Prepare 1 celery stick
  4. Take 1 punnet button mushrooms
  5. Take 1 tin tomatoes
  6. Take 1 pouch Merchant Gourmet Puy Lentils
  7. Prepare 1 beef stock cube
  8. Get 2 cloves garlic
  9. Prepare 1 sprig fresh Rosemary (or 1 tsp dried)
  10. Take 1 sprig fresh Sage (or 1 tsp dried)
  11. Get 1 sprig fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  13. Get Salt and pepper
  14. Make ready 200 g spaghetti
  15. Take Parmesan (to taste)
  16. Get Fresh parsley to serve
Instructions to make Puy Lentil Bolognese:
  1. Finely dice veg and add to a pan along with the olive oil
  2. Gently cook until the veg has softened and reduced but donâ€™t brown too much
  3. Add the garlic along with salt and pepper
  4. Dissolve the beef stock in 200ml of water and add to the veg
  5. Add the tinned tomatoes and the all of the herbs
  6. Add the pouch of lentils and reduce the heat to a simmer
  7. Meanwhile add spaghetti to a pan of boiling water
  8. Add a ladle full of the starchy spaghetti water to the lentils and stir through
  9. When the spaghetti is cooked mix through the lentil ragu and serve, topped with Parmesan and fresh parsley
