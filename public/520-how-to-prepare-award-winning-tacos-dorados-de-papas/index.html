The ingredients needed to make Tacos Dorados De papas:
  1. Make ready 2 tomatoes
  2. Take 1 small union
  3. Take 4 potatoes
  4. Take 1 bag of tortillas
  5. Get 1 bunch of cilantro
  6. Take 1/4 garlic salt
  7. Prepare 1 lettuce
  8. Make ready 1 mayonnaise
  9. Prepare 1 Mexican cheese
Instructions to make Tacos Dorados De papas:
  1. Boil the tomatoes with tea spoon of garlic salt
  2. Boil the potatoes mash the potatoes
  3. Cut the cilantro and the lettuce
  4. Add the mash potatoes to the soft tortilla
  5. Have the corn oil in the stove boiling
  6. Tacos ready to add the rest of the ingredients
