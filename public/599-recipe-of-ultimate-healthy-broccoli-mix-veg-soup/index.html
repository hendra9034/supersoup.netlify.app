The ingredients needed to make Healthy Broccoli Mix Veg Soup:
  1. Take carrot chopped
  2. Get capsicum chopped
  3. Make ready Garlic cloves chopped
  4. Take onions chopped
  5. Get Broccoli broken into small florets
  6. Make ready sweet corn kernels
  7. Take French Beans cut diagonally (small)
  8. Prepare vegetable broth (by boiling 1 cup mixed vegetables like potato, cabbage, onion, capsicum with a pinch salt and water and sieving them)
  9. Prepare sugar(optional)
  10. Take Black Pepper powder
  11. Prepare Olive oil
  12. Take white vinegar
  13. Take fresh cream(optional)
  14. Make ready oregano
  15. Get Salt
Instructions to make Healthy Broccoli Mix Veg Soup:
  1. Heat olive oil in a wok on high heat. - Add garlic and onions and stir fry for a few a minute.
  2. Add all carrot, capsicum, french beans, broccoli, corn kernels and stir fry for 2 minutes on high flame.
  3. Add the vegetable broth to the vegetables while stirring continuously.
  4. Add black pepper, vinegar, oregano sugar and salt.
  5. Mix well and add 1 cup of water. - Cook for 2-3 minutes and bring to a boil.
  6. Sprinkle some oregano, according to choice and enjoy hot and healthy soup
