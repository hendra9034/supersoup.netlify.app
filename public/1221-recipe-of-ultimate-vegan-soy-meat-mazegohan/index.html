The ingredients needed to make Vegan Soy Meat Mazegohan:
  1. Prepare 2 cups (*180ml cup) Japanese short grain rice
  2. Get 4-5 dried whole shiitake mushrooms
  3. Get 80 g dry soy meat *I used fillet type
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  6. Prepare 1 small piece ginger *cut into fine strips
  7. Take 1/2 carrot *cut into thin slices or strips
  8. Make ready 1/4-1/3 cup edamame beans *optional
  9. Take 1 spring onion *finely chopped
  10. Prepare Toasted sesame seeds
  11. Take Sauce
  12. Make ready 1 tablespoon Sugar *alter the amount to suit your taste
  13. Take 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  14. Get 2 tablespoons mirin
  15. Make ready 4 tablespoons water that is used to soak shiitake
Steps to make Vegan Soy Meat Mazegohan:
  1. Wash Rice and cook as normal.
  2. Soak dried shiitake in water for 1-2 hours or until soft. It can take quite a long time. When soft, drain and save water. Thinly slice shiitake.
  3. Soak dry soy meat in plenty of warm water for 30 minutes. Drain and gently squeeze to remove excess water.
  4. Combine all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
  5. Heat oils in a frying pan and cook ginger and carrot. Then add shiitake and softened soy meat. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  6. Add the sauce and cook until the sauce is thickened and almost gone. Add edamame beans and stir, then mix with cooked rice.
  7. Sprinkle some chopped spring onion and toasted sesame seeds, and serve.
