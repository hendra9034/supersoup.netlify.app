The ingredients needed to make Fresh Bamboo Shoot, Mushrooms & Chicken Soup:
  1. Get 500 g fresh bamboo shoots (removed and peeled off all old parts away)
  2. Prepare 1 pot boiling water
  3. Take a:
  4. Take 11/2 liters water
  5. Prepare 2 chicken whole legs (about 600g) (washed and cut into pieces)
  6. Take 10 fresh mushrooms (washed and cut into thick slices)
  7. Make ready b:
  8. Make ready 1 tsp chicken stock powder
  9. Get 1 tsp salt or to taste
  10. Take 1/2 tsp pepper
Steps to make Fresh Bamboo Shoot, Mushrooms & Chicken Soup:
  1. Put bamboo shoots into boiling water and boil for 11/2- 2 hours using medium lower heat. Drain and rinse in cold water. Cut into slices.
  2. 2 Put bamboo shoot slices, A into a pot and bring to boil. Lower heat and cook for 1 hour.
  3. 3 Season with B, taste and serve hot with rice.
