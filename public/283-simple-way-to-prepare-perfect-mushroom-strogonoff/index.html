The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Strogonoff:
  1. Get 1 tbsp butter
  2. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  3. Take 3 garlic cloves, minced
  4. Take 1 punnet mushrooms, sliced
  5. Prepare Salt and pepper, to taste
  6. Take 1 tsp Paprika
  7. Get 1 tsp Parsley
  8. Take Splash white wine
  9. Prepare 100 ml beef or vegetable stock
  10. Take Splash Worcestershire sauce
  11. Get 3 tbsp crème fraîche
Instructions to make Mushroom Strogonoff:
  1. Heat the butter in a pan
  2. Add the onions to the pan and fry until soft and translucent
  3. Add the breakout and mushrooms to the pan and fry until the mushrooms release their juices
  4. Add the salt, pepper, paprika and parsley
  5. Add the wine and the Worcestershire sauce
  6. Add the stock and cook until the sauce begins to thicken
  7. Add the crème fraîche and warm through
  8. Serve with rice or pasta or bread
