The ingredients needed to make The Union Square Cafes bar nuts:
  1. Prepare 2 1/4 cup assorted unsalted nuts (If you use the salted nuts, only add a dash of salt instead of teaspoons listed)
  2. Take 2 tbsp coarsely chopped fresh rosemary leaves
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  4. Prepare 2 tsp dark brown sugar
  5. Prepare 2 tsp Maldon or other sea salt (if using unsalted nuts)
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
