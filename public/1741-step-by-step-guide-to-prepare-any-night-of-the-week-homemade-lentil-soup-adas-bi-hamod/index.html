The ingredients needed to make Homemade Lentil Soup :Adas Bi Hamod:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup yellow lentils
  2. Prepare 1/2 kg mangold, leaves separated from stalk and cut thin
  3. Take 3 carrots, chopped into small pieces
  4. Take 1 large onions, cut into thin slices
  5. Prepare 3 cloves garlic, cruched with mortar
  6. Get 1 juice of 1 lemon
  7. Get 1/4 cup olive oil
  8. Get 1 1/2 liters water
  9. Make ready 1/2 tablespoons dried mint
  10. Get salt coarse
Instructions to make Homemade Lentil Soup :Adas Bi Hamod:
  1. Fry the onion in oil, medium fire for 5 mins
  2. Add lentils, mangold, carrots, salt and water, let boil for 20mins
  3. Add the crushed garlic and mint and keep boiling for 5 minutes
  4. Once done, add the lemon juice and serve
