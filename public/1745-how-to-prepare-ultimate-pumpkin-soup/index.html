  1. Make ready 150 g pumpkin peices
  2. Prepare 1 medium sized onion
  3. Prepare 2 carrots
  4. Get 50 grams red lentils
  5. Take 1 tbls butter, 1 tbls of vegetable oil
  6. Make ready Salt & pepper
