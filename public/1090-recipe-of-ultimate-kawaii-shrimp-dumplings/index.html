Instructions to make Kawaii Shrimp Dumplings:
  1. Mix 2 kinds of rice flour and divide into 4. Add hot water a little by little (about 2 tablespoon) and knead for a while to make soft dough.
  2. Add vegetable flour and hot water. Make soft dough.
  3. Wrap with film and rest.
  4. Cut shrimps and spring onion and mix with seasonings and starch or glutinous rice flour.
  5. Combine 2 colors dough and press with hand or rolling pin to make circle.
  6. Wrap shrimp mixture with dough.
  7. Press in moon cake mold.
  8. Steam for 20 minutes with high flame. Brush with sesame oil.
  9. These dumplings are so soft and kawaii. Eat with your favorite sauce.
