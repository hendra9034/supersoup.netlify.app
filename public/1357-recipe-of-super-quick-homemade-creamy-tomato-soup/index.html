The ingredients needed to make Creamy Tomato soup:
  1. Prepare 1/2 onion
  2. Prepare 1 strip bacon
  3. Prepare 6 mini tomatoes
  4. Take 2 cloves garlic
  5. Take 822 g Tomato puree
  6. Take 2 cups broth
  7. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  8. Make ready 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  9. Get Salt
  10. Get Pepper
  11. Prepare 5 Fresh basil leaves
