Instructions to make Grilled beef eye fillet steaks:
  1. Rub the herbs (mustard seeds, ginger, and Chinese five spices), salt and pepper into the beef steaks to marinade.
  2. Chop onion sideways to make onion rings. Separate any rings that stick together.
  3. Chop capsicum into long strips.
  4. Chop garlic finely into tiny cubes.
  5. Throw spinach into pan with the garlic. Turn setting to saute. Turn until spinach changes colour to dark green.
  6. Grill capsicum first, then add onions.
  7. Take capsicum and onion out, before adding beef steaks to grill.
  8. Butter a couple of slices of bread and cut into triangles.
  9. Throw it all together on a plate and enjoy!!
