The ingredients needed to make Chicken Chinese Noodles:
  1. Take Boneless chicken breast
  2. Take 1/2 cup veg oil (8tbsp)
  3. Take 1/2 cup corn flour (8tbsp)
  4. Make ready Egg noodles

Instructions to make Chicken Chinese Noodles:
  1. Cut chicken into bite sizes and place into a bowl, add the veg oil, soy sauce, baking soda, cornflour, salt and pepper. Stir and leave to marinate
  2. For the sauce, pour the chicken stock into the oyster sauce and heat up until cube dissolved. Add soy sauce, red chilli flakes, pepper & salt
  3. In a pan, medium heat pour some oil and sesame oil and cook the chicken until nicely brown. No need to cook the chicken fully. then leave aside
  4. In a pan pour oil and heat it up. Add in the ginger garlic paste and onions. Then add in all vegetables
  5. Add the chicken and egg noodles and sauce and stir. Add soy sauce if necessary
