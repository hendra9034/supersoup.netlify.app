The ingredients needed to make Beef Fillet Tataki:
  1. Get Steak
  2. Make ready 100 gr Fillet Mignon
  3. Prepare Grapeseed Oil (or Sesame Oil)
  4. Get Toppings
  5. Prepare Thinly diced cucumbers pickled in Ponzu over night
  6. Take Lemon Peel
  7. Make ready Parsley
  8. Take Sweet Wasabi Mayo
  9. Make ready Bed
  10. Prepare Ponzu Sauce
Instructions to make Beef Fillet Tataki:
  1. Pre-heat pan on high heat
  2. Add oil to the pan and bring to high temperature
  3. Sear Steak on each side
  4. Bring Steak to room temperature (can wrap in foil and leave in freezer or drop in ice bath for quick result. Or can just leave to rest)
  5. Pour Ponzu in a plate
  6. Slice Steak thinly and put it in plate
  7. Top with Sweet Wasabi Mayo, Cucumbers, Parsley and Lemon Peel
  8. Add some salt Flakes on top
