The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 1/3 cup carrots
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Take 4 cloves garlic
  4. Get 6 cups Vegetable Broth
  5. Take 3 cups cabbage
  6. Take 1 cup green beans
  7. Take 2 TBSP tomato paste
  8. Make ready 1 tsp basil
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 1 cup zucchini

Steps to make Vegetable Soup:
  1. See picture 1. Chop everything into really small pieces.
  2. Sauté carrots, onion, garlic on low heat (around 5 mins)
  3. Add in everything except zucchini and bring pot to boil, then switch to low and simmer for 15 mins.
  4. Add in zucchini and heat for 4ish minutes.
  5. Enjoy, you can also blend this.
