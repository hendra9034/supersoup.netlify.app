The ingredients needed to make Baked Lamb Chop in Honey and Spicy Xo Black Pepper Sauce:
  1. Get Angel Hair For 2 pacs
  2. Take 4 slice lamb shoulder chop
  3. Prepare 1 onion
  4. Make ready honey xo black pepper sauce
  5. Take 1 bottle black pepper sauce 1:1 water ratio
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp xo sauce
  7. Take 1 tbsp honey
Steps to make Baked Lamb Chop in Honey and Spicy Xo Black Pepper Sauce:
  1. Pat dry lamb then season with salt, layer onion on baking tray then lamb
  2. 1 bottle black pepper sauce and water 1:1, then spicy xo sauce and honey mix well and pour over lamb,Preheat The Oven then Baked 200 C for 15 minutes (flip over and baste and broil for another 15 to 20 minutes).
  3. Cook Angel Hair as usual 5 minutes then drain and put onto baked Lamb and mix well with it’s gravy and serve.
