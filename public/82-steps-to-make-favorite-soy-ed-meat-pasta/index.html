The ingredients needed to make Soy-ed meat pasta:
  1. Get Oli
  2. Get 500 g meat slices
  3. Get 1 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 tsp paprika
  5. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  6. Get 1 Onion
  7. Take 1 green pepper
  8. Take 1 Carrot
  9. Prepare 300 g pasta
  10. Prepare Sauce
  11. Get 80 ml Soy sauce
  12. Make ready 300 ml water
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp Cornstarch

Instructions to make Soy-ed meat pasta:
  1. Cut the meat into small cubes.
  2. In a medium heat, put a dose of oil in a Pan then the meat to fry, Add our seasoning (Salt, paprika and black pepper) and mix them well and cover the pan.
  3. After the water disappeared, put the cooked meat aside in a large bowl.
  4. In the same pan with the leftover of the meat sauce, we put small dose of oil then we add sliced onion, green pepper and carrot, and mix well for a little while.
  5. For our sauce, we get half cup of water then add the soy sauce to it then the Cornstarch and mix them well together.
  6. On the side, Cook your favorite pasta the normal way.
  7. Lastly, Put the meat back to the pan with our veggies, then add the pasta and our special sauce all to the same pan, mix them and leave it for 15min on low heat.
  8. Then.. Bon appetit!
