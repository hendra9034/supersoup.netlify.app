The ingredients needed to make Tuna Curry Takikomi Gohan:
  1. Get 2 cups (*180ml cup) Short / Medium Grain Rice
  2. Make ready 360 ml Chicken Stock
  3. Prepare 1-2 teaspoons Soy Sauce
  4. Take 1 tablespoon (15 g) Butter *cut into small cubes
  5. Take 1 tablespoon Curry Powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 Onion *finely chopped
  7. Prepare 1 Tomato *cut into small pieces
  8. Prepare 1 can (95 g) Tuna *drained, I used Tuna in Springwater
  9. Get 1/2 cup Frozen Peas *thawed
  10. Make ready Salt & Pepper
Instructions to make Tuna Curry Takikomi Gohan:
  1. Wash Rice, drain the water, and place into the rice cooker’s inner pot.
  2. Add Chicken Stock and Soy Sauce, and stir. Add Butter and sprinkle with Curry Powder. Then scatter Onion, Tomato and coarsely flaked Tuna. DO NOT stir.
  3. Press ‘COOK’ button to start cooking. When the rice is cooked, add Peas, and let it steamed for 10 minutes. Then mix gently. Add extra Salt & Pepper if required.
