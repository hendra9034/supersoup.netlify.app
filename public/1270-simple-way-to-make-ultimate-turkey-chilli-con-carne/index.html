The ingredients needed to make Turkey Chilli Con Carne:
  1. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
  2. Make ready 500 g minced turkey
  3. Take 3 garlic cloves, minced
  4. Make ready 1 celery stalk, sliced
  5. Prepare 1 cup frozen corn
  6. Get 1 red pepper, deseeded and chopped

  8. Make ready 1 cup frozen peas
  9. Make ready 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  10. Take 400 ml Chicken stock
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp paprika
  12. Get 2 tsp dried oregano
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp mild, medium or hot chilli powder
  14. Make ready 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  15. Prepare 4 tbsp tomato paste
  16. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  17. Prepare Bunch fresh coriander, chopped
  18. Get Salt to taste

Steps to make Turkey Chilli Con Carne:
  1. Heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onions, red pepper, garlic and celery (carrot and fresh chilli if using) and fry for a few minutes until soften.
  3. Add in the turkey mince and fry until browned. Break up large chunks with the spoon.
  4. Next add the chilli powder, paprika, oregano, cayenne powder (cumin if using), frozen corn and peas. Mix everything well.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, paste and stock. Mix and bring everything to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to a simmer and add the kidney beans. Cover with lid and simmer for 60-90 minutes to get the meat really soft and to reduce the sauce.
  7. Taste and season with salt and pepper. Stir in the coriander.
  8. Serve with choice of sides. I served mine with rice and nacho chips.
