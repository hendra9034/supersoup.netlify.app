The ingredients needed to make 3 Spices Turkish Lentil Soup:
  1. Take 1 tspn : cumin seed powder
  2. Take 1 tbspn : Red chilli flakes
  3. Get 1 tspn : Turmeric powder
  4. Make ready Salt to taste
  5. Get 2 : carrots
  6. Prepare 1 : potato
  7. Prepare 1 : onion
  8. Make ready 2 : garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  9. Get Half cup : Red lentil (soaked for 2 hours)
  10. Take 1 cup : chicken stock
  11. Prepare Butter
  12. Prepare 4-5 tbspn : olive oil
  13. Take Coriander to garnish
  14. Make ready 1 tbspn : corn flour

Steps to make 3 Spices Turkish Lentil Soup:
  1. Chop onions, carrots, potato and soak lentil for 2 hours
  2. In a pot add olive oil, add onions, carrots, potatoes, garlic cloves
  3. Add all the spices (cumin seeds powder, red chilli flakes, turmeric powder and mix well
  4. Add lentil into it and corn flour along with salt, mix well, and cook for few minutes
  5. Add chicken stock into it and one cup of water
  6. Turn the timer of instant pot on (16 minutes)…. Or in manual pressure cooker open the cooker lid 16 minutes after whistle blow
  7. Open the lid of instant pot after 16 minutes and blend the lentil with handblander
  8. In a fry pan add olive oil and butter, add crushed red chilli flakes, cook for few seconds and add into the lentil soup
  9. Turkish Red lentil soup is ready to be served, garnish it with coriander
