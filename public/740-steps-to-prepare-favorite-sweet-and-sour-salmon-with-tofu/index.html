The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Salmon with Tofu:
  1. Make ready 1 piece salmon
  2. Take 1 pack soft silken tofu
  3. Get 10 pieces cherry tomatoes
  4. Get Sauce:ketchup,sugar,,flour,vinegar,black pepper
  5. Prepare 2 shallot minced
Steps to make Sweet and Sour Salmon with Tofu:
  1. Fry salmon then set aside.
  2. In a heated wok with oil.Sautee shallots then tomatoes then the sauce.Simmer then.put back the fish.
  3. Slowly add the tofu then simmer it.Until done
