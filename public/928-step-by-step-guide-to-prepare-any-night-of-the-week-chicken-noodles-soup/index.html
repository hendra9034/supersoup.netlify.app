The ingredients needed to make Chicken noodles soup:
  1. Make ready 4 large chiken breast
  2. Prepare 4 spring onions
  3. Make ready 2 red chilies
  4. Prepare 1 quart ginger
  5. Get 1/4 french riesling (whitewine)
  6. Take 1 packages noodles
  7. Prepare 1 liter water

Steps to make Chicken noodles soup:
  1. boil the chicken breasts in the water until they're firm and cooked through
  2. while the chicken is in water slice the spring onion, ginger and chop the chili.
  3. remove the chiken and add the chili spring onion, ginger and the riesling. and let it boil for 10 minutes
  4. then add the noodles and give it 2 mintues. while you slice the chicken in 1cm thick slices
  5. add the chicken and ready to serve.
