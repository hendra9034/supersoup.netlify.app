The ingredients needed to make Simple Cheese Omelette:
  1. Prepare 4 eggs
  2. Make ready Handful grated cheddar
  3. Get Salt and pepper
  4. Prepare 2 tsp olive oil
Instructions to make Simple Cheese Omelette:
  1. Whisk the eggs in a jug or bowl. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Add the oil to a good non stick frying pan. Use a spatula to coat the bottom of the pan with the oil.
  3. Pour in your whisked egg. Use your spatula to pull in from the side if the pan into the centre. As the egg cooks on the base you’ll form little channels for the raw egg on top to run into and cook.
  4. After a few minutes, set your spatula aside and let the base set.
  5. Sprinkle the grated cheese on top and before the egg has fully set, foo it in half with the spatula.
  6. Leave the omelette for another few minutes so the egg in the middle cooks and the cheese melts.
  7. Serve!
