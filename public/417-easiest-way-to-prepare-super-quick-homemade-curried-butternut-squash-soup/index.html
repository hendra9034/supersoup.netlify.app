The ingredients needed to make Curried butternut squash soup:
  1. Prepare butternut squash peeled and chopped into chunks
  2. Get large carrots chopped into chunks
  3. Get 2 white onions chopped
  4. Make ready curry powder
  5. Make ready garam Marsala
  6. Take Large glug veg oil
  7. Get ground coriander
  8. Get turmeric
  9. Take gr cumin
  10. Make ready veg or chicken stock
  11. Make ready coconut milk
Instructions to make Curried butternut squash soup:
  1. Pre heat oven to 180 degrees C, put squash, carrot and onion in pan with oil, 1 tbsp curry powder and 1 tbsp garam Marsala season and mix and put in Oven for 30-40 minutes
  2. Take out veg and add to large saucepan, add enough stock to cover veg, add remaining spices and blend using hand blender.
  3. Once smooth, add in coconut milk to taste and season. Add more spices to taste and one more final blend
