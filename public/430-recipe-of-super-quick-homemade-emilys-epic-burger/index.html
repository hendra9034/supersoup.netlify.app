The ingredients needed to make Emilys Epic Burger:
  1. Make ready 2 lb good quality mince beef
  2. Take 2 tbsp steak seasoning
  3. Take Salt and Pepper (a really good sprinkle you need a good amount)
  4. Make ready Garnishes (optional)
  5. Prepare Slices Cheese
  6. Take Tomato sliced
  7. Get Ketchup or bbq sauce
  8. Make ready Lettuce
  9. Take Mustard thin layer on bun
  10. Get Mayonnaise
  11. Get Gherkins sliced
  12. Make ready Large bread bun toasted
Steps to make Emilys Epic Burger:
  1. Throw the minced beef in a large bowl, add the seasoning and salt and mix well!
  2. Leave to stand for 10 mins at least.
  3. I use a large cookie cutter for my burger mould. Spoon in a press down firmly. Underneath, I have baking paper so it doesnâ€™t stick!
  4. Let them rest in the fridge to firm up for 15 mins.
  5. I use an electronic griddle which speeds up the time it cooks! But you can place them in the oven, frying pan or grill they should take 6 mins each side if flipping them.
  6. Check they are cooked in the middle and have fun with your favourite ingredients layering up your burger bun!!
