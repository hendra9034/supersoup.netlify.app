The ingredients needed to make Kharkhariya:
  1. Take 3 cups plain flour or wheat flour
  2. Take 100 grams sugar
  3. Take 30 gram corn flour
  4. Prepare Pinch salt
  5. Make ready 50 gram ghee melted
  6. Take 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  7. Get 1/4 cup water
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup milk

Instructions to make Kharkhariya:
  1. In a pan on medium heat add milk, water and sugar. Bring it to boil ensuring all the sugar is dissolved. Let it cool for 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. In a wide bottom plate add flour, ghee, pinch of salt and sesame seeds and mix well. Add the milk mixture little at a time and make a stiff dough. Let it rest for 20 to 30 minutes covering with wet cloth.
  3. Knead the dough and make small balls. - Roll out thin puris from the dough and let it dry overnight. Deep fry on medium heat until lightly golden brown. Store in airtight container.
