The ingredients needed to make Cozy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Take 1 packages Reames Homestyle Egg Noodles 24 oz
  2. Get 1 cup Chopped Celery
  3. Prepare 1 cup Chopped Onion
  4. Make ready 1 cup Shredded Carrots
  5. Make ready 1 lb Boneless Chicken
  6. Prepare 8 cup Chicken Broth
  7. Make ready 2 cup Half and Half
  8. Prepare 8 tbsp Butter
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup Flour
  10. Get 1 tsp Black Pepper
  11. Prepare 1 tsp Parsley Flakes
  12. Get 1 tbsp Chopped Garlic
  13. Take 4 tbsp Olive Oil
Steps to make Cozy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. In a 4 quart stock pot, sauté the onions, celery, carrots and garlic with the olive oil on medium heat.
  2. Add cubed chicken and broth to the pot and bring to a boil for 20 minutes.
  3. In a sauce pan melt butter, then stir in flour and cook for two minutes.
  4. Add the noodles to the pot and simmer for twenty minutes. Add pepper, parsley then salt to taste. Add half and half then stir in flour mixture.
