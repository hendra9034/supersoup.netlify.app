The ingredients needed to make Harissa Beef and crispy potatoes:
  1. Take Olive oil
  2. Make ready 4 tsp harissa paste
  3. Prepare 100 g feta cheese
  4. Make ready 2 red onions
  5. Make ready 500 g beef mince
  6. Make ready 600 g potatoes cut rough chunks
  7. Prepare 400 g chopped tomatoes
  8. Take Freshly chopped mint, coriander and parsley

Steps to make Harissa Beef and crispy potatoes:
  1. Cut the onions into wedges and roast with potatoes in a little oil for 40 mins
  2. Brown mince off, add chopped tomatoes and 150ml water, bring to the boil add the harissa and simmer for 20mins
  3. Spoon the mince over potatoes, sprinkle with crumbled feta and grill for 5 mins
  4. Serve with chopped herbs
