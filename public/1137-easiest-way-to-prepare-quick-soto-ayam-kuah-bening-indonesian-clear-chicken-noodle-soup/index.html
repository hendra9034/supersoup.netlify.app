The ingredients needed to make Soto Ayam Kuah Bening (Indonesian Clear Chicken Noodle Soup):
  1. Make ready 1 medium size chicken, cut into 4 parts, wash thoroughly
  2. Get 3 tablespoon oil for sauted
  3. Get 1 tablespoons sugar
  4. Take 1 lemongrass stalk, bruised
  5. Take 1 cm ginger, bruised
  6. Prepare 5 lime leaves
  7. Get 3 bay leaves
  8. Get Ground Spices :
  9. Get 2 large red shallots
  10. Take 4 cloves garlic
  11. Make ready 5 candlenuts
  12. Take 1 cm turmeric
  13. Prepare Salt and sugar to taste
  14. Take Other Ingredient to serve :
  15. Take 100 grams vermicelli, soak until soft, drain
  16. Prepare 100 grams bean sprouts, soak in hot water, drain
  17. Get 2 boiled eggs, cut into pieces according to taste
  18. Take Fried shallots and Fried garlic to taste
  19. Get Sliced ​​spring onions and celery to taste
  20. Make ready Chillie Sambal and Lime juice to taste and extra rice
Instructions to make Soto Ayam Kuah Bening (Indonesian Clear Chicken Noodle Soup):
  1. Boil chicken with 2,5 liters of water in a saucepan, until half cooked.
  2. Saute all the spices until fragrant and cooked.  Add it to the chicken broth.  Continue to cook until the chicken is cooked.  Remove the chicken, drain and deep fry then shred the chicken according to taste.
  3. Prepare a serving bowl then arrange the vermicelli, bean sprouts, eggs, in a bowl.
  4. Drizzle with chicken soup.  Sprinkle with spring onions, celery, fried shallots, and fried garlic.
  5. Serve while hot, with chili sambal and crackers and don't forget to add lime juice before enjoying.
