The ingredients needed to make DJ one's Harty Chicken Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 whole chicken
  2. Make ready 5 stick celery
  3. Take 5 stick carrots
  4. Make ready 3 potatos
  5. Take 2 tbsp season all
  6. Get 1 tbsp pepper
  7. Take 10 Chicken bullion cubes
  8. Take 1 box Elbow Noodles
Steps to make DJ one's Harty Chicken Soup:
  1. Clean whole chickin and place in a larg pot. Fill water pot 3 inches above the whole chicken. Cook on high heat
  2. Quarter onion, chop celery, chop carrots, cube potato, add pepper, add season all and chicken bullion cubes and and to pot
  3. Cook about 1 hour in till chicken falls off the bone.
  4. Boil elbow noodles in a separate pot till tender.
  5. Place noodles in bowl and pour the soup over the noodles and serve.
