The ingredients needed to make Ripe Papaya Soup:
  1. Get 3 brown dates
  2. Take 5 red dates remove seed
  3. Make ready 1 handful peanut remove skin
  4. Take 1 piece white fungus or yellow fungus
  5. Take 1 ripe papaya
  6. Prepare Some almond
  7. Get 1/4 kg pork
  8. Get Salt
  9. Take 5 glasses water
  10. Make ready Half carrot
Steps to make Ripe Papaya Soup:
  1. Soak fungus in water.Boil water in the pot together with the pork.Boil for three mins then wash pork and throw water.
  2. Pour water in a pit and add all the ingredients exceot the white fungus.Cook it for an hour.Add the fungus 20 mins before done.Add salt to taste
