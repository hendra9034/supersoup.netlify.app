The ingredients needed to make Snow Fungus & Egg Soup:
  1. Prepare 10 g Dried Snow Fungus Mushrooms
  2. Make ready 4 cups (1000 ml) Chicken Stock *OR Stock of your choice
  3. Get 2 tablespoon Sake (Rice Wine)
  4. Take 1 small piece Ginger *grated
  5. Get 3-4 Shiitake Mushrooms *thinly sliced
  6. Prepare 50 g Bamboo Shoot *cut into thin strips
  7. Get 2 tablespoons Oyster Sauce
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon Sesame Oil
  9. Get Salt & White Pepper
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoons Potato Starch OR Corn Starch *mixed with 2 tablespoons Water
  11. Prepare 2 Eggs *whisked
  12. Make ready 1 Spring Onion *finely chopped
Steps to make Snow Fungus & Egg Soup:
  1. Soak Dried Snow Fungus in cold/warm water for 20 to 30 minutes OR until softened. Wash very well and drain. Remove the hard bottom bits if required, and tear into small pieces.
  2. Place Chicken Stock (OR Stock of your choice), Sake and Ginger in a large saucepan or pot. Add all vegetables and bring to the boil over medium heat. Once boiling, season with Oyster Sauce, Sesame Oil, Salt and White Pepper.
  3. Add Potato Starch mixture, stirring well. When the soup thickens, add lightly whisked Eggs slowly in a circular motion into the soup, and stir gently. When the Egg is softly cooked, remove from heat.
  4. Sprinkle with finely chopped Spring Onion and enjoy.
