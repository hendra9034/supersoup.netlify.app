The ingredients needed to make Honey and Mustard Chicken With Potatoes:
  1. Prepare 8 chicken thighs with skin
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Coleman's mustard
  3. Get 1 heaped tbsp wholegrain mustard
  4. Take 2 tbsp honey
  5. Prepare 4 garlic cloves
  6. Take Juice of one lemon
  7. Get Salt to taste
  8. Make ready Ground black pepper to taste
  9. Prepare 8-10 medium potatoes, peeled and cut in half
  10. Make ready 200 g fresh spinach (approx)
  11. Make ready 1 drizzle of olive oil
  12. Get 6-8 small onions, peeled and whole
Instructions to make Honey and Mustard Chicken With Potatoes:
  1. In a small bowl mix the garlic, lemon juice, Coleman's and wholegrain mustard aswell as salt and pepper.
  2. Mix the mustard marinade with the chicken and place in fridge for 2-4 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200c. Place chicken in an oven proof tray big enough to fit the potatoes aswell.
  4. Coat the potatoes in olive oil and season with a pinch of salt and pepper. Add to the tray around the chicken. Add the onions evenly spread out in the tray.
  5. Transfer to the oven and roast for 45 minutes or until vegetables and chicken are fully cooked through.
  6. About 10 minutes before add the spinach. I like it really wilted so if you want some crunch left in it, reduce its cooking time.
  7. Serve immediately with side. I served mine with some crispy green salad.
