The ingredients needed to make pasta and lentil soup:
  1. Prepare 5 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  2. Make ready 1/2 onion chopped
  3. Take 1/2 onion chopped
  4. Get 4 garlic cloves smashed
  5. Make ready 4 garlic cloves smashed
  6. Get 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp tomatoe paste
  8. Take 2 cup low sodium chicken broth
  9. Get 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese plus 1 piece of rind (optional)
  10. Make ready 8 oz orecchiette pasta
  11. Prepare 2 14 oz cans of lentils (1 drained and rinsed, 1 undrained)
  12. Get 1/3 cup roughly chopped fresh parsley
  13. Get 1 kosher salt to taste

Instructions to make pasta and lentil soup:
  1. heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat, add the onion,garlic and pepper flakes and cook,stirring occasionally,until the onion is slightly soft,about 2 minutes. Remove 1 tbsp of the flavored olive oil and set aside. Add tomatoe paste and cook,stirring, 2 more minutes. Add chicken broth, 4 cups water and the cheese rind if using. Bring to a boil, then add the pasta and cook, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes. Add lentils along with the liquid from one of the cans; reduce heat to medium low and simmer until the pasta is tender, 10-12 more minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, which 1 tbsp parsley and 1 tbsp of water into the reserved flavored oil. Stir 1/4 cup parmesan cheese and the remaining parsley into the soup; remove the cheese rind(if using) and add salt to taste. Ladle into bowls; drizzle with flavored olive oil and top with remaining parmesan cheese. serve with bread
