Steps to make Tex Mex Mac N Cheese with Meatballs:
  1. Mix the mince beef with salt and pepper to taste, 1 tsp paprika and 1 tsp cumin, then roll into small balls
  2. Saut√© the diced onion and garlic with some salt until translucent, add the diced peppers and soften
  3. Add the remaining spices and herbs and cook through for 2 minutes until fragrant
  4. Add tinned tomatoes and then refill the tin with water and add that too
  5. Add both oxo cubes and stir until dissolved
  6. Add the black beans and cook for 5 mins
  7. Add the dried pasta and stir often until 50% cooked
  8. Then add the cottage cheese and Philadelphia and stir through until combined
  9. Simmer and reduce for 5 minutes stirring often
  10. Meanwhile brown the meatballs
  11. Place the meatballs on top of the pasta and sauce, and grate over the cathedral city cheese
  12. Pop under the grill until melted and bubbly
  13. Sprinkle over chopped spring onions and serve
