The ingredients needed to make EASY 30 minute Broccoli cheddar soup:
  1. Make ready 1/3 cup real butter
  2. Take 1 purple onion chopped fine
  3. Get 4 cloves garlic minced fine
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup flour
  5. Make ready 2 cups chicken broth
  6. Get 3/4 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  8. Make ready 3 cups half and half or whole milk
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  10. Prepare 1 large broccoli crown cut into small florets
  11. Get 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (grate it off the block for better melting)

Steps to make EASY 30 minute Broccoli cheddar soup:
  1. Put butter onion, broccoli, garlic and spices and sauté for about 3-4 minutes. Mix in the flour forming a roux and whisk until golden brown.
  2. Reduce heat to medium low and slowly whisk in half and half and the chicken broth. Simmer 20 minutes uncovered then stir in the cheese. Pairs well with homemade rolls or garlic knots and a side salad.
