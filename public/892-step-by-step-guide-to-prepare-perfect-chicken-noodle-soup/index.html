The ingredients needed to make Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Get 1 Whole chicken
  2. Take 1 cup Chopped, celery
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Chopped, onion
  4. Make ready Salt & Pepper
  5. Take 1/4 tsp Celery seed
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Chicken seasoning
  7. Prepare 10 oz Package of baby carrots
  8. Get 1 packages Flat egg noodles
Steps to make Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Place chicken in a large stew pot or crockpot. Cover with water and add salt. Bring to boil.
  2. Turn heat off and let sit for an hour. Remove bones with a slotted spoon or tongs. If need be let the pot simmer to remove meat.
  3. Season to taste; add onion, celery, celery seed, and chicken seasoning. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add carrots and cook till tender. Add noodles and simmer for another 10-15 mins. Until tender.
