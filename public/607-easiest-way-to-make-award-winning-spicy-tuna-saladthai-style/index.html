The ingredients needed to make Spicy tuna salad(Thai-style):
  1. Make ready 100 g Tuna chunks in mineral water
  2. Get 2 Shallots, thinly sliced
  3. Take 1 Lime juice
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp Fish sauce
  5. Prepare 0.5 tbsp Brown sugar
  6. Take 1 Culantros, thinly sliced
  7. Make ready 3-5 Thai chilli or Bird’s eye chilli, coarsely chopped(Beware of the spiciness!)

Steps to make Spicy tuna salad(Thai-style):
  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. Use sieve to remove excessive fluid from tuna chunks. Transfer to a container.
  3. For the sauce, combine lime juice, fish sauce, and brown sugar. Mix well before adding Thai chilli and Culantro.
  4. Combine tuna and the sauce.
  5. Thai people usually serve with steamed rice, boiled egg and fresh vegetables e.g. cucumber, Chinese cabbage, carrots.
