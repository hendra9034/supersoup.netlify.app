The ingredients needed to make Patricia's Creamy Broccoli and Cheese soup.:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup Butter
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup flour
  3. Get 4 cup milk
  4. Take 4 cup Chicken stock
  5. Get 3 clove Garlic chopped
  6. Take 1/2 cup Onion chopped
  7. Prepare 1 Pepper to taste
  8. Take 1 Salt to taste
  9. Take 2 tbsp Worchestershire Sauce
  10. Get 1 dash Hot Sauce
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup Parsley
  12. Get 1 each Chicken Bullion
  13. Prepare 2 cup Shredded 3 cheese blend
  14. Take 1 cup sour cream
  15. Make ready 1 packages frozen broccoli
  16. Prepare 2 carrots chopped
  17. Make ready 2 potatoes ,peeled and chopped
Steps to make Patricia's Creamy Broccoli and Cheese soup.:
  1. Saute onion,garlic,carrots in butter until onion is translucent.
  2. Slowly add flour and stir into a paste
  3. Add milk ,stock and simmer on med heat
  4. Add seasonings and sauces and continue to simmer and stir.
  5. In separate pot, boil broccoli and potatoes until soft.
  6. When soft, add some of your soup and mix in blender until it turns into a soft cream. ( This step is our preference, but you can leave some broccoli whole if you like, but mash the potatoes)
  7. Add broc/potato cream to soup and blend thoroughly. Lower heat.
  8. Add sour cream and cheese and stir until blended.
  9. Garnish to taste, enjoy!
