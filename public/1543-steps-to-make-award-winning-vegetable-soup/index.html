The ingredients needed to make Vegetable soup:
  1. Make ready 10 pieces medium size potatoes
  2. Get 1/2 kg chicken breast
  3. Make ready 10 pieces medium size carrots
  4. Make ready 3 medium bulbs onion
  5. Get 1/2 cup peas
  6. Take 5 medium size tomatoes
  7. Make ready 10 medium size red bell peppers
  8. Make ready 1/2 tbsp curry powder
  9. Take 10 stock cubes
  10. Get 3 seasoning powder
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions to make Vegetable soup:
  1. Wash tomatoes,onion and pepper,grind and cook
  2. Fry with ½ cup vegetable oil
  3. Wash and dice onions,carrots and potatoes
  4. Add required amount of water and potatoes
  5. Add peas and chicken breast
  6. When the vegetables are soft then add carrots curry powder,stock cubes,and seasoning powderadd and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Serve with rice or pasta of your choice
