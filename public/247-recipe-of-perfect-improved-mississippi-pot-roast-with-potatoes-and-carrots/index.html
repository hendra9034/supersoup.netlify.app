The ingredients needed to make Improved Mississippi Pot Roast with Potatoes and Carrots:
  1. Make ready 3 lb. English Cut, Beef Shoulder Roast
  2. Get 1/3 cup sliced pepperoncini destemmed, plus 3 tbsp.juice
  3. Make ready 1 large sliced onion
  4. Make ready 3 crushed garlic cloves
  5. Make ready 1 cup baby carrots
  6. Get 1 packet Hidden Vallley Ranch
  7. Make ready 1 packet French Onion soup and dip mix
  8. Take 1 packet McCormick's mushroom gravy
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup water, plus 3 tbsps. Worcestershire
  10. Take 1 stick Butter
  11. Take 1 lb. large diced, whole potatoes with skin
Steps to make Improved Mississippi Pot Roast with Potatoes and Carrots:
  1. 3 pound Beef Roast
  2. Slice Onions and Pepperoncini with added pickling juice, get your packettes together.
  3. Put the roast in the bottom of your crockpot. Top with the powdered packets, add water and worcestireshire, lay the stick of butter. Add the carrots, onions, garlic, and pepperoncini.
  4. Chop the poatotoes and layer them.in.
  5. 8 hours, plus on "low" crockpot setting.
