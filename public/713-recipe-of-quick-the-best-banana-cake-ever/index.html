The ingredients needed to make The best Banana Cake ever:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1/2-2/3 cup sugar
  6. Take 1/2 cup oil
  7. Take 3 ripe bananas (see tip)
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Prepare 50 g dark chocolate chips

Instructions to make The best Banana Cake ever:
  1. Preheat your oven (150º C or 300º F)
  2. Prepare a baking pan: either put some baking paper inside or just cover with butter and flour. I use an 8 inch pan, but you can also use a loaf pan.
  3. In a bowl mix the flour with the baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar.
  4. Mash the bananas with a fork, a spoon, or whatever suits you!
  5. Add the bananas to your mix. Add the oil and the eggs and mix well.
  6. Add the chocolate chips and mix once more.
  7. Put the mix in your prepared pan (sprinkle some more chocolate chips on top if you wish) and put it in the preheated oven, for an hour.
