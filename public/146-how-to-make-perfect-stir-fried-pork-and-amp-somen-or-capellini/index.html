The ingredients needed to make Stir-fried Pork & Somen (OR Capellini):
  1. Get 80-100 g Thinly Sliced Pork *cut into small pieces
  2. Prepare 1 small clove Garlic *finely chopped
  3. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon Sesame Oil
  4. Get 1 thick Spring Onions *thinly sliced diagonally
  5. Get Some additional Vegetables *Today I added Carrot and Shiitake Mushrooms
  6. Take Salt & Pepper
  7. Take 1 serving Somen Noodles OR Capellini (Angel Hair Pasta)
  8. Make ready 3-4 tablespoons Mentsuyu *OR use the Sauce below
  9. Take Shichimi (Japanese Chilli Spice Mix) *optional
  10. Prepare Sauce
  11. Prepare 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  12. Take 1 tablespoon Water
  13. Get 2/3 tablespoon (2 teaspoons) Mirin
  14. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon Dashi Powder

Steps to make Stir-fried Pork & Somen (OR Capellini):
  1. Combine all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Heat Sesame Oil in a frying pan over medium heat, cook Pork, Garlic and additional Vegetables that require longer time to cook, such as Carrot. Lightly season with Salt & Pepper.
  3. Meanwhile, cook Somen (OR Capellini) until firmly cooked, and drain. *Note: I recommend to rinse Somen to remove starch, then drain well.
  4. Add Spring Onion and any Vegetables that can be cooked quickly to the frying pan, add the sauce and combine, then add drained Somen (OR Capellini) and mix to combine.
  5. Sprinkle with some Shichimi (Japanese Chilli Spice Mix) if you like it, and enjoy.
