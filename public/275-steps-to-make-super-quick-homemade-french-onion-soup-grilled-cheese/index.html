The ingredients needed to make French onion soup grilled cheese:
  1. Take 1 onion
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp red wine
  4. Make ready 4 pieces good bread
  5. Get 2 tbsp mayo
  6. Prepare 1/2 lb gruyere cheese, thinly sliced

Steps to make French onion soup grilled cheese:
  1. Heat 1 tbsp butter over medium heat until fully melted
  2. Add in onions, stirring every few minutes until they become deep golden brown, around 30-35 min
  3. Add red wine to deglaze the pan, scraping all the bits off the bottom.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and let cool slightly
  5. Spread mayo on one side of each piece of bread
  6. Put onions and gruyere on the unmayoed side, and top with another piece of bread
  7. Melt remain butter in a skillet. Put your sandwich in when the pan is very hot
  8. Flip and brown the other side
