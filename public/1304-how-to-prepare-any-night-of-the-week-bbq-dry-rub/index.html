The ingredients needed to make BBQ Dry Rub:
  1. Get 1.5 tbsp quality smoked paprika,
  2. Prepare 1 heaped tbsp soft dark brown sugar,
  3. Take 1/2 tbsp mustard powder,
  4. Take 2 tsp garlic powder,
  5. Prepare 1 tsp ground black pepper corns,
  6. Take 1 tsp corriander seeds,
  7. Prepare 1 tsp cayenne pepper,
  8. Get 1 tsp cumin powder,
  9. Make ready 1 tsp celery salt,
  10. Take 1 tsp salt,

Steps to make BBQ Dry Rub:
  1. Add the black pepper corns and corriander seeds to a mortar and pestle and crush then grind then up into a coarse powder.
  2. Add them into a bowl along with the brown sugar and mix. Next add in all the remaining ingredients a d mix together well.
  3. Rub well into your chosen meat and get it completely covered all over then place on a plate or tray and leave to marinate for at least two hours or ideally overnight to allow the flavours to absorb into the meat.
  4. Next you can cook it at leave as it is or baste in BBQ sauce to finish. See my BBQ sauce recipe if you'd like to try it out.
