The ingredients needed to make Tomato Soup For Weight Loss:
  1. Take 6 tomatoes
  2. Get 1 carrot
  3. Take 2.5 cups water
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp black salt
  5. Get 1/3 tsp black pepper
  6. Take 2 tsp chopped coriander and garlic (optional)
Instructions to make Tomato Soup For Weight Loss:
  1. Peel and wash tomatoes and carrot.Cut the carrot into small pieces and tomatoes into halves (so that tomatoes can be easily peeled after boiling).
  2. Take a pressure cooker, tranfer both vegetables, chopped coriander and ginger, water and salt and pepper in it.
  3. Now cook it on medium flame till 3-4 whistles blow.
  4. Once it's done, open the lid, let it cool down little bit, take out the peels of tomatoes using tongs(chimta).
  5. Use hand blender to blend it,
  6. Relish hot tomato carrot soup which will surely help you to lose weight and make you feel energetic.
