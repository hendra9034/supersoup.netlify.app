The ingredients needed to make Jaggery Rice:
  1. Take 1 cup : Basmati rice
  2. Get 1 cup : jaggery crushed
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup : milk
  4. Take 2-3 : Green cardamoms
  5. Prepare 15-20 : almonds
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup : coconut chunks
  7. Prepare 15-20 : pistachio
  8. Take 1 tbspn : fennel seeds
  9. Prepare 1 tbspn : oil
Steps to make Jaggery Rice:
  1. Soak rice for 20 to 30 minutes and then boil and strain the water and keep aside
  2. In a pot add milk and jaggery and cook until jaggery melts and set aside
  3. In a pot add oil and add green cardamom, fennel seeds and mix well
  4. Add almonds, pistachio and coconut chunks into it and saute for few minutes
  5. Now add melted jaggery and milk mixture into it and mix well
  6. Add boiled rice into it, mix gently and cook until liquid dries up
  7. Steam cook on low flame for few minutes. Jaggery rice ready to be served, garnish with nuts
