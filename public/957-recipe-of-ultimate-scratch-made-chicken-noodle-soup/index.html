The ingredients needed to make Scratch made Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Get 2 each chicken legs and thighs, skin on, bone in
  2. Take 1 celery stalk, with leaves, cut in half
  3. Prepare 1 carrot, unpeeled, cut in half
  4. Prepare 1/2 large onion cut into wedges
  5. Get 1-2 springs parsley
  6. Prepare 4 1/2 cups water, boxed chicken stock, or any combination
  7. Make ready For the soup:
  8. Take 6 cups chicken broth from stock pot
  9. Take 1 cup peeled sliced carrot
  10. Make ready 1 cup sliced celery
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup diced onion
  12. Get 8 whole allspice berries
  13. Prepare Diced, cooked chicken
  14. Take 1 1/2 cups wide egg noodles
  15. Get to taste Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Scratch made Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. To make the stock: in a stock pot, place chicken, celery, carrot, onion, parsley and broth, water, or a combination of the two, to equal 4 1/2 cups. Bring to a boil, uncovered, reduce heat to simmer and cover. Simmer for 45 to 50 minutes.
  2. Remove chicken from pot to cool. Strain and measure the broth, adding water to equal 6 cups. Wipe out stock pot.
  3. In the stock pot, add the stock sliced carrots and celery, onion, salt and allspice. Bring to a boil, uncovered. Cover and reduce to simmer. Simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Uncover add noodles and chicken. Bring to a boil, and boil until noodles are tender, 7 to 10 minutes. Adjust seasoning.
