The ingredients needed to make Holodnik cold soup:
  1. Take 4-5 large beets
  2. Take 6 large eggs, hard boiled
  3. Make ready 2.5 large cucumbers
  4. Take Green onions
  5. Take Fresh dill
  6. Get 500 g sour cream
  7. Get White vinegar
  8. Get Brown hot mustard
  9. Prepare Salt

Instructions to make Holodnik cold soup:
  1. Boil beets until ready, peel and shred
  2. Chop boiled eggs, add to beets
  3. Chop cucumbers, add to mixture
  4. Add chopped green onions and fresh dill (as much as desired)
  5. Add 3 - 3.2 L of cooled boiled water
  6. Mix in sour cream, then add salt, mustard and vinegar to desired taste
  7. Let soup sit in a fridge for a couple hours. Enjoy
