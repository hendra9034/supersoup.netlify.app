Instructions to make Cream of mushroom soup:
  1. In a pot, put your butter with the white onions and wait for the onion to soften and get brown
  2. Add your coriander and basil, then add your spices
  3. Add your cooking cream and wait for all ingredients to cook together for a while
  4. Add your milk/water and your secret ingredients if you want
  5. Add your mushrooms and let it all simmer a bit
  6. Note: if you used water and felt you needed it to be thicker but not use more cream, you can mix 1/4 cup of cold water with flour in a separate bowl and add it to your soup.
