The ingredients needed to make Thai Beef Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 piece rump steak
  2. Take 1 tbsp cooking oil
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp fish sauce
  4. Take Good sprinkling of black pepper
  5. Make ready Chilli flakes a sprinkling depends on how hot you like it
  6. Prepare For the salad
  7. Get Baby gem lettuce finely shredded
  8. Make ready Handful cherry tomatoes quartered
  9. Prepare Celery stick sliced, keep the leaves for garnish
  10. Take Handful bean sprouts
  11. Make ready Half a cucumber sliced seeds removed
  12. Get 1 red onion finely sliced
  13. Make ready 3 spring onions sliced
  14. Get Handful cooked noodles
  15. Make ready Handful chopped fresh coriander
  16. Take The Sauce
  17. Take 2 tbsp brown sugar
  18. Take Juice of a large lemon or preferably a lime
  19. Get Chilli flakes to your taste
  20. Prepare Fresh Birdseye chillis sliced finely to your taste
  21. Make ready 2 cloves garlic finely diced
  22. Get Large splash of fish sauce
  23. Prepare 1-2 tsp grounded toasted rice, optional (2 tbsp of jasmine rice toasted on the pan until golden brown then blend it in the herb grinder)
  24. Get Optional tsp of chilli jam

Steps to make Thai Beef Salad:
  1. First of all prepare the steak, rub on the fish sauce, chilli flakes a oil! Leave to marinate, it can be left over night for an even better flavour.
  2. Whilst the steak is marinating, chop up all the salad veggies, warm through some noodles in hot water, being careful not to over cook them, then drain and leave to cook. Put all the salad into a large bowl, then add the noodles once there cooled.
  3. Make the sauce, squeeze the lemon or lime into a bowl, add fresh chilli and dried chilli flakes, garlic, chilli jam, sugar and fish sauce! Give it a good stir so the sugar dissolves!
