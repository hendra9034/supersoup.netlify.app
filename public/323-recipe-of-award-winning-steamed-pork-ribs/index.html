The ingredients needed to make Steamed Pork Ribs:
  1. Get Pork Ribs (cut into small pcs)
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup black beans (washed)
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp corn starch
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  7. Prepare 1/2 tbsp soya sauce
  8. Prepare 2 gloves of garlic (minced)
  9. Prepare 1 inch ginger (minced)
  10. Make ready small piece of orange peel (thinly chopped)
  11. Get 1 red chilli
  12. Prepare 1 spring onion (chopped)

Steps to make Steamed Pork Ribs:
  1. Wash the black beans and soak it in water.
  2. Mix pork with 1tbsp corn starch in a bowl. Rinse the ribs in water and massage the ribs and clean the ribs.
  3. Pat dry the pork with kitchen towel.
  4. Add oyster sauce, salt, sugar, soya sauce, ginger, garlic, orange peel. and mix them well with the pork.
  5. Chop the black bean and mix it into the pork as well.
  6. Add 2 tbsp of corn starch into the pork and mix.
  7. Place the pork evenly on a flat plate and place red chilli on top, steam it. (make sure the water covers the holder and reach the bottom of the plate).
  8. On high heat, steam for 20 mins.
  9. Serve with spring onions.
