The ingredients needed to make Smoked Sausage and Prawn Paella:
  1. Get 1 onion, chopped or 1 cup frozen chopped onion
  2. Make ready 1-2 cloves garlic, grated or crushed
  3. Make ready Pinch saffron, soaked in 1-2 tbsp boiling water
  4. Take 1 cup short grain/ paella rice
  5. Make ready 1/2 Mattesons smoked sausage, sliced
  6. Get 1 cup frozen prawns, defrosted
  7. Get 1 cup frozen sliced peppers
  8. Get 1/2 cup frozen peas
  9. Get 1/2 cup frozen broad beans
  10. Make ready Salt and pepper
  11. Make ready 2-3 tbsp Olive oil
Instructions to make Smoked Sausage and Prawn Paella:
  1. Heat olive oil in a heavy pan.
  2. Saute the onions until golden.
  3. Add the peppers and the garlic and sauté for another 2-3 minutes
  4. Add the sausage, broad beans, rice, some salt, 2 cups of water (or ham/chicken stock) and saffron water.
  5. Bring to the boil then cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Remove lid, turn off the heat and throw in the peas and the drained defrosted prawns. Stir gently, season, stir again.
  7. Leave to stand for 5 minutes.
  8. Season and stir.
  9. Serve.
