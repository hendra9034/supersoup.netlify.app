The ingredients needed to make Lentil Soup:
  1. Take 500 grams brown Lentils
  2. Make ready 1/4 liter chicken stock
  3. Prepare 2 bay leaves
  4. Take 1 parsley
  5. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  6. Get 1 medium onion
  7. Get 1 garlic
  8. Prepare 2 small carrots
  9. Get 1 small tomatoe, chopped
  10. Get 2 tsp sweet paprika
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp cumin, ground
  12. Prepare 50 grams rice
  13. Prepare 50 ml sherry

Instructions to make Lentil Soup:
  1. place lentils with stock or water, bay leaves & parsley in sauspan.
  2. Fry onion in frying pan for 5 min
  3. Add garlic, carrot, tomato &red pepper. fry for 5 min. season with salt, paprica, cumin.
  4. add mixture & rice to soup. simmer for 45 min, add sherry & serve
