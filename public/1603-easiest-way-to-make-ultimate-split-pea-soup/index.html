The ingredients needed to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. Get 3 cup dried split peas
  2. Make ready 7 cup water
  3. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  4. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  5. Take 2 medium carrots, grated
  6. Prepare 1 small potato, thinly sliced (or sweet potato)
  7. Prepare 1 tsp dry mustard
  8. Make ready 2 cup onion, minced
  9. Get 5 clove garlic, minced
  10. Get 3 stalks celery, chopped
  11. Prepare 4 tbsp red wine vinegar

Steps to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. In a pot, bring to a boil the split peas, water, bay leaf, and salt. Boil for about 20 minutes.
  2. Add vegetables and spices and simmer for 40 minutes.
  3. Add pepper and vinegar to taste.
