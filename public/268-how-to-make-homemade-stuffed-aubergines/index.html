The ingredients needed to make Stuffed aubergines:
  1. Prepare 1 kg aubergines
  2. Take 120 gr minced beef
  3. Take 1 clove garlic minced
  4. Get 1 spoon parsley
  5. Get 2 eggs
  6. Take 60 gr grated cheese (parmesan/pecorino)
  7. Prepare Breadcrumbs
  8. Take Oil salt and pepper

Steps to make Stuffed aubergines:
  1. After removing the stalks from the aubergines, cut them in half, lengthwise.
  2. Sprinkle with salt and leave to rest for a few hours, so that they lose the vegetation water (bitter).
  3. Wash them, dry them with a cloth and fry them in boiling oil, turning them a couple of times.
  4. After 10 minutes, remove them from the heat, with a spoon hollow out part of the pulp, which you place (keep) in a bowl, to mix it with minced meat, minced garlic and parsley, with eggs and pecorino cheese; season with salt and pepper, mix everything properly
  5. With this filling, stuff the eggplant boats
  6. Now, place them neatly in a baking pan, just wet the bottom with a drizzle of oil, sprinkle the filling with breadcrumbs and put in the oven (already hot at 200 °) to brown them. Serve hot.
