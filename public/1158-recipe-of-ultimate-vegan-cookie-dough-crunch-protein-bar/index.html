The ingredients needed to make Vegan Cookie Dough Crunch Protein Bar:
  1. Get 63 g dark chocolate 85%
  2. Make ready 63 g puffed rice crisps
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp natural crunchy peanut butter
  4. Get 92 g ground almonds
  5. Take 43 g oat flour
  6. Get 4 tbsp vegan chocolate protein
  7. Take 2 tbsp maple syrup
  8. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 1.5 tbsp chopped dark chocolate 85%
Instructions to make Vegan Cookie Dough Crunch Protein Bar:
  1. Line a 4” by 7” tray with grease proof paper and set a side.
  2. Melt the 36g of dark chocolate in a bowl over hot water until fully melted.
  3. In a bowl combine the peanut butter and puffed rice
  4. Add your melted chocolate to the rice mix and mix thouroughly to encorporate all the chocolate.
  5. Arrange in the lined tray and press down flat.
  6. Freeze for 20 minutes until firm
  7. To prepare the cooking dough. Mix the oat flour, ground almonds and protein powder in a bowl. Add the vanilla essence, maple syrup and combine to form a paste.
  8. Add a drop of water if mixture is crumbly.
  9. Stir in the chopped chocolate pieces into the mix.
  10. Remove the rice base from the freezer and top with the cookie dough mix. Press the mix down onto the base and return to the freezer.
  11. Allow another 20 minutes to set and remove from the freezer.
  12. Slice into 7 thin bars
