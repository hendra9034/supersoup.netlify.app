The ingredients needed to make Smoked Veal and Split pea soup:
  1. Make ready 500 g smoked veal bones with meat
  2. Take 2 litres water
  3. Prepare 400 g dried green split peas
  4. Prepare 1 large potato or parsnip
  5. Prepare 1 onion
  6. Prepare 2 medium carrots
  7. Prepare 2 medium sweet potatoes
Instructions to make Smoked Veal and Split pea soup:
  1. Place bones (not cut) into 2 litres of water. Bring to the boil.
  2. Simmer for 2 hours.
  3. Take bones out of broth, and cut the meat into chunks. Return meat and bones to the broth, then add in the split peas.
  4. Simmer for 1 hour.
  5. Grate all the vegetables and add to the stock for a further hour (sometimes slightly more). The vegetables should basically fall apart in the stock.
