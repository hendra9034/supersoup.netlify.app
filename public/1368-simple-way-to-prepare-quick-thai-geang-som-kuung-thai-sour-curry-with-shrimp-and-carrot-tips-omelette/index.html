The ingredients needed to make Thai Geang Som Kuung - Thai Sour curry with shrimp and carrot tips omelette üçõ üå∂:
  1. Get 150 g raw kind prawns, unshelled
  2. Take 1/2 Chinese cabbage, chopped into bite size
  3. Make ready 1 handful green beans
  4. Make ready 1 handful baby corn
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Thai red curry paste (see recipe)
  6. Get 3-4 cups water
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp fishsauce
  8. Make ready 2-3 tbsp palm sugar
  9. Make ready 3 tbsp tamarind paste
  10. Get Omelette part
  11. Make ready 1 big handful of young carrot tips, washed, finely chopped (or if you can find Acacia is perfect to use!)
  12. Make ready 2 eggs
  13. Take Pinch salt and pepper
  14. Make ready 2-3 tbsp vegetable cooking oil
Instructions to make Thai Geang Som Kuung - Thai Sour curry with shrimp and carrot tips omelette üçõ üå∂:
  1. Making omelette, mix finely sliced carrot tips with eggs. Seasoning with salt and pepper and beaten until combine. 1. Add some vegetable cooking oil on a medium heat pan, add the egg mixture and spread it out evenly. Once the bottom side cooked, turn over and let the other side cook until golden and crisps. Take it out from the heat and cut into bite size. 1. To make curry; add some red curry paste in a medium heat saucepan, add about 1 cup of water and mix until curry paste dissolved. Add some Chinese cabbage, baby corn and mix well. 1. Seasoning with palm sugar, tamarind paste, fish sauce and bring it to boil. 1. Add some green bean, carrot tips omelette and stir well. Taste it, it should be sweet, sour and salty. Once the sauce start boiling add king prawns in and quick mix until your prawns cooked. 1. Serve and have it with Thai jasmine rice.
