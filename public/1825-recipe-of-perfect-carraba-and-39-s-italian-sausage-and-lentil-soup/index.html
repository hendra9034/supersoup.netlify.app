The ingredients needed to make Carraba's Italian Sausage and Lentil Soup:
  1. Take 1 lb italian sausage
  2. Take 1 large onion, chopped
  3. Take 1 stalk celery, chopped
  4. Get 2 large carrots, chopped
  5. Get 1 small zucchini
  6. Take 6 cup chicken broth
  7. Make ready 2 clove garlic, minced
  8. Get 2 can 14.5 oz diced tomatoes, undrained
  9. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  10. Get 2 cup dried lentils. rinsed
  11. Get 1 black pepper, red pepper flakes, basil, oregano, parsley, thyme to taste

Steps to make Carraba's Italian Sausage and Lentil Soup:
  1. Brown sausage; drain off fat
  2. In a large pot combine all ingredients; bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat; cover
  4. Simmer for about 1 hour or until lentils are tender. Add water; if necessary for desired consistency
  5. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, serve.
