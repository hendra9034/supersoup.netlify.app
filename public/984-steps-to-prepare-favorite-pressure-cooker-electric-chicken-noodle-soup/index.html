The ingredients needed to make Pressure Cooker (Electric) Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup onion
  2. Make ready 1 large garlic clove, minced
  3. Make ready 2 stalks celery
  4. Take 1 medium carrot
  5. Make ready 2 chicken breasts (already cooked)
  6. Take 1 tbsp oil (to brown)
  7. Take 1 tsp fresh ginger, minced
  8. Get 1 tsp salt
  9. Get 1/4 tsp peppercorn
  10. Prepare 1 Bay leaves
  11. Make ready to taste cayenne pepper (I put cayenne in everything)
  12. Take 4 cup chicken broth
  13. Get 1/4 packages egg noodles (up to you how much you want)
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup peas
  15. Prepare 1/2 lemon, juiced (to freshen it up)
Steps to make Pressure Cooker (Electric) Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Cut carrot, onion, and celery to uniform size, sauté in pressure cooker with oil. Add minced garlic
  2. Add minced ginger and seasonings and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Cut chicken breasts into chunks or shred and add to pot. Add chicken broth and set the electric pressure cooker to "soup" for 30-minutes.
  4. I cook the noodles in a separate pot of boiling water, drain. Remove lid from pressure cooker and add the noodles and peas. Cook for a few minutes to heat peas.
  5. Adjust seasoning, add a squeeze of 1/2 lemon and serve.
