The ingredients needed to make Scraggly Soup:
  1. Make ready Soup
  2. Take 2 cups beef trimmings
  3. Make ready 1 large potato
  4. Prepare 1 large onion
  5. Make ready 2 cups chopped smoked Boston butt pork roast
  6. Prepare 1 quart beef broth
  7. Get 1/2 stick butter divided
  8. Take 2 cups baby carrots
  9. Take To taste salt
  10. Make ready To taste ground black pepper
  11. Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  12. Make ready 2 tablespoon rice vinegar
  13. Make ready 1-1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  14. Take 15 ounces canned diced tomatoes
  15. Take 15 ounces canned corn whole kernel
  16. Prepare Cheese option
  17. Prepare To taste grated Parmesan cheese optional
Steps to make Scraggly Soup:
  1. Chop the vegetables, slice the beef, and slice the pork roast.
  2. Heat a pot caramelize the onions, in half the butter. When the onions are caramelized add all the ingredients. Bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer 40 minutes. Serve with cheese, and crusty bread. I hope you enjoy!! Shown below with the cheese option.
