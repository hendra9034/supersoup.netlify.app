The ingredients needed to make Lentil, Kale and Chorizo Soup:
  1. Take 1 lb Sausage, Chorizo
  2. Get 2 cup Lentil
  3. Prepare 3 cup Kale
  4. Get 4 cup Vegetable Stock
  5. Prepare 1 each Medium Onion
  6. Make ready 2 each Carrots
  7. Make ready 2 each Celery
  8. Make ready 3 clove garlic cloves
  9. Make ready 3 tbsp tomato paste
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
Steps to make Lentil, Kale and Chorizo Soup:
  1. Rinse lentils at least 3 times until water runs clear.
  2. Brown Chorizo with olive oil.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Add vegetable stock, tomato paste, and rinsed lentils.
  5. Simmer for at least 90 mins until lentils are tinder.
  6. Add chopped kale and cook for another 30 mins.
