The ingredients needed to make Laksa noodle soup recipe:
  1. Take 4 Dry Red chillies
  2. Get 8 No’s Cashew Nuts (soaked in warm water)
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon Galangal
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon Lemongrass
  5. Take 4 shallots
  6. Make ready 3 Garlic
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon Chilli powder/ paprika
  9. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Coriander powder
  11. Take 1 teaspoon Peanut oil
  12. Take 2 tablespoon peanut oil
  13. Prepare 1.5 cups Rice Noodles
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup chicken
  15. Prepare 3 kaffir lime leaves
  16. Prepare 1/2 lemon (about, 2 teaspoon lemon juice)
  17. Make ready 1/2 cup coconut milk
  18. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  19. Prepare 1 tablespoon coriander leaves
  20. Prepare 4 tablespoon Laksa Paste
  21. Take 2 cups Chicken stock (optional or water)
  22. Make ready 2 Lemon wedges
