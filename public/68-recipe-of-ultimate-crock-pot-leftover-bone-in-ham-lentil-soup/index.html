The ingredients needed to make Crock pot leftover bone in ham lentil soup:
  1. Make ready 1 1/4 cup lentil beans
  2. Make ready 1.5 pound left over bone in ham or less to like
  3. Prepare 5 pieces hickory thick bacon
  4. Get 3 carrots
  5. Prepare 3 red potatoes
  6. Make ready 3 celery sticks
  7. Get 1 red tomato
  8. Prepare 1/4 white onion
  9. Take 1 clove garlic
  10. Take 2 tsp pepper
  11. Take 1 tsp salt
  12. Take 2 leaves epazote herb
  13. Prepare 2-3 cups water
  14. Get Crock pot 6-8 quart oval

Steps to make Crock pot leftover bone in ham lentil soup:
  1. Start by placing lentil beans in a bowl. Soak in water for 1 hour or until you see water absorbed.
  2. Cut potatoes, carrots, celery, onions, garlic. Place in crock pot.
  3. Rinse lentil beans and set on top of vegetables.
  4. Pour in water into crock pot to cover ingredients. Add water to be about 1/4 inch above lentil.
  5. Place bacon, then place cut up ham. Then add salt, and pepper, last add epazote. Cover and set time on crock pot for 4-8 hours. I usually cook overnight and in the morning it smells amazing.
