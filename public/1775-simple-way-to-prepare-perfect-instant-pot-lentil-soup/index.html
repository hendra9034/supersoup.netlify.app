The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Lentil Soup:
  1. Make ready 8 oz. kielbasa (Turkey or pork)
  2. Prepare 1 cup chopped onion
  3. Make ready 1 cup chopped celery
  4. Take 1 cup diced carrots
  5. Get 1 cup diced potatoes
  6. Take 2 Tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  7. Prepare 2 garlic cloves, minced
  8. Get 1 cup uncooked brown lentils
  9. Get 32 oz. beef or chicken broth
  10. Take 28 oz. can petite diced tomatoes
  11. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  12. Get 1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  13. Take 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
Steps to make Instant Pot Lentil Soup:
  1. Slice kielbasa into 1 inch thick pieces
  2. Place kielbasa, onion, celery, carrot, potato, parsley, and garlic into Instant Pot.
  3. Rinse and drain lentils, then add to Instant Pot.
  4. Add broth, tomatoes, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Stir to mix.
  5. Place Instant Pot lid on with pressure release valve sealed.
  6. Cook for 20 minutes on the soup setting.
  7. When done, use the quick release method to release pressure before opening.
