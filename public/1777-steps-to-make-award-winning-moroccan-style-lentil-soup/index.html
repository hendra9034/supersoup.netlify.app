The ingredients needed to make Moroccan Style Lentil Soup:
  1. Take 1 white onion, chopped finely
  2. Prepare 3 celery sticks, chopped finely
  3. Make ready 4-5 tomatoes, grated
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup small black lentils, soaked overnight
  5. Take 1/2 cup white rice
  6. Get 1 tbs sweet paprika
  7. Take 2 tbs chicken consommé powder
  8. Take 1 teaspoon tomato paste (optional)
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  10. Get 1 teaspoon turmeric
  11. Get 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  12. Take 4 tbs Vegetable / Canola oil
  13. Prepare Boiling water
Instructions to make Moroccan Style Lentil Soup:
  1. Use a medium size soup pot
  2. Add grated tomatoes and stir fry for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add chopped celery sticks and stir fry for 1 minute
  4. Add boiling water to cover 3/4 of the pot
  5. Add all spices, stir and cover the pot for 5 minutes
  6. Add rice and lentils and cook on a high heat for 10 minutes
  7. Lower the heat and cook for another 30-40 minutes
  8. Fry onion in canola oil until golden brown
