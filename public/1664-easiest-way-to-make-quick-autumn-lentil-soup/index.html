The ingredients needed to make Autumn Lentil Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 large sweet onion chopped
  2. Prepare 3 Tbsp olive oil
  3. Take 2 garlic cloves chopped
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  5. Prepare 1 cup mixed lentils
  6. Prepare 1 tsp crushed chilies
  7. Take 4 carrots diced
  8. Make ready 4 stalks celery diced
  9. Get 2 bay leaves
  10. Make ready 2 cartons vegetable broth
  11. Make ready 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar

Steps to make Autumn Lentil Soup:
  1. Saute onions in olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and cumin and saute a bit longer until all is starting to turn a golden colour.
  3. Add remaining ingredients, except vinegar and bring to a boil.
  4. Cover and reduce heat to low. Simmer, covered for about 40 minutes.
  5. Uncover and add vinegar. Simmer an additional 10 to 15 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
