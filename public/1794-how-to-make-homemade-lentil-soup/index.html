The ingredients needed to make Lentil soup:
  1. Get 1/2 can lentils
  2. Take 1/2 onion
  3. Make ready 1 clove garlic
  4. Get 1 bunch leek
  5. Make ready 1 tsp ground ginger
  6. Get 1/4 cabbage
  7. Prepare 250 grams tomato
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato paste
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp yogurt
  10. Get 1 liter vegetable stock

Steps to make Lentil soup:
  1. Fry onion, leek and cabbage
  2. Add lentils, garlic, ginger and tomato paste
  3. Add vegetable stock and tomatoes. Lower heat and simmer for 40 mins
