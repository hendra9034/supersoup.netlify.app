The ingredients needed to make Prawn vada:
  1. Take 80 g chana daal
  2. Take 10 pieces of clean prawn
  3. Prepare Curry leaves 5
  4. Get 1 Green chilli
  5. Take 1 Onion small
  6. Get Salt
  7. Get Turmeric powder
  8. Prepare Chilli powder
  9. Prepare Chilli flakes
  10. Take Oil to fry
  11. Take 2 tbsp Flour
  12. Get 1 tbsp Water

Instructions to make Prawn vada:
  1. Wash chana daal and soak it for 30 mins
  2. Mariate cleaned prawn with 1 tsp of salt, 1 tsp of turmeric powder and 1 tsp of chilli powder. Mix well and set it aside.
  3. Now grind the soaked daal into a fine paste.
  4. Add cubed onion, well chopped green chillies and curry leaves. Mix them well.
  5. According to your preference add chilli flakes and salt (remember salt has added to the prawns already). Mix well and keep it aside.
  6. To make flour paste add water step by step to the flour and mix them until it turn into a paste consistency
  7. Now make flat vada using chana daal paste. Then apply sum glour paste and keep a marinated prawn
  8. Fry the vada carefully in medium flame for 5 mins (both side)
