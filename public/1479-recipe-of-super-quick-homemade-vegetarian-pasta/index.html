The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Pasta:
  1. Get 1 onion chopped
  2. Make ready 1 carrot peeled and chopped
  3. Make ready Olive oil
  4. Take Handful mushrooms
  5. Prepare Tomato puree
  6. Make ready 1 can chopped tomatoes
  7. Prepare Pasta of choice
  8. Prepare Salt
  9. Take Parmesan cheese
Instructions to make Vegetarian Pasta:
  1. Chop onions finely and put in sauce pan with olive oil to gently fry.
  2. Peel and chop carrots and add this to the saucepan.
  3. Add mushrooms that are chopped to saucepan
  4. Add tomato puree and mix until combined. Leave for 2 mins.
  5. Add chopped tomato tins. Add salt. Cover and leave to simmer until well thickened.
  6. Meanwhile cook pasta to instructions on packet.
  7. Serve together with parmesan cheese
