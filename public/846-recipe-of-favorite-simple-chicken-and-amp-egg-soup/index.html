The ingredients needed to make Simple Chicken & Egg Soup:
  1. Prepare 4 cup boiling water
  2. Make ready 2 ramen noodles packages (chicken flavor)
  3. Take 15 oz can chicken chunks
  4. Prepare 12 oz can of carrots
  5. Make ready 2 eggs
  6. Get 2 tsp complete seasoning or any you like
  7. Get 1 scallion chopped (optional)
  8. Prepare 1 Hot sauce (optional)
Instructions to make Simple Chicken & Egg Soup:
  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil place noodles in cook for 4 mins, reserve chicken seasoning from ramen noodles packages, after 4 mins included chicken & carrots cook for another 3 minutes.
  2. In a bowl scramble eggs & pour in soup & stir. You will see the soup get thick. Included seasoning & chicken flavor from packages. Boil for 2 mins. Serve with chopped scallion & hot sauce
