The ingredients needed to make Sweet Apple & Snow Fungus Soup:
  1. Prepare 5-6 red dates
  2. Prepare 2 large Fuji apples, peeled, cored, + quartered
  3. Get 1/2 tbsp fresh ginger root, bruised
  4. Prepare 5-6 pieces snow fungus, trimmed
  5. Make ready 7 cups water
