  11. Get 2 eggs
  12. Take 140 g rice

Steps to make Teri-quacki duck with egg fried rice:
  1. Pre heat oven to 200Â°c. Place duck legs on a baking tray and roast for 25-30 minutes. Cook rice following package guidelines.
  2. Dice onion and pepper. Peel and grate garlic (or use a garlic press). Thinly slice the radish.
  3. Heat a spot of oil in a frying pan and add in the peppers, onion, garlic and radish. Stir fry for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add the Chinese 5 spice and the chilli flakes. Stir and cook for 2-3 minutes
  5. Once the duck has fully cooked remove it from the oven and use 2 forks to pull the meat off the bone and shred it.
  6. Add the shredded meat to your frying pan and stir everything together. Add the teriyaki sauce and stir thoroughly.
  7. Heat a little oil to another pan and crack in the eggs. Scramble the eggs until cooked.
  8. Add in cooked rice and soy sauce. Stir thoroughly.
  9. Plate up the rice then place some of the duck and veg mix on top. Sprinkle over chopped coriander and salted peanuts.
