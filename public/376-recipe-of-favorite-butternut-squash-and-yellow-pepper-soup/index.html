The ingredients needed to make Butternut Squash and Yellow Pepper Soup:
  1. Get butternut squash diced
  2. Make ready yellow peppers diced
  3. Make ready I onion finely chopped
  4. Prepare Oliver oil
  5. Make ready tsp chilli flakes
  6. Take Salt and black pepper
  7. Prepare vegetable stock
  8. Get Creme fraiche
  9. Prepare garlic
Instructions to make Butternut Squash and Yellow Pepper Soup:
  1. Drizzle olive oil over the butternut squash, peppers and onion, season and roast in the oven for 40mins
  2. Add a little oil to a large saucepan, fry the garlic for a minute then add the vegetable stock and bring to a boil, add the roasted vegetable along with chilli flakes
  3. Remove from heat and using a hand blender, blend until smooth
  4. Return to the heat then season to taste and serve topped with a little creme fraiche
