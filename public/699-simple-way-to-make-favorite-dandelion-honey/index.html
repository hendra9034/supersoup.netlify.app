The ingredients needed to make Dandelion honey:
  1. Prepare 400 dandelion flowers
  2. Make ready 1,5 l water
  3. Get 2 lemons
  4. Get 1-1,5 kg sugar

Easy to make & produces a great vegan alternative to honey as well as a frugal one. Dandelion Honey is a monofloral honey that is produced from dandelion nectar. Steps to make Dandelion honey:
  1. Step one, collect dandelion flowers ðŸ˜„ It is ideal to collect them a day or two after the rain so that they don't need to be washed, but this only applies if they don't grow by the road. Then clean them from stems and other green parts as much as possible.
  2. Wash the lemons and chop them to slices. Add lemons with dandelions to the water and bring to boil. Lower heat to medium and cook for 15 minutes. Then turn off the heat and let it cool and stand for 24 hours.
  3. After 24 hours, strain the mixture through a cloth. Add sugar to the liquid and cook on low heat for 1,5 - 2 hours, allowing to reduce and thicken to honey consistency. We used brown sugar so our honey is darker than the "classic" one.
  4. Pour cooked honey into jars and close them tightly. You can also turn the jars upside down to make sure they aren't leaking. Enjoy ðŸ˜‹
