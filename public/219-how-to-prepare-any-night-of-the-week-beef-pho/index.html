Steps to make Beef Pho:
  1. In a big pot, add the beef knuckles and water, bring to boil and leave it for 3 mins. Remove the grease stuffs from the soup.
  2. Take the beef out and place it in another pot, add clean water.
  3. Put cinnamon, star anise and cloves into a sachet and put it into the pot.
  4. Add ginger, onion, spring onions to the pot, leave it on medium heat and cook for 30 mins.
  5. Remove the sachet from the pot.
  6. Add noodles into the pot. Once cooked then remove it from the pot.
  7. Place the noodles into the bowl, add beef slices, when you pour the soup over the beef, it will cook through.
  8. Add bean sprouts, spring onion, and pour the soup over them.
  9. Serve with mint leave, thin onions coriander and red chillies for garnishing.
