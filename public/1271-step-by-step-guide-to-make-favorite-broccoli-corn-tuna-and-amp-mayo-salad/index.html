The ingredients needed to make Broccoli, Corn, Tuna & Mayo Salad:
  1. Get 1 head (*about 350g) Broccoli *cut into small florets, thick stem sliced
  2. Make ready 1-2 teaspoons Oil
  3. Prepare 2 pinches Salt
  4. Make ready 2-3 tablespoons Water
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Frozen Corn Kernels
  6. Take 1 can (95 g) Tuna *I used Tuna in Springwater
  7. Get 1/8 Onion *optional, finely chopped
  8. Get Salt & Black Pepper
  9. Make ready 4-5 tablespoons Japanese Mayonnaise

Instructions to make Broccoli, Corn, Tuna & Mayo Salad:
  1. Drizzle Oil over a frying pan and arrange Broccoli pieces. Sprinkle with Water and Salt. Cover with a lid and cook over medium heat for a few minutes.
  2. When the Water in the pan is evaporated, remove the lid, and stir. Then transfer to a mixing bowl. Add Frozen Corn Kernels and mix.
  3. Add drained Tuna and Onion, season with Salt & Pepper, and mix. Finally add Japanese Mayonnaise and combine.
