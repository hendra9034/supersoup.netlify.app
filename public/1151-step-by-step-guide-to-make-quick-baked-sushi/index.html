The ingredients needed to make Baked Sushi:
  1. Prepare 3 cups rice, cooked
  2. Get 200 grams crab sticks
  3. Get 1 bottle Japanese mayonnaise
  4. Prepare 2 T furikake (seaweed, sesame seeds, dried fish, sugar, salt)
  5. Prepare 1 pack crushed cracker
  6. Make ready 3 mangoes, sliced
  7. Prepare 1 T vinegar
  8. Make ready 2 packs seaweed snack
Steps to make Baked Sushi:
  1. Put the rice in a baking pan
  2. Pour the vinegar to the rice and mix well
  3. Sprinkle the furikake seasoning into the rice mixture
  4. Add the sliced mayonnaise, crushed crackers, seaweeds, and mangoes
  5. Put the mayonnaise on top
  6. Put it in the oven and ready to serve
