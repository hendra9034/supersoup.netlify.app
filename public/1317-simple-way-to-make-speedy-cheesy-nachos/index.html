The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Nachos:
  1. Take 16 small corn tortillas
  2. Take Any amount of grated cheddar cheese, depending on how cheesy you want your nachos to be
  3. Get 3 pinches salt
Instructions to make Cheesy Nachos:
  1. Using a pizza cutter, cut the tortillas into sixths.
  2. Lay on a large baking tray, and sprinkle with the grated cheese.
  3. Bake at 180 degrees Celsius until golden brown.
  4. Eat while warm. Serves 6 - 8 people.
