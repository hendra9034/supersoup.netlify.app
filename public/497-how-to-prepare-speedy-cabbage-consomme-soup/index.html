The ingredients needed to make Cabbage Consommé Soup:
  1. Prepare 100 grams Cabbage
  2. Get 2 Wiener sausages
  3. Prepare 2 Consomme cubes
  4. Take 1 tsp Margarine (butter)
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Whole grain mustard
  6. Get 1 dash Coarsely ground black pepper
Instructions to make Cabbage Consommé Soup:
  1. Chop the cabbage and slice the sausages diagonally.
  2. Melt margarine in a pan and cook the sausages. Add the cabbage and cook until they start to char.
  3. Add 600 ml of water and consomme cubes and cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes, then add the mustard and ground black pepper.
