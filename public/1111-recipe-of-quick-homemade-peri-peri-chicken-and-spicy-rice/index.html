The ingredients needed to make Homemade Peri Peri Chicken and Spicy Rice:
  1. Make ready 2 Chicken Breasts
  2. Make ready Peri Peri Marinade
  3. Get 1 Red Onion
  4. Take 1 Sweet Red Pepper
  5. Take 3 Garlic Cloves
  6. Make ready 2 Fresh Red Chillies
  7. Get 1 tbsp Dried Oregano
  8. Take 1 tbsp Hot Paprkia
  9. Prepare 4 tbsp olive oil
  10. Make ready 1 lemon juice
  11. Get 1/2 tsp dried chilli flakes
  12. Make ready Salt
  13. Prepare Pepper
  14. Prepare Spicy Rice
  15. Make ready 1 Red Pepper
  16. Prepare 1 White Onion
  17. Take 150 g Long Grain Rice
  18. Prepare 1 tsp Ground Turmeric
  19. Get 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  20. Prepare 100 g Fresh Peas
  21. Get 300 ml Chicken Stock
  22. Get Oilve oil
  23. Make ready Salt
  24. Take Pepper
Instructions to make Homemade Peri Peri Chicken and Spicy Rice:
  1. To Make the Peri Peri Marinade, roughly dice your sweet pepper, red onion and garlic and sweat off in a pan with oil.
  2. Once softened, remove the seeds from your chillies and chop. Add to the pan alongside the lemon juice, olive oil, hot paprika, oregano and chilli flakes.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, add to a blender and blend until smooth. Set aside and allow to cool slightly.
  4. Marinade the chicken breasts with the peri peri sauce and set aside.
  5. For the spicy rice, dice your red pepper and white onion to a 1cm dice and soften in a pan with olive oil.
  6. Once soft, add your turmeric and smoked paprika and cook for a 1 minute.
  7. Add the rice into the pan and allow to toast for a 1 minute while stirring.
  8. Add the chicken stock and an extra 100ml of water and bring to the boil. Check the seasoning and adjust. Boil for 5 minutes
  9. After 5 minutes, stir in the peas and switch off the heat. Cover with a lid and allow all the liquid to absorb into the rice until cooked.
  10. Pre heat your oven to 180 degrees Celsius. While the rice is cooking, seal your marinated chicken in a hot griddle pan, sealing all sides. Form a criss cross affect and finish in the oven until the chicken reaches 75 degrees.
  11. Once cooked, slice the chicken or leave whole and serve with the rice, a choice of salad and extra peri peri sauce if you want it a little hotter.
