The ingredients needed to make Lou's noodleless chicken noodle soup:
  1. Prepare medium or large boneless/skinless chicken breasts
  2. Take Swanson's chicken broth
  3. Prepare chicken top-ramen seasoning packet
  4. Prepare large white onion chopped
  5. Prepare cilantro chopped
  6. Take celery stalks chopped
  7. Prepare frozen peas and carrots mix
  8. Make ready heaping fork full of minced garlic
  9. Get soy sauce
  10. Get Salt and pepper

Steps to make Lou's noodleless chicken noodle soup:
  1. Rub top-ramen seasoning on the top of chicken breasts.
  2. Place in Crock-Pot with enough water to almost reach the top of the chicken.
  3. Cook in Crock-Pot on high until chicken is fully cooked and juicy.
  4. Place all of the chopped ingredients including spices, soy sauce, minced garlic and broth into a pot and cook on medium heat.
  5. Shred chicken and add to broth mixture. Add as much or as little of the cooked chicken breast to your liking.
  6. Allow to simmer for 20 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve and enjoy with parsley or cilantro garnish!

Can chicken noodle soup be frozen? Yes, for up to three months. Chill it fully in the fridge before freezing.
