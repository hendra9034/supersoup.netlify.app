The ingredients needed to make Homemade potato soup:
  1. Get 5 large potatoes
  2. Take 1 celery
  3. Make ready 1 can mixed vegetables
  4. Make ready 1 dash oregano
  5. Get 1 tsp Minced onion
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Minced garlic
  7. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken
  8. Make ready 1 can chicken stock

Steps to make Homemade potato soup:
  1. Cut your potatoes into little cubes and add them to the pot with water only fill half way pour in chicken stock.
  2. Pour in mixed vegetables and seasonings. And the can of cream of chicken crumble your bacon and grease add to pot.
  3. Then cook on stove top on low for 2 hours or crockpot for 4 hrs.
