Instructions to make Sesame and Palm Sugar Energy Balls:
  1. In a low heat frying pan, add sesame seed in and dry roast the sesame seeds for about 5 minutes then add coconut in and keep stirring until the coconut turns golden and sesame seeds releasing the oil.
  2. In another saucepan add palm sugar in on medium heat, you can a tiny bit of water in to help melt the sugar. Keep stir until palm sugar melt. Keep on low heat.
  3. Add your toasted sesame seeds and coconut into palm sugar saucepan, mix well. Once all the mixture corporate turn the heat off. Leave it to cool down.
  4. Form into small balls and keep in the container for a yummy snack.
