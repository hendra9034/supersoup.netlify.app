The ingredients needed to make Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 3 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 5 potatoes, peeled & cubed
  3. Take 5 carrots, peeled & sliced
  4. Get 2 cups cauliflour, chopped
  5. Get 1 yellow onion, sliced
  6. Make ready 2 garlic cloves
  7. Make ready 3 celery sticks, sliced
  8. Make ready 1 can whole kernel corn
  9. Get 32 oz chicken broth
  10. Get 2 tbsp garlic salt
  11. Take 1 tbsp dried minced onion
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp oregano
  13. Make ready to taste salt & pepper
  14. Prepare 16 oz box elbow macaroni noodles (or whichever you prefer)
Steps to make Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Start off by boiling chicken breasts in a deep soup pot, add garlic cloves. (Don't fill completely with water. Only enough to boil chicken)
  2. While chicken is boiling, peel potatoes & cube in medium size cubes, set aside. Then peel & slice carrots, celery, onion & cauliflour; set aside until chicken is done boiling.
  3. When chicken is done boiling, remove the breast from the water, place on a plate to cool down. Then add prepared veggies to the same water used to boil the breasts.
  4. Once cooled a little, shred chicken with a fork & return chicken back to the pot with veggies.
  5. Add chicken broth, can of corn (do not drain) & seasonings. Boil for another hour or until veggies are tender.
  6. While soup is boiling, take this time to boil noodles according to directions on the box, drain & set aside to add later to your own personal bowl of soup. (I do this because I have picky eaters in my house who don't like noodles in their soup) or (if you want) you can add your noodles to the soup the last 10-15 mins. Serve with crackers (optional) & enjoy!
