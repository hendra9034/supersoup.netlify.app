The ingredients needed to make Rice Cake with Red Bean Paste:
  1. Make ready 6 inches cake pan, steam in high heat for 25 minutes
  2. Prepare Ingredients
  3. Prepare 180 g Rice flour
  4. Take 70 g Glutinous rice flour
  5. Make ready 20 g Butter
  6. Take 18 g Sugar
  7. Get Red bean or chestnut paste
  8. Make ready 145-150 ml milk
Steps to make Rice Cake with Red Bean Paste:
  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Get a cake pan, covered by banking paper
  3. Put the rice flour, glutinous rice flour, butter and sugar in a mixing bowl
  4. Run the butter into the mixture and mux even
  5. Pour in part of the milk
  6. Hand mix them until moisture and crumple. But not stick together
  7. Flat the red bean paste to a round shape about the same size of cake pan
  8. Sieve the flour mixture to a bowl
  9. Then put the sieved mixture into the cake pan. At about half brush even. Not to Pat them compact. Keep them loose
  10. Lay the red bean paste on the mixture
  11. Continue sieve another half if the flour mixture into the cake pan. When finish, brush the top level an+ flat
  12. Put the cake pan into a steamer to steam at high heat for 25 minutes
  13. Ready to serve
