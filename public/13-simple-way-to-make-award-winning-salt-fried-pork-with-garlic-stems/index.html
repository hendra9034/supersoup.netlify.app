The ingredients needed to make Salt fried pork with garlic stems:
  1. Take 100 g streaky belly pork, without skin
  2. Prepare 150 g garlic stem
  3. Take 3 tbsp cooking oil or lard
  4. Take 1 tbsp Sichuan chilli bean paste
  5. Take 1 tbsp fermented black bean, rinsed and drained
  6. Take 1 tsp sugar
  7. Make ready 2 tsp soy sauce
