Instructions to make Cabbage and beef soup:
  1. Brown ground beef with garlic, onion, bell pepper, salt and pepper. Drain.
  2. Add diced tomato, tomato sauce, chicken broth, pork broth and caraway seed to meat mixture. Bring to boil.
  3. Add cabbage, carrots, and celery to mixture. Simmer on low for 30 minutes.
