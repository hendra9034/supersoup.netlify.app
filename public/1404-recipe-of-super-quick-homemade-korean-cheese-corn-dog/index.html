The ingredients needed to make Korean Cheese Corn Dog:
  1. Prepare 400 g bread flour
  2. Get 60 g sugar
  3. Take 10 g salt
  4. Get 8 g yeast
  5. Get 300 ml warm water
  6. Get Breadcrumbs (2-3 slices) *twist it up with parsley
  7. Prepare Frankfurter sausage
  8. Make ready sticks Cheese
  9. Make ready Skewers

Steps to make Korean Cheese Corn Dog:
  1. In a bowl, add flour, sugar and salt, and mix well
  2. In another bowl add yeast and warm water and mix well
  3. Put wet mix in dry mix, and mix with a spatula.
  4. Cover with cling film, and let poof for 2 hours
  5. Cut sausage and cheese in half. And then put together on skewer. (Half sausage on bottom, half cheese on top)
  6. Heat oil on high medium heat. When ready to cook change to 140-150 degrees.
  7. Coat skewers in bread dough. Then roll in bread crumbs
  8. Fry until golden brown - Leave to drain
  9. Ready to eat. - *Optional you can coat in sugar for korean style, and add some ketchup and honey mustard
