The ingredients needed to make Cock-a-leekie Soup:
  1. Get 1 medium free range chicken
  2. Take Enough chicken stock to cover ingredients
  3. Prepare Half a leek
  4. Make ready 1 medium onion
  5. Get 1 handful curly kale
  6. Make ready 1 handful baby spinach
  7. Prepare 100 g pearl barley
  8. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  9. Make ready Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make Cock-a-leekie Soup:
  1. Soak the barley for about 4 hours
  2. Joint the chicken, slice leeks, Chop onions, shred kale
  3. Sweat off the onions until soft
  4. Add chicken to the pot, fry for a couple of minutes but without colour
  5. Pour enough chicken stock to cover the chicken, add bay leaf and barley
  6. Bring to boil and then simmer until chicken and barley is cooked.
  7. When chicken is cooked, remove from soup, take the chicken meat off the bones, dispose of bones and put back in soup
  8. Add the kale and sliced leeks and cook out for a few minutes
  9. Finish with baby spinach and season to taste
