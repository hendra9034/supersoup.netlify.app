The ingredients needed to make Short Rib Ragu Pasta:
  1. Make ready 3-4 pieces short rib beef
  2. Get 500 g dry tagliatelle
  3. Take 800 ml beef stock
  4. Get Can tomatoes
  5. Get 1/2 tbs smoked paprika
  6. Take Onion, Garlic, Carrots, Celery
  7. Take 1/2 bottle red wine
  8. Take Thyme
Steps to make Short Rib Ragu Pasta:
  1. Prepare short ribs by trimming excess fat, connective tissue and skin
  2. Salt each side and leave overnight or for a few hours
  3. Pad meat dry and season with pepper, brown meat on high heat and set aside
  4. Add diced celery, onion and carrot to the same pot with a pinch of salt
  5. Add garlic and fry for another minute until vegetables are caramelized nicely
  6. Add can of tomatoes, smoked paprika and reduce by half and deglaze fond on bottom of the pot
  7. Add red wine, beef stock, thyme and bay leaves to pot
  8. Re-add short ribs and place in oven at 180 degrees celsius
  9. Cook until extremely tender (approx 3-4hrs), add equal parts stock and wine into pot if liquid levels drop before meat is ready
  10. Remove meat and shred removing any excess fat or connective tissue
  11. Re-add meat to sauce and reduce on hob until sauce consistency is reached
  12. Serve with tagliatelle, grated parmesan, fresh chopped parsley and a glass of wine ;)
