The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Wide Green String Beans (Loubieh Bzeit):
  1. Take 800 g wide string beans
  2. Make ready 3 tomatoes
  3. Take 2 onions
  4. Get 4 garlic
  5. Prepare 1 spoon tomato paste
Steps to make Vegetarian Wide Green String Beans (Loubieh Bzeit):
  1. Clean and cut the string beans and boil them till they are cooked enough
  2. Fry the onion and the garlic and the tomatoes and than add the tomato paste and keep them simmer
  3. Add the cooked string beans and let them simmer together for 10 mins on a low fire
  4. Served at the room temperature with Lebanese bread snd some spring onion and green bell pepper a side. Bon appétit.
