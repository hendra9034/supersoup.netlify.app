The ingredients needed to make Sausage corn macaroni pasta:
  1. Take 350 grms macaroni
  2. Take 250 grms beef sausages
  3. Prepare 100 grms frozen corn
  4. Get 2 chopped garlic
  5. Take 100 grms canned tomatoes/ chopped tomatoes
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp soya sauce
  7. Prepare 1 Tbsp Vinegar
  8. Get 1 Tsp crushed red chillies
  9. Make ready 1 Tsp crushed black pepper
  10. Make ready 1 Tsp white paper powder
  11. Take 1 Tsp mixed herbs/ oregano
  12. Take Some mozzarella or Cheddarcheese
  13. Make ready Salt to taste
  14. Make ready Oil

Instructions to make Sausage corn macaroni pasta:
  1. First boil macaroni with some salt and oil till al dante… Al dante means 2/3rd cooked.
  2. In a non-stick pan, take 1tbsp of oil. Add in chopped garlic in it. Saute garlic till golden brown.
  3. Then add sliced sausages in it. Cook for 2 minutes. Then add in corn. - Note, frozen corns are half cooked.
  4. Adding chopped tomatoes or canned tomatoes. Mix well. As it's already mushy, it won't take long. Just a mix is enough.
  5. Then adding crushed red chillies, crushed black pepper and white paper in it. Mix it.
  6. Adding salt and mixed herbs. Oregano can be replaced with mixed herbs.
  7. Now adding soya sauce and vinegar in it.
  8. Finally add boiled macaroni. Then add a handful of cheese in it and mix well.
  9. Sausage and corn macaroni is ready to eat… Viola
