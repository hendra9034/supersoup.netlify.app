The ingredients needed to make Swedish Meatball Sauce:
  1. Prepare 100 ml double cream
  2. Get 200 ml boiling water or beef stock
  3. Get Soy sauce to taste
  4. Make ready Ground white pepper to taste
  5. Get 1 tbsp flour (to thicken the sauce)
  6. Get Salt to taste
Steps to make Swedish Meatball Sauce:
  1. In a small saucepan boil the water, then reduce heat and whisk in the double cream.
  2. Season to taste with salt, pepper and soy sauce.
  3. Whisk in the flour. Continue to whisk until the sauce has thickened.
  4. I strained the sauce using a colander to remove any lumps from the sauce but that is optional.
  5. Serve the sauce over over boiled potatoes and meatballs.
