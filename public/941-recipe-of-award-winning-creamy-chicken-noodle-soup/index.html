The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Take 3 cups cooked chicken (can use rotissiere cicken)
  2. Take 1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cheese soup
  3. Get 6 cups chicken broth
  4. Make ready 1 cup milk
  5. Take 2 stalks celery thinly sliced or chopped
  6. Make ready 2 carrots thinly sliced or chopped
  7. Get 1 (1 oz) packet of powdered ranch dressing mix
  8. Take 1 (2.25 oz) package real bacon bits
  9. Take 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Make ready 8 oz packaged uncooked egg noodles
