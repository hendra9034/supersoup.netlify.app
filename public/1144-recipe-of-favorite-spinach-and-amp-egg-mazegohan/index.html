The ingredients needed to make Spinach & Egg Mazegohan:
  1. Get 2 cups (*180ml cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  2. Get 2-3 Eggs
  3. Take Salt & White Pepper
  4. Take Oil for cooking
  5. Take 120 g Baby Spinach
  6. Take 1 tablespoon Sesame Oil
  7. Get 1 tablespoon Toasted Sesame
  8. Take 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon Oyster Sauce
  10. Get White Pepper

Instructions to make Spinach & Egg Mazegohan:
  1. Wash Rice and cook as normal. *Note: You can cook the rice in Stock, such as Chicken Stock, instead of Water.
  2. Whisk Eggs in a small bowl and season with Salt and Pepper. Heat a small amount of Oil in a frying pan over medium low heat, cook Egg into small pieces.
  3. Place Baby Spinach in a large mixing bowl. Add Sesame Oil, Toasted Sesame, Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce and some White Pepper, then mix well to combine.
  4. Add cooked hot rice into the bowl, and mix to combine. Then add Egg and combine.
