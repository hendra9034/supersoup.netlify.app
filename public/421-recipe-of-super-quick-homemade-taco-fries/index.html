The ingredients needed to make Taco Fries:
  1. Get 150 g Sweet Potato
  2. Prepare 140 g Lean Beef Mince
  3. Take 1/2 red pepper
  4. Make ready 1/2 onion
  5. Get 2 cloves Garlic
  6. Take 200 g Chopped Tomatoes
  7. Make ready 30 g Tomato Puree
  8. Prepare 30 g Light Cheddar Cheese
  9. Get 50 g Red Kidney Beans
  10. Prepare 30 g Lighter than Light Mayo
  11. Prepare 1 beef stock cube
  12. Prepare 1 tsp each of Chilli Powder, Cumin, Paprika, Garlic Powder
  13. Make ready Low cal spray

Instructions to make Taco Fries:
  1. Chop sweet potato into chips and place on an oven tray with salt and pepper. Cook for 20-25 Mins at 225°C
  2. Heat pan with low cal spray and sautée onions and garlic cloves. Add mince and cook until brown. Add peppers. Add your spices. Stir in tinned tomatoes, purée, kidney beans and beef stock and allow to simmer.
  3. Taco Sauce. Mix together 30g lighter than light mayo, 1 tbsp tomato purée, 1/2 tsp chilli, 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Top your fries with the mince mix. Add your taco sauce. Sprinkle with cheese and voilà.
