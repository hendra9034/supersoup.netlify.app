The ingredients needed to make Lentil-Tomato Soup:
  1. Take 4 1/2 cup water
  2. Prepare 4 medium carrots
  3. Get 1 medium onion
  4. Make ready 2/3 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  5. Make ready 1 can (6oz) tomato paste
  6. Get 2 tbsp minced fresh parsley
  7. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp white vinegar
  9. Get 1 tsp garlic salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  11. Take 1/4 tsp dill weed
  12. Make ready 1/4 tsp dried tarragon
  13. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
Steps to make Lentil-Tomato Soup:
  1. In a large sauce pan, combine the water, carrots, onion and lentils; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until vegetables and lentils are tender.
  3. Stir in the remaining ingredients; return to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
