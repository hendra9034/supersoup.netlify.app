The ingredients needed to make Cilantro Lime Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 cups dry -wide noodles- cooked,and rinsed to stop the cooking
  2. Make ready 2 quarts chicken stock
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 pounds chicken- cooked – diced 1/4" (rotisserie chickens are very good)
  4. Take 3 ounce oil
  5. Make ready 1 cup carrots – small diced
  6. Get 1 cup sweet onions – small dice
  7. Take 1 stalk celery – small dice
  8. Prepare 4 cloves garlic finely chopped
  9. Make ready 2 cups potatoes – small dice- place in water so as not to brown
  10. Take 3 tablespoons flour
  11. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  12. Make ready 1 teaspoon cumin
  13. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon rubbed sage
  14. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  15. Get 1/2 teaspoon Kosher Salt
  16. Make ready 8 tablespoons cilantro- lightly chopped with no stems
  17. Take 2 ounce lime juice

Steps to make Cilantro Lime Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Cook wide noodles in a pot of boiling water per the product directions. Drain and rinse with cold water then place in a bowl for later
  2. Place oil, carrots, onions, celery, drained potatoes and garlic in a 4 qt sauce pan. Sweat the vegetable, covered at a moderate heat for 5 minutes we are not trying to brown, just draw out the liquid.
  3. Add the cooked chicken, thyme, sage and cumin.
  4. Sprinkle and stir in the flour.
  5. Add the stock and bring to a boil then turn to a simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Add the noodles,cilantro and lime juice and adjust the seasoning as needed.
