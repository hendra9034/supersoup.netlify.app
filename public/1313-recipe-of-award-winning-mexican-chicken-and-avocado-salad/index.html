The ingredients needed to make Mexican Chicken and Avocado Salad:
  1. Take 3 avocado
  2. Get 1 chicken breast
  3. Prepare 3 leafy greens (spinach, salad leaves)
  4. Prepare 3 tbs olive oil
  5. Take 3 tbs honey
  6. Prepare 1 tsp garlic paste
  7. Get To taste Salt and pepper
  8. Prepare 1 tbs paprika
  9. Take 1 tbs oregano
  10. Prepare 4-5 cherry tomatoes
  11. Get 100 gm corn
  12. Make ready 2 tsp dried mint
  13. Take 3-4 tbsp lemon juice

    1. Grilling the chicken : marinate chicken breast with salad dressing and for 30 minutes.
    1. In a pan add 2 spoon oil and fry/grill chicken breast and then cut it into strips.
    1. Cut avocado, tomatoes, onions, and leafy green into pieces. Take a large bowl and put all the veggies into it along with grilled chicken and pour the salad dressing over it. Mix it well. Mexican chicken and avocado salad is ready to be served.
