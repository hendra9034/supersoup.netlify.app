The ingredients needed to make Cabbage And Corn Cob Soup:
  1. Make ready 10 slice Bacon
  2. Take 1 medium sweet cabbage
  3. Make ready 1 can baby corn & juice
  4. Get 1 tbsp Garlic
  5. Prepare 2 liter water
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  7. Get 1 dash salt & pepper & soysauce
  8. Get 1 large Corn Cob
  9. Get 1 cup brocoli
Steps to make Cabbage And Corn Cob Soup:
  1. Pan fry slice bacon with Garlic for few minutes then add the corn cob and water and bring it to a boil
  2. then simmer down and add cabbage & light soy sauce for 5 minutes
  3. add the whole can of babycorn & its juice
  4. Then add a dash of salt & pepper
