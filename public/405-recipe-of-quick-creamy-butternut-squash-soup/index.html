Instructions to make Creamy butternut squash soup:
  1. Saute a chopped onion in a large saucepan.
  2. Cut a peeled butternut squash into small pieces and add it to the caramelised onion. If you prefer your soup thicker, add grated potato.
  3. Add broth so that it covers the butternut squash and cook for approximately 30 minutes.
  4. Turn down the heat and then blend the soup using an immersion blender.
  5. Mix in single cream and add salt, pepper and nutmeg to your taste.
  6. Enjoy!
