The ingredients needed to make Grated Cassava Ball with Palm Sugar (Jemblem / Klenyem):
  1. Make ready 1 kg grated cassava
  2. Prepare 200 gram Grated coconut
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon sugar
  5. Take Salt to taste
  6. Get Enough oil to deep frying
  7. Prepare For the Filling :
  8. Get 150 gram palm sugar, thinly sliced
Instructions to make Grated Cassava Ball with Palm Sugar (Jemblem / Klenyem):
  1. Mix the cassava that has been soft with grated coconut, vanilla and sugar stir until well blended.
  2. Shape the dough according to taste, for example oval or round, then fill it with brown sugar or according to taste, then compact it.
  3. Heat the oil, then fry the dough cake until golden brown.  Fry over medium heat so it doesn't burn.
  4. The cake is ready to be served while it's warm with your morning tea or coffee or your afternoon tea and coffee.
