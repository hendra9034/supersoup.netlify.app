Steps to make Herb Chicken Carrot Corn Soup:
  1. Boil 1 liter water and all herbs & 500 grams chicken with bones in a pressure cooker for quickie & later take out chicken meat n make tiny pieces of chicken in blender for 5 seconds only & remove the cloves out
  2. Heat broth again, add vinegar 10 grams, hot sauce 5 grams (optional) fine carrots, sweetcorn, add olive oil 10ml, very finely chopped rosemary & coriander
  3. Add cornstarch in cold water n mix well and add to soup it will thicken the viscosity of soup, add pinch of turmeric for colour Garnish with black pepper and red chilli on too
