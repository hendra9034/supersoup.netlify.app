The ingredients needed to make Broccoli cheese soup:
  1. Get 1/4 cup chopped onions
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp flour
  4. Get 2 cup milk
  5. Take 4 oz Philadelphia cream cheese cubed
  6. Make ready 1/2 lb velvetta cheese
  7. Get 10 oz frozen broccoli cooked
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  9. Get 1/4 tsp pepper

Steps to make Broccoli cheese soup:
  1. Cook and stir onions in butter in medium saucepan on medium high heat for 3 to 5 minutes until onions are crisp and tender
  2. Whisk in flour until well blended add milk cook and stir on medium heat 2 minutes stirring occasionally
  3. Add cream cheese cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes or until melted
  4. Stir in remaining ingredients cook 5 minutes or until heated stirring occasionally
