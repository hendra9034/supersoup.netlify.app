The ingredients needed to make #2  lightly salted rice with sweet potato:
  1. Get 6 cm length of medium-sized sweet potato
  2. Take 400 cc rice
  3. Take 3 g salt
Instructions to make #2  lightly salted rice with sweet potato:
  1. ①wash sweet potato and remove dirt if there is on its surface then cut into 1.5cm width.
  2. ②cut ① length-wide in quarter and also crosscut in quarter and souse them in water to scum.
  3. ③ pick up sweet potato from water and mix with 1.5g salt then cover and microwave for 3 minutes.
  4. ④ cook rice with 1.5g salt and measured water
  5. ⑤ put ③ into rice cooker and steam 3 minutes.
