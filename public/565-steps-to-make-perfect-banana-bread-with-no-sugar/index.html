The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread with No Sugar:
  1. Get 75 g extra virgin olive oil
  2. Prepare 155 g maple syrup
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Make ready 2 large mashed bananas (I used Ladyfingers)
  5. Prepare 55 ml milk
  6. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon sugar
  10. Prepare 220 g white whole wheat flour
  11. Prepare Optional: chopped pecans, walnuts, almonds or choc chips
Steps to make Banana Bread with No Sugar:
  1. Preheat oven to 160 degree.
  2. Whisk olive oil, maple syrup and eggs together. Add flour (sifted), mashed bananas, baking soda, vanilla essence, salt, ground cinnamon sugar and pecans/walnuts/almonds/choc chips if using.
  3. Add milk. Whisk in stand mixer (or hand mixer, manual whisk will do) under medium speed for 5 mins until smooth.
