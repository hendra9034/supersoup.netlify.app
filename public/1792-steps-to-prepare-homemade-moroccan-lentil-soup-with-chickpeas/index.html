The ingredients needed to make Moroccan Lentil Soup With Chickpeas:
  1. Get 2 tbsp herb-infused olive oil
  2. Get 7 oz diced mirepoix
  3. Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
  4. Make ready 16 oz green lentils, sorted & rinsed
  5. Get 28 oz kitchen cut roma tomatos with basil (canned)
  6. Get 64 oz vegetable stock
  7. Get 1 tsp ground coriander
  8. Take 1 tsp cumin
  9. Make ready 1 tsp tumeric
  10. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
  11. Get 1/4 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  12. Take 16 oz canned garbanzo beans, drained
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  14. Prepare 1 dash Salt to taste

Instructions to make Moroccan Lentil Soup With Chickpeas:
  1. Add oil to stockpot on MEDIUM. Add mirepoix and garlic; cook, stirring, 5-6 min, until soft but not browned.
  2. Stir in lentils, tomatoes, stock, coriander, cumin, turmeric, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper. Cook, covered, 20-25 min.
  3. Puree briefly (about 15 seconds) with handheld blender, just to thicken (don't puree all the lentils).
  4. Stir in garbanzo beans and cilantro. Season to taste with salt. Simmer about 5 min on MEDIUM until heated through.
