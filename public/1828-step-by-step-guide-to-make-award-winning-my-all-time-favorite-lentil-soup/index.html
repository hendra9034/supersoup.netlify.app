The ingredients needed to make My all-time favorite Lentil Soup:
  1. Get 1 cup brown lentils
  2. Get 3 1/2 cups water
  3. Take 1 large onions, cut into small pieces
  4. Take salt coarse
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon cumin
  6. Take 2 1/2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps to make My all-time favorite Lentil Soup:
  1. Fry the sliced onion in oil, medium fire for 5 mins
  2. Boil the lentils in water and salt for 30-40mins
  3. Add the onion to the lentils, add the cumin, and let cook for another 5 mins
  4. Serve with the bread croutons
