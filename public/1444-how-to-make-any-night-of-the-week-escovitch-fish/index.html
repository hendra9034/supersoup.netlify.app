Steps to make Escovitch fish:
  1. Season snappers, leave to marinate for a few hours
  2. Fry on both sides with vegetable oil
  3. Add onions to the vegetable oil, after taking out fish
  4. Cook onions under low heat 5 mins
  5. Add sliced peppers and peppercorns cook a further 5mins
  6. Add white vinegar. Cook out for 2 mins
  7. Pour vegetables over the fish and leave to marinate
  8. Can be served hot or cold
