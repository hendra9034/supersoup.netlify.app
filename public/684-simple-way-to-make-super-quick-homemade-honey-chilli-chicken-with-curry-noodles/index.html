The ingredients needed to make Honey Chilli Chicken with Curry Noodles:
  1. Prepare For the chicken
  2. Make ready 4 chicken breasts cut into strips
  3. Make ready Splash soy sauce
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp flour
  5. Get 2 tsp cornflour
  6. Take Oil for frying
  7. Take For the sauce
  8. Get Splash sesame oil
  9. Prepare 4 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  10. Get 2 tbsp honey
  11. Take 2 tbsp Ketjap Manis or soy sauce
  12. Get 1 tbsp cornflour
  13. Make ready 5 tbsp water
  14. Make ready 2 garlic cloves chopped
  15. Prepare 10 g chopped ginger
  16. Prepare 1/2 chopped red chilli
  17. Make ready 1/2 lemon juice and 1/2 lemon zest
  18. Take Handful chopped fresh coriander
  19. Prepare 100 g cashew nuts
  20. Take 1 carrot peeled and cut into matchsticks
  21. Prepare 1/2 bunch scallions
  22. Prepare 1/2 onion finely sliced
  23. Prepare For the curry noodles
  24. Get Splash vegetable oil
  25. Get 2 tbsp cornflour
  26. Take 500 ml chicken stock
  27. Take 2 garlic cloves
  28. Prepare 3 tbsp curry powder
  29. Take 1 tbsp turmeric
  30. Take 1 tsp chilli powder
  31. Take 1 tsp chilli flakes
  32. Make ready 1 tsp ground ginger
  33. Make ready 1 tsp caster sugar
  34. Prepare Splash soy sauce
  35. Make ready 1/2 thinly sliced onion
  36. Take 4 portions egg noodles
Steps to make Honey Chilli Chicken with Curry Noodles:
  1. Preheat fryer to 180 degrees. Place chicken in a bowl, add splash soy sauce then flour and cornflour and coat well until fully covered. Place chicken strips in fryer approximately six at a time. They only take 1-2 mins. Place cooked chicken in 100 degrees oven until all batches cooked.
  2. For the sauce, add cornflour, water, sweet chilli sauce, soy sauce and honey to a bowl and mix well. Add lemon juice and lemon zest. Heat pan and add sesame oil, garlic, ginger and chilli. Stir fry for 1-2 mins then add onions and carrots and then the honey chilli sauce for another 1-2 mins. If sauce thickens too much, add a little water to the pan.
  3. Add chicken strips to sauce and mix well. Add cashew nuts, scallions and coriander and toss for 1-2 minutes.
  4. For the noodles, add cornflour to the stock mixture, mix well and set aside. Heat oil in pan then add garlic, sugar and all the spices and cook for few minutes without burning spices. Add a little stock if needed. Add the onion and cook for 1-2 minutes then add the stock and bring to the boil. Simmer for 20 minutes and season with a little soy sauce.
  5. In another pan, boil the noodles as per pack instructions then drain them and toss in the curry and onion. Serve portion of curry noodles topped with honey chilli chicken.
