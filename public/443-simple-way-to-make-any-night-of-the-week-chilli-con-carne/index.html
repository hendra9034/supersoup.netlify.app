The ingredients needed to make Chilli Con Carne:
  1. Make ready 450 g Beef Mince
  2. Make ready 2 large onions
  3. Get About 4 large red Chillies
  4. Take About 150ml cold water
  5. Take 200 g - 250g / 1 tin of Red Kidney Beans
  6. Prepare 200 g - 250g / Cannelini Beans,or use 2 tin's of Red Kidney Bean
  7. Take 400 g - 2 tin's of chopped tomatoes with tomato juice
  8. Take 1/2 tbsp. Sea Salt
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp. Olive Oil
  10. Get 1 packet Chilli Con Carne seasoning
  11. Prepare 400 g raw white rice,One large mug
Instructions to make Chilli Con Carne:
  1. Add 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil too a large frying pan with lid,add chopped onions too the large frying pan,add deseeded Chillies and minced beef within - about a 10 minute period.
  2. Put 3 large mugs of cold water in a medium sized saucepan.Place rice in a rice sieve,drain under the cold water tap,add the One large mug of rice to the saucepan of warm water,or boiling water and then cook until the rice has absorbed the water,once done can turn the hob off and leave the saucepan lid on.
  3. Add the tinned chopped tomatoes with it's tomato juice too the large frying pan - along with the cooked beef mince,chopped onions and roughly chopped mild red Chillies - open the tin's of beans,wash each variety of beans in the rice sieve under the cold water tap and then eventually add them to the large frying pan as well,add Sea Salt if you have not already done so - add the Packet of Chilli Con Carne seasoning to the large frying pan and about 150ml of cold water,boil,then simmer with lid on
  4. After simmering for 20 minutes,should be done - so serve some of the Chilli Con Carne with cooked boiled rice and then enjoy.(2 portions,half of Chilli Con Carne and half of cooked rice provides a very filling meal.)
