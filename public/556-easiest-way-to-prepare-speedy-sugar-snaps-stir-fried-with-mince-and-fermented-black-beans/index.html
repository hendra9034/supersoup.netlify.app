The ingredients needed to make Sugar Snaps Stir Fried with Mince and Fermented Black Beans:
  1. Prepare 150 g Sugar Snaps, cut to bite size pieces
  2. Make ready 120 g Pork Mince
  3. Get 2 Tbsp Fermented Black Beans, soaked in hot water, then roughly chopped
  4. Make ready Dried Red Chillies, cut to small pieces 3
  5. Take 1 Tbsp Garlic, minced
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Shaoxing Wine
  8. Take Oil
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp Sugar

Instructions to make Sugar Snaps Stir Fried with Mince and Fermented Black Beans:
  1. Heat 1 tsp oil in a pan over high heat. - When the pan is hot, add sugar snaps. - Fry until the skin is slightly charred. - It should NOT be fully cooked at this point. - Transfer to plate.
  2. Using the same pan, add 1 Tbsp oil. - Reduce to Medium heat. - Add garlic + dried chillies + fermented black beans. - - Fry until fragrant
  3. Add pork mince. - Keep stir frying until the meat is well browned and dry.
  4. Add sugar snaps. - Season with soy sauce + shaoxing wine + sugar. - Mix well. - Taste & season.
  5. Stir fry until the beans are cooked through. - - Don't rush this step. - Let the beans absorb the flavours.
  6. Serve with steamed rice
