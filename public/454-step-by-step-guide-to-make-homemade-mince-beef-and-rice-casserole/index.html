The ingredients needed to make Mince Beef And Rice Casserole:
  1. Get 800 g mince beef
  2. Make ready 2 cups rice (approx)
  3. Make ready 6-8 slices bacon (roughly chopped)
  4. Prepare 1 onion, diced
  5. Get 2-3 tsp garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1 Bay leaf
  7. Prepare 1 carton passata sauce (approx 500g)
  8. Get Salt to taste
  9. Take Ground black pepper to taste
  10. Get 1 drizzle of olive oil
  11. Get 100 ml white wine
  12. Get 1 stock cube
  13. Take 1 egg, beaten
Instructions to make Mince Beef And Rice Casserole:
  1. Boil the rice in water with a stock cube until cooked through. Drain the rice if necessary once cooked.
  2. Next heat a pan with olive oil then add the onion and bay leaf. Fry until the onion is soft.
  3. Add 2/3 of the bacon and fry for a couple more minutes.
  4. Add the meat into the pan. Season with salt, garlic powder and pepper.
  5. When the meat is cooked add the wine and allow to evaporate then add the passata sauce.
  6. Transfer the meat to an oven proof dish and level out. Add the rice to the top and level out evenly with a spoon.
  7. Egg wash the top of the rice and sprinkle with the remaining bacon.
  8. Cook in a preheat oven for 15-20 minutes or until the bacon is cooked and the rice around the edge is slightly browned.
