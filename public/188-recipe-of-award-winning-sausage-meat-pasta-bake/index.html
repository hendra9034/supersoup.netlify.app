The ingredients needed to make Sausage meat pasta bake:
  1. Take 450 g sausage meat or 6 sausages, skins removed
  2. Get 1 clove garlic
  3. Get 300 g Passata
  4. Prepare 350 g pasta
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  6. Prepare 400 ml water
  7. Take 1 tsp sugar
  8. Make ready 1 slice bread, crumped
  9. Take 5 basil leaves, coarsely chopped
  10. Take 100 g mature cheddar, grated

Steps to make Sausage meat pasta bake:
  1. Heat oil in pan and brown the sausage meat
  2. Add chopped garlic to the sausage meat and cook for 1 min
  3. Add the passata, water, sugar and salt and pepper. Simmer for 15min
  4. While meat is simmering, preheat the oven to 180oC and cook the pasta until al dente
  5. Once the sauce has simmered, add chopped basil and simmer for another minute
  6. Stir in pasta to sauce ensuring all the pasta is covered in sauce
  7. Pour into oven proof dish (ours is 20x30cm). Top with grated cheese and bread crumps
  8. Cook for 20min
