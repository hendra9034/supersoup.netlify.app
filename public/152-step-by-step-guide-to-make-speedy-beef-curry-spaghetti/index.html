The ingredients needed to make Beef Curry Spaghetti:
  1. Take 1 serving Spaghetti
  2. Take 1 teaspoon Oil
  3. Get 1/2 clove Garlic *finely chopped
  4. Make ready 1/4 Onion
  5. Prepare 1/4 Carrot
  6. Take 1/4 Capsicum of any colour
  7. Get 60-80 g Beef Steak *can be replaced with other meat
  8. Prepare Salt & Pepper
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup Water
  10. Prepare 15 g Curry Roux Block *thinly sliced
Instructions to make Beef Curry Spaghetti:
  1. Cook Spaghetti in a large saucepan of salted boiling water as instructed.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare Beef and Vegetables. Cut them into thin strips.
  3. Heat Oil in a frying pan over medium heat, cook Beef and Vegetables. Lightly season with Salt & Pepper.
  4. When Vegetables are all cooked, add Water and thinly sliced Curry Roux Block, add stir. Add cooked Spaghetti and mix to combine.
