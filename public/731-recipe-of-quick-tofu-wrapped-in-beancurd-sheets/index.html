The ingredients needed to make Tofu Wrapped in Beancurd Sheets:
  1. Get 1 block tofu
  2. Prepare sheets Beancurd
  3. Get 250 g ground pork
  4. Take 2 tbsp black.vinegar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  6. Take Dash black pepper
  7. Get 1 stalk spring.onion
  8. Take Half cup cooking.oil
  9. Take 2 minced shallots
  10. Take 1 egg
  11. Make ready 2 slices ginger
Steps to make Tofu Wrapped in Beancurd Sheets:
  1. Mash tofu then combine with pork,black pepper,egg and chopped spring onion.Mix it well 1.
  2. Lay flat the beancurs sheets then scoop one tsp of mixture and roll it
  3. Heat wok then.pour cooking oil.Fry the rolled beancurd until brown.When done,remove.
  4. In the same wok stir fry shallots and ginger.Then pour a little water,vinegar and sugar.Then.return the rolls.
  5. Cover and cook it until the sauce absorbs.Remove and serve
