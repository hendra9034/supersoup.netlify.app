The ingredients needed to make Chinese Stew Duck Tongue (lidah bebek ungkep):
  1. Take 1 LB (400 gr) of duck tongue
  2. Prepare 400ml water
  3. Take 1 tbs dark sauce
  4. Prepare 2 tbs oysters sauce
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbs soy sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tbs cooking wine
  7. Get 2 pcs small Onion

  14. Make ready 2 pcs Small chili
