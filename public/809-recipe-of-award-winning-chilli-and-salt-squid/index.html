The ingredients needed to make Chilli and salt squid:
  1. Make ready 300 g cleaned tubes fresh squid
  2. Make ready 100 g corn flour
  3. Prepare 1 egg beaten
  4. Make ready Rice bran oil for deep frying
  5. Get Sichuan salt and pepper:
  6. Take 100 g sea salt
  7. Prepare 16 g Sichuan peppercorn
  8. Take 50 g dried galangal powder
  9. Make ready 15 g chinese 13 spices
  10. Make ready 35 g caster sugar
  11. Get 10 g chilli powder

Steps to make Chilli and salt squid:
