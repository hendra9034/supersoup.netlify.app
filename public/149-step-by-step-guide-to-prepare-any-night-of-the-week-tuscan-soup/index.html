The ingredients needed to make Tuscan Soup:
  1. Take 1 box mushrooms
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Prepare 5 teeth of garlic
  4. Get 1 leek
  5. Prepare Vegetable broth
  6. Get 1 celery
  7. Prepare 5 carrots
  8. Make ready 5 potatoes
  9. Make ready 1 box kale
  10. Get Sweet paprika, nut Meg, black pepper
  11. Take 4 burgers or vegan meat
  12. Take sweet cream, heavy (30%+)
Steps to make Tuscan Soup:
  1. Heat small chunks of mushrooms
  2. Add onion and sliced garlic
  3. Add sliced leek
  4. Add vegetable broth to cover all ingredients
  5. Add sliced celery, 5 sticks or so
  6. Add 5 carrots slices into cubes
  7. Add 5 potatoes slices into cubes
  8. Chop kale and add, make sure vegetable broth covers it all
  9. Can add paprika, nut Meg, black pepper
  10. Let it cook for a bit
  11. Grill on the side vegan burgers sliced to cubes. When ready, pan is roasted, use some water to "clean" the pan and add with the meat for flavor
  12. Mix it all together
  13. Add sweet cream and heat for another 10' or so
  14. Serve and enjoy
