The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tortilla Soup:
  1. Make ready cubed chicken
  2. Get salt (or to taste)
  3. Make ready Chili powder (to taste)
  4. Prepare Cayenne (optional, to taste)
  5. Get Onion powder (to taste)
  6. Take vegetable oil (or more)
  7. Take corn tortillas in 1-inch strips (or just buy a bag of chips)
  8. Make ready finely chopped white onions
  9. Make ready chopped bell peppers
  10. Prepare minced garlic
  11. Get seeded, chopped tomatoes (optional)
  12. Get minced Serrano chilies (optional)
  13. Make ready chicken stock
  14. Prepare lime juice (to taste)
  15. Prepare chopped cilantro (or more)
  16. Get avacodo
Instructions to make Chicken Tortilla Soup:
  1. Heat 1/2 cup of the oil over high heat in a large pot.
  2. Fry the tortillas in batches until golden brown on both sides, adding more oil as needed. Drain on paper towels.
  3. Season the cubed chicken with the onion powder, salt, and cover in chili powder, add a touch of cayenne if desired.
  4. Cook the seasoned chicken in a pan.
  5. Saute the onion, and bell peppers. Throw the garlic in near the end.
  6. Put everything except the avacodo in a pot and simmer for about 10 minutes (or until how you like).
  7. Cut avacodo into small pieces and put into soup before serving.
