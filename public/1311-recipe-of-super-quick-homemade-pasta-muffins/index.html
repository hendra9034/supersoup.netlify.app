The ingredients needed to make Pasta muffins::
  1. Take 3 cups cooked pasta
  2. Prepare 1 red capsicum, deseeded and diced finely
  3. Take 1 onion finely chopped
  4. Get 1 small carrot finely chopped
  5. Take 1 cup grated cheese or as needed
  6. Take Oil for grease
  7. Get 6 large eggs
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup frozen peas
  9. Take 1/2 cup corn kernels
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup spring onion
  11. Prepare 1 green chilli chopped
  12. Get 1/4 cup fresh coriander chopped
  13. Get 1 cup milk
  14. Get Salt to taste
  15. Take Crush black pepper to taste
  16. Prepare Crush red chilli flakes to taste (optional)
