The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup mix:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup Onion Flakes
  2. Get 2 each Beef Stock Cubes
  3. Get 1/4 tsp Onion Powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp Parsley Flakes
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Paprika 1/8
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Celery Seeds 1/8
  7. Get 1 tsp Cracked Black Pepper 1/8
Instructions to make French Onion Soup mix:
  1. Place all ingredients except for Onion Flakes into pestle and mix/grind.
  2. Place newly ground ingredients and Onion Flakes into magic bullet and mix.
