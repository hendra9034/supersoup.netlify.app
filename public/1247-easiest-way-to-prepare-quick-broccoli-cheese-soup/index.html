The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Get Main Ingredients
  2. Prepare 5 bunch Broccoli
  3. Get 1/2 head Cauliflower
  4. Prepare 4 large Potatoes
  5. Prepare 1 Onion
  6. Take Liquids
  7. Take 1 box Chicken Broth
  8. Take 5 cup Milk
  9. Take 2 stick Butter
  10. Prepare Spices
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp Garlic Salt
  12. Get 1 tbsp Cayenne
  13. Get 1 tsp Poultry Seasoning
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp Mrs. Dash
  15. Make ready 1 packages Velveeta Original

Steps to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Chop all the main ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a large soup pot.
  3. Place chopped ingredients in the pot
  4. Pour about a fourth of the chicken broth in
  5. Cover and let cook until ingredients are all soft and easy to cut in half with a fork
  6. Now pour remaining chicken broth and milk
  7. When hot put in the cheese
  8. Now put in the spices and continue cooking and stirring until to a light rolling boil
  9. Turn off the burner
  10. EAT!
