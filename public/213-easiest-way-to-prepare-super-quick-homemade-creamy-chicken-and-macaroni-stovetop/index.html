The ingredients needed to make Creamy, Chicken, and Macaroni Stovetop:
  1. Take Chicken and vegetables
  2. Take 1-1/2 quart chicken broth
  3. Get 2 pound chicken breast boneless and skinless
  4. Get 1 teaspoon ground white pepper powder
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground mustard powder
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon granulated onion powder
  7. Make ready 1-1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  8. Prepare 2 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt
  9. Take 6 ounces frozen chopped Spinach
  10. Take 1-1/2 pound baby carrots
  11. Take 1/2 pound macaroni elbows
  12. Get 1 cup sliced celery
  13. Take 10 ounces condensed cream of chicken soup
  14. Take 3 large new white potatoes
  15. Make ready 2 tablespoons dijon mustard heaping both
  16. Prepare Toasted Breadcrumb
  17. Make ready 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  18. Get 1/2 stick butter
  19. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  20. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon granulated onion powder
  21. Make ready To taste pink Himalayan salt
Steps to make Creamy, Chicken, and Macaroni Stovetop:
  1. Add all the ingredients except the macaroni and cream of chicken soup. Bring to a boil. Cook for 30 minutes, covered. Then take the chicken out and dice it.
  2. After the diced chicken is added back in dice the potatoes and put them into the chicken mixture.
  3. After you add in the potatoes add the dijon mustard and mix well.
  4. Melt butter with granulated garlic powder and granulated onion powder. Toast the breadcrumbs. When toasted add salt to taste.
  5. Simmer 20 minutes. Add the macaroni and cream of chicken soup.
  6. Simmer 15 minutes let rest 10 minutes covered. Stir. Serve, with toasted breadcrumbs on top. I hope you enjoy!!!!!!
