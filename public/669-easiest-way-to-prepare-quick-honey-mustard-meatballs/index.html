The ingredients needed to make Honey mustard meatballs:
  1. Take 250 g pork mince
  2. Prepare 250 g lean beef mince
  3. Make ready 1 onion
  4. Take 2 slices white bread (without crust)
  5. Get 150 ml milk
  6. Get 2 tsp mustard
  7. Make ready 1 tsp honey
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  9. Make ready 1 tsp salt

Instructions to make Honey mustard meatballs:
  1. Very finely chop the onion and fry it in a bit of butter until soft. Let cool down.
  2. Tear the bread up into small pieces and soak in milk for 5 minutes. Add onions, honey, mustard, salt and pepper and mix well.
  3. Add the mince to the mixture and mix until well combined. Roll into small balls (about 30-40 balls) and put on a baking tray.
  4. Heat the oven up to the highest setting and bake them for about 10-15 minutes, until nicely browned and cooked through.
  5. Enjoy!
