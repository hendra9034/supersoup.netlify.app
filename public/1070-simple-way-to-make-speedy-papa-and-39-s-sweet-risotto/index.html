  7. Make ready Olive oil

Steps to make Papa's sweet risotto:
  1. Boil the rice and let it rest for 20 min aprox if you have the time
  2. Chop the onion and fry it in a pan with olive to get it golden and crispy
  3. Add the raisins and the cinnamon and mix it for a couple of minutes
  4. Add the rice and keep frying for 2-5 minutes
  5. Add the greek yoghurt to the pan and mix it well with the rice
  6. Turn the heat to a minimum and fry it to get the desired consistency.
  7. Serve the dishes and add some fresh minced parsley on top to give a touch of color and a different taste to the dish.
  8. Enjoy!!
