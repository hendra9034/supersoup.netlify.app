The ingredients needed to make Roast Beef Rib:
  1. Get 1 Beef Rib
  2. Make ready Lump of fat (just for roasties)
Instructions to make Roast Beef Rib:
  1. Remove Beef rib from the fridge around 1 hour prior to cooking.
  2. Pre heat the oven 200 degrees
  3. Place meat it an oven proof dish, cook at 200 degrees for 20 minutes
  4. After 20 minutes turn oven down to 130 degrees cook for 1 hour 20 minutes. (Remove earlier if you would like medium/rare)
  5. Leave to rest before cutting and enjoy!
