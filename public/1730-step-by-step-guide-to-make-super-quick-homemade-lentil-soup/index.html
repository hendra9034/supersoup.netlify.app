The ingredients needed to make lentil soup.:
  1. Get 4 potatoes
  2. Prepare 3 stick celery
  3. Take 4 carrots
  4. Prepare 1 medium leek
  5. Take 1/2 savoy cabbage
  6. Prepare 1 large bacon shank
  7. Take 3/4 lb red lentils

Steps to make lentil soup.:
  1. cook the shank in enough water to cover it
  2. simmer for approx. 1hour
  3. when its cooked remove the shank and let the liquid cool
  4. strain the liquid through a strainer
  5. add the washed lentils
  6. wash and dice the veg
  7. add to the soup
  8. simmer gently until the veg is almost cooked adding more water if needed
  9. cut all the fat off the meat, dice it and add it to the soup
  10. check taste before adding any salt
