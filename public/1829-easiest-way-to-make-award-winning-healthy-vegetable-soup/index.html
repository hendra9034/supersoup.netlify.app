The ingredients needed to make Healthy Vegetable soup:
  1. Get 4 cups low sodium beef or chicken broth
  2. Prepare 2/3 cup corn kernals
  3. Make ready 1 cup green peas( or snap peas
  4. Prepare 1 cup diced potatoes
  5. Take 1 cup carrots
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup lentils
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup onions
  9. Get Spices
  10. Take 1 tsp salt
  11. Take 1 tsp pepper
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp chives
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Steps to make Healthy Vegetable soup:
  1. First Bring broth to a boil and add spices.
  2. In pan, saute the onions, then add to broth
  3. Then add the vegetables and cover with lid.
  4. Let it boil for 3 minutes.
  5. Turn the heat to low and let it simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Once it's done, serve immediately
  7. You can add diced chicken, beef or turkey if you wish
