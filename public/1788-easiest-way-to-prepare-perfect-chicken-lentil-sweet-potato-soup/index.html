The ingredients needed to make Chicken Lentil Sweet Potato Soup:
  1. Get 1 lb chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1 packages red lentils
  3. Get 1 cup chopped onion
  4. Take 1/2 cup chopped celery
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp minced garlic
  6. Make ready 32 oz chicken stock
  7. Get 1 medium sweet potato
  8. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  9. Make ready 1 tsp pink Himalayan sea salt
  10. Get 1 tsp curry powder
  11. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  12. Take 1/2 tsp allspice

Steps to make Chicken Lentil Sweet Potato Soup:
  1. Cut chicken breasts into small pieces. Season with pink Himalayan sea salt and black pepper. Set aside. Cut sweet potato into chunks, steam for 5 min. Set aside
  2. Heat olive oil in large pot. Sautee onions, celery and garlic for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add chicken to olive oil, onions, celery and mixed garlic. Sautee until chicken is done and browning. Add curry powder and allspice
  4. Add chicken broth and potatoes. Cook under medium flame until boiling, then simmer for 20 min.
