The ingredients needed to make Green Bean Casserole:
  1. Prepare 2 cans French style green beans, drained
  2. Make ready 1 (15 oz.) Can Cream of mushroom soup
  3. Take 3/4 C. Milk
  4. Prepare 1 tsp. Black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 container French fried onions

Instructions to make Green Bean Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a rimmed baking dish and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl combine drained green beans, cream of mushroom soup, milk, and pepper. Fold in 3/4 of the French onions.
  3. Pour into greased baking dish and put in the oven for 30 minutes. Top with remaining French fried onions and cook another 5 minutes or until lightly browned.
