The ingredients needed to make Nan's Chinese Casserole:
  1. Take 500 g Cooked Mince (Beef of Quorn will do!)
  2. Take 300 g Frozen Peas
  3. Prepare Chopped Celery
  4. Take Mixed Herbs
  5. Make ready 1 Tin Condensed Mushroom Soup
  6. Prepare 1 Finely Chopped Onion
  7. Make ready Worcestershire sauce
  8. Make ready 2 packets Ready Salted Crisps
  9. Take Plain Noodles
  10. Prepare 2 tablespoons single cream
Steps to make Nan's Chinese Casserole:
  1. Heat oven to 175c
  2. Mix the condensed mushroom soup, single cream, chopped onion and add 1/2 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  3. Place the and mix the cooked mince, frozen peas and celery in a casserole dish and pour over the sauce made in 2. Throw in some Beansprouts if you have some!
  4. Break up the packets of salted crisps into finely broken peices and spread out evenly over dish
  5. Cook in oven for 30 minutes until bubbling and the crisps are browned
  6. Place cooked noodles on the plates to serve and a big portion of the casserole on top and enjoy!! :)
