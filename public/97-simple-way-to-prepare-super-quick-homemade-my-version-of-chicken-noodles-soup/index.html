The ingredients needed to make My version of chicken noodles soup:
  1. Take 250 gcubes Chicken breast cut
  2. Make ready 200 g Chicken bone with a bit of meat on it
  3. Take 1 lt Water
  4. Get Half Onion
  5. Make ready 5 pieces Garlic
  6. Take Salt
  7. Take 1 tbsp Pepper
  8. Take 1 cube Chicken stock
  9. Prepare 1 pieces Carrot
  10. Prepare 1 pieces Potato
  11. Make ready 50 g Macaroni noodles
  12. Get leaf Coriander
Instructions to make My version of chicken noodles soup:
  1. Cut the chicken into the cubes
  2. Heat up the pan put some olive oil or butter add the garlic and onion untill golden brown or smells the fragrance
  3. Add the chicken bone
  4. Add the chicken stock and the water
  5. Wait untill boiling than add carrot, potatoes and macaroni
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. After all the ingredients soft, turn off the heat and add the coriander leaf. And Ready to enjoy
