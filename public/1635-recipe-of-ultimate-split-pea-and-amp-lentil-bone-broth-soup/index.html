The ingredients needed to make Split pea & lentil bone broth soup:
  1. Take 1 large beef bone
  2. Prepare 3 slices ginger
  3. Take 8 cups water
  4. Make ready 1 lb dry split peas
  5. Get 1/2-1 lb red lentil (use full bag if you like thicker soup)
  6. Prepare 2 red potatoes, cubed
  7. Get 2 medium carrots, cubed

  10. Take 1 onion, diced
  11. Take 1 tsp dried rosemary
  12. Make ready 1 tsp thyme
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  14. Make ready 1 tsp salt

Steps to make Split pea & lentil bone broth soup:
  1. Put the beef bone with ginger slices in 8 cups of water in the slower cooker, cook overnight for at least 8-12 hours, on LOW
  2. Allow the soup to cool down enough for you to take the bone out of the broth, scrap of any meat on or inside the bone. Put the meat back into the broth. Discard the bone or gave it to your dog as a treat!
  3. Put split pea in the broth, cook for 2-3 hours on high
  4. Add the remaining ingredients to the soup and cook another 3 hr on LOW (or 2 hr on HIGH), until the veggies are tender.
