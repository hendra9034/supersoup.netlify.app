Steps to make easy soup can be made into stew as well:
  1. heat stove to around medium heat. Add the chopped onions. Brown the onions till light golden brown colour
  2. add the chicken cubes along with the salt and mixed spices of your choice
  3. cook for around 10 -15 minutes or until chicken is white in colour
  4. mix the cup of soup with warm - boiling water. Add to pot. Stir and allow to simmer for 5 minutes
  5. add around 1 litre of boiling water, along with your potatoes carrots and corn. Add the mixed veg once carrots are semi soft as the mixed veg cook quickly
  6. Taste your food. The vegetables soak up the spices and salt if u feel it needs more seasoning add some more
  7. Make sure to cover with the lid as this allows the flavour to be sealed in. allow to boil for 45 minutes or until the potatoes and vegetables have softened
  8. if you wish to eat this as a stew, lessen the amount of water u add. Once fully cooked mix 1 - 2 tablespoon of corn flower into 1 tablespoon of cold water and add to the soup to thicken. Best served on fluffy white rice
