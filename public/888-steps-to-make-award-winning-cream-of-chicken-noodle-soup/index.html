The ingredients needed to make Cream of Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Take 1 PK boneless chicken breasts (4-6 large breasts)
  2. Prepare 1 pkag of egg noodles
  3. Take At least 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  4. Get Any fresh cut up, or canned veggies you want
Instructions to make Cream of Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Place boneless chicken breasts in a pot of water.
  2. Heat until boiling.
  3. Tear or cut chicken into bite sizes
  4. Place back into pot. Cover, and let simmer for at least 30 minutes to have good broth.
  5. In another pot, cook egg noodles as directed.
  6. When noodles are done cooking, drain, and empty into pot with chicken
  7. Add whatever kind of veggies to the soup you want. Doesn't matter if they are fresh and cut up or canned.
  8. Open two cans of cream of chicken soup and add it to chicken, noodles, and veggies.
  9. Cook on medium for 30 minutes, and meal should be good to go.
  10. Enjoy!!!!
