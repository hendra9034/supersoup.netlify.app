The ingredients needed to make #mystyle Spicy Beefburgers:
  1. Make ready 1 kg minced beef(5% fat)
  2. Prepare 1 Large white onion, finely chopped
  3. Prepare 2 Fat garlic cloves, crushed & finely minced
  4. Take 1 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoon dried chilli flakes
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  7. Take 1 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  8. Take 1 tablespoon Worcestershire
  9. Make ready 2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  10. Make ready 2 teaspoon wholegrain mustard
  11. Get 1/4 teaspoon salt
  12. Make ready 2 Large free range eggs
  13. Get 4 tablespoon dried breadcrumbs
  14. Get Plain flour for sprinkling before frying
  15. Prepare 2-4 tablespoons Olive oil for cooking
Steps to make #mystyle Spicy Beefburgers:
  1. Make 24 hours before cooking, to let the
  2. Heat a non-stick frying pan on low heat, add 1 teaspoon olive oil & fry onions until soft, add the garlic & fry gently until fragrant & soft, add to the bowl with the mince.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients up to & including the breadcrumbs.
  4. Mix well, I use my hands to mix & blend everything together.
  5. Cover tightly with clingfilm & leave in fridge 5 hours minimum but overnight is better, I have found.
  6. Cut 8 pieces of non-stick paper into squares that are just bigger than the burgers or 8 lightly greased pieces of foil & place on a large tray that will hold 8 burgers.(the paper or foil makes it easier to move the burgers from tray when ready to fry)
  7. Use your hands to form 8 burgers using equal amounts of meat. I used a large, ring cutter to help me shape the burgers neatly.
  8. When ready to cook the burgers. Heat a large nonstick frying pan on low heat until a sprinkle of flour sizzles.
  9. Sprinkle a burger very lightly with flour & place in pan, if your pan is big enough, you may be able to fit 4 in a pan, I was able to fit 3. Cook burgers 10 minutes each side for well done burgers.
  10. Serve however you like!
