The ingredients needed to make Beef Mince & Tofu in Sukiyaki Sauce:
  1. Get 1 teaspoon Oil
  2. Prepare 500 g Lean Beef Mince
  3. Prepare 100-150 g Shiitake *OR Shimeji
  4. Get 450 g Firm Tofu *torn or cut into small pieces
  5. Get 4 Spring Onions *cut into 4-5cm pieces
  6. Prepare Sauce
  7. Get 1 small piece Ginger *grated
  8. Make ready 5 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  9. Make ready 2 tablespoons Sugar
  10. Prepare 2 tablespoons Mirin
  11. Get 1/4 cup Sake (Rice Wine), Dashi Stock or Water
Steps to make Beef Mince & Tofu in Sukiyaki Sauce:
  1. Combine all the Sukiyaki Sauce ingredients.
  2. Heat Oil in a large frying pan over medium heat, cook Beef Mince. When it changes colour, add the sauce, and bring to the boil.
  3. Place Tofu Pieces and Spring Onion, and cook for 5 to 10 minutes or until the sauce is thickened. Stir to combine evenly. Enjoy with freshly cooked Rice.
