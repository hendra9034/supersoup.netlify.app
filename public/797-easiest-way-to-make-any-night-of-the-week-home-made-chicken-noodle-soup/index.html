The ingredients needed to make home made chicken noodle soup:
  1. Take 1 lb chicken (with bone)
  2. Get 3 large carrots chopped
  3. Make ready 3 large sticks of celery chopped
  4. Make ready 2 lb cubed red potatoes with skinn
  5. Take 2 tbsp salt( if you do not use canned broth)
  6. Prepare 2 clove garlic (minced)
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp basil
  8. Get 1 tbsp parsly
  9. Get 1 tbsp oragano
  10. Take 1 tsp ground pepper
  11. Prepare 4 cup water
  12. Take 1/2 lb essenhaus extra wide homestyle noodles

Instructions to make home made chicken noodle soup:
  1. pour water in large stock pot.
  2. heat on high to boiling then add chicken with skinned and bone.
  3. add in salt.
  4. chopp celery, carrots, and potatoes and add them accordingly.
  5. mince the garlic and add to broth.
  6. add herbs and chop the onion and add in.
  7. wait ten mins while mixture boils. then take the chicken out with a meat fork and cut the meat off bone. meat may be a bit raw but that's ok. Put debond meat back in the pot. turn mixture down to simmer.
  8. at this time you can add the noodles in. soup may still be at a boil. that's ok. wait another ten mins while noodles soften.
  9. now always taste your soup before you serve to make sure it is to your taste.
