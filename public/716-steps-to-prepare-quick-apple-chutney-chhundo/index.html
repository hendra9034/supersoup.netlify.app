The ingredients needed to make Apple chutney (chhundo):
  1. Make ready 2 cups grated cooking apples
  2. Get 2 tablespoon achar masala
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoon Kashmiri chilli powder
  6. Get 2 tablespoon salt
  7. Take 3 tablespoon oil
  8. Take 3-4 cloves

Steps to make Apple chutney (chhundo):
  1. Peel the apples and grate them. - In a pan add oil and cloves. Once heated add grated apples and sauté for couple of minutes.
  2. Add turmeric, salt and chilli powder and mix well let it cook for further minute. - Add in achar masala and mix well. - Finally add sugar and cook until sugar has melted and oil leaving sides. This will take about 4 to 5 minutes on medium heat. - Turn of the gas and let it cool.
  4. Transfer in an air tight jar and store. - Enjoy with puri, paratha, naan, bread sticks etc.
