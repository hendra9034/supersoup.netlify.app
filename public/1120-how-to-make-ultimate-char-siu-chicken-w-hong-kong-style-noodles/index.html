The ingredients needed to make Char Siu Chicken w/ Hong Kong Style Noodles:
  1. Get 2 chicken breasts
  2. Get 3 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Take 3 tbsp honey
  4. Get 3 tbsp ketchup
  5. Make ready 5 tbsp light soy sauce
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp beetroot powder
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  8. Take 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  9. Get 1/2 tsp Chinese 5 spice
  10. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  11. Get 2 tsp pepper
  12. Take 140 g dry egg noodles
  13. Take 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  14. Make ready 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
  15. Take 2 tbsp sesame oil
  16. Get 2 eggs
  17. Make ready 6 spring onions
  18. Take Pinch sesame seeds
  19. Prepare Pinch chilli flakes
  20. Make ready 80 ml hot water
Steps to make Char Siu Chicken w/ Hong Kong Style Noodles:
  1. Start by marinading your chicken in brown sugar, honey, ketchup, 3 tbsp light soy sauce, beetroot powder, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, and Chinese 5 spice for at least 30 minutes
  2. Fry chicken for 3 minutes each side in sesame oil until you’ve got a good colour and then get in the oven in tin foil at 180c for 20 minutes until cooked through
  3. Boil your egg for 6 minutes and get in a bowl of ice cold water to stop further cooking
  4. Char your spring onions in a pan for 2 minutes each side
  5. Boil your noodles for 4 minutes and then get in a pan with dark soy sauce, 2 tbsp light soy sauce, oyster sauce, water and fry for 5 minutes
  6. Serve your chicken on your noodles, with egg, spring onion and garnish with chilli flakes and sesame seeds
