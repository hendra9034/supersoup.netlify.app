The ingredients needed to make Basil and dried shrimps fried rice:
  1. Make ready 2 bowls rice
  2. Make ready 1/4 of red onion
  3. Make ready 1 handful basil leaves
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp dried shrimps
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp chicken salt
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  7. Take 1 egg

Instructions to make Basil and dried shrimps fried rice:
  1. Prepared all the ingredients as shown in the photo
  2. Stirfry onion until soft, add the chopped dried shrimps, toss it around a bit. Turn off the heat. Add rice, stir them well with chicken salt and chilli sauce - (see recipe)
  3. Turn the heat back on, add egg, bury it in the rice, put all the chopped basil leaves. Leave it for 2 mins. Then stir them well. Once the leaves are wilted, ready to serve.
