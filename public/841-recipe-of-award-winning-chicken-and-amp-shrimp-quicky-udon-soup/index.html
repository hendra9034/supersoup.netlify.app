The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Shrimp Quicky Udon Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 packages Chicken flavored udon noodles
  2. Take 1/2 cup Ground chicken
  3. Take 1 Precook end diced chicken breast
  4. Get 1/2 cup Asian slaw mix
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  6. Get 1 Precooked shrimp
  7. Prepare 1 boiled egg
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Ginger paste to taste
  9. Prepare 1 Parsley flakes to taste
  10. Make ready 1 Chopped green onions

Instructions to make Chicken & Shrimp Quicky Udon Soup:
  1. Start by boiling egg let cool and chop
  2. Cook your ground chicken, I added a little fruit seasoning with lime to enhance the flavor let cool
  3. The shrimp is Precooked at temperature so just set aside
  4. In a bowl I added all my other ingredients
  5. Now in a small pot add water as much as desired add ginger stir till resolved add in seasoning packed from noodles and parsley flakes bring to a slight boil. Now add in noodles cook as directed
  6. Now add your noodles and broth and all your toppings as you desire! Enjoy
