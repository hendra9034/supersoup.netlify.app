The ingredients needed to make Scrumptious Salmon teriyaki üêü:
  1. Take 2 pieces Salmon
  2. Get Salt and pepper
  3. Get 1 tbsp vegetable cooking oil
  4. Get Teriyaki sauce
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp grated ginger
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp honey
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp brown sugar
  9. Get 1-2 tsp mirin
  10. Take 1 tsp sake or chinese rice wine
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp corn flour
  13. Take 1/4 cup water or chicken stock
  14. Take (Crispy garlic and toasted sesame is optional)
Instructions to make Scrumptious Salmon teriyaki üêü:
  1. In a small saucepan on the medium heat hop, mix all of teriyaki sauce ingredients together stir well. Add water if needed. Put water and corn flour together in a small bowl, mix it well and pour into teriyaki sauce. Stir well. Leave it aside
  2. Marinate your salmon with salt and pepper and vegetable cooking oil for 10 min. Pan fry on non stick pan or griddle pan for 5-6 min and turn over and cook the other side for 5-6 mins.
  3. Add teriyaki sauce into the salmon pan, let it simmer for 2-3 min. Serve with japanese rice or jasmine rice
