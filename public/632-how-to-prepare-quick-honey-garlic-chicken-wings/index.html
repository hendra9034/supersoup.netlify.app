The ingredients needed to make 🍗🍯Honey Garlic Chicken Wings🍯🍗:
  1. Make ready 20-22 pieces chicken wings (without skin)
  2. Get For the Sauce
  3. Prepare 1 tsp salt or (to taste)
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup honey
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup soy sauce
  6. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  7. Take 2 tbsp sriracha hot chilli sauce or (to taste)
  8. Make ready 2-3 tblsp oil
  9. Prepare 1/2 tbsp lemon juice(optional)
  10. Make ready For the Chicken Wings Coating
  11. Take 1 cup flour
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  13. Get 1/4 tsp black pepper
  14. Make ready 1 & 2/1 tbsp corn flour
  15. Take Vegetable oil for frying
  16. Prepare Prepare Chicken Wings
  17. Get 1 tsp garlic ginger paste
  18. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  19. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  20. Prepare Garnishing
  21. Get 1 tsp sesame seeds
Steps to make 🍗🍯Honey Garlic Chicken Wings🍯🍗:
  1. Combine honey, soy sauce, garlic powder, sriracha hot sauce,lemon juice & oil in a sauce pan. Stir and let it boil. Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. Prepare the chicken wings by removing tips and then cutting a part at joint. Season with black pepper, salt & garlic ginger paste cook 7 to 8 minutes medium heigh flame without cover when the all water dried up or chicken half done switch off the flame and cool completely.
  3. In a large bowl mix flour, corn flour, black pepper & salt n mix well - Roll each piece in flour and deep fry or wok in heated oil until wings turn golden brown, around 3 to 4 minutes on each side. Drain on paper towel.
  4. In a large saucepan or bowl, toss wings with the honey garlic sauce. Transfer to a serving plate sprinkle sesame seeds serve hot with fries & fresh salad.
