Steps to make Lentil soup:
  1. In a big deep pan add olive oil and the smashed garlic cloves at low heat
  2. Dice all the peppers, celery and onions very small.
  3. Let simmer for about 2 minutes or until the onions turn translucent
  4. add the chicken stock and the can of fire roasted tomatoes
  5. Now add all the spices and let it cook at low heat for about 45 minutes covered with a lid til the lentils are cooked till the lentis are tender
