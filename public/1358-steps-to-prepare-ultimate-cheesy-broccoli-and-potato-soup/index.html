The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Broccoli and Potato Soup:
  1. Take 2 Heads Broccoli, chopped
  2. Get 2-3 Lg. Russet Potatoes, washed n scrubbed n cubed
  3. Get 1 Sm. Yellow Onion, minced
  4. Prepare 2 1/2 Qts. Chicken Broth
  5. Prepare 3 15oz.Cans Nacho Cheese Sauce
  6. Take 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream or Whole Milk
  7. Prepare 1 tsp. Salt and Pepper, to taste
  8. Take 2 tsps. Onion Powder
  9. Prepare 2 tsps. Garlic Powder
Instructions to make Cheesy Broccoli and Potato Soup:
  1. In a Large pot add the broccoli, potatoes, onion, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil over high heat stirring occasionally. Once to a boil turn heat down and let simmer til potatoes are tender.
