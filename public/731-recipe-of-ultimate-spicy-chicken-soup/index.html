Steps to make Spicy chicken soup:
  1. Mix garlic, onion, chili, chili powder, shrimp paste. blend
  2. Marinate chicken with : turmeric powder, salt, ajinomoto, corn starch
  3. Heat oil with medium fire, stir fry ingredients until color changed
  4. Enter chicken, mix for awhile and put water
  5. High fire until chicken cook, add salt more then taste it
  6. Lastly squeeze lemon into soup and leave it with cover at least 5 minutes then serve
