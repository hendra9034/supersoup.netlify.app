The ingredients needed to make Spicy bean soup:
  1. Get 5 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp rosemary
  3. Prepare 7 oz pancetta - finely chopped
  4. Take 2 carrots - diced
  5. Get 1 celery stick - diced
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp dry chile flakes
  7. Take 4 pork sausages - sliced
  8. Take 14 oz cannelli beans
  9. Take 14 oz borlotti beans
  10. Take 14 can garbanzo beans
  11. Prepare 14 can green lentils
  12. Get 2 cup vegetable stock
  13. Make ready 3 oz pecorino cheese - shaved
  14. Make ready 3 clove garlic - crushed

  2. add beans and lentils with stock. bring to a boil, reduce the heat, then half cover the pan with a lid. cook for 30 mins stirring occasionally.
