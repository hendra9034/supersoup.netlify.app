The ingredients needed to make Spicy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 3 Chicken thigh fillet
  2. Take 3 medium potatoes, washed
  3. Make ready 2 medium carrots, sliced
  4. Prepare 3 Rasher bacon, diced
  5. Make ready 1 medium brown onion, chopped
  6. Take 2 Celery stalks, sliced
  7. Get 3/4 cup Peas and corn
  8. Take 2 tsp Ground cumin
  9. Get 2 tsp Ground coriander
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  11. Take 1 tsp Turmeric
  12. Get 2 tbsp Chopped parsley
  13. Make ready 2 Litres Chicken stock
  14. Get 1 pack 2 min noodles, crushed
  15. Prepare 1 Salt & pepper to taste
Instructions to make Spicy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Coat chicken in parsley, cumin, coriander, cayenne and turmeric. Cover and place in fridge while you prepare your veges.
  2. In a large non stick pot, fry onion and bacon and set aside. Brown chicken and set aside. Now add stock, potatoes, carrot, celery and bacon and onion. Bring to boil and simmer until veges have softened.
  3. Chop chicken 2cm pieces and throw into soup along with peas and corn and noodle. Cook until chicken is finished cooking through and noodles are soft. Salt and pepper to serve. So easy, so very tasty. Enjoy!
