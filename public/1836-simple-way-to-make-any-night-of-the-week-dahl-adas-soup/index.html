The ingredients needed to make dahl adas soup:
  1. Make ready 1 onion
  2. Get 3 tbsp rice
  3. Take 3 tbsp bulgor
  4. Get 3 tbsp dahl lentil
  5. Get 3 tbsp flour
  6. Prepare 1 tsp dried mint
  7. Get 1 tbsp tomato puree
  8. Make ready 1 dash salt
  9. Take 2 tsp lime juice
  10. Make ready 1 dash peper
  11. Make ready 3 tsp butter

Instructions to make dahl adas soup:
  1. let rice and bulgar and lentil remain in water for 30 min
  2. peel and mince onion. put butter in skillet and cook onion until brown
  3. add flour and mix until flour become golden
  4. add salt and peper. and then tomato puree and mint and mix them well
  5. add 1cup of boiled water and stir until all ingredient become smooth and consistant
  6. add 1 cup of cold water and then add rice, bulgar and lentil. let them cook.
  7. serve with lime or lemon juice
