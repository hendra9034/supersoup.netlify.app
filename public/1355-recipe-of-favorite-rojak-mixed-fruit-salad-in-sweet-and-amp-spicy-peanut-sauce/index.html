  1. Make ready A. Veggies & Fruit
  2. Take 2 C pineapple - thinly cubed/sliced
  3. Make ready 1 C sliced jicama/sweet turnip
  4. Get 1/2 C sliced cucumber

  6. Get B. The Sauce

Steps to make Rojak (Mixed-Fruit Salad) In Sweet & Spicy Peanut Sauce:
  1. Prep A - cut/slice veggies & fruit. Place in a big mixing bowl.
  2. Prep B - Mix corn flour & water, put aside. Next, place crushed coconut sugar, white sugar, sweet soy sauce, chili sauce & dark soy sauce in a sauce pan. Cook over low heat, keep stirring until sugar is dissolved and everything is well mixed. Add corn flour mixture. Stir to mix. Turn off the heat.
