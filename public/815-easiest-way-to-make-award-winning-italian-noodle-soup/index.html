The ingredients needed to make Italian Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 4 cup Chicken or Vegetable Broth
  2. Get 12 oz Fettuccine Noodles (or another noodle type)
  3. Take 8 oz Spinach
  4. Make ready 3 can Diced Tomatoes (15 oz. cans), Not Drained
  5. Prepare 1 Onion, Chopped
  6. Make ready 5 clove Garlic
  7. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Dried Basil
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Dried Oregano
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  10. Get OPTIONAL:
  11. Get 1 lb Ground Beef
Instructions to make Italian Noodle Soup:
  1. If you are adding ground beef, brown it first and drain fat.
  2. Put broth in a large soup pot, heat until bubbling.
  3. Add in all other ingredients (break noodles in half to fit in pot) and cook until noodles are al dente (cooked).
  4. Serve hot with French bread!
