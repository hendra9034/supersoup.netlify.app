  2. Preheat oven to 180 C°. Bring griddle pan to high heat, put the meat roll into the heated pan, brown well from both sides, about 6-7 minutes from each side. In preheated oven cook meat through for another 12 to 15 minutes until cooked to the point to your liking. Remove from oven. Be very careful as handle of pan might be hot, rest the loin well.
