The ingredients needed to make Chickpeas & pork soup:
  1. Prepare 8 pork ribs
  2. Get 200 gr dried chickpeas
  3. Make ready 20 gr butter
  4. Prepare 1 small potato
  5. Get 1 liter broth
  6. Make ready 1/2 onion
  7. Get Croutons
  8. Prepare Salt & pepper

Instructions to make Chickpeas & pork soup:
  1. Leave chickpeas 12 hour in water. Cook them in boiling water for 2 hours
  2. Chop the onion,put it in a saucepan with 30 gr of butter and fry for 5 minutes
  3. Drain chickpeas and put them together with the sofritto in a pot with 1 liter of boiling broth,then put ribs and pealed potato. Season with salt
  4. The soup will be ready in 20 minutes. Mash potato to thicken,sprinkle with pepper and serve with croutons
