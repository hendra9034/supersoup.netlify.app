Instructions to make Egg Drop Soup:
  1. Add water and other ingredients of broth into a large pot and bring to a boil. Once boiling bring temperature down and wait until reduced by half. (Skim any excess fat on top with a spoon)
  2. After broth is ready, take all the chicken out into a separate bowl. Strain the rest of the broth into another bowl.
  3. Pull all the meat out of the chicken. Make sure not to use any fatty parts, bones or skin.
  4. Add broth, chicken and corn into a pot.
  5. Add corn starch and mix
  6. Turn of the heat.
  7. Put eggs into a bowl and stir well. Then add into the soup.
  8. Add salt to taste but make sure to use little salt as Soy Sauce will add extra saltiness too.
  9. Add Soy Sauce to taste (I did 3 tb spns)
