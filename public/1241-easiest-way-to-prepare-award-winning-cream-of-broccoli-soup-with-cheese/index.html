The ingredients needed to make Cream of broccoli soup with cheese:
  1. Prepare 3 large broccoli bunches
  2. Get 1 medium onion, diced
  3. Take 1 can of chicken broth
  4. Prepare 2 quart warm water
  5. Take 4 stalks celery, diced
  6. Get 1 packages dry dairy free creamer
  7. Take 2/3 cup reduced calorie margarine
  8. Prepare 2/3 cup flour
  9. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
  10. Take 1 grated cheese to taste

Steps to make Cream of broccoli soup with cheese:
  1. Clean broccoli and cut into small pieces to steam/simmer along with onion and celery (approx. 20 minutes) in butter and broth. When tender, mix dry creamer and water in a separate large bowl.
  2. Slowly stir in 2/3-1 cup flour into broccoli, celery, and onion mix until absorbed. Slowly stir in milk mixture over medium heat until thickened. Add cheese to taste, cook until melted.
