The ingredients needed to make Healthy Turkey Chilli Con Carne:
  1. Prepare Low calorie cooking spray
  2. Prepare 500 g Lean Minced Turkey
  3. Make ready 2 medium red onions
  4. Make ready 3 tsp minced garlic
  5. Prepare 1 courgette cut into 1cm cubes
  6. Prepare 2 peppers (I used 1 orange and 1 green)
  7. Prepare 2 tsp ground cumin
  8. Prepare 1 tsp smoked paprika
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready 1 tsp hot chilli powder
  11. Get 400 g can mixed beans (drained and rinsed)
  12. Get 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  13. Prepare 350 ml beef stock
  14. Get Rice to serve
Instructions to make Healthy Turkey Chilli Con Carne:
  1. Spray a large non-stick pan with cooking spray. Add the turkey and stir-fry over a high heat for 5–6 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, courgette and peppers and stir-fry for a further 3–4 minutes. Add the cumin, smoked paprika, cinnamon and chilli powder and stir-fry for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add the kidney beans, tomatoes and stock and season with black pepper. Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer, cover and cook for 25–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Uncover, stir and cook over a medium heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Cook rice as per instructions and serve.
