Steps to make Pimped-up lipton chicken noodle soup:
  1. Prepare soup as per directions on package
  2. Meanwhile, coarsly chop the spinach or kale leafs and put aside
  3. Wisk the eggs in a small bowl and put aside
  4. When soup is ready, add spinach/kale to soup and stir
  5. Pour in the eggs while stirring with a fork
  6. Add fresh ground black pepper to taste and stir well
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
