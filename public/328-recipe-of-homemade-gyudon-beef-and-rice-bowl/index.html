The ingredients needed to make Gyudon (beef and rice bowl):
  1. Prepare 200 g rib eye beef, very thinly sliced or pre sliced beef meat from butchers
  2. Prepare Gyudon sauce:
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or any cooking oil)
  4. Make ready 1 small brown onion, finely skiced
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon fresh or minced garlic
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon fresh or minced ginger
  7. Make ready 8 tablespoons water
  8. Take 4 tablespoons soba noodle sauce or 1/4 teaspoon dashi powder
  9. Make ready 4 tablespoons light soy sauce
  10. Take 4 tablespoons mirin
  11. Prepare 1-2 tablespoons white sugar (caster or granulated)
  12. Take Garnish:
  13. Take 1/2 spring onions, finely sliced
  14. Make ready Sprinkles of Shichimi (Japanese 7-spice)
  15. Take 1 egg yolk (optional)
  16. Take Sprinkle black sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions to make Gyudon (beef and rice bowl):
  1. Thinly slice the rib eye, transfer onto a plate and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan add on medium high heat, add the cooking oil and then add the sliced onions.
  3. Sautee onions until soft, then add the thinly sliced beef along with the ginger and garlic paste. Cook the meat until it is no longer pink.
  4. Then to the onions and meat add water, soba noodle sauce or dashi powder, light soya sauce, mirin and sugar. Stir continuously until sugar has dissolved. Reduce heat to medium low and cover with a lid. Let the broth cook and reduce for about 7-10 minutes. Taste and amend if necessary (add more soya sauce, sugar, water etc).
  5. Once the meat has cooked and the sauce has reduced. Turn heat off and remove saucepan from the heat.
  6. To serve place steamed rice in each bowl at the bottom, then using chopsticks is tongs place beef and onions over the rice. Add the egg yolk (optional) and garnish with spring onions, shichimi and sesame seeds (optional).
  7. Spoon over as much it little of the broth and pour it over the meat, onions and rice.
