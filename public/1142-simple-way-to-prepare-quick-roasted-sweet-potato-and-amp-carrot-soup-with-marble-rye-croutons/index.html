  2. Take 1 large sweet potato, peeled & cubed
  3. Get 1 small onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 1 half red bell pepper
  5. Take 4 carrots peeled & chopped
  6. Take 3 (12 oz) cans vegetable broth
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil
  8. Get Fresh oregano
