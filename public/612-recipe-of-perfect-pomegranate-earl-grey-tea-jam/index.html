The ingredients needed to make ★Pomegranate Earl Grey tea Jam★:
  1. Get 2 pomegranates
  2. Take 6 T Sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 Teaspoon Earl Gray Tea leaf
Instructions to make ★Pomegranate Earl Grey tea Jam★:
  1. Two pomegranates bear fruit
  2. Mix the pomegranate in a blender
  3. Strain the pomegranate fruit in a blender and put it in a pan.
  4. Add sugar and Earl Gray tea leaves to pomegranate juice, heat and simmer until the total amount is reduced to 1/3.　Choose fine (powder-like) leaves for Earl Gray tea. I used the contents of the tea bag.
  5. Earl Gray tea leaves may be added when the pomegranate is placed in a mixer.
