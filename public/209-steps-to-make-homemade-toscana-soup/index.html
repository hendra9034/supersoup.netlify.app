The ingredients needed to make Toscana soup:
  1. Take 2 3/4 cups chicken stock or broth
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup heavy cream
  3. Get 3 Medium russet potatoes
  4. Prepare 2 cups Chopped kale
  5. Make ready 1/2 pound sweet Italian sausage or spicy
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Steps to make Toscana soup:
  1. Combine stock and cream in a sauce pan over medium heat
  2. Sliced or unpeeled potatoes and a half inch slices then quarter the slices and add to the soup
  3. Add kale
  4. Grill or sauté the sausage. When cooked, slice the sausage into about 1/4 to half inch thick slices. Add the sausage to the soup.
  5. Add spices and let soup simmer for about an hour. Stir occasionally.
