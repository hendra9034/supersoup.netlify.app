The ingredients needed to make Roasted Beef With My Garden Herbs:
  1. Take 250 g good beef
  2. Make ready Salt
  3. Get herbs
Steps to make Roasted Beef With My Garden Herbs:
  1. Sprinkle beef with salt and pepper. Fry all the surface in pan.
  2. Take various kinds of herbs from the garden, put them around the beef and wrap them in oven paper.
  3. Wrap with aluminum film and put it in ziplock.
  4. Put it in rice cooker and fill the boiling water then cover with cup or bowl. And turn on keeping warm button (70℃)
  5. After 1 hour take it out and cool. Slice beef and sprinkle salt a little.
  6. Enjoy with fried beef rice.
