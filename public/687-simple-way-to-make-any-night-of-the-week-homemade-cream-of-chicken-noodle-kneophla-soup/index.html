The ingredients needed to make Homemade Cream Of Chicken Noodle (Kneophla) Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 sticks butter
  2. Make ready 1 diced onion
  3. Prepare 2 large chicken breasts
  4. Take Kneophla noodles
  5. Make ready 3 cups flour
  6. Make ready 1 cup cold water
  7. Get 2 Lrg eggs
  8. Make ready Add to saut√©ed onions
  9. Make ready 8 cups hot water
  10. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  11. Prepare 3 1/2 Tbls powder chicken broth
  12. Take Add after noodles are cooked
  13. Make ready 16 oz frozen mixed vegetables
  14. Take 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  15. Take Parsley flakes
Instructions to make Homemade Cream Of Chicken Noodle (Kneophla) Soup:
  1. Cook 2 large chicken breasts. I personally use my pressure cooker, but you can boil or lightly fry chicken.
  2. While you are cooking chicken start to saut√© your onions. Use all butter and a whole chopped onion.
  3. While the onions are cooking start making your noodles. Roll 1/4 sections at a time with a rolling pin, and cut into small squares with a pizza cutter. Scrap off the counter with a pastry scraper along with extra flour and put on a flat pan.
  4. Add chicken broth to saut√©ed onions, water and bay leaves. Bring to a boil. Add noodles after boiling.
  5. After noodles are cooked add frozen vegetables and diced chicken breasts. Cook until vegetables are hot then add Cream and parsley flakes.
