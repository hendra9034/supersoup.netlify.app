The ingredients needed to make Creamed Wild Mushroom Pasta:
  1. Take Handful dried wild mushrooms soaked for 30 mins in 400ml of hot water
  2. Take 6 chestnut mushrooms
  3. Take 1 small leek
  4. Prepare 2 cloves garlic crushed
  5. Get About 8 baby corns cut into 4 length ways
  6. Make ready Handful fresh rocket
  7. Take 200 ml oat cream
  8. Make ready 5 large Basil leaves finely shredded
  9. Take 1 tbsp chopped celery leaf
  10. Get 1 tsp chopped fresh thyme
  11. Prepare 100 ml dark rum
  12. Get S&P
  13. Take 1/2 pk gluten free & eggless pasta spaghetti (optional)
  14. Make ready As much Parmesan as you wish. I used vegan Parmesan in this recipe
  15. Get Tip..
  16. Prepare Do not refresh the pasta in cold water. Just use a little olive oil. This allows the sauce to coat the pasta better
Instructions to make Creamed Wild Mushroom Pasta:
  1. Cook spaghetti as required 1 min before the end drop in the baby corn. Drain & coat in a little olive oil.
  2. Drain soaked mushrooms & chop small. Keep liquid for mushroom stock.
  3. In a pan over medium heat add a little olive oil & cook the leeks for a few minutes then add the garlic & thyme. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add all the mushrooms & cook for a few minutes. Add the rum & flambé. Pour in stock & rapidly reduce by 2 thirds.
  5. Add the cream & simmer for a few minutes & add the rocket till it wilts. Season to taste. Mix in the Basil then the spaghetti. Coat in the sauce.
  6. Serve & top with the celery leaf
