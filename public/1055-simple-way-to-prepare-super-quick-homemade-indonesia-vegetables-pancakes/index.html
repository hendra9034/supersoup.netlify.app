The ingredients needed to make Indonesia vegetables pancakes:
  1. Make ready 100 gr cabbage cut it thin small slice
  2. Prepare 100 gr carrots cut it thin small slice
  3. Get 2 stalks spring onion cut into small pieces
  4. Take 100 gr all-purpose flour
  5. Get 100 ml water
  6. Make ready 1 egg
  7. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Get 3 pieces garlic shredded or 1tsp garlic powder
  9. Take 1/2 tsp chicken powder
  10. Prepare 1 tsp ground pepper
  11. Make ready 500 gr oil for deep frying
  12. Get 1 tbs rice flour
