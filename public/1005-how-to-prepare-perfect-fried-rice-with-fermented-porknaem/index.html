The ingredients needed to make Fried rice with fermented pork(Naem):
  1. Make ready 1 cup Steamed rice
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Fermented pork
  3. Prepare 1/2 Onion, finely chopped
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Get 1 tbsp Vegetable oil
  6. Get 1 tbsp Oyster sauce for seasoning
  7. Get 1-2 Spring onion, finely chopped

Instructions to make Fried rice with fermented pork(Naem):

  4. Add egg and scramble it with all the ingredients. Keep the egg mildly undercooked.
