The ingredients needed to make Baked Chicken Tenderloins:
  1. Get 500-600 g Chicken Tenderloins *Fish fillets can be used
  2. Take Oil to grease the baking dish *I used Olive Oil
  3. Prepare Japanese Mayonnaise
  4. Make ready Cheese Mix for Baking *e.g. I used a mix of Cheddar, Parmesan and Mozzarella
  5. Take Seasoning Mix
  6. Take 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Ground Pepper OR Ground Chilli
  8. Get 1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  9. Take 1/2 teaspoon Onion Powder
  10. Make ready 2 teaspoons Curry Powder
  11. Prepare *Note: Create your own Spice Mix. Other suggestions include Ground Cumin, Ground Coriander Seeds, Paprika, Dried Oregano, Dried Basil, etc
Steps to make Baked Chicken Tenderloins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 220℃. Oil a baking dish OR line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. In a small bowl, combine Salt, Ground Pepper, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder and Curry Powder (OR spices of your choice). Sprinkle the mix over Chicken Tenderloins and coat evenly.
  3. Place Chicken Tenderloins in the prepared baking dish (OR on the tray), drizzle some Japanese Mayonnaise over, and sprinkle with some shredded Cheese.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes OR until nicely browned and Chicken Tenderloins are cooked. *Note: Chicken Tenderloins can be cooked very quickly and it is easy to over-cook them.
