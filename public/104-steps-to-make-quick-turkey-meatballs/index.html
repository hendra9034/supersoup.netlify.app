The ingredients needed to make Turkey Meatballs:
  1. Get 800 g turkey mince
  2. Take 1 egg
  3. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  5. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1 garlic clove
  7. Take salt and pepper
  8. Make ready 40 g maple syrup
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp djion mustard
  11. Take 125 g unsweetened applesauce

Instructions to make Turkey Meatballs:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (fan oven)
  2. In a large bowl beat the egg, then add the turkey mince, paprika, cayenne, cinnamon, garlic and salt and pepper and mix until well combined
  3. Roll 15 meatballs from the mixture and place on a lined baking tray
  4. Cook in preheated oven for 20 minutes
  5. In a large frying pan combine maple syrup, white wine vinegar, djion mustard and apple sauce and cook over a medium heat for 5 minutes
  6. Add the meatballs to the pan and coat in the sauce for another 5 minutes
  7. We served with courgetti spaghetti and a homemade tomato sauce with basil!
