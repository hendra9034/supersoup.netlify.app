The ingredients needed to make French onion soup:
  1. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
  2. Prepare 3 onions chopped or sliced fine
  3. Get 1 tsp sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp flour
  5. Get 1 box of beef broth
  6. Make ready 6 slice of french bread
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup red wine
  8. Make ready 1 thin slices if gruyere cheese
  9. Prepare 1 cup water

Steps to make French onion soup:
  1. melt the butter in a pot, add onions, stir and cover
  2. cook 15 mins stirring often. sprinkle with flour cook 1min.
  3. uncover. add sugar and cook on med. for 10 min.stirring.
  4. stir in broth, wine and water. simmer 5 min.
  5. best broiler. place bread on cookie sheet and toast.
  6. place 6 crocks on baking sheet.
  7. divide soup into bowls. place toast on top, top with cheese.
  8. heat under broiler until cheese is melted and bubbly. remove carefully from oven and serve.
