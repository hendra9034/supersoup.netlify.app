The ingredients needed to make Lamb meat and turnip curry: (Shaljam gosht):
  1. Get 1 lbs lamb meat with bones, washed
  2. Get 2 medium onion finely chopped
  3. Make ready 2 medium tomatoes / pureed
  4. Prepare 4 /5 medium turnips, peeled and cut into quarters
  5. Take 1 Tbsp fresh ginger and garlic paste
  6. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  7. Get 7 . 4 cloves
  8. Make ready 8 . 4 pepper corns
  9. Get 2 black cardamoms
  10. Make ready 2 green cardamoms
  11. Prepare 1 stick cinnamon
  12. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
  13. Make ready 1 green chilli chopped
  14. Get 1 green chilli whole
  15. Take 2 tsp salt or to taste
  16. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder

Steps to make Lamb meat and turnip curry: (Shaljam gosht):
  1. In about a 1/4 cup of hot oil, fry cumin, cinnamon, bay leaves, pepper corns, cloves for a few minutes until cumin seeds splutter. - - Add onion and fry until golden brown. - Add meat and garlic-ginger paste and chopped green chili. - Fry on low-medium until the rawness of the ginger garlic goes away.
  2. Add all the spices, salt and mix well. - Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, on low. - You may need to add 1/2 cup water to help meat's cooking process. - When meat is about 3/4 cooked, add tomatoes / pur√©e fry well till all water dry then add turnips and the whole green chilli fry 6-7 mins.
  3. Add another 1/2 cup of boiled water. Mix, cover and cook.(if you need more gravy so you can add more water) - Be sure to stir from time to time. - When oil separates and the meat is fully cooked, the turnips will also be tender, not mushy. - At this point curry is done, sprinkle 1/2 tsp of cinnamon powder add coriander, give it a stir, and enjoy with roti/rice or naan.
