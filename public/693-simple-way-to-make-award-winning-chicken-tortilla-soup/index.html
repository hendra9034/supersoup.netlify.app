The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tortilla Soup:
  1. Get Butter
  2. Take medium Onion (chopped)
  3. Make ready Garlic (minced)
  4. Take Celery (chopped small)
  5. Make ready Chicken (chopped)
  6. Prepare Salt (course)
  7. Make ready Black Pepper (ground)
  8. Make ready Cumin (ground)
  9. Take Paprika (ground)
  10. Make ready Cayenne Pepper (ground)
  11. Make ready Chicken Broth
  12. Prepare medium Carrots (chopped)
  13. Prepare Green Onions (chopped)
  14. Make ready Cilantro Leaves
  15. Get medium Lime
  16. Get large Tomato
  17. Take medium Acocado
  18. Get Cilantro
  19. Take Cheese (shredded)
Instructions to make Chicken Tortilla Soup:
  1. Chop celery and add to pot. Cook until onions are translucent, around 8 minutes.
  2. Melt butter in large pot on medium heat.
  3. Chop onion. Add onions and garlic to pot.
  4. Clean chicken and chop into bite size pieces. Season with salt and black pepper.
  5. Add chicken, paprika, cumin, and cayenne to pot. Increase heat to medium-high and cook, stirring occasionally, around 8 minutes.
  6. Add chicken broth to pot and stir.
  7. Chop carrots and add to pot.
  8. Bring soup to a boil for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to low and cover, simmer for 30 minutes.
  9. Chop onions and cilantro. Mix into soup and boil another 5 minutes.
  10. Slice lime and squeeze half into soup. Cover and let soup cool for 10 minutes.
  11. Serve with fresh chopped cilantro, lime, tomato, avocado and shredded cheese.
