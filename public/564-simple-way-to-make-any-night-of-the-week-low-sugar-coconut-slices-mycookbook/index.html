The ingredients needed to make Low sugar coconut slices #mycookbook:
  1. Prepare 200 g butter, melted
  2. Make ready 50 g caster sugar
  3. Get 140 g flour
  4. Take 100 g dessicated coconut
  5. Prepare 100 g ground almonds
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Prepare 15 g flour
  8. Make ready 30 g butter
  9. Prepare 150 g chopped dried apricots
  10. Prepare 1 tsp mixed spice

Instructions to make Low sugar coconut slices #mycookbook:
  1. Mix together the first 5 ingredients
  2. In a pan, heat gently the 30g butter with the fruit and spice, then set aside.
  3. Put three quarters of the base mixture into a lined rectangular tin (approx. 8” x 10”)
  4. Quickly mix the eggs and the 15g flour into the topping mix. Spread over the base.
  5. Take the remaining quarter of the base mix and crumble it over the top.
  6. Bake for approx. 20-25 minutes at 160 degrees until the crumble topping is golden. Allow to cool completely before cutting into squares or bars.
