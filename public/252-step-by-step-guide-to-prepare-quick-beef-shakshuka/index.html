The ingredients needed to make Beef shakshuka:
  1. Prepare 200 g mince beef
  2. Take 200 g tomato purée
  3. Prepare 3 sweet peppers, chopped
  4. Take 4 eggs
  5. Get 1/2 onion, diced
  6. Prepare 4 garlic cloves, finely chooped
  7. Get 2 tsp paprika
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cumin
  9. Get 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  10. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  11. Take 1/2 cup feta cheese
  12. Take Salt and pepper to taste
  13. Get Pita or any kind of bread, for serving

Instructions to make Beef shakshuka:
  1. Heat the olive oil in a large sauté pan. Add the sweet pepper and onion. Cook on low-medium heat until the onions turn translucent.
  2. Add garlic, paprika, cumin and chilli powder.
  3. Add tomato purée. Add salt and pepper to taste. Let it simmer for 5-10 min.
  4. Make small wells in the sauce and crack the eggs into each well. Cover the pan for 5 mins.
  5. Generously sprinkle feta cheese. Serve hot with pita or any kinds of bread.
