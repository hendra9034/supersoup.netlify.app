The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Chinese Beef soup:
  1. Make ready 3 lb beef stew meat, cubed into 1 inch
  2. Get 1 Tbl cooking oil
  3. Prepare 1 (28 oz) can of Crushed tomatoes
  4. Prepare 12 oz water
  5. Get 1/3 Cup soy sauce
  6. Take 1/2 Cup cooking wine
  7. Get 1 cinnamon stick
  8. Prepare 2 bay leaves
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Chinese Five spice seasoning
  10. Take 2 Cups carrots, cut into 1 inch length
  11. Get 3 ribs of celery, cut into 1 inch length
  12. Get 1 large onion, cut into 1 inch cubes
  13. Get Salt and pepper to taste
  14. Take Chopped scallion or cilantro for decor
Instructions to make Instant Pot Chinese Beef soup:
  1. Use the saute setting on Instant pot, stir fry the beef in cooking oil until brown.
  2. Add in all ingredients except scallion or cilantro and use the pressure cooker setting on "Stew" on Medium and cook for 35 minutes
  3. Once done, quick pressure release (be careful, lots of steam will come out) the pot and your stew is ready!!!
  4. Add chopped scallion or cilantro to your stew and enjoy.
  5. This soup goes great with noodles, rice, or bread as dipping!
