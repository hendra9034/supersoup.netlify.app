The ingredients needed to make Beef Fried Rice Noodle 乾炒牛河:
  1. Get Rice noodles
  2. Get Beef (thinly sliced)
  3. Make ready Spring Onions (white part and green part separated, cut in 2 inches)
  4. Make ready 1/2 onions (thinly sliced)
  5. Make ready chicken stock powder (optional)
  6. Prepare 1st step Marinate
  7. Take 1 tbsp soya sauce
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp white sugar
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp shao xing wine
  10. Take sprinkle of white pepper
  11. Get 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  12. Take 2nd step marinate
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp water
  14. Take 3nd step marinate
  15. Get 1 tbsp GF flour
  16. Get 1 tbsp water
Steps to make Beef Fried Rice Noodle 乾炒牛河:
  1. Soak beef in warm water and clean them to remove smell.
  2. Drain the water and towel pat dry. Marinate the beef with the 1step ingredients and mix them wait for a min.
  3. Once the beef absorbed the ingredients, add 2nd step marinate to the beef.
  4. Mix the 3rd step marinate to the beef. then leave it in the fridge for at least 20 mins. Add 1 tsp oil to the beef before cooking.
  5. Heat up the wok with 2 tbsp oil. In with beef. do not stir it. remove them once cooked.
  6. In with onions, cook for a min. Add noodles into the wok, do not stir them, let it cook for a while and the flip over. once both side cooked, stir fry for a short while.
  7. Add white spring onions and bean sprouts.
  8. Add beef back into the wok.
  9. Add green spring onions, 1 tbsp soya sauce, 1 tsp soya sauce. - stir fry.
  10. Add chicken stock powder (optional).
  11. Serve with fried egg.
