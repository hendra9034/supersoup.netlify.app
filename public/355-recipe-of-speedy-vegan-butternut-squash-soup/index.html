The ingredients needed to make Vegan Butternut Squash Soup:
  1. Prepare olive oil
  2. Make ready medium onion
  3. Take garlic cloves minced
  4. Take butternut squash peeled seeds removed and cubed
  5. Prepare sea salt
  6. Get ground black pepper
  7. Take fresh thyme leaves (We also used sage and ginger)
  8. Make ready vegetable broth
  9. Make ready Silk Original Almondmilk
  10. Get lime juice
Instructions to make Vegan Butternut Squash Soup:
  1. In a large stock pot, heat the olive oil over medium high heat until shimmering.
  2. Add in the butternut squash, stir to combine, and sauté for about 7 minutes until the butternut squash releases some flavour and the onions become translucent.
  3. Add in the salt, pepper, thyme, vegetable broth, almond milk, stir to combine.
  4. Bring to a boil and then to a simmer for another 10 minutes, until the butternut squash becomes fork tender. Test it with a fork to ensure it is properly cooked through.
  5. Remove from heat. Pour the soup into a large blender. Now add the lime juice into the blender too. If you're not using a large blender (holds at least 6 cups) then do this in increments. Using the soup setting on your blender, or a very powerful blend option, blend until creamy and smooth. My blender automatically blends for about 3 minutes continuously. OR, you can use an immersion blender and puree the soup ingredients until combined and smooth.
  6. Taste and add salt and pepper to taste if necessary.
  7. Serve into bowls and top with garnish - I love extra nutmeg, parsley and a bit of extra almond milk or a touch of coconut milk on top (OPTIONAL).
