The ingredients needed to make Lentil and Sausage Soup:
  1. Get 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  2. Prepare 2 cup sliced leeks (some greens)
  3. Get 1 tbsp minced garlic
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1 tsp dried thyme leaves
  6. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  8. Get 1 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 cup chopped celery
  10. Make ready 6 cup chicken broth
  11. Prepare 16 oz Lentils, rinsed and drained
  12. Take 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  13. Get 1 lb kielbasa, cut in half, lengthwise, sliced

Steps to make Lentil and Sausage Soup:
  1. Heat oil in large stock pot over medium heat.
  2. Saute' leeks, garlic, salt, thyme, black pepper, and cumin until leeks are softened.
  3. Stir in carrots and celery, saute 5 minutes.
  4. Add in chicken broth, lentils and tomato paste.
  5. Cover and simmer for 50 minutes.
  6. Stir in kielbasa; simmer 10 minutes.
  7. Serve with Parmesan Cheese, if desired.
