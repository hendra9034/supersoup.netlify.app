The ingredients needed to make Lentil Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 cups green lentils
  2. Make ready 1 cup pork chicharones
  3. Prepare As much as you want Spinach
  4. Make ready As much as you want Garlic
  5. Make ready 1 medium chopped onion
  6. Get 1 small chopped tomato
  7. Prepare 1 pork buillon cube
  8. Take 1 tbsp oil
  9. Get 3 cups water
  10. Make ready Salt & pepper

Steps to make Lentil Soup:
  1. Set a pot on medium heat. Saute garlic, onion and tomato.
  2. Add lentils, pork buillon and water. Cook for about 20 mins or until lentils looks a little mushy. Add water if it gets too dry.
  3. Add chicharones, spinach, salt & pepper and cook for 5 mins.
