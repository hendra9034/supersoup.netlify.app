The ingredients needed to make Fideo Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 large bag of fideo noodles
  2. Prepare 1 chicken breast
  3. Take 1 clove garlic
  4. Make ready 1 slice onion
  5. Prepare 6 oz tomato paste
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp chicken flavor
  7. Take Water
  8. Make ready Oil
  9. Make ready Desired vegetables
Instructions to make Fideo Soup:
  1. Put chicken to boil
  2. Put oil in a pot and start cooking noodles. stir and make sure not to over cook. at the same time…
  3. Add paste, chicken flavor, onion, garlic and some water in a blender and blend till smooth
  4. After noodles are slightly browned and chicken is done, add the chicken broth and blended ingredients. put to boil.
  5. After chicken has cooled, shred and add. add desired vegetables
  6. Let boil for 15 mins or until noodles are done
  7. Serve
