The ingredients needed to make Shrimp with Corn:

  2. Prepare Half head garlic,minced
  3. Get Thumb size ginger minced
  4. Prepare 12 pieces shrimps
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp cooking oil
  6. Prepare 1 little bit water
  7. Take 1 tsp sugar
  8. Get 1 tbsp cooking wine
  9. Prepare 1 tsp soy
