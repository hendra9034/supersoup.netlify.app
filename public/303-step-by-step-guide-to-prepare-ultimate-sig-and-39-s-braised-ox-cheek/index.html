The ingredients needed to make Sig's braised ox cheek:
  1. Prepare 600 gram ox cheek
  2. Make ready 75 gr seasoned flour (salt, pepper, pimento or paprika)
  3. Make ready 2 large carrots

  14. Make ready 1 sprinkling As El Hanout (optional,gives more exotic flavour)

Steps to make Sig's braised ox cheek:
  1. Cut the ox cheek into bite size cubes, chop the onions roughly. Add seasoned flour into a bag with the cubed meat, shake about until the meat is covered slightly with the flour.
  2. Heat a frying pan with the butter and oil. In the meant heat your crockpot or slowcooker on low heat. Add the meat into the frying pan in batches. Brown all over, remove from pan. You may have to add a little more butter or oil. Transfer the browned meat into the heated crockpot.
  3. Add the onions into the same pan you cooked the meat in without cleaning the oil out of pan.
