The ingredients needed to make Beef, Bacon, and Vegetable Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 cups chopped cabbage
  2. Get 1 small white onion
  3. Make ready 1 small red onion
  4. Make ready 32 ounces meat spaghetti sauce I used Mids brand
  5. Make ready 1 large carrot
  6. Take 1 cup sliced celery
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon white ground pepper
  8. Make ready To taste salt
  9. Get 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  10. Make ready 1/4 pound smoked bacon
  11. Get 1 pound ground sirloin
  12. Get 1/2 pound frozen French fries
  13. Take 1/2 teaspoon fennel seed
  14. Prepare 1 pint water
Instructions to make Beef, Bacon, and Vegetable Soup:
  1. Wash and chop the vegetables.
  2. Slice the bacon and cook render the fat. Add the pepper. Stir for a minute.
  3. Add the fennel seeds, French fries, beef, and vegetables, stir coating them all. Add salt, cover and simmer till tender stirring as needed.
  4. When the vegetables are tender add the garlic give a good stir. Add the sauce. Add the water. Simmer 15 minutes.
  5. Serve I hope you enjoy!
