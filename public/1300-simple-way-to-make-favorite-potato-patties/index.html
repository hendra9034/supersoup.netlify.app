Steps to make Potato patties:
  1. Wash, peel and grate the potatoes and squeeze the water out of them 1. Do the same with the courgette and add it to the potatoes
  2. Add eggs and cheese and corn flour
  3. Season and mix well together using your hand. If too sticky, add more flour. I like to season it with salt, basil and parsley
  4. Heat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Form patties
  5. Pop them in the oven until golden brown, around 30ish minutes. - Enjoy!
