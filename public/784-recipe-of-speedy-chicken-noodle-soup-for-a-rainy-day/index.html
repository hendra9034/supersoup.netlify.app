The ingredients needed to make Chicken Noodle Soup : For a rainy day:
  1. Prepare noodles from Lidil
  2. Get Tea spoon of ground pepper
  3. Take salt to taste
  4. Make ready of knorr cubes (Maggie)
  5. Get Diced chicken
Steps to make Chicken Noodle Soup : For a rainy day:
  1. Put the chicken in a pot and cook until it well cooked 1. Pour out the water from chicken and rinse chicken
  2. Add 5 cups of boiling water into a clean pot, add the chicken
  3. Add the 2 packet of noodles
  4. Add the seasoning that came with the noodles
  5. Add 1/2 of knorr cube
  6. Add pinch of salt to taste
  7. Add 1 tea spoon of ground pepper (optional)
  8. Cook together for 10 minutes
  9. And it done !
  10. Enjoy with cold drink
