The ingredients needed to make Country Style Potato Soup:
  1. Take 14 Potatoes, medium, peeled and diced
  2. Get 1 qt Chicken broth, organic
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons bacon fat
  4. Get 1/2 onion, large, finely chopped
  5. Make ready 3 garlic cloves, smashed and coarsely minced
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  8. Make ready 1 cup heavy whipping cream
Instructions to make Country Style Potato Soup:
  1. Simmer the potatoes and broth in a medium pot over medium heat. Watch out for rising foam. Simply stir the potatoes to
  2. Do not cover so the liquid can reduce to make room for the eventual cream. So you may want to turn that over the oven fan on.
  3. Saute together 2 tablespoons of bacon fat with half of a large sweet onion, finely chopped.
  4. Add the garlic and continue to cook till the edges turn brown-gold and add this to the soup. Stir well.
  5. Simmer for 5-10 more minutes. Remove from the heat. Add the salt and pepper to your tastes, then the cream. Stir well
  6. Allow the soup to cool for 5-10 minutes. Soup should be airy, creamy with a silky appearance. Serve hot garnished with shredded cheddar and chives.
