The ingredients needed to make Keto Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Take 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  2. Make ready 2 cups chicken broth
  3. Take 1 cup broccoli, chopped into bite size pieces
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp. butter
  5. Take 1/8 cup white onion
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp. garlic
  7. Make ready 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  8. Get 2 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
Steps to make Keto Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Saute onion and garlic with butter
  2. Add broth and broccoli and cook until tender.
  3. Add salt, pepper and desired seasoning.
  4. Place cream cheese in small bowl and heat in microwave for 30seconds until soft.
  5. Stir heavy whipping cream and cream cheese into soup and bring to a boil.
  6. Turn off heat and stir in cheddar cheese. Top with bacon if desired.
