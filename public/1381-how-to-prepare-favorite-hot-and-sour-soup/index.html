The ingredients needed to make 🍜🌽🌶🥕 Hot and Sour Soup:
  1. Get 1 large chicken breast (boiled & shredded)
  2. Get 6 cups chicken stock
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup corn kernel
  4. Take 1 carrots (peeled & shredded)
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup white cabbage (shredded)
  6. Get 4 tblsp sesame oil
  7. Get 6 tblsp cornflour
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup white vinegar
  9. Get 1 egg
  10. Make ready 4 tblsp dark soya sauce
  11. Make ready 1 tsp salt or to taste
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  13. Make ready Sauce_1
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup white vinegar
  15. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  16. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  17. Make ready Sauce_2
  18. Prepare 1/2 cup white vinegar
  19. Prepare 2 fresh green chilli (sliced)
  20. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
Instructions to make 🍜🌽🌶🥕 Hot and Sour Soup:
  1. 1: in a pan add the chicken breast and 8 cups of water, boil the chicken till cooked completely. - - 2: remove the chicken once boiled, shred the chicken and keep the stock aside.
  2. 3: in a pan add the chicken stock, bring it to a boil & add the shredded chicken and cook for 2minutes. - - 4: now add the carrots, cabbage & corn kernel mix well and keep the flame on medium low and cook for 1 minute.
  3. 5: now add the salt, soya sauce, black pepper and vinegar mix well and cook for 1 more minute. - - 6: mix cornflour and 1/2 cup of water to make the paste, add this to the soup, keep stirring while adding the cornflour as you might get lumps.
  4. 7: once the soup is thickened, beat the egg and slowly add it to the soup. - - 8: heat the oil & add it to the soup. (purpose of reheat soup next day) - - 9: soup is ready serve hot.
  5. #Sauce_1 - - mix it well to make the sauce to serve with the soup. - - #Sauce_2 - - mix it well to make the sauce to serve with the soup.
