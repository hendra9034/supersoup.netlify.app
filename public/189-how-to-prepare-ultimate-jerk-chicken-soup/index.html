The ingredients needed to make Jerk chicken soup:
  1. Get 5 small boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  2. Prepare 1 small sweet potato
  3. Take 3/4 a yellow onion
  4. Get Drizzle of olive oil
  5. Make ready 1/4 a green pepper
  6. Take Spoonful minced garlic
  7. Prepare Jamacian jerk seasoning
  8. Get Garlic salt
  9. Get 6 cups chicken broth
  10. Get 8 cups water
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup coconut milk (unsweetened)
  12. Make ready Can red kidney beans, drained
  13. Take 8.5 oz can whole kernel corn, drained
  14. Take 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  15. Take 1 bullion cube

Steps to make Jerk chicken soup:
  1. Put 6 cups of chicken broth, 8 cups of water, 1/2 cup of coconut milk, bullion cube, 1/2 tsp black pepper & 3-5 shakes of jerk seasoning into pot, let boil.
  2. While your waiting for broth to boil, rinse off chicken & pat dry. Sprinkle garlic salt & a generous amount of jerk seasoning onto chicken. Flip over & repeat.
  3. Once mixture is boiling (med high ish) add chicken, beans & corn. Partially cover & let cook 20 minutes.
  4. While chicken is cooking, drizzle olive oil onto pan & put on medium heat. Dice onion & green pepper add to pan. Spoon garlic into mixture. Peel & dice sweet potato, add to pan stirring occasionally.
  5. Once 20 minutes is up, add vegetable mixture and remove chicken onto cutting board.
  6. Shred chicken & add back to pot. Add a few more shakes of jerk seasoning to pot. Let cook on medium heat for about 20 minutes, partially covered.
