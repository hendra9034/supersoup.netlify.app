The ingredients needed to make Shredded Chicken 🐔 Tomato 🍅 Soup 🍜:
  1. Get shredded chicken
  2. Take Olive oil (just enough to cover the bottom of your pot)
  3. Prepare onion, chopped
  4. Take green pepper, chopped
  5. Take garlic, minced
  6. Get vegetable bouillon cube
  7. Make ready black pepper
  8. Prepare can crushed tomatoes
  9. Take low sodium chicken broth
  10. Take cannelloni beans, undrained
  11. Get Bread 🥖
Steps to make Shredded Chicken 🐔 Tomato 🍅 Soup 🍜:
  1. Place your shredded chicken in a bowl and set to the side……
  2. Heat olive oil in a pot……
  3. Add in your onion and green pepper, sauté until veggies are tender, then add minced garlic, sauté for several more minutes……
  4. Add to pot your vegetable bullion cube, sauté for several more minutes, breaking up bullion cube as it cooks…..
  5. Add to pot your shredded chicken, black pepper, crushed tomatoes, chicken broth and cannelloni beans, heat to boiling…..
  6. Cover pot and heat on medium heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally……..
  7. Serve with some crusty bread and enjoy 😉!!
