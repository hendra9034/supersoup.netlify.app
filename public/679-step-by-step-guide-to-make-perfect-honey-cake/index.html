The ingredients needed to make Honey cake:
  1. Prepare 170 g clear runny honey
  2. Make ready 140 g butter
  3. Take 85 g light brown muscovado sugar
  4. Make ready 2 eggs beaten
  5. Make ready 200 g self raising flour
  6. Prepare water
  7. Get for the icing
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp honey
  9. Take 55 g icing sugar
  10. Get hot water

Instructions to make Honey cake:
  1. Pre heat the oven on gas mark 4 and butter line a cake tin 7in/18cm cake tin
  2. In a large saucepan on a low heat add the sugar butter and honey and mix untill melted do not bring to a boil and keep stiring once melted take of the heat
  3. To the honey mixture add the flour and eggs and mix to get a silky batter
  4. Spoon the mixture into the greased cake tin and bake for 40 to 45 mins until springy to touch
  5. Cool slightly before removing from the pan
  6. Mix the icing sugar with 2 to3 tsp of warm water and the 1 tbsp honey to get a runny consistency and drizzle over the cake whislt its still warm
  7. Enjoy
