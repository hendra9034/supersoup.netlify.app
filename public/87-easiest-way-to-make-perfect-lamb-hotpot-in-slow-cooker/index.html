The ingredients needed to make Lamb Hotpot in Slow Cooker:
  1. Take 400 g lamb loin fillets
  2. Prepare 100 g frozen peas
  3. Get 4 carrots
  4. Make ready 2 jacket potatoes
  5. Prepare 2 onions
  6. Make ready 1 lamb stock cube
  7. Take 3 tbsp plain flour
  8. Take 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp mint sauce
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp thyme

Instructions to make Lamb Hotpot in Slow Cooker:
  1. Boil the kettle and make 300ml of stock.
  2. Dice the onions.
  3. Peel and slice the carrots.
  4. Peel and cut the potatoes into chunks.
  5. Cut the lamb fillets into chunks.
  6. Season the meat with salt and pepper.
  7. Add a little oil to a frying pan and cook the lamb until browned on all sides.
  8. Add the lamb, onions, carrots and potaoes to the slow cooker.
  9. Sprinkle with flour and stir well.
  10. Pour in the stock. Add the Worcester sauce, mint sauce, thyme and a little pepper. Stir again.
  11. Cook on high for 3-4 hours.
  12. 5 minutes before serving add in the peas.
  13. Serve.
