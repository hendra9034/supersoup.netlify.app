The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Crack Butter Chicken Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1 box vegetabke stock
  3. Prepare 1 box SHAN Butter Chicken seasoning
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp garlic paste
  5. Prepare 1 container cream cheese
  6. Make ready 1 bunch spinach leaves
  7. Make ready 2 cups cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Instant Pot Crack Butter Chicken Soup:
  1. Marinate the chicken with shan seasoning and garlic paste
  2. Add all the ingredients in the pot.
  3. Turn on pressure cooker for 20 min. Close pressure knob.
  4. Release pressure, and slowly took out the chicken breasts and pull apart with fork.
  5. Put back the chicken and satuee soup for 5 min.
  6. Garnish with fried onions and cilantro!
