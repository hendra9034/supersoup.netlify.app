The ingredients needed to make 7 minute microwave Chocolate Cake:
  1. Take 1 cup flour
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 2 teaspoons baking powder
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
  5. Take 4 tablespoons oil
  6. Prepare 3 tablespoons cocoa
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Take 1 tablespoon coffee
  9. Make ready 1 cup boiling water
Steps to make 7 minute microwave Chocolate Cake:
  1. Add all ingredients into a bowl and combine EXCEPT the coffee and boiling water.
  2. Mix coffee and the boiling water then add to the mixture and combine well
  3. Pour mixture into a 2L greased ice-cream container or any other container of similar size and bake in microwave for 7 MINUTES without lid - - ALLOW cake to sit in Microwave for 10 minutes without opening.
  4. Sandwich cake and decorate with chocolate Ganache or any decoration of choice - - (Chocolate ganache recipe available on my Cookpad recipes). - - Yummy…
  5. Enjoy
