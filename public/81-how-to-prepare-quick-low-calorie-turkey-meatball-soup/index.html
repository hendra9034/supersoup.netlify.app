The ingredients needed to make Low calorie Turkey meatball soup:
  1. Take 8 oz ground turkey
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Take 4 tbsp onion powder
  4. Make ready 4 tbsp garlic powder
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp parsley flakes
  7. Take 1 tsp essence of emeril
  8. Make ready 4 small new potatoes diced
  9. Make ready 15 baby diced carrots
  10. Prepare 1 cup diced celery
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup yellow onions
  12. Take 1 can stewed tomatoes
  13. Get 1 container low sodium chicken broth
  14. Get 1 cup baby spinach chopped
  15. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  16. Make ready 2 tbsp knorr chicken Bouillon seasoning
  17. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  18. Get 2cups of water
