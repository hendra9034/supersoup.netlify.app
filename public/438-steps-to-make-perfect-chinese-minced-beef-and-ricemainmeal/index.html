The ingredients needed to make Chinese Minced Beef and Rice.ðŸ¥°#Mainmeal:
  1. Take Enough Rice for 2 persons
  2. Prepare 200 g Beef Mince
  3. Make ready 1 small onion chopped finely
  4. Take 2 Cloves Garlic crushed
  5. Take Sauce………..
  6. Prepare 1 +1/2 Tbl Hoisin Sauce
  7. Get 1 +1/2 tbls Dark Soy sauce
  8. Take 2 tbls Oyster Sauce
  9. Take 1/4 cup Chicken stock or Miso or chinese wine
  10. Take 1 tsp Sugar
  11. Make ready 1/4 tsp white pepper
  12. Get 1 tsp Sesame oil
Instructions to make Chinese Minced Beef and Rice.ðŸ¥°#Mainmeal:
  1. Start boiling your rice
  2. Add a little oil in the wok get it hot then add the onions and Garlic stir in and then add the minced beef keep turning it and make sure all the pink has gone. Turn low.
  3. Meanwhile make the sauce. Add the Soy sauce then add the Hoisin sauce in a little bowl holds about 1/2 cup
  4. Next add the Oyster sauce also the sugar
  5. Next the Sesame oil and the 1/4 cup of chicken stock.then add the white Pepper
  6. Add the sauce to the wok and bring up the heat and stir in for about 1 minute. Let simmer for 3 minutes.
  8. Add your Rice to a bowl or plate then pour on top the minced beef. Then sprinkle the chopped spring onion to finish. If you have any over it will keep in the fridge for 3 days.
