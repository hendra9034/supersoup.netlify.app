The ingredients needed to make Butter & Soy Sauce Brussels Sprouts:
  1. Take 20 g Butter
  2. Get 12 Brussels Sprouts *trimmed and cut in half
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Frozen Corn Kernels
  4. Get 4 tablespoons Water
  5. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
  6. Take 2-3 teaspoons Soy Sauce
  7. Make ready Additional Ingredients
  8. Get 1 clove Garlic *finely chopped
  9. Get Chilli Flakes OR Ground Chilli

Steps to make Butter & Soy Sauce Brussels Sprouts:
  1. Melt Butter in a frying pan over medium heat, pack with Brussels Sprouts with cut side down, scatter Corn Kernels (and chopped Garlic and Chilli if you add them), add Water and Salt, cover with a lid, and cook for a few minutes.
  2. When the Water in the pan is evaporated, remove the lid and keep cooking until Brussels Sprouts are nicely browned, then add Soy Sauce and quickly stir to combine.
