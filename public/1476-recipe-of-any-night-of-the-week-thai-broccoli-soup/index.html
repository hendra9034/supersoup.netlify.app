The ingredients needed to make Thai broccoli soup:
  1. Get 3 cup broccoli florets
  2. Get 1 chopped onion
  3. Make ready 3 gloves of garlic chopped
  4. Get 1 can coconut milk
  5. Take 2 cups chicken broth
  6. Get Salt
  7. Prepare Pepper
  8. Get 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup cilantro dry
  10. Get 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  11. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon ginger dry
  12. Take 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg fresh
  13. Make ready 2 teaspoon olive oil

Steps to make Thai broccoli soup:
  1. Over medium heat salute onion with oil cook for few minutes then add broccoli and garlic and cook for 10 minutes on law medium heat
  2. Add chicken broth and spices and cook for another 10 minutes then add the milk use the blinder to smooth the soup
  3. To thicken the soup I used one tablespoon of cornstarch with 1/4 cup cold broth and mix well then add to soup and cook for few minutes enjoy
