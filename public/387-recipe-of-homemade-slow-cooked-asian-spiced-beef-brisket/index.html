The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooked Asian Spiced Beef Brisket:
  1. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  2. Get 1/2 tsp coriander
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp hot chilli powder
  4. Prepare 4 garlic cloves crushed
  5. Get 1 knuckle frozen ginger, finely grated (see tip)
  6. Get 4 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Make ready 400 g tinned tomatoes
  8. Make ready 500 g beef shin or brisket
  9. Get 100 mls coconut milk
  10. Get Handful fresh baby spinach
Steps to make Slow Cooked Asian Spiced Beef Brisket:
  1. In a small bowl mix together the soy, spices, ginger and garlic.
  2. Cover the base of your slow cooker with the tinned tomatoes.
  3. Put the beef in and rub your soy and spice mix all over the meat. Pour it all in, the excess simply runs into the tomatoes on the base.
  4. Cook on low for 8-10 hours.
  5. Once cooked and the beef is tender, use two forks to shred the beef apart. Pull out any flabby bits of fat that you come across.
  6. Stir in your coconut milk and spinach leaves and serve with rice or noodles.
