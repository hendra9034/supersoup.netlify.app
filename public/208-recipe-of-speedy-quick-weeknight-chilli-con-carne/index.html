The ingredients needed to make Quick weeknight chilli con carne:
  1. Make ready 600 g minced beef meat
  2. Prepare 1 onion
  3. Prepare 1 tin kidney beans
  4. Make ready 2 spoons concentrated tomatoes
  5. Get 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon cumin
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  9. Take 1 beef stock cube (or veg is also fine)
  10. Get Worcester sauce a sprinkle
  11. Take Coriander to serve

Steps to make Quick weeknight chilli con carne:
  1. Start by fixing the onion and fry it on low heat in a frying pan with olive oil and a pinch of salt (it helps the onion sweat). Add the mince and cook until it all becomes brown mixing it often.
  2. Add all the spices, the tomatoes and the stock cube crumbled and cook for about 15 min covered. Add the beans (stained) and cook for another 10 min. Sprinkle coriander (if using) and serve with rice.
