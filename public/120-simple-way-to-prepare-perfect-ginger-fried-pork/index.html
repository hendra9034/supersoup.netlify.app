The ingredients needed to make Ginger Fried Pork:
  1. Get 3 Thin sliced pork
  2. Get Dash Salt and pepper
  3. Make ready 1/4 Onion
  4. Prepare Dash Oil
  5. Make ready Sauce
  6. Take 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  7. Take 1 tbsp Mirin (Sweet sake)
  8. Get 1 tbsp Sake
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp Honey
  10. Prepare Adjust to suit your needs Grated ginger
Steps to make Ginger Fried Pork:
  1. Slice the onion thinly.
  2. Partly cut the strings between the fat and the red meat to prevent the shrink. Then sprinkle the salt and pepper.
  3. Put all sauce ingredients except the ginger into a bowl. and mix them.
  4. Put the oil and sliced onion into a frying pan, and stir-fry.
  5. Then add the thin-sliced pork in the frying pan and fry.
  6. Add the sauce and grated ginger, and coat the pork
  7. Put the pork on a plate so as not to overcook it, cook down the sauce a little to thicken, and put on the pork.
