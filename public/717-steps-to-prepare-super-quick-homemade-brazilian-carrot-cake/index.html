The ingredients needed to make Brazilian Carrot Cake:
  1. Prepare Dough
  2. Take 1/2 cup sunflower oil
  3. Take 3 chopped carrots
  4. Take 4 eggs
  5. Prepare 4 cups caster sugar
  6. Take 2 cups plain flour
  7. Take 1 tbsp baking powder
  8. Take Chocolate Topping
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp spreadable or butter
  10. Take 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  11. Take 6 tbsp caster sugar
  12. Get 1 cup milk
Instructions to make Brazilian Carrot Cake:
  1. Blend together the oil, carrots and eggs. While blending slowly add the sugar.
  2. Blend until homogeneous and start adding the baking power and flour. Blend it as much as possible adding the flour in small amounts. If the blender is not powerful enough transfer the dough to a big bowl add the rest of the flour and finish mixing with a hand mixer.
  3. Pour the dough in a cake tin greased with butter. Put the tin in a preheated oven at 180C Fan for 30 to 35 min.
  4. To prepare the topping add all the topping ingredients to a pan at a medium heat. Keep mixing it until everything is melted and homogeneous, mix it and keep it in heat until the cream gets as thick as you would like.
  5. Pour the topping cream over the cake and it is ready.
