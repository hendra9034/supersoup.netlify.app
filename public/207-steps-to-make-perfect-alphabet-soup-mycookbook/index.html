The ingredients needed to make Alphabet Soup

#mycookbook:

  1. Make ready 1/2 cup onion chopped finely
  2. Take 1/2 cup tomato chopped finely
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
  4. Take 2 Maggi cubes / chicken stock
  5. Take Salt (optional)
  6. Make ready Black pepper crushed as per your choice
  7. Get 1/2 cup alphabet/ macaroni of your choice
  8. Take 1/2 cup diced potatoes (optional)
  9. Get 1/2 cup carrots(optional)
  10. Get 3 and 1/2 cups of water
  11. Make ready 2 tablespoons oil
Instructions to make Alphabet Soup

#mycookbook:

  1. In a saucepan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil.
  2. Add in the chopped onion and saut√© till translucent
