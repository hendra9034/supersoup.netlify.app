The ingredients needed to make "Baked French Onion" Soup:
  1. Make ready 4 Onions
  2. Prepare 96 oz (3- 32 oz. containers) beef broth or beef stock
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp thyme
  5. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  6. Take 4 tbsp butter
  7. Prepare 1 salt and pepper
  8. Get 1 french bread
  9. Get 1 freshly shredded cheese of your choice. I used alehouse cheddar
Instructions to make "Baked French Onion" Soup:
  1. Slice onions
  2. On medium high heat, melt 4 tablespoons of butter in pot and put all onions in. Salt and pepper thoroughly.
  3. Stir occasionally until unions get soft and brown. About 30-45 minutes.
  4. Add Worcestershire sause, thyme and bay leaf while cooking the onions.
  5. When browned, add in all the broth. Stir well and let simmer for a couple hours.
  6. Slice thin slices of french bread and toast. Butter both side if you wish.
  7. Fill oven safe bowl with soup place toasted french bread on top and top with cheese.
  8. Broil until melted and slightly brown.
  9. Enjoy!
  10. Served with a panini.
