The ingredients needed to make Delicious Nutricious Lentil Soup!:
  1. Prepare soup
  2. Get 1 cup red (split) lentils
  3. Get 1 onion, chopped
  4. Prepare 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  5. Get 1 cup chicken stock
  6. Prepare 5 cup boiled water
  7. Get 1 chopped tomato (or a can of tomato dices)
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp tomato paste
  9. Take 1 tbsp flour
  10. Take 5 tbsp oil (i used sunflower)
  11. Prepare 1 immersion blender
  12. Make ready 1 salt and pepper
  13. Get serving
  14. Take 1 some red pepper flakes
  15. Get 1 some lemon slices
  16. Get spicy-lovers
  17. Prepare 1 tbsp sambal
Steps to make Delicious Nutricious Lentil Soup!:
  1. Prepare :)
  2. Heat the oil in a soup pan on middlelow fire. Make sure the whole bottom is covered. When it is hot add the chopped onion. When they get glazy add the chopped garlic.
  3. Stir for about 2 minutes. Now add the flour, the tomato paste and the lentils. If you are the spicy lover you can now add the sambal. Mix everything!
  4. Now add the chicken stock, the boiled water, the tomato and some pepper and salt. Stir it. Now cover the pan and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes on a low fire. You should stirr quite often.
  5. Everything is now soft enough to be blended. Blend the soup in the pan with the immersion blender untill it is all smooth.
  6. Taste and add ingredients as you like. Now your soup is ready to be served! Make sure its hot, otherwise heat it again. Serve it with the pepperflakes and the lemon. Enjoy:D
