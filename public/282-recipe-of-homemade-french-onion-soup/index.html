The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Get 6 each large red or yellow onion
  2. Take 1 olive oil
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp sugar
  4. Get 2 clove garlic, minced
  5. Prepare 8 cup beef or chicken stock or combo (traditionally soup is made with beef stock)
  6. Take 1/2 cup dry white whine
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp thyme
  8. Prepare 1 salt and pepper
  9. Get 8 slice toasted french bread
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 cup swiss cheese

Instructions to make French Onion Soup:
  1. in lg. sauce pan saute onions in oliv oil until well browned (about 30-40 min)
  2. add sugar about 10 min into the process to help carmelization
  3. add garlic and saute for 1 min
  4. add stock, whine and thyme
  5. cover partially and simmer until flavors are well blended about 30 min.
  6. preheat oven to 350°F
  7. season to taste with salt and pepper
  8. ladle soup into bowls cover with the toast and sprinkle with cheese
  9. put into broiler for 10 min. at 350°F or until the cheese bubbles and is slightly browned
  10. serve immediately
