The ingredients needed to make Crock Pot Lentil Soup:
  1. Get 3 tbsp Vegeta Stock
  2. Take 2 liter Water
  3. Prepare 500 grams Whole Brown Lentils
  4. Prepare 500 grams Whole Red Lentils
  5. Get 250 grams Brown Rice
  6. Get 1 dash Olive Oil

Steps to make Crock Pot Lentil Soup:
  1. Fill crockpot basin with water
  2. Add Red lentils, Brown lentils, vegeta and oil to water
  3. Cook on high for 8 hours on low, keeping watch, stir every hour to test the lentils for their level of tenderness
  4. When lentils are tender remove about half of the lentils and using a stick blender blend the remaining lentils into a puree
  5. Put half of the lentils removed back into the crock and cook for two more hours
  6. When re-adding the lentils, add the rice to the.mixture to cook for another two hour on lowest possible setting
  7. Check soup and serve at required texture and taste
