The ingredients needed to make French Onion Gratin Soup - using a cocotte:
  1. Prepare 3 onions
  2. Take 70 g butter
  3. Get 1/4 white wine
  4. Prepare 1 cube stock
  5. Get 1.25 l water
  6. Make ready salt & pepper
  7. Prepare Gratin
  8. Take bread pices
  9. Prepare Gryere cheese
Steps to make French Onion Gratin Soup - using a cocotte:
  1. Slice onions thin, and against fiber - (see tip)
  2. Heat the cocotte, pour butter
  3. Put all sliced onions, stir to be mixed with butter
  4. Preheat the oven 200c and put the cocotte for 60 min, without lid, roughly. Stir a few times not to be burnt, and turn the onion into golden brown
  5. Take out the cocotte and pour the white wine, simmer till it dries 1. I use a cube stock like this
  6. Pour the water with stock. Take out the scum carefullly. Roughly 30 min in low heat.
  7. Toast pieces of bread, shred Gryere chese, and preheat the oven 220c.
  8. Put into the oven again for 10 min, without the lid. Serve it on the table directly. Careful, it's HOT!
