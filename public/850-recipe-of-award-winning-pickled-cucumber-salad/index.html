The ingredients needed to make Pickled cucumber salad:
  1. Take 3 kg cucumbers
  2. Get 4-5 bigger onions
  3. Prepare Pickle Ingredients
  4. Get 300 ml vinegar
  5. Prepare 150 g sugar
  6. Get 100 ml oil
  7. Make ready 2 heaped tbl spoon Deko (pickling spices, which contains salt, mustard seeds, coriander, bay leaf, pepper, cloves, chili, ginger, allspice)

Instructions to make Pickled cucumber salad:
  1. Peel cucumbers and onions and cut them into thin slices (or use a grater). Put them in a bigger bowl.
  2. Boil pickle from ingredients above. Pour hot pickle (through a sieve if you don't like seeds in a salad as we do) into the bowl and mix well. Let sit for 1 hour. Cucumbers will release juice.
  3. Then put cucumbers with juice into jars and close them tightly. Sterilize for 10 minutes at 90°C. Turn the jars upside down (for few minutes) to make sure they aren't leaking. Let them cool slowly (for 24 hours) covering them with a blanket.
