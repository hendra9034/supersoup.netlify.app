The ingredients needed to make Veggie Buffalo chicken soup:
  1. Get 1 cup spinach
  2. Make ready 1 cup cauliflower
  3. Prepare 1 cup broccoli
  4. Take 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  5. Get 2 cans chicken
  6. Take 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup ranch
Steps to make Veggie Buffalo chicken soup:
  1. Mix together chicken, buffalo sauce, ranch and cream cheese cook about 8 minutes or until creamy.
  2. Bring veggies to a boil cook until done to your liking then drain
  3. Mix both together and add heavy whipping cream
