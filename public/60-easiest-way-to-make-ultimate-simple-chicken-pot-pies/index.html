The ingredients needed to make Simple Chicken Pot Pies:
  1. Prepare 3 pie crust for 9 inch pie
  2. Prepare 4 pounds chicken thighs bone in skin on
  3. Prepare 1 pound mixed vegetables*
  4. Make ready 2 cups diced potatoes
  5. Get 31-1/2 ounces concentrated chicken soup
  6. Make ready 10 ounces water
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  8. Take To taste kosher salt
  9. Take To taste ground white pepper
  10. Get 2 cups chicken stock
  11. Take 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
Instructions to make Simple Chicken Pot Pies:
  1. Preheat oven to 425° Fahrenheit. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic. Heat a large skillet with the olive oil in it. Add seasoned chicken thighs in the hot oil and sear it for 4 minutes per side.
  2. When the chicken has been seared both side add 1 cup broth and allow to reduce till 2/3 has been reduced. Remove chicken and add the diced potatoes. Sauté till good and browned.
  3. When potatoes are browned add in the mixed vegetables. * My vegetables consisted of sweet peas, green beans, carrots, Lima beans, and corn. Stir in two can of condensed soup. Stir well. Add 1 can water 1 cup broth. Simmer while you remove the chicken from the bones and chop the chicken.
  4. Add the chicken to the vegetables with last can of soup. Simmer 10 minutes. Carefully put 2 pie crust into an oven safe dish. Add the filling to the pie shells.
  5. Split the third pie crust in half and cover the pie. Seal them up. Cut 4 slits into the top crust. Bake in oven 40-45 minutes. Allow to rest 20-30 minutes.
  6. After they have rested serve. I hope you enjoy!!!
