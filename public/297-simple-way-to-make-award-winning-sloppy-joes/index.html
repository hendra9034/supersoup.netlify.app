The ingredients needed to make Sloppy Joes:
  1. Take 240 g beef mince
  2. Take 1 carrot
  3. Take 200 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  4. Get 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  5. Prepare 1 tsp smoked paprika
  6. Prepare 1 tsp paprika
  7. Make ready 1 tsp chilli powder
  8. Get 1 tsp garlic granules
  9. Get 1 tsp onion granules
  10. Take 1 avocado
  11. Get 1/2 lime
  12. Prepare 2 burger rolls
Instructions to make Sloppy Joes:
  1. Heat a small amount of oil in a frying pan. Add the beef mince and cook until browned. Breaking the mince up with a spoon as it cooks
  2. Peel and grate the carrot. Add it to the mince along with the paprika, smoked paprika, chilli powder, onion granules and the garlic granules. Stir well to combine.
  3. Add the ketchup and chopped tomatoes. Stir well and cook 4-5 minutes.
  4. Preheat grill and grate cheese. Halve the burger rolls and place on baking tray.
  5. Divide the mince between the 4 halves and top with the grated cheese.
  6. Place on top shelf of grill and cook until cheese has melted.
  7. Half the avocado and remove the stone. Use a spoon to scoop the flesh out into a bowl. Salt and pepper to taste and add the juice from half a lime. Mash together to make guacamole.
  8. Plate up burger halves and spoon on the guacamole.
  9. Serve with your choice of sides and enjoy.
