The ingredients needed to make Szechuan Beef w/ Rice:
  1. Take 1 sirloin steak
  2. Make ready 3 tsp cornflour
  3. Take 5 tsp cracked black pepper
  4. Get 2 tsp ginger paste
  5. Take 2 tsp garlic paste
  6. Get 1 green pepper
  7. Prepare 1 sliced carrot
  8. Get 1 sliced onion
  9. Take 2 red chilies
  10. Take 3 tbsp soy sauce
  11. Take 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  12. Prepare 3 tbsp chilli-garlic sauce
  13. Get 2 tsp sesame oil
  14. Make ready 100 ml water
  15. Take 120 g basmati rice
Steps to make Szechuan Beef w/ Rice:
  1. Season your steak with pepper and 1 tsp of cornflour and leave in the fridge for half an hour
  2. Fry your steak for 1 minute each side and leave aside
  3. Fry garlic and ginger paste for three minutes
  4. Add green pepper, carrot and onion and fry for 5 minutes
  5. Add red chilli and fry for two minutes and take pan of heat
  6. In another pan, add soy sauce, rice vinegar, chilli-garlic sauce sesame oil and water and simmer for 15 minutes
  7. Add 2 tsp of cornflour with a splash of water and add to the sauce and simmer until thickened
  8. Add cooked steak and vegetables and leave for 10 minutes
  9. Boil basmati rice for 10 minutes
  10. Serve
