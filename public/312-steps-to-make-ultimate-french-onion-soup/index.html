The ingredients needed to make ğŸğŸ²French Onion SoupğŸ²ğŸ:
  1. Prepare 300 g (10 oz) sliced onions
  2. Take 1 & 1/2 tblsp plain flour
  3. Make ready 25 g (1 oz) butter
  4. Get 1 bay leaf
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp thyme
  6. Prepare 1 garlic cloves, chopped
  7. Make ready 1 litres (2 pints) vegetable stock
  8. Get Slices toasted baguette
  9. Make ready Gruyere or emmental cheese, grated
Steps to make ğŸğŸ²French Onion SoupğŸ²ğŸ:
  1. Melt the butter in a large pan and add in the onions. Cook gently on a low heat for about 25 minutes or until the onions are soft and brown. - Add in the garlic and the flour and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Pour in the vegetable stock and bring to the boil. - Add in the bay leaf and the thyme, cover and simmer gently for 25 minutes.
