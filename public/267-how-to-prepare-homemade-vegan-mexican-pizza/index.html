The ingredients needed to make Vegan Mexican Pizza:
  1. Make ready Pizza
  2. Take 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  3. Prepare 120 g veggie ground beef
  4. Get 2 tbsp Mexican-inspired spice mix
  5. Make ready 2 (20 cm) ready-made pizza crusts
  6. Make ready 1 tsp olive oil
  7. Prepare 5 tbsp (140 ml) canned tomato paste
  8. Take 1 red bell pepper, diced
  9. Prepare 1/2 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  10. Take 55 g canned corn
  11. Get 1/4 cup (38 g) pickled jalapeño slices
  12. Take 10 cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  13. Prepare 1 avocado, sliced
  14. Take 1 stalk green onion, sliced
  15. Make ready Cilantro yogurt mayo
  16. Prepare 60 ml unsweetened soy yogurt
  17. Prepare 2 Tbsp (30 ml) vegan mayonnaise
  18. Take 2 tbsp fresh cilantro
  19. Take 1/4 tsp salt
Steps to make Vegan Mexican Pizza:
  1. Add oil to a small pan on high heat. When hot, add the garlic and cook for 2 minutes until the garlic is lightly golden.
  2. Then add the vegan soy veggie ground, spice mix, and 1 tbsp water. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  4. Lightly brush the top of the pizza crusts with some olive oil, then spread over the tomato paste.
  5. Divide the soy ground mixture over the two pizzas, and top with the bell pepper, onion, corn, jalapeños, and cherry tomatoes.
  6. Bake in the centre rack of the oven for 12 - 15 minutes, or until the crust is lightly golden.
  7. While pizza is baking, make cilantro sauce by mixing the ingredients in a small bowl.
  8. When the pizza is done, top it with dollops of the yogurt sauce, some sliced avocado, and sliced green onions. Enjoy!
