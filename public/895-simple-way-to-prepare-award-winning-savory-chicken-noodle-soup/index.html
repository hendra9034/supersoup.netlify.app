The ingredients needed to make Savory chicken noodle soup:
  1. Get 12 cup water
  2. Prepare 5 tbsp chicken bouillon
  3. Make ready 1/2 packages egg noodles
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp marjoram
  5. Take 1 can carrots
  6. Take 1/2 celery
  7. Get 2 each boneless chicken breast

Steps to make Savory chicken noodle soup:
  1. Fill a pot up with water and add bouillon.
  2. bring to boil and allow bouillon to dissolve.
  3. Dice 2 boneless chicken breasts.
  4. Add chicken an boil till chicken turns mostly white.or to 155°.should take 35min if water stays at a boil.
  5. Add your eggnoodles.
  6. Allow noodles to cook untill mostly tender.taste one and should taste a little under cooked/raw.
  7. Dice half a stock of celery or 2 cups,add to pot.
  8. Add marjoram,now wait 8 mins.now add carrots.
  9. Check a piece of chicken to make sure it's done and at 165°
  10. Enjoy :-) might want some crackers.
