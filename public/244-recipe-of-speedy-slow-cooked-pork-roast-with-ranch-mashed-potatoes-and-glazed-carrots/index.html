The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooked Pork Roast with Ranch Mashed Potatoes and Glazed Carrots:
  1. Make ready 1 Boneless Pork Roast
  2. Take 1 Sm. Can Cream of Mushroom Soup, undiluted
  3. Make ready 1 Packet French Onion Soup Mix
  4. Take 1/2 tsp. Black Pepper
  5. Take 1/2 tsp. Salt
  6. Make ready 6 Lg. Russet Potatoes, peeled and cubed
  7. Make ready 1 Cup Buttermilk Ranch Dressing
  8. Get 3 tsps. Softened Butter
  9. Make ready 2 Bags Mini Carrots, cut in half
  10. Prepare 2 Tbsp. Butter
  11. Get 1/2 Cup Packed Brown Sugar

Steps to make Slow Cooked Pork Roast with Ranch Mashed Potatoes and Glazed Carrots:
  1. Add the thawed out roast to a slow cooker. Add the Cream Mushroom Soup and French Onion Soup Mix. Cover Slow cooker and cook on high for 6-8 hours til it pulls apart with a fork. Turn cooker down to keep warm.
  2. In a large pot add the cubed potatoes. Fill pot up half way with enough water to cover potatoes. Bring to a boil. Turn heat down and let simmer til potatoes are tender. Drain the water and add potatoes back to pot. Add the butter and mash all the Potatoes. Once mashed add the dressing and mix to combine well. Set aside.
  3. In another pot add the carrots and fill pot with enough water to cover carrots. Bring to a boil. Turn heat down and let simmer til carrots are tender. Drain the water and add carrots back into pot. Add the butter and brown sugar. Stir til brown sugar is dissolved and everything is mixed well and coated. Set aside. Once everything is done.
