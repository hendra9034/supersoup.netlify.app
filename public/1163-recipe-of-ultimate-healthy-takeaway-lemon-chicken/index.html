The ingredients needed to make Healthy Takeaway Lemon Chicken:
  1. Take 400 g diced chicken
  2. Make ready 2 peppers
  3. Make ready 2 carrots
  4. Prepare 1 lemon
  5. Take 1 bunch spring onions
  6. Take 1 chicken stock cube
  7. Take 200 g tenderstem broccoli
  8. Get 3 tsp very lazy garlic
  9. Take 3 tsp cornflour
  10. Get 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  11. Take Serve with rice or noodles of your choice
Instructions to make Healthy Takeaway Lemon Chicken:
  1. Boil the kettle.
  2. Peel and slice the carrots.
  3. Cut the peppers into large chunks.
  4. Trim the ends of the broccoli.
  5. Slice the spring onions.
  6. Zest the lemon and cut in half.
  7. Make up 300ml of chicken stock.
  8. Heat wok with a little oil on high.
  9. Add the chicken and cook for 3 minutes.
  10. Move the chicken to a bowl to reuse the wok.
  11. Add a little more oil, the peppers and carrots for 3 minutes.
  12. Add the broccoli, lemon zest and garlic for 2 minutes.
  13. Meanwhile in a little bowl mix the cornflour, soy sauce and squeeze in the juice from the lemons.
  14. Add the stock, lemon juice mixture, spring onions and the chicken back into the wok for 3 minutes.
  15. Serve.
