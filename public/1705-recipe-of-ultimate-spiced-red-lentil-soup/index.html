The ingredients needed to make Spiced Red Lentil Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  2. Prepare 1 brown/cooking onion, finely chopped
  3. Take 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  5. Take 1/2 tsp or more chilli flakes
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp tumeric
  7. Take 2 carrots, diced
  8. Get 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  10. Take 2 cup canned tomatoes
  11. Get 1 cup red lentils
  12. Take 3 cup vegetable stock
  13. Get greek yoghurt and chopped mint leaves
Instructions to make Spiced Red Lentil Soup:
  1. In a large pot melt the butter. Add onion, garlic, ginger and spices and cook stirring until onion is soft
  2. Add carrots, tomatoes, lentils and stock and gently bring to the boil
  3. Cover and simmer for approximately 40 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure lentils don't burn
  4. Season to perfection, ladle into bowls and top each bowl with a dollop of yoghurt and fresh mint
