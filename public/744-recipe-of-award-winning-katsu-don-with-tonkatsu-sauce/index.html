The ingredients needed to make Katsu Don with Tonkatsu Sauce:
  1. Get 4 servings Tonkatsu (Pork Cutlets), Chicken Katsu (Chicken Cutlet), OR Crumbed Fried Fish (see recipe)
  2. Prepare Finely sliced OR shaved Cabbage
  3. Prepare 4 servings Freshly Cooked Rice
  4. Get ‘Tonkatsu’ Sauce *store-bought ‘Tonkatsu’ Sauce is delicious, but you can make your own. Try my recipe below
  5. Make ready ‘Tonkatsu’ Sauce
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup Tomato Ketchup
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoons Worcestershire (Lea & Perrins)
  8. Prepare 1 & 1/2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  9. Prepare 2-3 teaspoons Sugar
