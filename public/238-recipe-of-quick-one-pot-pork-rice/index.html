The ingredients needed to make One Pot Pork Rice:
  1. Take 150 g pork
  2. Prepare 1 carrot
  3. Take 2 shiitake mushroom
  4. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  5. Get 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon Sake or water
  7. Prepare 200 ml glutinous rice
  8. Make ready Seasoning stock
  9. Take 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  10. Take 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  12. Prepare 1 tablespoon Sake
  13. Make ready Shiitake mushroom water
Instructions to make One Pot Pork Rice:
  1. Soak glutinous rice and shiitake mushroom in water. Cut pork, carrot and shiitake mushroom into dice pieces.
  2. Fry pork in rice cooker pot and season with soy sauce, sugar and Sake.
  3. Add carrot and mushroom and fry. Put soaked glutinous rice on pork and vegetables. Make 180ml seasoning stock adding shiitake mushroom water. And pour on it.
  4. Set in rice cooker and turn on the switch.
