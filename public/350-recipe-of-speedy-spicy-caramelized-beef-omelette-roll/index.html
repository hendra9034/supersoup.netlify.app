The ingredients needed to make SPICY CARAMELIZED BEEF OMELETTE ROLL 香辣牛肉鸡蛋卷:
  1. Get 4 pieces thin omelette
  2. Make ready 250 g (1/2 lb) ground beef
  3. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  4. Take 1 tbsp ginger, finely grated
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup carrot, diced
  6. Prepare 1 small sweet onion, diced
  7. Get 1 tbsp chilli flakes or powder (if needed)
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp brown sugar
  11. Prepare 100 ml vegetable stock
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  13. Get 1 tsp sesame oil
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  15. Get 1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions to make SPICY CARAMELIZED BEEF OMELETTE ROLL 香辣牛肉鸡蛋卷:
  1. Mix together the beef, garlic, ginger, chilli powder, salt, pepper and soy sauce in a bowl. Cover and place in the fridge for an hour.
  2. Heat one tablespoon of vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the beef mixture and fry, stir in sugar, stirring regularly until it gets very brown and crispy. Once you hear it start crackling add the sesame oil, then vegetable stock and mix through. Simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce thickened.
  4. Spread the cooked mince filling on an omelette and roll tightly. Serve with greens and steamed rice.
