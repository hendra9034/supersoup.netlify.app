Steps to make Japanese beef stir-fried udon:
  1. Wash the shimeji mushroom and cut / separate them
  2. Boil the udon for around 1 minute till soft
  3. Fry the mushrooms for a while before putting in the beef slices as mushrooms take longer to cook
  4. Add in the udon
  5. Add in Japanese beef sauce + 1-2 teaspoons of brown sugar and stir
  6. Add in sesame oil, stir and serve!
