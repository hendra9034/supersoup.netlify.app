The ingredients needed to make Smokey Lemon Roasted Mediterranean Veg:
  1. Make ready 1 lemon, quartered
  2. Get Half a yellow bell pepper
  3. Take 1 corn cob
  4. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  5. Get Smoked paprika
  6. Get Salt and pepper
  7. Take Olive oil
  8. Make ready Handful spinach
Steps to make Smokey Lemon Roasted Mediterranean Veg:
  1. Use a sharp knife to cut the corn from the cob. Then lay all the veg out on a foil lined tray, make sure the edges of the foil are turned up to catch the roasting oil and veg juices.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. Roast for 25-30 mins. Just before serving, sauté your spinach in a small pan for a few minutes, just enough to wilt it.
  4. Spoon the veg onto a plate and spoon all the oils and juices from inside the foil over top. Squeeze the roasted lemon over the top.
  5. I served mine with salmon and honestly it goes perfectly.
