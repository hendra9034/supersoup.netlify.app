The ingredients needed to make Noom Friendly Instant Pot Split Lentil Soup:
  1. Take 1 cup washed split red lentil
  2. Get 1/2 cup washed brown rice
  3. Prepare 2 diced red onion
  4. Make ready 1 diced carrot
  5. Take 4 cups chicken broth
  6. Make ready 2 cup water
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt and pepper
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon cumin
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon turmeric
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  12. Take 2 tablespoon olive oil
Steps to make Noom Friendly Instant Pot Split Lentil Soup:
  1. Add everything to the Instant Pot and stir. Remember to wash the lentils and rice. A good trick to having the Instant Pot start cooking faster is to boil the water in a kettle beforehand.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to cook on high for 30 minutes. Let it cool naturally. Stir and serve.
