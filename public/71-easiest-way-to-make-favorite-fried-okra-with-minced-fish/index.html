The ingredients needed to make Fried Okra with Minced Fish:
  1. Prepare 1 pack minced fish
  2. Prepare 1 cup cooking.oil
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Take 2 packs okra
Steps to make Fried Okra with Minced Fish:
  1. Wash okra then trim the head.Slit it then insert the minced fish. 1. Beat egg and roll it.one by one before frying.
  2. Heat pan and add oil.Make sure not too hot or else it eill be easy to burn the okra.
  3. Roll in the beaten egg then fry it.Minced fish must be facing down.To easily cooked the minced fish.Served with ketchup.
