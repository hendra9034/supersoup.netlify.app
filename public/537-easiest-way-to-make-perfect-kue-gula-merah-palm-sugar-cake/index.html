The ingredients needed to make KUE GULA MERAH (PALM SUGAR CAKE):
  1. Prepare 120 grams plain flour
  2. Prepare 120 grams palm sugar
  3. Make ready 120 ml coconut milk
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp vanilla powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 30 ml oil

Instructions to make KUE GULA MERAH (PALM SUGAR CAKE):
  1. Boil coconut milk, brown sugar, until it boils. Turn off the heat and let cool and strain.
  2. Mix the brown sugar solution with all ingredients except oil. Stir well.
  3. Heat the steamer.
  4. Pour the oil into the dough. Stir until all ingredients are combined. - Pour the dough into a mold that has been lined with paper cups.
  5. Steam for about 20 minutes until cooked. Serve and enjoy your afternoon tea ☕
