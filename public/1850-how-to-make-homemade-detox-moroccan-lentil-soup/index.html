The ingredients needed to make üç≤ü•óDetox Moroccan Lentil Soupüç≤ü•ó:
  1. Take 1 tblsp oil
  2. Make ready 1 cup chopped onion
  3. Prepare 1 cup chopped celery
  4. Take 1 cup chopped carrot
  5. Get 1 cup chopped potato
  6. Get 2 garlic cloves, minced
  7. Get 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
  8. Get 1 tsp black black pepper (or to taste)
  9. Take 2 tsp turmeric powder
  10. Prepare 2 tsp ground cumin
  11. Get 2 tsp ground ginger
  12. Make ready 2 tsp paprika powder
  13. Prepare 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  14. Get 1 cup brown lentil
  15. Prepare 1 cup red lentil
  16. Get 4 cups vegetable broth
  17. Prepare 2 cups water
  18. Make ready 1/4 cup tomato paste
  19. Get 1 cup almond milk(or coconut milk depending on your preference)
  20. Get 1 tblsp lemon juice
  21. Prepare 2-3 cups spinach
Instructions to make üç≤ü•óDetox Moroccan Lentil Soupüç≤ü•ó:
  1. Heat the oil in a large heavy bottom pan. Add onions, celery, carrot, potato and garlic and saute for about 5 minutes until everything softens slightly. Season with salt, pepper and the spices and cook about 2 minutes.
  2. Add lentils and saute 1 - 2 minutes, then add the broth, water and tomato paste. Stir to combine and until the tomato paste has dissolved. Bring the soup to a boil, cover and reduce to simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in almond milk, lemon juice, and spinach, and stir until the spinach has wilted.
  4. Serve immediately and top with your desired toppings. - For garnishes, like dollop of coconut yogurt, some fresh parsley, pepper flakes, cracked pepper and jalapeno slices. A little spicy, a little creamy and so good!
