The ingredients needed to make 🍧🍨Peshawari Ice Cream🍨🍧:
  1. Take 3 cups milk
  2. Make ready 2 & 1/2 cups double/heavy cream
  3. Get 1 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions to make 🍧🍨Peshawari Ice Cream🍨🍧:
  1. Take a Pot add milk and boiled it (Use medium flame) add Sugar and stir it until sugar dissolved into the milk.
  2. Now add cream and mix it well and turn off the flame stir it well add vanilla mix it well now rest it until it become into a room temperature.
  3. After that take a air tite container and put all mixture into it cover it properly with cling film and rest it into freezer for overnight.
  4. After that let it rest 5 minutes open it cut into a big pieces and take a food processor or blender now blend it for 5 minute or until smooth. Now once again put it into air tite container and again rest it into a freezer for overnight.
  5. After that your Peshawari Ice Cream is ready and serve with pour some raspberry, chocolate or strawberry syrup. Enjoy!
