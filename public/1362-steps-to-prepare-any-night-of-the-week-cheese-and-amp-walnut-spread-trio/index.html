The ingredients needed to make Cheese & Walnut Spread Trio:
  1. Get 200 g tub Cream cheese
  2. Take 100 g/1 cup Walnut halves
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp snipped Chives
  4. Make ready Coarse-ground black pepper (freshly ground)
  5. Make ready 50 g/half cup corn kernels, fresh or frozen
  6. Get Small handful coriander leaves/cilantro
  7. Take (optional) dash of green jalapeno Tabasco
  8. Get Squeeze lime juice
  9. Get 1 small cooked beetroot
  10. Prepare Small sprig mint leaves
  11. Make ready (optional) pinch smoked salt

Instructions to make Cheese & Walnut Spread Trio:
  1. Take the cream cheese out of the fridge a half hour before making the spreads
  2. Heat the oven to 150C
  3. Put the walnuts on a small baking tray and toast in the oven for 7 minutes exactly.
  4. Take the walnuts out of the oven and allow to cool slightly before chopping them quite finely.
  5. Stir the walnuts and cream cheese together in a bowl (this is the base for all three spreads)
  6. Finely dice the beetroot and place in a small bowl.
  7. Add finely chopped mint leaves, smoked salt, if using, and one quarter of the cream cheese mixture. Mix well. Spoon into a serving dish.
  8. Toast the corn kernels in a dry non-stick frying pan. Take care because they can 'pop' like popcorn when they get hot. You want to get a nice brown colour.   9. In another bowl, combine the corn kernels, lime juice, coriander/cilantro, finely chopped, Tabasco (if using) and half the remaining cream cheese mixture. Spoon into another serving dish.
  10. Finally, into the remaining cream cheese mixture twist some coarsely ground black pepper, mix in and spoon into a third serving dish.
  11. If you have time, cover with wrap, and put in the fridge for half an hour, to let the flavours meld and the spreads to firm up to a good spread consistency. This is not essential, and they can be eaten straight away.
  12. Garnish with herbs (optional)
  13. Serve with crackers, salted tortilla chips or seeded oatcakes.
