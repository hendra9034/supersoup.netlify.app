The ingredients needed to make Savory cream cheese bread bun with meat fillings:
  1. Prepare Tool
  2. Take Dough machine
  3. Prepare Ingredients
  4. Prepare 300 g Baker's flour
  5. Prepare 70 g Cream cheese
  6. Get 10-40 g Sugar
  7. Take 15 g Milk powder
  8. Take 40 g Egg
  9. Prepare 4 g Salt
  10. Make ready 140 g Milk around
  11. Prepare 4 g Yeast
  12. Take 35 g Unslated butter

Steps to make Savory cream cheese bread bun with meat fillings:
  1. Collect all the ingredients
  2. Put flour, yeast and sugar in the mixing bowl and mix; - Than add milk powder and salt and mix; - And then pour in beaten egg and milk, mix
  3. Mix with machine. - Beat with low to medium speed first - Mix till doughy
  4. Add in soften unsalted butter. Mix and window pane test. Can stretch into thinner pane. Semi transparent.
  5. Sort the dough into smooth ball shape and place into a bowl - The dough temperature should cool down to lower than 28 degree
  6. Wrap the dough to cool down in fridge. Till less than 28 degree.
  7. Make fillings. - While dough raising. - Prepare meat mince, mushroom, shallots, ginger and garlic
  8. Heat the oil in a sauce pan. - Add in ginger and garlic paste - Cook in hit oil until aroma come out
  9. Add in meat mince stir fry till more than half way cooked - Add in chopped mushroom, stir fry till 80% cooked - Turn off the heat and add in shallots. - Cook the mixture with residual heat - Set aside to cool down
  10. Raise the dough in 24 degree till double size. Around 1.5 to 2 hours. - And many bubbles
  11. Take the dough out and divide into 8 pieces. Roll them into 8 balls and cover with glad wrap
  12. Palm press the dough flat to de-gas - Roll the dough flat and round - Add cooked mince - Wrap up and sort into a ball
  13. Leave them in oven with hot water for second raise. Around 1 hour
  14. After second raise, sift some flour or sprinkle some cheese on top of the bread
  15. After 15 minutes, if the top of the bread is too brown. Turn to bottom element with fan force.
