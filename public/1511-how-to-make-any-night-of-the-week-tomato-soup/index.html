The ingredients needed to make Tomato soup:
  1. Get 1/4 kg tomato
  2. Prepare 1 beetroot
  3. Get 1 inch Ginger piece
  4. Prepare 4-5 cloves
  5. Take 5-6 black pepper
  6. Prepare 2 cinnamon sticks (1")
  7. Take 1-2 green chillyz (optional)
  8. Get 1 Tb spoon corn flour
  9. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  10. Prepare to taste Salt

Instructions to make Tomato soup:
  1. Boil tomato and beetroot.
