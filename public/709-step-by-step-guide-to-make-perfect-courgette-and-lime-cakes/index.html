The ingredients needed to make Courgette and lime cakes:
  1. Prepare 275 g washed courgettes, unpeeled
  2. Get 2 large eggs
  3. Take 130ml olive oil
  4. Prepare 150g caster sugar
  5. Prepare 230g self-raising flour
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  8. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  9. Get Grated peel of 1 lime
  10. Make ready Icing
  11. Make ready Juice of 1.5 limes
  12. Make ready Icing sugar

Steps to make Courgette and lime cakes:
  1. Grate the courgettes and all place in a sieve over a bowl to drain.
  2. Place the eggs, sugar and oil in a bowl and whisk until lighter and creamy.
  3. Add the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, vanilla essence and mix well. Then stir in the grated courgettes and grated peel of 1 lime.
  4. Divide equally between cup cake holders - I made 15
  5. Bake at 180 degrees for 15-20 minutes until cooked (springy)
  6. Cool
  7. To make the icing mix the lime juice with icing sugar until you have preferred consistency.
  8. Spoon over cup cakes. Decorate with grated lime peel or whatever you like as a topping
