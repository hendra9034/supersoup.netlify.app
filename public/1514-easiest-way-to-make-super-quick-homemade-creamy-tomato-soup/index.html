The ingredients needed to make Creamy tomato soup:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp Oil
  2. Take 4 Tomato,chopped
  3. Prepare 1 Onion,finely diced
  4. Get 2 garlic cloves
  5. Get 1 tsp Brown sugar
  6. Take 1 tbsp tomato paste
  7. Prepare 1 cup Water
  8. Make ready to taste Salt
  9. Get to taste Pepper
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup Cream

Instructions to make Creamy tomato soup:
  1. In a pan,heat oil over medium-high heat.add onion and stir fry until become translucent.add garlic, tomato and let them cook for about 5 minutes.
  2. Add Brown sugar,tomato paste,salt,pepper,water bring to boil.reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour soup in the blender mix until smooth.pour in pan app cream mix it
  4. Enjoy
