The ingredients needed to make Salt fried pork with garlic stems:
  1. Prepare 100 g streaky belly pork, without skin
  2. Get 150 g garlic stem
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp cooking oil or lard
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Sichuan chilli bean paste
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp fermented black bean, rinsed and drained
  6. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  7. Make ready 2 tsp soy sauce
