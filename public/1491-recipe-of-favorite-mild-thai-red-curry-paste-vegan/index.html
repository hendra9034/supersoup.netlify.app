The ingredients needed to make Mild Thai Red Curry Paste #vegan:
  1. Get 5-6 mild red chillies, finely chopped
  2. Prepare 5 small shallots, or 1 large onion
  3. Make ready 5-6 cloves garlic
  4. Take 2 tbs chopped galangel or ginger (more to taste)
  5. Take 2 tbs cashew nuts, walnuts or almonds
  6. Make ready 1 tbs lime peel (from unwaxed limes)
  7. Get 1 tbs lemongrass, use 2/3 starting from the root. (Note: can be substituted by adding lime peel instead)
  8. Make ready juice of 1/2 or 1 lime (to taste- the paste should have a bit of sourness in it)
  9. Prepare 1 tbs ‘fish sauce’ (vegetarian) or soya sauce
  10. Take 1 tsp ground cumin
  11. Make ready 1 tsp ground coriander
  12. Take 1 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 1 tsp white or black pepper (to taste)
  14. Prepare 1-2 hot red chillies, dry or fresh (optional, to taste)
Instructions to make Mild Thai Red Curry Paste #vegan:
  1. Chop shallots/onions, garlic and ginger and lemongrass, if you are using it. Wash your limes and create peel using a fine grater.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a food processor and mix until the paste is smooth but still has a bit of texture Depending on how juicy your onions are and if you are adding a lime juice, you may need to add a bit of water.
  3. Put a small amount of taste on your tongue to check spiciness and favours, and adjust as needed.
  4. Add 2-4 tablespoons of paste (based on taste) to a can of coconut milk when making a curry. The paste freezes well in small glass containers or ice cube trays which can be used when busy to create a delicious meal in under 30minutes. See my recipe for red Thai curry HERE
