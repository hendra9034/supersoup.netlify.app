The ingredients needed to make Fat Burning Cabbage Soup:
  1. Take onion, chopped
  2. Take Carrot, chopped
  3. Get Whole peeled tomato, with liquid
  4. Make ready Cut green beans or a bunch of fresh beans, cut
  5. Prepare cabbage, chopped
  6. Prepare Green bell pepper, diced
  7. Take Celery, chopped
  8. Take Garlic, chopped
  9. Make ready Tomato juice
  10. Make ready Beef Broth or 1 cup of water + 1 beef bouilion cube
  11. Get of any spices of your perference, to enhance the taste. I use Mixed Spices and lots of black pepper
Instructions to make Fat Burning Cabbage Soup:
  1. Place everything in a large pot. Add enough water to cover the vegetables.
  2. Simmer until the vegetables are tender. Maybe stored in the fridge for serveral days.
  3. Replace 1 or 2 of your main meals with this soup for gradual weight loss.
  4. Lean minced meat can be added for some protein intake. Cook the minced meat, drain the oil from the meat, add some cabbage soup and bring it to a boil. Then enjoy!
