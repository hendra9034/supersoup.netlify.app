The ingredients needed to make Spicy asian greek salad:
  1. Get 1 green pepper
  2. Prepare 3-4 small tomatoes
  3. Get Half white onion
  4. Prepare 100 g fetta cheese
  5. Get Salt
  6. Take Olive oil
  7. Take Some Peanuts
  8. Make ready Herbs seasoning (oregano, parsley, so many delicious herbs)
  9. Prepare 1 chilli
  10. Prepare Half lime's juice
  11. Get Sesame seeds

Instructions to make Spicy asian greek salad:
  1. Peel and chop the tomatoes, add the to a bowl to make the base of the salad
  2. Chop the onion and put it on water with salt for 5 minutes to remove its scent and flavour a bit
  3. Chop the green pepper and add it on top of the tomatoes
  4. Add the onion on top
  5. Add abundant olive oil, salt, the seasoning, sesame seeds and the crushed peanuts on top
  6. Chop the chilli and addnit to the salad
  7. Cut the fetta cheese into small cubes and add it on the sides of the salad
  8. Lastly, squeeze the half lime on top to finish the salad
  9. Serve and enjoy!!
