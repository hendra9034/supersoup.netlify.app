The ingredients needed to make Fried Tofu with Pork Floss Recipe [Welcome The Year!]:
  1. Prepare Fried Tofu With Pork Floss
  2. Take 1 packet firm Tofu
  3. Prepare 1 packet pork floss
  4. Prepare Some Corn Flour
  5. Take Ginger Breadman Creation
  6. Make ready Some rice
  7. Make ready Some seaweed
  8. Take Some soya sauce
Steps to make Fried Tofu with Pork Floss Recipe [Welcome The Year!]:
  1. Dry the tofu with a paper towel. Cut it into cube size as per picture above.
  2. Coat the cube Tofu with corn flour. Heat the deep frying oil, add the cube Tofu to deep fry.
  3. When is cooked crispy, ready to serve. Add some pork floss on top. Can serve it with the soba sauce we buy from supermarket.
  4. Creation of the Ginger Breadman: Mixed the cooked rice with soya sauce, mould it into a man shape. Cut seeweed to add the features of the man. Roll some cooked white rice as the eyeballs of one of the man.
  5. Merry Christmas and Enjoy the meal.
