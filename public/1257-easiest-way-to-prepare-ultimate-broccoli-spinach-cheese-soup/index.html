The ingredients needed to make broccoli spinach cheese soup:
  1. Make ready 2 cup of milk i used 2%
  2. Prepare 1 3/4 cup cheddar cheese or any kind you like
  3. Take 1/2 cup broccoil
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup spinach
  5. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp black pepper
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup frozen corn - optional if wanted
  8. Prepare 1 tsp 1/8 italian seasoning
  9. Make ready 2 1/2 tbsp flour or corn starch
  10. Take 1/2 onion chopped

Instructions to make broccoli spinach cheese soup:
  1. In medium soup pot add 1tbsp butter to pot let it melt then add the chopped onion to pot cook until soft and tender
  2. when the onion is tender add milk bring it to boil then add the cheese when it completely melted add broccoli and spinach to pot let it cook for 15 minutes then
  3. In a small bowl mix the flour with about 4 1/2 tbsp milk stir together and add to soup and cook until thickened
  4. serve and enjoy I hope you like it :)
  5. oh and also if it's not cheesy enough add about 1/4 cup of more cheese to it
