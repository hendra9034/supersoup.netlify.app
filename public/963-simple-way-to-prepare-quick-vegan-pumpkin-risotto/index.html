The ingredients needed to make Vegan Pumpkin Risotto:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 medium onion
  3. Make ready 3/4 pumpkin
  4. Take 2 tsp pumpkin spice
  5. Make ready 1 tsp celery salt
  6. Make ready 1 vegetable stock cube
  7. Get 1/3 cup hot water
  8. Take 120 g Arborio rice
  9. Get 1/3 cup white wine
  10. Prepare 200 ml coconut milk (1/2 can)
  11. Make ready 2 handful spinach
Instructions to make Vegan Pumpkin Risotto:
  1. Put oil in a pan and let it warm up. - Add chopped onion and let it cook for 5 mins stirring it.
  2. Add chopped pumpkin, spices and stir.
  3. Add add the stock and water and stir. Cover with a lid and stir occasionally till pumpkin is soft.
  4. Add the rice, stir and add wine, turn the heat up till it's dry.
  5. Keep stirring. - Add coconut milk till the rice is "al dente".
  6. Turn the heat off. - Add spinach. - Mix.
  7. Ready to be served.
