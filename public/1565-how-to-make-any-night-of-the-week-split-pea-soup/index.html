The ingredients needed to make split pea soup:
  1. Prepare 2 carrots
  2. Get 2 piece celery
  3. Make ready 1 onion
  4. Make ready 1 piece thick back bacon
  5. Take 1 smoked ham bone
  6. Take 2 clove garlic
  7. Make ready 2 cup split peas
  8. Prepare water

Steps to make split pea soup:
  1. Dice onion, carrot, celery, bacon, garlic. Place in slow cooker or pot.
  2. Let cook until onions are softened. Add bone, split peas and water to cover
  3. Simmer until the peas fall apart. Add more water as needed. Remove ham bone and serve. Garnish with fresh rosemary or thyme leaves.
