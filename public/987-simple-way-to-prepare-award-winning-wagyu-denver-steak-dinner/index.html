The ingredients needed to make Wagyu Denver Steak Dinner:
  1. Take 2 baking potatoes (or, large, 1 cut in half to serve)
  2. Get Salt
  3. Prepare 1-2 carrots, sliced
  4. Take 2 knobs butter
  5. Prepare 2 tbsps rapeseed oil (cold-pressed recommended)
  6. Get 1 large clove garlic, thinly sliced
  7. Make ready 1-2 courgettes, thickly sliced
  8. Get 2 Wagyu beef Denver-cut steaks (we had a small extra piece thrown in 🤣 which I also cooked)
  9. Take Ground black pepper
  10. Take 2-4 sprigs fresh thyme

Steps to make Wagyu Denver Steak Dinner:
  1. Pre-heat the oven to Gas Mark 9 or electric equivalent (Circotherm 190C)
  2. Pierce the potatoes with a fork a few times and rub in a little salt. Cook in the pre-heated oven for 75 or so minutes.
  3. After about 60 minutes cook the carrots in salted water and season the steaks.
  4. After a couple of minutes bring 1 knob butter and 1 tbsp oil to a medium heat, add the garlic and courgettes and sauté them.
  5. Simultaneously put a strong frying pan on a high heat. Once hot, add 1 tbsp oil: if sufficiently hot it will splatter! Brush it across the pan and put the steaks in. Cook for 2 1/2 to 3 minutes, by which time a crust should have formed on the bottom side. Turn over and place 1 or 2 sprigs of fresh thyme on each steak. Cook for a further 2 to 3 minutes. Turn off the heat but add a knob of butter to the pan and, once melted, quickly spoon it over the steaks.
  6. Remove the steaks from the pan and discard the thyme sprigs. Pour the pan juices over the steaks and rest them for 6 minutes: then serve onto pre-heated plates with the vegetables.
