The ingredients needed to make Cheddar cheese potato soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  2. Get 1 onion chopped
  3. Take 2 tbsp butter
  4. Get 1/4 cup flour
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 1/2 tsp pepper
  7. Make ready 6 cup chicken broth
  8. Prepare 4 cup potatoes peeled and diced
  9. Get 1 cup corn
  10. Get 1 cup broccoli
  11. Make ready 1 cup half & half
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup cheese

You could also use cauliflower instead of broccoli in this potato soup recipe. A few simple ingredients make a sumptuous soup. Be sure to use a high-quality cheddar; it's crucial to the dish's flavor. Choose a yellow cheese for the richest color.

Instructions to make Cheddar cheese potato soup:
  1. in a large stockpots over medium heat saute onions in oil until translucent about 10 minutes.
  2. add butter. stir in flour salt and pepper and cook 3min
  3. add chicken stock and potato and broccoli bring to boil and simmer uncovered. 15 min until. potato are tender. add corn and cook 10 min.
  4. add half & half and cheese
  5. cook 5 min more until cheese is melted
  6. season to taste with salt and pepper.
  7. serve hot.
