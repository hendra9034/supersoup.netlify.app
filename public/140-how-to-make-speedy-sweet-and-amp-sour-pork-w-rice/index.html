The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Pork w/ Rice:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup honey
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  3. Make ready 2 tsp soy sauce
  4. Get 1 tbsp tomato purée
  5. Prepare 2 tsp water
  6. Get 113 g pork tenderloin
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Take 1 tsp pepper
  9. Get 1/4 cup flour
  10. Take 3 tbsp cornflour
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp minced garlic
  12. Get 1 egg
  13. Take 1 white onion
  14. Get 1 carrot
  15. Prepare 1 green bell pepper
  16. Get 1 can pineapple chunks
  17. Take 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  18. Take 250 g basmati rice
Instructions to make Sweet & Sour Pork w/ Rice:
  1. Mix honey, rice vinegar, soy sauce, tomato purée for the sweet & sour sauce and set aside.
  2. Combine cornstarch and water and set aside.
  3. Season pork with salt & pepper.
  4. Mix flour and cornflour in a bowl, set aside. Whisk the egg and set aside.
  5. Batter each piece of pork by coating it with the flour mixture, then dip in the whisked egg, then a final coat in the flour mixture.
  6. In a wok, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the battered pork and fry until golden brown and pork is cooked through and then set aside.
  7. In the same wok, fry minced garlic and onions for 30 seconds.
  8. Add green pepper, carrot and fry for 1 minute.
  9. Add pineapple and fry for 1 minute.
  10. Add in the pork and the sweet and sour sauce, stir to combine and allow the sauce to come to a boil.
  11. Add the cornflour and water mixture to the pan, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
  12. Serve with basmati rice.
