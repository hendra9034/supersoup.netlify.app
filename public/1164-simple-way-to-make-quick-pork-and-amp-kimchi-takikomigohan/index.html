The ingredients needed to make Pork & Kimchi Takikomigohan:
  1. Take 200-250 g Pork thinly sliced and cut into small pieces
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  3. Get 1 tablespoon Mirin
  4. Take 1 teaspoon Sesame Oil
  5. Make ready White Pepper
  6. Take 2 cups (*180ml cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  7. Take 400 ml Chicken Stock *OR 400ml Water & 1 teaspoon Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Salt *alter the amount depending on the saltiness of the Stock
  9. Take 1/2 cup Kimchi *cut into the size that is easy to eat
  10. Take 2 Spring Onions *finely chopped

Steps to make Pork & Kimchi Takikomigohan:
  1. Thinly slice Pork and cut into small pieces, add Soy Sauce, Mirin, Sesame Oil and White Pepper, massage, and set aside while you are preparing other ingredients.
  2. Wash Rice a few times, drain well, and place it in the rice cooker’s inner pot. Add Chicken Stock, sprinkle with Salt, and gently stir.
  3. Place Pork pieces with all liquid on top of the Rice, and place Kimchi over the Pork, but DO NOT stir. Just spread evenly. Press ‘COOK’ button to start cooking.
  4. When rice is cooked, let it steamed for 10 minutes. Add Spring Onion and mix gently.
