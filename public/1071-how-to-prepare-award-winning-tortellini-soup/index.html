The ingredients needed to make Tortellini soup:
  1. Take 1/2 lb ground Italian sausage
  2. Get 10 oz tortellini
  3. Prepare 4 oz cream cheese
  4. Get 1-2 cups spinach
  5. Take 1 can diced tomatoes
  6. Take 2 cups veggie or chicken broth

Instructions to make Tortellini soup:
  1. Brown sausage
  2. Add sausage, broth, cream cheese tomatoes to show cooker on low for 4 hours.
  3. Mix tortellini and spinach into slow cooker for an additional 30 minutes. Frozen spinach can be used but use 1/2 cup
  4. Serve!
