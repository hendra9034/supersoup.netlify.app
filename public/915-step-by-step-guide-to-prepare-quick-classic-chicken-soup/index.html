The ingredients needed to make Classic Chicken Soup:
  1. Take 8 cup Water
  2. Get 4 medium Carrots, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  3. Prepare 4 medium Stalks celery, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  4. Take 1 small Onion, chopped
  5. Make ready 2 Bay leaves
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Dried thyme
  7. Make ready 1 Salt
  8. Prepare 1 Ground black pepper
  9. Take 3 1/2 lb Whole chicken
  10. Take 3 cup Egg noodles, uncooked
Instructions to make Classic Chicken Soup:
  1. In 4 1/2 to 6- quart slow-cooker, combine water, carrots, celery, onion, bay leaves, thyme, 4 teaspoons salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Place whole chicken on top of vegetables. Cover slow cooker with lid and cook as manufacturer directs on low setting 8 to 10 hours or on high setting 4 to 5 hours.
  2. Transfer chicken to cutting board. Discard bay leaves. Add noodles to slow cooker; cover with lid and cook (on low or high) 20 minutes.
  3. While noodles cook, remove and discard skin, fat and bones from chicken; shred meat.
  4. Return chicken to soup to serve.
