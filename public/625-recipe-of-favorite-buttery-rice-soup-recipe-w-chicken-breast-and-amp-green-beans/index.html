The ingredients needed to make Buttery rice soup recipe w/ chicken breast &green beans:
  1. Make ready 2 cups Butter (salted)
  2. Take 3 cups Water
  3. Take 1/4 cup Parsley (fresh or dried)
  4. Prepare Tbsp Garlic salt (optional)
  5. Take Tbsp Black Pepper (optional)
  6. Prepare 2 cups Rice (White/or Brown)

Steps to make Buttery rice soup recipe w/ chicken breast &green beans:
  1. Combine water, butter, parsley to a pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Let cook for 3 minutes or until butter is melted.
  3. Combine Rice to mixture stirring occasionally, let cook for about 30-50 minutes. Add additional water if needed.
  4. Cover the rice and let cool for about 3-5 minutes or enjoy a hot bowl!!
  5. (Optional) place rice into a fancy dish and add fresh parsley ontop.
