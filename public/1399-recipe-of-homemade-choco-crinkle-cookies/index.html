The ingredients needed to make Choco crinkle cookies:
  1. Prepare 130 gr all-purpose flour
  2. Get 2 tbsp corn flour
  3. Get 40 gr chocolate powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1/6 tsp himalayan salt
  6. Take 120 gr dark brown sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp instan coffee / esspresso
  8. Make ready 75 ml vegetable oil/EVO
  9. Get 2 large eggs
  10. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Prepare 90 gr icing sugar to cover the cookies

Steps to make Choco crinkle cookies:
  1. In a bowl put both flours, chocolate, salt and baking soda. Mixed. set aside
  2. In different big bowl, put the brown sugar and oil, mix with whisk, add the eggs and vanilla, mixed with whisk or spatula.
  3. Add the dry bowl into the wet bowl, little by little by shifting it. so there is no crumble. Mixed well, and cover with cling film, put this in fridge for minimum of 4 hours (or overnight).
  4. After 4 hours/next day: prepare baking sheet, layer with baking paper. Prepare the oven 170c
  5. Prepare a plate/bowl with icing sugar
  6. Now, using tablespoon, take 1 tablespoon (mine is about 20gr) and make a ball (or oval) with your hand. then put this in icing bowl and try to roll it so it all cover with icing sugar.
  7. Move this into the baking pan. Dont put too close to each other. Bake for about 22 minutes.
  8. It will still be soft, but it will be bit harder when its cold.
