The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Kale & Sausage Gnocchi Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 package gnocchi
  2. Prepare 1.5 tbsp butter
  3. Get 1.5 tbsp all-purpose flour
  4. Prepare 2 cup milk
  5. Prepare 2 cups stock + 1 tbsp cornstarch
  6. Prepare 1 cup carrots, diced
  7. Make ready 2 plant-based sausages, diced
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp red roasted peppers, diced
  9. Get Salt and pepper to taste
  10. Make ready 1 tsp thyme
  11. Make ready 1 tsp sage
  12. Prepare 1 cup water
  13. Make ready 5 cups kale, coarse chop

Steps to make Vegetarian Kale & Sausage Gnocchi Soup:
  1. In a pot, blanch gnocchi for 1-2 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In another pot, melt butter and add in flour. Mix until roux forms.
  3. Gradually add in milk and stock mixture. Whisk and bring to a boil.
  4. Add carrots, sausages, red peppers, thyme, sage, salt, and pepper.
  5. Boil for 30 mins or until carrots soften. Add water.
  6. Bring to a boil. Stir in kale and gnocchi.
