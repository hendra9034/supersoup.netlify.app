The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Tomato Soup:
  1. Get 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  2. Get 2 large onions, chopped
  3. Take 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  4. Get 1 tablespoon curry paste
  5. Take 4 large cloves garlic, finely chopped
  6. Make ready 2 teaspoons fresh ginger, grated
  7. Make ready 4 lbs tomatoes, coarsely chopped
  8. Take 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  9. Take 1 cup vegetable broth
  10. Get 1 tablespoon honey
  11. Take 1/2 cup coconut cream
  12. Take Hot sauce
  13. Get Salt and pepper
  14. Get Parmesan cheese, grated
  15. Prepare Fresh basil, chopped
Instructions to make Instant Pot Tomato Soup:
  1. Set instant pot to “Sauté” and once hot, add coconut oil
  2. Add onion, carrot and curry paste, and sauté until onion is translucent
  3. Add garlic and ginger and sauté two more minutes
  4. Turn off sauté, add tomatoes, tomato paste, honey and broth, and stir until evenly mixed
  5. Close lid, pressure cook on high pressure for 15 minutes, then let sit for ten minutes, then vent.
  6. Remove lid, add salt and pepper, cream, and hot sauce, then stir until evenly mixed
  7. Pour into blender and blend on high for 15 seconds. Will likely require two batches to blend. Pour from blender into large mixing bowl.
  8. Sprinkle parmasean and basil to taste. Serve with grilled cheese or toasted, thick slices of seedy oaty bread.
