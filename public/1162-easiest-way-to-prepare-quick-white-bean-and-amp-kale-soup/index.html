The ingredients needed to make White Bean & Kale Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
  2. Take 4 ounces bacon bits
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  4. Take 1 can diced tomatoes
  5. Get 1/2 onion
  6. Take 3 carrots
  7. Prepare 4 celery ribs (optional)
  8. Prepare 3 potato’s
  9. Get 3 garlic gloves (optional)
  10. Get 8 cups bone broth
  11. Get 1/2 bag soaked white beans (or 1 1/2 cans)
  12. Prepare 2 Bay leaves (optional)
  13. Get Kale - amount to liking
  14. Take Pinch salt
Instructions to make White Bean & Kale Soup:
  1. Warm the olive oil over medium heat, and stir in bacon bits, cook 6 to 8 minutes. Stir in the red pepper flakes, tomatoes, onions, carrots, celery, potatoes and garlic and stir about 5 minutes.
  2. Pour in bone broth and beans, and then drop in the bay leaves if you want. Cover the pot and simmer until the vegetables are cooked through, about 20 minutes. Uncover the pot, turn off the heat, add salt and kale. Cover the pot again and allow the kale to wilt in the residual heat of the broth, about 5 minutes further. And then done!
