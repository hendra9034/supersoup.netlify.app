The ingredients needed to make Chicken dumpling soup:
  1. Make ready 1 Onions, diced
  2. Prepare Potatoes
  3. Take Beans, optional
  4. Get 3 Celery stalks, chopped
  5. Take 2-3 c Chicken, cubed
  6. Prepare 5 Carrots, chopped
  7. Get Chicken bouillon or broth (1 qt)
  8. Make ready Salt & pepper
  9. Make ready 2 t basil
  10. Take 2 t thyme
  11. Take 3 cloves garlic
  12. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  13. Make ready 4 eggs
  14. Get 1/4 c water
  15. Prepare 1 1/2 c flour
  16. Make ready 1/4 c oil
  17. Make ready 1/2 salt

Steps to make Chicken dumpling soup:
  1. Cook chicken with celery & onions. Add garlic. Add chicken bouillon. Add seasoning and salt & pepper to taste. Take meat off bone. Add more water if needed. Simmer. Add shredded carrots & potatoes.
  2. Dumplings: beat eggs. Add water, flour, oil & salt and mix. Take 1/2 t dumpling and dip spoon into boiling water so falls off. Cook dumpling about 15 m8nutes.
