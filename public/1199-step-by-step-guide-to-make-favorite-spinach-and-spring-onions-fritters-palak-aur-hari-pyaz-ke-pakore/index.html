The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Spring Onions Fritters:

(Palak aur Hari Pyaz ke Pakore)::

  1. Prepare 2 cups spinach
  2. Take 150 g spring onions chopped
  3. Take 1 onion cut into sliced
  4. Get 1 potato cut into thin strips (Julienne)
  5. Take 2-3 green chilies chopped
  6. Get 1/4 cup mint & coriander leaves
  7. Prepare 1/3 tsp salt or to taste
  8. Take 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  9. Get 1 & 1/2 cup gram flour (besan) 2 tbsps more if needed)
  10. Make ready 3 Tbsp rice flour
  11. Prepare 1 tsp corn flour
  12. Make ready 1 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  13. Prepare Oil for deep frying
