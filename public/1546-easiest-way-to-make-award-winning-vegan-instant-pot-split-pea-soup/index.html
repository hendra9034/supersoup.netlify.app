The ingredients needed to make Vegan Instant Pot Split Pea Soup:
  1. Take 1 large yellow onion, diced
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready 2 large carrots, diced
  4. Make ready 3 celery ribs, diced
  5. Make ready 3-4 large green onions
  6. Make ready 1 large sweet potato, diced
  7. Make ready 6 cups veggie broth + 1 cup water
  8. Make ready 3 cups rinsed split peas
  9. Take 1-1/2 tsp sea salt
  10. Make ready 1 heaping tsp smoked paprika
  11. Take 1 heaping tsp thyme
  12. Get 1 heaping tsp basil
  13. Prepare 3 bay leaves
  14. Get 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
Instructions to make Vegan Instant Pot Split Pea Soup:
  1. Add onions and olive oil to instant pot and saute. Add remaining veggies and stir frequently.
  2. Turn off saute, add liquids and slices and switch to pressure cook.
  3. If you prefer a more liquid split pea soup, add a few extra cups of liquid, cause this is a thicker soup.
  4. Set pressure cook for 15 minutes and let slow release for at least 20 minutes afterwards.
