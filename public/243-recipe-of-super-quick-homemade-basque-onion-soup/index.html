The ingredients needed to make Basque Onion Soup:
  1. Take 1 can campbell's tomato juice (12 ounce(
  2. Get 1 can campbell's French onion soup
  3. Take 4 slices white bread
  4. Get Liquid cheese
  5. Prepare Any available meat or vegetable solids as desired for the soup
  6. Make ready Croutons or cheese balls for garnish
  7. Take Vegetable oil
  8. Prepare Olive oil spray
Instructions to make Basque Onion Soup:
  1. In a medium saucepan, mix tomato juice, french onion soup mix, and soup solids
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer till ready to serve
  3. Lightly cover the bottom of the frying pan with vegetable oil
  4. In a separate bowl, dip the white bread in the liquid cheese till soaked on both sides
  5. Fry cheese soaked bread in frying pan on both sides in medium heat
  6. Spray top of each slice of cheese bread lightly on both sides with olive oil spray
  7. Serve as desired.
