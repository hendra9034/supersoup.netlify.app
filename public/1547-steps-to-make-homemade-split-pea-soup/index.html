The ingredients needed to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  2. Prepare 1 onion, diced
  3. Prepare 1-2 carrots, diced
  4. Get 1 tbsp thyme
  5. Take 2-3 bay leaves
  6. Prepare Dash pepper
  7. Get Dash celery salt
  8. Get 8 cups stock
  9. Take 1 lb yellow split peas
  10. Take 2 oz vegetarian ham, diced
  11. Get Water
Steps to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. In stock pot heat oil, onions, and carrots. Season with thyme, bay leaf, pepper, and celery salt.
  2. Pour in stock and split peas. Stir and bring to a boil. Remove from heat. Cover for 20 mins. Add water as needed.
  3. Bring to a boil. Add ham. Reduce heat and simmer until peas soften.
