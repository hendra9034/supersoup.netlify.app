Steps to make Sweet butternut squash soup:
  1. Put butter and onion in large soup pot on medium heat for about 2 minutes to soften.
  2. Add apples, carrot, celery, squash and potatoes into pot for about 5 minutes or until lightly browned.
  3. Add chicken broth. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and let simmer for 40 minutes.
  4. With a hand held stick blender, blend soup ingredients until smooth. Serve hot.
