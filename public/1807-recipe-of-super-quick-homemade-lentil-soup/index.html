The ingredients needed to make lentil soup:
  1. Make ready 1 cup lentils
  2. Get 1 cup kale
  3. Prepare 1 cup chopped turkey sausage
  4. Make ready 1 cup chopped carrotts
  5. Make ready 1 cup sliced onions
  6. Prepare 1 cup chopped celery
  7. Prepare 4 cup veggie broth
  8. Make ready 2 tsp cumin
  9. Take 1 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 1 tsp fresh ground pepper

Steps to make lentil soup:
  1. add all ingredients except kale in medium crockpot
  2. set crockpot to low heat
  3. cook for a at least 6 hours
  4. 15-20 min before serving add kale
