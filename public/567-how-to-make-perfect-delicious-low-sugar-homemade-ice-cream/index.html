Instructions to make Delicious low-sugar homemade ice-cream:
  1. Throw the fruit, sugar and milk into an immersion blender cup.
  2. Take the stick blender, and slowly and gently press down on the fruit.
  3. Use a swaying motion to slowly progress down the cup. Don't press too hard or you might damage the blender!
  4. Once you reach the bottom, pull the blender out, use a teaspoon to get all the fruit off it, and repeat the process until smooth and creamy.
  5. Serve immediately, don't refreeze!
