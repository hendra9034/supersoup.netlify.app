The ingredients needed to make Mexican slow cooked beef:
  1. Make ready 400 g chopped casserole beef
  2. Make ready 1 large onion
  3. Make ready 4 cloves of garlic
  4. Make ready 2 chillies
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon oregano
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  8. Make ready 2 teaspoons chipotle cooking paste
  9. Get 200 g chopped tinned tomatoes
  10. Take Toppings
  11. Get Sliced avocado
  12. Get Finely sliced red chilli
  13. Get Finely sliced red cabbage
  14. Prepare Chopped coriander
  15. Make ready Sour cream

Instructions to make Mexican slow cooked beef:
  1. Chop onion, garlic and finely slice chillies
  2. Heat oil in a large saucepan and brown the meat in batches. Set aside in a bowl
  3. Cook the onion, garlic and chillies in the saucepan with oil on a low heat for 8/10 minutes
  4. Add oregano, cumin and allspice. Cook for further 2 minutes.
  5. Add chipotle paste, cook for a minute, tomatoes, cook for a further 1 minute.
  6. Add the beef and stir. Add boiling water. Just enough to cover the beef. Season with salt and pepper. Stir and bring to the boil.
  7. Transfer all the contents to a slow cooker and heat on low for at least 6 hours.
  8. Just before it’s ready. Chop coriander, finely slice red chilli. Slice or chop avocado and finely slice red cabbage.
  9. Serve with rice and garnish with coriander, chilli, avocado, red cabbage and a dollop of sour cream.
