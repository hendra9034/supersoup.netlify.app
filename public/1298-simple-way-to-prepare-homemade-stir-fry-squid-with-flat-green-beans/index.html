The ingredients needed to make Stir Fry Squid with Flat Green Beans:
  1. Make ready 200 gr squid
  2. Get 250 gr flat green beans
  3. Take 1/4 piece bell pepper
  4. Make ready 2 slice ginger
  5. Prepare 4 pieces garlic chop finely
  6. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tbs oysters sauce
  8. Get 1 tsp corn starch
  9. Take 1 tbs water
  10. Make ready 1 tsp chili oil
  11. Prepare 50 ml water
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
