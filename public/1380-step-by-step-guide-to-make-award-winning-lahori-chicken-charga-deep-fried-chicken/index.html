The ingredients needed to make Lahori Chicken Charga (deep fried chicken):
  1. Prepare 1 chicken of 2 kg (cut in to half or full your choice)
  2. Prepare 1 tsp salt or to taste
  3. Make ready 1 Tbsp red chili powder
  4. Make ready 1 Tbsp keshmiri red chilli powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 inch ginger
  6. Make ready 3-4 cloves garlic
  7. Take 4-5 Tbsp tamarind sauce or lemon juice
  8. Prepare pinch food color (red food colour)
  9. Prepare 1 & 1/2 cup Greek style yogurt
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garam Masala powder
  11. Prepare black pepper corns
  12. Take 4-5 cloves
  13. Make ready 1 nutmeg
  14. Prepare 1 black cardamom
  15. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
  16. Get 1/2 inch piece of cinnamon

Instructions to make Lahori Chicken Charga (deep fried chicken):
  1. Crush nutmeg,black pepper corn, cinnamon,clove,cardamom & cumin seeds. - Peel ginger and garlic and grind/blend it with a little yogurt so that it blends well. - Wash and pat dry chicken with a kitchen towel well. - Put cuts on the chicken so that it absorbs the marinade well.
  2. Take a large bowl add all ingredients and mix well. - - Take marinade in your hand and apply it all over the chicken. Wear gloves to avoid spice burns. - - Let it rest Marinate chicken for at least 4 hours on the counter in a cool place or preferably overnight in the fridge.
