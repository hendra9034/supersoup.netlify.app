The ingredients needed to make Red Lentil & Tomato Soup üçÖ:
  1. Get 1 large white onion, chopped
  2. Make ready 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  3. Take 1 tsp cumin seeds
  4. Prepare 2 tsp ground coriander
  5. Make ready Olive oil
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 2 large tomatoes, diced
  8. Prepare 150 g red lentils
  9. Take 400 mls light coconut oil
  10. Make ready 600 mls veg or chicken stock stock
  11. Get 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice

Instructions to make Red Lentil & Tomato Soup üçÖ:
  1. Toast the cumin seeds in a dry pan until fragrant then remove and put to one side.
  2. Add a splash of olive oil to the pan and gently cook your onions and garlic for 5 minutes. Add the seeds, salt and coriander powder and stir well.
  3. Stir in the lentils and tomatoes, stir to coat everything in the spices. Then add the stock and leave to simmer on a medium heat with the lid on for 25 minutes.
  4. When the lentils are soft, stir in the coconut milk and lemon juice. Blend u til smooth. Taste and season with salt and pepper as required.
