The ingredients needed to make No sugar chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Take 600 gr all purposes flour
  2. Take 150 gr thyme honey
  3. Prepare 100 ml fresh orange juice
  4. Take orange zest (from 1 orange is quite enough)
  5. Make ready 2 tea spoons baking powder
  6. Get 1 pinch salt
  7. Prepare 200 ml extra virgin olive oil
  8. Get 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  10. Prepare 60 gr oats
  11. Prepare 100 gr dark chocolate chips

Instructions to make No sugar chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Whisk to mix thoroughly the olive oil, with the honey and the orange juice.
  2. Add the vanilla extract and the cinnamon and stir some more.
  3. Add the salt the oats and half of the flour and mix until combined.
  4. Add the rest of the flour and mix thoroughly until absorbed. Add the chocolate chips and mix in the dough.
  5. Preheat the oven on 170oC and lay baking paper on a baking tray. Make bit size cookies and place on the baking tray.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes. Let cool and keep them in an air tight container.
