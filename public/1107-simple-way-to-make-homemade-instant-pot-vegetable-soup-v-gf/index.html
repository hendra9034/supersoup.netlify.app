The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Vegetable Soup (V/GF):
  1. Make ready 6 cups vegetable broth
  2. Prepare 3-4 large yukon golden potatoes
  3. Take 1 yellow onion
  4. Make ready 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  5. Make ready 4 cups choppe veggies (cauli., green beans, carrots, celery) etc
  6. Take 2-3 zucchini chopped
  7. Prepare 1-18 oz jar diced tomatoes
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  9. Make ready 1 tsp granulated garlic
  10. Make ready 1 tsp rosemary
  11. Get 1 tsp tyme
  12. Get 1 1/2 tsp parsley
  13. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  15. Make ready 2 bay leaves
Instructions to make Instant Pot Vegetable Soup (V/GF):
  1. Saute (in instant pot) onions, garlic and olive oil until golden brown
  2. Add veggies and sauté for 5 minutes
  3. Add broth, seasonings and diced tomatoes
  4. Pressure cook for 30 minutes. Quick release and serve.
