The ingredients needed to make Crock Pot Split Pea Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 pkg dried split peas
  2. Take 9 cups chicken broth
  3. Get 4 carrots, coarsely chooped
  4. Get 3 medium potatoes, diced small
  5. Prepare 3 celery stalks, chopped
  6. Prepare 1 onion, finely chopped
  7. Get 2-3 cups diced ham
  8. Get 1 tsp minced garlic
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Kosher Salt
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
  11. Prepare 1 bay leaf
Instructions to make Crock Pot Split Pea Soup:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a large crock pot.
  2. Cook on low for 7-8 hours or high for 4-5.
  3. Remove bay leaf before serving
