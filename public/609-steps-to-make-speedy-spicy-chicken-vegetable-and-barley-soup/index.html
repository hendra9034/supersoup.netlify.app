The ingredients needed to make Spicy Chicken,Vegetable, and Barley soup:
  1. Make ready chicken tenderloins
  2. Prepare low sodium chicken broth
  3. Make ready quick cook barley
  4. Take small cans rotel hot diced tomatoes and green chilies
  5. Prepare Salt and Peppers to taste
  6. Get frozen vegitales (Broccoli, Cauliflower, and Carrots)
  7. Take frozen onion celery and peppers
  8. Take Spices
  9. Make ready EVO
  10. Make ready curry
  11. Take onion powder
  12. Make ready garlic powder
  13. Take cajun spice blend
  14. Get Salt and Peppers to taste
Steps to make Spicy Chicken,Vegetable, and Barley soup:
  1. Leg pit add EVO and onion celery and peppers over medium heat
  2. Cut chicken tenberloins into 1 inch cubes then add to pot cook until almost cooked
  3. Add broth, tomatoes and, all vegetables bring to boil cover and reduce heat until chicken is done
  4. Add Barley pre-cooked mix well cover reduce the heat and simmer until the barley is well incoroerated
  5. Serve and enjoy "Note: this is very spicy you can cut the heat by using regular diced tomatoes without the chiles"
