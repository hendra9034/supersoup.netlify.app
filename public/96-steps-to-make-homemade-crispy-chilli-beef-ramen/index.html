The ingredients needed to make Crispy chilli beef ramen:
  1. Prepare 125 g beef mince
  2. Make ready 2 tsp chilli paste
  3. Take 2 tsp garlic paste
  4. Prepare 2 tsp ginger paste
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  6. Prepare 100 ml beef stock
  7. Prepare 60 g egg noodles
  8. Prepare 1 pak choi
  9. Take 1 egg
  10. Make ready 2 spring onion
  11. Prepare Pinch sesame seeds
  12. Take Pinch chilli flakes

Instructions to make Crispy chilli beef ramen:
  1. Fry your mince until crispy with chilli, garlic, ginger and soy sauce, usually 30 minutes on a medium heat stirring often
  2. Boil beef stock and simmer
  3. Add noodles, and pak choi to the stock and cook for 5 minutes
  4. In the meantime, boil an egg for 6 minutes if you like it runny, 10 minutes if you like it hard
  5. Chop spring onions
  6. Plate by pouring in your broth first, then beef, noodles, pak choi, egg, spring onions
  7. Garnish with chilli flakes and sesame seeds
