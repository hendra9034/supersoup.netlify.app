The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Take 4 tbsp Olive Oil
  2. Get 2 tbsp Butter, or butter alternative
  3. Make ready 42 oz Beef Broth, recommend organic
  4. Take 6 medium Onions, any kind/color is fine
  5. Get 1 Bay Leaf
  6. Get 2 clove Garlic, crushed
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Dry Red Wine
  8. Get 1 Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  9. Take 1 as needed Fresh basil leaves. We add 9-10 leaves to the entire batch.

Instructions to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Heat butter, oil, 1 garlic clove, Bay leaf, and half of the basil leaves on low.
  2. Add thinly sliced onions and cook gently for 45 min, or until mostly clear.
  3. Add beef broth and bring to slow simmer.
  4. Reduce heat, add wine, second garlic clove, and remaining basil.
  5. Simmer on low heat for 30 min
  6. Place slices of bread in oven with choice of sliced or shredded cheese on top.
  7. Place a slice of cheesy bread on top of each persons bowl of soup!
  8. Enjoy!
