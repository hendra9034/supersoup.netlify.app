The ingredients needed to make Braised Pork Belly, Chicken Wings & Eggs Chinese Style:
  1. Make ready 400 gr pork belly,
  2. Take 400 gr chicken wings,
  3. Prepare 4 pieces of eggs
  4. Make ready 1000 ml water
  5. Make ready 100 ml soy sauce
  6. Get 60 ml dark sauce
  7. Get 50 gr Rock sugar
  8. Make ready 2 slice Ginger
  9. Take 2 tbs of cooking wine
  10. Prepare 1 piece small onion
  11. Get 6 pieces garlic crust it with the skin
  12. Prepare 1 piece star anise
  13. Take 2 pieces bay leaves
  14. Make ready 2 pieces lime leaves
  15. Take spring onion
Instructions to make Braised Pork Belly, Chicken Wings & Eggs Chinese Style:
  1. Wash clean the Pork belly & chicken wings. - -boil the water, after boiling add 1tbs cooking wine then add in the Pork belly & the chicken wings put to boil for 1minutes then take out wash again - -boil the eggs for 5minutes then soak in Cold water for 15minutes after that peels the skin
  2. Sauteed the garlic, small Onion, Ginger until smells fragrant - - then add in the rock sugar sauteed until the rock sugar mealty then add water,soy sauce,dark sauce, spring onion,bay leaves,lime leaves, cooking wine, start anise bring to boil. - -after boiling add in the Pork belly bring to cook until soft about 45minutes

  4. Then take out, cooling down then slice it the pork belly put in the plate ready to serve
