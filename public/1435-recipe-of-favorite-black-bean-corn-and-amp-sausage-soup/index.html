The ingredients needed to make Black Bean, Corn & Sausage Soup:
  1. Get 1 lb hot or Cajun sausage
  2. Take 1 can corn
  3. Take 2 cans black beans
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 cans water
  5. Get to taste Salt & pepper
  6. Take 1 tbsp Ground cumin
  7. Get 1 yellow onion diced
  8. Prepare Dash ground thyme
  9. Prepare 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  10. Get Shredded cheddar cheese to garnish
  11. Prepare Sour cream to garnish
  12. Prepare Sriracha to garnish
  13. Prepare Cilantro to garnish

Steps to make Black Bean, Corn & Sausage Soup:
  1. Dice onion, grate fresh garlic, and slice sausage into pennies.
  2. Sauté on medium heat for approximately 5 minutes with salt, pepper, cumin, thyme, oregano, and cinnamon.
  3. Add corn and black beans, plus 1 1/2 cans of water.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes and serve hot.
  5. Add desired garnishes!
