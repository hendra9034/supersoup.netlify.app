The ingredients needed to make Spanish Bean Soup Batch 3 Instapot:
  1. Prepare Meat
  2. Make ready 1-1/2 cup smoked ham
  3. Make ready 1 pound beef shank with bone in
  4. Prepare 3/4 pound smoked ham hock bone in skin on
  5. Get 7 ounces chorizo sausage
  6. Get 1/4 pound salt pork
  7. Prepare Vegetables
  8. Get 1 large onion
  9. Take 1 pound (13 ounces) garbanzo beans, chickpeas
  10. Prepare 1-1/3 pound potatoes
  11. Get Spices
  12. Make ready 1/2 gram saffron
  13. Prepare To taste kosher salt
  14. Take 1 teaspoon granulated garlic
  15. Make ready 4 leaves bay leaf
  16. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  17. Prepare 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin

Steps to make Spanish Bean Soup Batch 3 Instapot:
  1. Slice a chunk off the ham hock to allow flavors to incorporate better. Take the beef shank add to pot. I got mine slice across so the bone marrow can get in the soup. Slice the chorizo on a bias.
  2. I used small baby potatoes. I quartered the bigger ones and halved the smaller. Dice the onion and add. Slice the salt pork add all these ingredients along with the spices into the pot.
  3. Add water to cover and give it a stir. Set on pressure cook high setting. Pressure cook 1 hour keep warm and under pressure till ready to serve. Release pressure. Serve, traditionally with Cuban sandwiches. I hope you enjoy!!
