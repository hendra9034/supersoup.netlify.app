The ingredients needed to make Bean, Potato,Onion, Sausage Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 lbs White Bean
  2. Take 4-6 Potatoes
  3. Get 1 lbs Smoked Sausage
  4. Take 1 Onion
  5. Make ready to taste Salt & Pepper
  6. Make ready 1 cap Oliveoil
Steps to make Bean, Potato,Onion, Sausage Soup:
  1. Add beans to pot
  2. Peel & dice potatoes,onion, sausage.
  3. Put in pot
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Add water and let cook
