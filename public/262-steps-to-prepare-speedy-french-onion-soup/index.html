The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Take 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  2. Take 2 tbsp butter
  3. Make ready 6 Vidalia onions thinly sliced
  4. Prepare 1 salt and pepper
  5. Make ready 2 tsp fresh thyme
  6. Take 1 bay leaf
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup dry sherry
  8. Get 6 cup beef stock
  9. Make ready 6 slice toasted, thickly sliced and lightly toasted
  10. Make ready 2 cup Mozzarella cheese to your liking (1-)
Steps to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Heat butter and oil over medium heat. Add onions, season with salt, pepper and thyme. Cook onions until tender, sweet and caramel colored.
  2. Add sherry, bay leaf and deglaze. Add beef stock, cover and bring to a boil. Arrange ovenproof bowls on a baking sheet. Preheat broiler. Ladle soup into bowls, float toasted bread on soup and cover with mozzarella . Broil until cheese melts and is bubbly and lightly browned.
