The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 medium onions
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  3. Make ready 2 tsp flour
  4. Make ready 2 can beef broth
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  6. Make ready 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  7. Make ready 1 pinch garlic salt
  8. Make ready 1 croutons
  9. Get 2 oz mozzarella cheese

Instructions to make French Onion Soup:
  1. thinly slice onion
  2. saute until slightly brown
  3. stir in flour
  4. add broth, water and seasonings
  5. bring to boil
  6. put in oven proof bowls
  7. add crutons
  8. bake at 350
  9. add cheese and melt
