The ingredients needed to make Curried Lentil Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 bag (16oz) dried lentils
  2. Get 8 cup chicken broth
  3. Get 2 medium carrots
  4. Prepare 2 stalks celery
  5. Get 1 medium onion
  6. Make ready 2 clove garlic
  7. Get 3 tsp curry powder
  8. Get 1 tsp salt
  9. Get 2 bay leaves
  10. Get 2 can diced tomatoes
  11. Take 2 cup fresh spinach
