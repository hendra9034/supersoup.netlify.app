The ingredients needed to make CRISPY HOISIN BEEF w/ RICE:
  1. Get 250 g lean beef mince
  2. Prepare 3 spring onions
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  4. Take 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  5. Make ready 2 tsp minced garlic
  6. Get 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  7. Prepare 225 g boiled rice
  8. Prepare 2 eggs
  9. Get 1 tsp chilli oil
  10. Make ready Sesame seeds for garnish
  11. Take Chilli flakes for garlic

Steps to make CRISPY HOISIN BEEF w/ RICE:
  1. Fry beef until the fat content has evaporated and add spring onions, soy sauce, garlic, oyster sauce, and hoisin sauce and fry for 15 minutes stirring often until crisp
  2. Boil rice for 10 minutes
  3. Fry eggs until crispy
  4. Serve everything with chilli oil on the egg, and sesame seeds and chilli flakes for garnish
