The ingredients needed to make lomi soup:
  1. Make ready 1 kg chicken or pork soup pack
  2. Make ready 5 cup water
  3. Make ready 1 large onion
  4. Get 6 clove garlic
  5. Prepare 1/4 kg shrimp - shelled, deveined
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp fish sauce
  7. Get 150 grams squidballs - halved
  8. Take 150 grams fishball - halved
  9. Make ready 150 grams kikiam - diagonal cut
  10. Get 150 grams orlian - diagonal cut
  11. Prepare 1 carrot - julienned
  12. Prepare 1 celery - julienned
  13. Make ready 1/4 cup baguio beans
  14. Make ready 1/2 kg lomi noodles
  15. Get 1/2 cup cabbage
  16. Make ready 1 chinese cabbage - chopped
  17. Get 1 tbsp cornstarch
  18. Get 1 dash salt
  19. Make ready 1 dash pepper
  20. Make ready 1 large egg - beaten lightly

Steps to make lomi soup:
  1. boil and simmer soup pack until you get good stock. separate meat from stock. flake meat off from bones and set aside
  2. saute onion, garlic, shrimp. add fish sauce, then combination balls.
  3. add stock and boil. add carrots, beans and celery.
  4. add noodles, and then both cabbages. cook till noodles are tender.
  5. make slurry using cornstarch. add to soup slowly to thicken.
  6. stir in flaked meat. add seasoning and taste.
  7. once flavor is set, turn off heat. stir in the beaten egg.
  8. plate and serve hot.
