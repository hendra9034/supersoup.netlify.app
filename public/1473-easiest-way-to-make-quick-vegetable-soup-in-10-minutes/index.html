The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Soup in 10 minutes:
  1. Get 250 grams Mixed vegetable, leafy greens of your choice
  2. Prepare 1 cup Water to pressure cook
  3. Prepare to taste Salt
  4. Take Puree of 1 small tomato
  5. Get 1 teaspoon butter or olive oil
  6. Make ready 2 cloves garlic(peeled,chopped)
  7. Make ready Other seasoning and garnish of your choice
Steps to make Vegetable Soup in 10 minutes:
  1. Rinse, peel and chop the vegetables and greens in small pieces
  2. Add them to a pressure cooker and cook the vegetables untill tender or upto one whistle.
  3. Get the vegetables out of the cooker once the steam has run out
  4. Blend the vegetables into a puree using the water used to pressure cook
  5. Melt butter in a pot
  6. Add and saute the chopped garlic till light brown
  7. Next add the vegetables puree
  8. Mix well and cook on low heat for 2 minutes
  9. As the soup is bubbling,add the tomato puree and mix well(you can choose to add tomato while pressure cooking the vegetables too)
  10. Cook for a minute more before adding salt and other seasoning of your choice
  11. Mix well and take off heat
  12. Divide into soup bowls and serve warm with your choice of garnish
  13. Note: this soup will taste excellent with croutons, fresh creme garnish. Kids can be served with some grated cheese or boiled noodles can be added to make this a filling meal
