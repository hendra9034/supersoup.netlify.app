The ingredients needed to make Italian meatballs with orzo pasta and chilli:
  1. Take 500 g Steak minced beef
  2. Make ready 5 tbsp fine white fresh breadcrumbs
  3. Prepare 1 little milk, to soak breadcrumbs
  4. Get 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano (dried is fine, but add it to the onion, chilli and garlic fry)
  5. Get 100 g cubed pancetta
  6. Make ready 1 red onion peeled and finely sliced (reserve a small amount for later)
  7. Prepare 2 garlic cloves, finely sliced (reserve 1 clove for later)
  8. Get 2 red chillis, finely sliced (reserve a small amount for later)
  9. Make ready Handful Italian tomatoes quartered
  10. Prepare Small bunch of fresh basil
  11. Prepare Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  12. Get 300 g dried orzo pasta
  13. Prepare Drop olive oil

Instructions to make Italian meatballs with orzo pasta and chilli:
  1. In a large bowl add your beef mince. - - In a smaller bowl add the breadcrumbs and a drop of milk. Add fresh oregano to the breadcrumbs. Season with salt and pepper, then mix until you have a slightly doughy texture. - - Sauté the onion, garlic and chilli.
  2. Add the breadcrumb mix into the bowl of beef mince and combine together by hand. - Then when cooled down add the chilli, garlic, onions it to the beef mixture and combine everything again by hand. - When this is done, wet your hands and roll the beef mince into balls.
  3. In the meantime bring a medium saucepan to the boil and add the orzo pasta and just a pinch of salt, cook to the packet instructions.
  4. Add the olive oil to the heated pan and gently fry the meatballs until golden and thoroughly cooked through. - Add the remaining chilli, garlic and onion to the pan of meatballs along with the quartered tomatoes and pancetta and fry until the tomatoes and onions have softened and the pancetta is fully cooked and slightly golden. - Then add the orzo to the meatballs and gently mix, taste for seasoning and serve on a warm plate with a scattering of fresh basil.
