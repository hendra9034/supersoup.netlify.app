The ingredients needed to make Red Lentil Soup:
  1. Take soup
  2. Get 2 tbsp oil
  3. Take 2 medium onion, chopped
  4. Prepare 1 cup carrots
  5. Get 2 tbsp minced or chopped garlic
  6. Take 1 tsp ground ginger
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  8. Prepare 1 cup red lentils, rinsed & sorted
  9. Prepare 15 oz can of diced tomato
  10. Prepare 2 cup water
  11. Prepare kosher salt
  12. Prepare water
  13. Get dressing
  14. Take 1/4 cup oil
  15. Take 1 tsp mustard seed
  16. Get 1/4 tsp ground coriander seed
  17. Get 1/2 tsp paprika
  18. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  19. Take kosher salt
Instructions to make Red Lentil Soup:
  1. Chop up onions and carrots, toss them into the Dutch oven with the oil. Cook until soft.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes. Sautee until you can smell the spices and the bottom of the Dutch oven becomes browned.
  3. Add tomato, lentils, salt, and water. Cover and let simmer for 15 to 20 minites, stirring occasionally to make sure the lentils don't stick.
  4. While the soup is simmering, put the quarter cup oil in a small sauce pan on low heat.
  5. Warm the oil, and then add mustard seed, coriander, cumin, paprika and salt. Stir occasionally.
  6. When lentils have started to break down, turn off the heat for the lentil mixture. Put the lentils mixture into the food processor, and pulse until smooth. Add water as needed to loosen mixture.
  7. Serve lentil soup, top with dressing.
