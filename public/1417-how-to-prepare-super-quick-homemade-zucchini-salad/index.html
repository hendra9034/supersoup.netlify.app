The ingredients needed to make Zucchini salad:
  1. Prepare 3-4 medium zucchini
  2. Make ready 150 g cherry tomatoes
  3. Prepare 100 g cheese
  4. Prepare 1 can corn
  5. Make ready 1 lemon
  6. Get 3 garlic cloves
  7. Prepare salt
  8. Take pepper
  9. Prepare olive oil
  10. Get dried oregano
  11. Get dried thyme

Steps to make Zucchini salad:
  1. Prepare ingredients. Peel and chop the garlic to small pieces.
  2. Wash and chop zucchinis and cherry tomatoes, then shred the cheese.
  3. On the pan, cook garlic with olive oil until golden brown. Add chopped zucchini, cherry tomatoes and corn, season with salt, pepper, dried thyme and oregano and the juice of 1 lemon. Mix it well. Cook until everything is soft.
