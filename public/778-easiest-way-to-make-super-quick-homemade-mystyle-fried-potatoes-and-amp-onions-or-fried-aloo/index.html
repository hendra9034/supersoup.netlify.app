The ingredients needed to make #mystyle Fried Potatoes & Onions or Fried Aloo:
  1. Get 8 new potatoes(any all round type works)
  2. Make ready Sliced the new potatoes, the thickness of a UK £1 coin
  3. Take 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  4. Prepare 1 white cooking onion, cut in half & thinly sliced
  5. Get 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
  6. Get 1/4 teaspoon salt(or to taste)
  7. Get 2 tablespoon olive oil for cooking
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon whole cumin seeds
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon dried chilli flakes
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  11. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground tumeric
Instructions to make #mystyle Fried Potatoes & Onions or Fried Aloo:
  1. Heat a heavy based, wide frying pan on low heat with olive oil until hot enough(check with a cumin- if it instantly crackles & pop- it's ready)
  2. Add cumin seeds, let them crackle for a few seconds, until popped & fragrant, shake the pan, avoid burning. Add the onions & garlic- stir well.
  3. Add the potatoes with all the spices except the salt. Stir well & cover, leave heat on low. Check at 10 minutes, let the condensation in the lid drip back into the pan. Stir well to make sure it's not burning on the bottom.
  4. Re-cover, cook another 10 minutes, check if the potatoes are soft & cuts with your cooking spoon.
  5. Season with salt (to taste) & some freshly ground black pepper.
  6. Serve however liked. We have it with chargrilled chicken/pork chops/sausages.
  7. If having it with pork chops- I normally core an eating apple, sliced with skin on, then add it at the during the second part of the 20mins cooking time.
