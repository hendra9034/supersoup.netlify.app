The ingredients needed to make Ginger soy chicken wings:
  1. Prepare 600 gram chicken wings
  2. Get Ginger, julienne
  3. Get slice Red onion, thinly
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  5. Get 1 tsp white pepper
  6. Get 1 tbsp chicken salt
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions to make Ginger soy chicken wings:
  1. Peel, wash and cut onion and ginger as shown in photo
  2. Sauté onion and ginger with olive oil until soft, the aroma is so nice.
  3. Add the chicken wings, stir well. Turn them around well to make sure they won't burn.
  4. Add dark soy sauce, white pepper, chicken salt and water, stir well. Add water again when the sauce dry up. Let it sit for half an hour until all the wings are soft and sauce become thick. Turn the chicken often to cook them evenly.
  5. Now plate up and ready to eat. Serve with warm steam rice.
