The ingredients needed to make Fried Eggs in Tomato Sauce:
  1. Prepare 4 large ripe tomatoes
  2. Prepare Salt to taste
  3. Get pinch sugar
  4. Prepare pinch dry oregano
  5. Make ready olive oil
  6. Make ready 4 eggs
Instructions to make Fried Eggs in Tomato Sauce:
  1. Cut tomatoes into small cubes. Add in a frying pan and place over high heat. Add a pinch of sugar and bring to a boil until all tomato juices evaporate.
  2. Add plenty of olive oil and give it a stir. Season to taste and add the oregano.
  3. Create 4 small holes in the tomato sauce and break the eggs into those “holes”.
  4. Lower the heat to minimum and cover until the eggs are cooked. Be careful the yolk must be all runny and gooey.
  5. For the ultimate taste experience serve with French fries.
