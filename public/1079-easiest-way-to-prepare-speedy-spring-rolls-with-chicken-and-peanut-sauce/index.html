The ingredients needed to make Spring rolls with chicken and peanut sauce:
  1. Take Chicken
  2. Make ready 300 g chicken breasts, chopped
  3. Make ready 4 tbl sp soy sauce
  4. Make ready 1 lime, juiced
  5. Take 1-2 tbl sp garlic powder
  6. Take 2 tbl sp water
  7. Take Peanut sauce
  8. Prepare 2-3 tbl sp peanut butter
  9. Make ready 2-3 tbl sp water
  10. Take 2 tbl sp honey
  11. Prepare 1 tbl sp soy sauce
  12. Prepare 1 tea sp vinegar
  13. Get 1 tea sp lime juice
  14. Get Other
  15. Prepare 1-2 carrots
  16. Prepare 1 cucumber
  17. Make ready 1 yellow bell pepper
  18. Make ready rice papers

Instructions to make Spring rolls with chicken and peanut sauce:
  1. Marinate chopped chicken breasts in a marinade of soy sauce, juice of 1 lime, garlic powder and water for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile chop carrots and bell pepper into about 8 cm rectangles and cucumber on wheels. Then prepare peanut sauce by mixing its ingredients.
  2. Cook the meat on the pan until golden brown and let it cool a little bit. Soak rice paper in warm water for few seconds according to instructions. Place it on a plate, place veggies and meat in the middle and roll it up.
