The ingredients needed to make Classic Pancakes:
  1. Get 200 g plain flour
  2. Take 1 Tbsp baking powder
  3. Take 1 tsp white sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 2 eggs
  6. Take 20 g butter, melted
  7. Prepare 250 ml milk
  8. Take Knob butter

Instructions to make Classic Pancakes:
  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, sugar eggs, butter, mill in a food processor. Blitz or whisk ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  2. In a non-stick pan on medium heat, melt knob of butter. Using a tablespoon, dollop a few globs of batter into a frying pan.
  3. When cooking batter, cook each side of pancake till it has bubbles on it (for 2 to 3 mins for each side)
  4. Serve hot!
