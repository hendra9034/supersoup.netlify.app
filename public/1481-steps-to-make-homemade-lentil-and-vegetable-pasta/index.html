The ingredients needed to make Lentil and Vegetable Pasta:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Prepare 1 medium onion, diced
  3. Make ready 4 garlic cloves, diced
  4. Take 400 g carrots, peeled and diced
  5. Take 1 celery stalk, diced
  6. Get 230 g red lentils
  7. Make ready 1.3 L boiling water with 2 vegetarian oxo cubes dissolved
  8. Get 150 g favorite shaped pasta
  9. Get 150 g natural low fat yogurt
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
Instructions to make Lentil and Vegetable Pasta:
  1. Heat the olive oil in a large non-stick sauce pan. Over medium heat fry the onion, garlic, celery and carrots for 8 minutes until the vegetables are soft on the outside.
  2. Add the lentils and stock. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil. Simmer uncovered for 15-20 minutes until lentils are cooked. Allow to cool for 20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile cook pasta in another sauce pan according to packet instructions. Drain well and set aside until needed.
  4. Blend the soup either in a blender or using a hand blender until smooth.
  5. Add the pasta to the soup. Bring the pan back to a simmer until piping hot.
  6. Remove soup from the heat and stir in the yogurt. Add more salt and pepper if needed.
  7. To serve sprinkle with fresh parsley and add a dollop of yogurt along with a crusty soda bread.
