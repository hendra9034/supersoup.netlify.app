The ingredients needed to make Babi Ketjap:
  1. Take 500 gr pork shoulder
  2. Make ready 4-5 TS oil (sunflower, corn, but not olive)
  3. Get 2 large onions
  4. Make ready Half a garlic
  5. Get 3 ts ginger powder
  6. Make ready 2 TS fresh ginger
  7. Take 4 TS ketjap Manis (or sweet soy sauce)
  8. Prepare 2 TS vinegar
  9. Prepare 1 ts Sambal
  10. Make ready Pepper and salt

Instructions to make Babi Ketjap:
  1. Chop the meat in large pieces, if you have bones then use them as well. - Rub the ginger powder salt and pepper in it and leave it in the fridge for a couple of hours.
  2. Use a big Dutch pot for the cooking. - Hear the oil and caramelise the onions and garlic.
  3. Add the meat and brown it on all sides. - Add the sambal.
  4. Pour in the vinegar, the ketjap, ginger and some water. - Put a lid on it and leave it simmer for half an hour
  5. Serve with rice and perhaps some Indonesian garnish like Atjar Tjampoer
