The ingredients needed to make Fillet steak ramen:
  1. Get 1 fillet steak
  2. Take 2 tsp chilli paste
  3. Prepare 2 tsp garlic paste
  4. Get 2 tsp ginger paste
  5. Get 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  6. Make ready 100 ml beef stock
  7. Get 60 g egg noodles
  8. Take 1 pak choi
  9. Make ready 1 egg
  10. Make ready 2 spring onion
  11. Take 2 red chilli
  12. Get Pinch sesame seeds
  13. Take Pinch chilli flakes
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp sesame oil

Steps to make Fillet steak ramen:
  1. Season steak with chilli, garlic, ginger and soy sauce, and fry in sesame oil for 4 minutes each side, then rest
  2. In the meantime, boil beef stock and simmer
  3. Add noodles, pak choi to the stock and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Boil an egg for 6 minutes if you like it runny, 10 minutes if you prefer a hard boiled egg
  5. Chop spring onions and red chilli
  6. Plate by putting beef broth in first, then steak, noodles, pak choi, egg, spring onions and red chilli
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and chilli flakes
