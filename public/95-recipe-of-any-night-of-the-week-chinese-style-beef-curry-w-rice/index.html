The ingredients needed to make Chinese Style Beef Curry w/ Rice:
  1. Make ready 1 sirloin steak
  2. Make ready 100 ml chicken stock
  3. Take 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp hot curry powder
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp sweetner
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp turmeric
  7. Take Pinch salt
  8. Take 1 tbsp Chinese 5 spice
  9. Get 2 tbsp cornflour
  10. Take 1/2 white onion
  11. Prepare 1/2 green pepper
  12. Make ready 10 g frozen peas
  13. Make ready 1 tsp minced garlic
  14. Take 125 g basmati rice
Instructions to make Chinese Style Beef Curry w/ Rice:
  1. Slice steak into strips and set aside
  2. Add chicken stock into a bowl along with, soy sauce, curry powder, sweetener, turmeric, salt, Chinese 5 spice and cornflour and mix well
  3. Chop your onion and pepper and fry for a few minutes until softened
  4. Add your sliced steak and fry for a couple of minutes
  5. Add peas, minced garlic and sauce and simmer pan a medium heat for half an hour until sauce thickens
  6. Boil your rice in water until fluffy and then serve everything
