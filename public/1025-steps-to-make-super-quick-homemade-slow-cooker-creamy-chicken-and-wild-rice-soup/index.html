The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Soup:
  1. Get 4 1/2 cups- chicken broth
  2. Make ready 2 cups- water
  3. Take 2 - boneless skinless chicken breasts (I add 1-2 more depending on size since we love our chicken.)
  4. Get 1 package- rice-a-roni long grain and wild rice. (any brand works fine.)
  5. Get salt- to taste
  6. Get pepper- to taste
  7. Take 1/2 cup- all-purpose flour
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup- butter
  9. Make ready 2 cups- half and half
  10. Take 1 cup- diced carrots
  11. Get 1 cup- diced celery
Steps to make Slow Cooker Creamy Chicken and Wild Rice Soup:
  1. Combine broth, water, carrots, celery, chicken and rice along with the seasoning packet in a large slow cooker. (I leave the chicken in bigger pieces so they are easy to find and fish out later.) - Cook on low for 6 hours or high for 4 hours.
  2. Once done, take chicken out and shred, then place back into crockpot.
  3. In a small bowl combine, salt, pepper and flour.
  4. In a medium sized saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  5. Once butter is melted, stir in flour mixture by tablespoon to form a roux.
  6. Whisk in cream a little at a time, until fully incorporated and smooth.
  7. Stir cream mixture into crockpot, and then let it cook on low for 15 more minutes.
