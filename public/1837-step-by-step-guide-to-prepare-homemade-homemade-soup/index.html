Instructions to make homemade soup:
  1. chop vegetables, half potatoes
  2. heat stock in pressure cooker pan
  3. add vegetables to stock, pressure cook for 5 minutes, add salt and pepper to taste
  4. take out potatoes and blend/pulse veg till smooth
  5. add potatoes back to pot
