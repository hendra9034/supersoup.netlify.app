The ingredients needed to make Stuffed_zucchini_cooked_in_yogurt:
  1. Take 1 kg zucchini - medium size
  2. Prepare 200 g grounded meat
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp sevenspice
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp pine nuts
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp ghee
  7. Prepare 1 cup Egyptian rice
  8. Take 1 kg yoghurt
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp corn starch
  10. Make ready 1 tsp mint flakes
  11. Take 5 garlic cloves - crushed
  12. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp pinenuts to garnish - optional

Steps to make Stuffed_zucchini_cooked_in_yogurt:
  1. Gently wash and dry the zucchini & place in a bowl, then using a corer start by removing the inside flesh from the zucchini to create a cavity for stuffing.
  2. Wash the rice & soak for 30 minutes then strain & add grounded meat, salt & spices & mix well.
  3. Preheat ghee & fry the pinenuts until golden then add to the filling mix & mix well.
  4. Now staft by stuffing the cored zucchini till half way full.
  5. In a suitable pot, add the yoghurt & add corn starch bringing them to a boil with continuous stirring.
  6. Once boiled, add the zucchini then add crushed garlic, mint flakes & simmer over low heat for approximately 2 hours or as needed until fully cooked.
  7. Stir constantly to avoid the zucchini from sticking & make sure that the yoghurt covers all the zucchini
  8. Once cooked, remove from the heat and serve hot.
