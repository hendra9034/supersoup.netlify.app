The ingredients needed to make Slow cooker black bean chili:
  1. Make ready 2 cups soaked for 24 hrs black beans
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup red lentils
  3. Take 1 can red beans
  4. Get 1 bell pepper
  5. Get 1 cup cubed pumpkin
  6. Prepare 2 pimento peppers
  7. Take 1 large onion
  8. Prepare 1 tsp geera (cumin)*(increase if necessary)
  9. Get 1 1/2 tsp salt * (can be increased)
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp thyme
  11. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  12. Make ready 1/4 tsp chili powder
  13. Get 1/2 tsp paprika
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  15. Make ready 1 can corn* (add before or after)
  16. Prepare 3 cloves crushed garlic
  17. Make ready 2 tbsp tomato paste
  18. Prepare 2 tomatoes diced
  19. Make ready 250 ml unsalted chicken or vegetable stock
  20. Take Water to cover
