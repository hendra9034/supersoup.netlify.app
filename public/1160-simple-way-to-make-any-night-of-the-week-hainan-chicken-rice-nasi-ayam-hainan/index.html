The ingredients needed to make Hainan Chicken Rice (Nasi Ayam Hainan):
  1. Make ready 2,5 cup rice
  2. Prepare 1, 2 kg chicken (whole chicken)
  3. Make ready 400 ml Chicken broth
  4. Prepare 7 pcs garlic chop finely
  5. Take 1pcs pandan leafs
  6. Get Marinade chicken:
  7. Get 1/2 tbs Ginger juice
  8. Prepare 1 tbs salt
  9. Take 1 tbs Sand ginger powder
  10. Get Sauce:
  11. Prepare chop finely spring onion
  12. Make ready grounded ginger
  13. Prepare salt + hot oil
Steps to make Hainan Chicken Rice (Nasi Ayam Hainan):
  1. Wash the rice & drain well, fry the garlic until smell fragrant add in the rice stir fried for 3 minutes then put in Rice cooker
  2. A.After the rice put in add chicken broth & pandan leafs press button cook as usually cook rice. - - The chicken broth made from: chicken bone, ginger, spring onion boil 30minutes then add salt. - - B. marinade the chicken with:sand ginger powder, ginger juice, salt keep in the fridge for 3hours. then take out put to stem 30 minutes
  3.
