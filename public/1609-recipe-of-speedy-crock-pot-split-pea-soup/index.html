The ingredients needed to make crock pot split pea soup:
  1. Get 16 oz package split peas, soarted and rinsed
  2. Get 2 cup diced cooked ham
  3. Take 3 carrots
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup diced onions
  5. Get 2 clove fresh garlic, minced
  6. Get 1 bay leaf
  7. Prepare 12 sprigs fresh parsley
  8. Take 1 tbsp seasoned salt
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
  10. Take 1 1/2 quart water

Steps to make crock pot split pea soup:
  1. prepare ingredients by slicing carrots. chop parsley. dice onions.
  2. layer ingredients in crock pot in order listed.
  3. do not stir to mix!
  4. pour water over layers.
  5. cook on high 4-5 hours or low 8-10 hours until peas are soft.
  6. remove bay leaf; mash peas to thicken & stir to blend soup.
