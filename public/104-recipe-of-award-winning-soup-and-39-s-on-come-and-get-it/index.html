The ingredients needed to make Soup's On! Come and get it!!!:
  1. Get 1-1/2 pound stew pork
  2. Get 1/2 pound beef meatballs
  3. Make ready 2 cups cooked Jasmine rice
  4. Make ready 1 pound whole green beans
  5. Make ready 1 stalk/rib celery
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped chives
  7. Prepare 48 ounces chicken broth
  8. Make ready 3/4 stick butter
  9. Prepare 2 cups multicolored potatoes diced
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  11. Take 1 teaspoon granulated onion powder
  12. Take 1 teaspoon ground white pepper powder divided
  13. Make ready 2 teaspoon salt divided
  14. Prepare 1 stalk green onions
Steps to make Soup's On! Come and get it!!!:
  1. Melt the butter. Dice the potatoes. Thinly slice the celery. Season the pork and cut into manageable portions.
  2. Add the potatoes, celery, and pork to the butter in pot. Sauté for 10 minutes.
  3. Slice the green beans into manageable pieces. Season with some of the salt. Let rest 10 minutes. Season the pot with the remaining salt and pepper. Add the rice and give it a stir.
  4. Sir together. Slice the meatballs into quarters. Stir in and allow to cook. Stir in the chicken broth.
  5. Add the chives. Simmer covered for 20 minutes. Slice the green onions. When the green beans are at your desired doneness serve with green onions on top. I hope you enjoy!!!
