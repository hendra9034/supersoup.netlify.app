The ingredients needed to make Another Avocado Appetiser:
  1. Prepare 2 avocados
  2. Prepare 1 clove garlic, crushed
  3. Get 1 tsp paprika
  4. Get 1/2 red chilli, chopped (with or without seeds as preferred)
  5. Get 2 tsp juice from fresh lime
  6. Make ready 150 g cooked prawns
  7. Make ready Salt
  8. Prepare Ground black pepper
  9. Take 2 slices granary toast (4 crustless slices if starter)
  10. Get 1 vine tomato, sliced
  11. Take 6-8 leaves fresh coriander, chopped

Instructions to make Another Avocado Appetiser:
  1. Mash the avocados in a bowl, then stir in the garlic, paprika, chilli and lime juice.
  2. Add the prawns and gently stir. Season to taste.
  3. Gently spread the mixture over the toast and top up with tomato slices. Sprinkle the coriander on top and serve.
