The ingredients needed to make Broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. Get broccoli and cheese
  2. Prepare 1 medium onion, chopped
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  4. Take 2 each 2-10oz bags of frozen broccoli
  5. Get 2 can cream of mushroom soup
  6. Prepare 4 cup milk
  7. Take 7 oz Velveeta cheese
  8. Get 1 tbsp salt and pepper

Instructions to make Broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. Saute onion in the butter.
  2. Boil or steam the broccoli, then chop.
  3. Mix all ingredients in a large pot and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Soup is done when its all blended and broccoli is tender!
