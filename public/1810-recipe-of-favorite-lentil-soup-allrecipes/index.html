Steps to make Lentil Soup Allrecipes:
  1. In large soup pot, heat oil over med. heat. Add onions, carrots, and celery; cook and stir until onion is tender.
  2. stir in garlic, bay leaf, and basil. cook for two minutes.
  3. Stir in lentils, and add water and tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for at least 1 hour.
  4. add potato and sausage for last 30 minutes.
  5. When ready to serve, stir in spinach, and cook until it wilts. Stir in vinegar and season to taste with salt and pepper, and moore vinegar if desired
  6. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
