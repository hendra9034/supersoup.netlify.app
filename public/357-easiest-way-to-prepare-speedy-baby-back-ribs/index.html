The ingredients needed to make Baby Back Ribs 烤豬肋骨:
  1. Get Full rack pork ribs
  2. Get Paprika
  3. Take Black pepper
  4. Make ready Cayenne pepper
  5. Prepare Garlic power
  6. Make ready Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce, or your favourite pick
Steps to make Baby Back Ribs 烤豬肋骨:
  1. Mix 0.5 teaspoon Paprika, 1 teaspoon salt, 0.5 teaspoon black pepper, 0.5 teaspoon cayenne pepper and 2 teaspoon garlic powder for making a powder mixture
  2. Prepare an oven tray with aluminium foil
  3. Pre-heat the oven to 130°C
  4. Wash and dry the ribs
  5. Slightly slice the membranes of the ribs
  6. Rub the ribs with powder mixture
  7. Place the ribs with bone side up and wrap the foil
  8. Bake the ribs for 2 hours
  9. Take the ribs out and pre-heat the oven to 200°C
  10. Brush BBQ sauce on both sides and bake for 15 minutes
  11. Brush the BBQ sauce again before serving
