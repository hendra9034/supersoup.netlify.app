The ingredients needed to make comforting chicken noodle soup:
  1. Prepare 2 quart water
  2. Make ready 8 cubes chicken bullion
  3. Make ready 6 1/2 cup uncooked wide egg noodles
  4. Prepare 2 can cream of chicken soup (10 3/4 oz)
  5. Make ready 3 cup cubed cooked chicken
  6. Make ready 1 cup sour cream

Instructions to make comforting chicken noodle soup:
  1. in large saucepan, bring water and bullion to a boil
  2. add noodles, cook uncovered until tender about 10 minutes
  3. do not drain
  4. add soup & chicken
  5. heat through
  6. remove from heat
  7. stir in sour cream
