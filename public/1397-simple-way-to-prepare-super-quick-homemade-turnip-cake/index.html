The ingredients needed to make Turnip Cake:
  1. Make ready 900 g daikon
  2. Prepare 2 Chinese sausages
  3. Get 4 shiitake mushrooms
  4. Get 70 g rice flour
  5. Prepare 70 g corn starch
  6. Take 200 ml daikon + mushroom water
  7. Get 2 cloves minced garlic
  8. Prepare Seasoning (ingredient mix)
  9. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  10. Make ready 1 tsp soy sauce
  11. Get 1/2 tbsp cooking wine
  12. Take (For the daikon)
  13. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  14. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  15. Prepare 1/2 tsp white pepper
Instructions to make Turnip Cake:
  1. Soak the dried mushrooms for 3 hours. Squeeze out water, remove the stems and cut into small dices
  2. Steam the Chinese sausages for 10 minutes and cut into small dices
  3. Peel and grate half of the daikon and cut the other half into thick slices(optional). Squeeze water out from the grated daikons and reserve for later use
  4. Add sausages to a cold wok and let the fat render. Add the mushrooms and seasoning, then sauté for a few minutes. Remove from heat
  5. In the same pan, sauté garlic until fragrant. Add the daikons, lid on and cook over medium heat for around 10 minutes or until soften
  6. Meanwhile, mix rice flour, corn starch with water (use the residual water from the daikon and from soaking the mushrooms). Add water if the liquid doesn’t make up 200 ml
  7. Season the daikons, add the ingredient mix and sauté for a few minutes 1. Lower heat then slowly pour in the batter over a strainer to remove any lumps. Stir and cook for a few minutes 1. Grease a baking pan/ aluminium tray with oil then pour the batter in. Make sure the batter is pressed into the pan and the surface is flat
  8. Cover with cling film/ tinfoil and steam for 45-60 minutes. Refrigerate overnight and serve pan-fried or steamed
