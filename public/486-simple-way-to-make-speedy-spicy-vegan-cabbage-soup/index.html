The ingredients needed to make Spicy Vegan Cabbage Soup:
  1. Take cabbage
  2. Prepare mini king oyster mushroom
  3. Get cherry tomato
  4. Make ready onion
  5. Take coriander leaf
  6. Prepare water
  7. Take lightly crush white peppercone in a tea bag
  8. Take deseeded chili pepper or bird'eye chili
  9. Prepare green bean
  10. Take spring onion
  11. Make ready vegetable bouillon (optional additional add on )
  12. Get each of orange and red jalapeños
  13. Take seasoning
  14. Take light soy sauce
  15. Get salt
Instructions to make Spicy Vegan Cabbage Soup:
  1. PREASURE COOKER OPTION OR
  2. put all into a preasure cooker on high for 10 minute
  3. STOVE COOKING OPTION OR
  4. put all in a pot bring it to a boil then simmer 35 minutes
  5. ADD SEASONING
  6. add seasoning then simmer another 5 minutes
  7. SERVING OPTION
  8. spicy vegan cabbage soup or seive it and use as spicy vegan stock
