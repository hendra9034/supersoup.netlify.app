The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Potato Soup (V/GF):
  1. Prepare 1 yellow onion, diced
  2. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  4. Get 8 cups cubed yukon potatoes
  5. Make ready 1 bunch dino kale, chopped
  6. Take 4 ribs celery, diced
  7. Take 4 carrots, diced
  8. Take 6 cups veggie broth
  9. Take 2 tsp oregano
  10. Prepare 1 tsp parsley
  11. Take 1 tsp tyme
  12. Prepare 1 tsp sea salt
Instructions to make Instant Pot Potato Soup (V/GF):
  1. Saute in instant pot onions, garlic and olive oil until golden brown.
  2. Add all vegetables, broth and spices.
  3. Seal instant pot and select soup mode. Quick release after timer goes off.
