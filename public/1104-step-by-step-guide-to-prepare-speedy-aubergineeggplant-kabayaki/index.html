  7. Prepare 2 tbsp. Sugar

Steps to make Aubergine(eggplant) KABAYAKI:
  1. Cook rice
  2. Put sake, mirin, soy sauce and sugar into pan and heat low until thickened.
  3. Cut off the calyx of the aubergine and peel it.
  4. Wrap it and heat in the microwave for 2 minutes.
  5. After cooling a little, cut it into thin rectangular shapes.Sprinkle with potato starch.
  6. Put oil in the pan and grill aubergine until become brown both sides.
  7. Put half of sauce into the pan and cover well.
  8. Serve rice in a bowl and put aubergine and the rest of the sauce on top.
