The ingredients needed to make Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Soup:
  1. Get olive oil
  2. Make ready chopped sweet onion
  3. Make ready large clove chopped garlic
  4. Get dried basil
  5. Get dried oregano
  6. Take chicken broth
  7. Prepare can diced tomatoes
  8. Prepare medium potatoes chopped
  9. Take diced carrots
  10. Prepare diced celery
  11. Prepare grilled chicken breast chopped
  12. Prepare chopped green beans
Steps to make Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Soup:
  1. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil in a large saucepan.
  2. Add basil, oregano and chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add tomatoes, potatoes, carrots, celery and chicken breast. Lower heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add green beans and simmer for another 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
