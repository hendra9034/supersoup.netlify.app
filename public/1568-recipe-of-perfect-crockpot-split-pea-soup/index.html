The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Split Pea Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 cup dried green split peas
  2. Get 1 bay leaf
  3. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 2 carrots, sliced
  5. Get 2 stalks of celery, sliced
  6. Take 1 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  8. Prepare 2 large ham hocks
  9. Make ready 2 quart water
  10. Get 1 cup mashed potatoes, opt
Steps to make Crockpot Split Pea Soup:
  1. Wash and sort split peas.
  2. Add everything to crockpot except for mashed potatoes.
  3. Cook on low for six to eight hours.
  4. When veggies are tender, take out ham hocks, remove meat, and add meat to soup. Add mashed potatoes to soup to thicken. If you do not have mashed potatoes, you can puree two cups of soup when warm.
