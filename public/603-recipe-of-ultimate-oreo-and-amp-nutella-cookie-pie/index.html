The ingredients needed to make Oreo & Nutella Cookie Pie:
  1. Take 200 g unsalted butter softened
  2. Prepare 250 g light brown sugar
  3. Prepare 2 egg yolks
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 320 g plain flour
  6. Prepare 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  7. Prepare 150 g white chocolate broken into pieces
  8. Make ready 120 g oreos broken
  9. Take For the filling:-
  10. Take 300 g Nutella
  11. Prepare 11 oreos
  12. Take 300 g cookies and cream spread
Steps to make Oreo & Nutella Cookie Pie:
  1. For the dough, mix together all of the top listed ingredients into a dough mix, if a bit sticky just add more flour.
  2. Tin size 8"(9" also fine) place grease proof paper in tin. Then mould 2/3 of the dough mix in a thin layer on the base and around the sides of the tin.
  3. Then for the filling, break up oreo biscuits and spread on base and then spread nutella and cookies and cream spread inside the pie.
  4. With the remaining dough mix make a lid for the top of the pie.
  5. Heat oven to 160oc and cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Allow to cool fully in tin.
