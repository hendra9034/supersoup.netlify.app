The ingredients needed to make Winter Soup with Chicken & Cheese Tortellini:
  1. Take 2 Chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 5 cups Chicken Broth
  3. Take 1 Carrot thinly sliced
  4. Get 2 cups Spinach
  5. Make ready 1 package Cheese Tortellini
  6. Prepare Salt and Pepper
  7. Prepare Parmesan Cheese for topping

Instructions to make Winter Soup with Chicken & Cheese Tortellini:
  1. Chop chicken breasts into small pieces and boil for 15 minutes.
  2. Drain and mince into small pieces and set aside to cool.
  3. Wash Spinach thoroughly and drain.
  4. Add Tortellini and Spinach into boiling water.
  5. In a large pot add water the chicken broth, carrots, and boil until tortellini is cooked. Add in salt and pepper and chicken boil for 10 minutes.
