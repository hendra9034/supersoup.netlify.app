The ingredients needed to make Pork chop with Miso and Apple Jam Sauce:
  1. Get 200 g pork chop
  2. Make ready 1 tsp miso
  3. Take 3 tsp apple jam
  4. Get To taste little salt and pepper
  5. Prepare cooking oil
Instructions to make Pork chop with Miso and Apple Jam Sauce:
  1. Sprinkle pork chop with a little salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat oil in frying pan and brown pork chop both sides.
  3. Make sauce, mix I tsp miso with 3 tsp apple jam.
  4. After cooking, remove pork chop from heat and serve immediately, coat with miso and apple jam sauce. - Salad garnish to taste!
