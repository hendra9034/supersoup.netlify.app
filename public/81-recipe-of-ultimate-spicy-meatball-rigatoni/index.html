The ingredients needed to make Spicy meatball rigatoni:
  1. Prepare 240 g dried rigatoni
  2. Make ready 125 g lean beef mince
  3. Prepare 125 g pork mince
  4. Make ready 2 tsp chilli flakes
  5. Get 20 g breadcrumbs
  6. Prepare 2 tsp diced oregano
  7. Get 400 g tin chopped tomatoes
  8. Prepare 1 tin water
  9. Get 4 basil leaves
  10. Take 30 g grated parmesan
  11. Get Chopped parsley to serve

Instructions to make Spicy meatball rigatoni:
  1. Mix your beef and pork mince with oregano, chilli flakes, breadcrumbs and shape into golf sized balls
  2. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 200c
  3. Get your sauce going by adding chopped tomatoes, water from the same tin of chopped tomatoes, and basil leaves and simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Boil a pan of salted water and cook your rigatoni for 8 minutes
  5. Add your cooked meatballs to your sauce and add your pasta and stir. Leave for 10 minutes
  6. Serve everything with grated parmesan and freshly diced parsley
