The ingredients needed to make Marinated Pork Chops on BBQ:
  1. Take Pork Chops
  2. Prepare Sausages
  3. Prepare Sunflower Oil
  4. Take Oregano
  5. Prepare Red/White Wine
  6. Take Garlic Clove & Granules
  7. Make ready Salt & Pepper
  8. Take Potatoes
  9. Get Thyme
  10. Get Vinegar
  11. Prepare Honey
  12. Make ready Cucumber
  13. Make ready Baby Tomatoes
  14. Take Lettuce
  15. Get Beetroot
Steps to make Marinated Pork Chops on BBQ:
  1. Season pork chops with oil, oregano, wine, garlic, salt, pepper
  2. Poke sausages with a fork
  3. Cook pork chops and sausages on BBQ for 15-20min
  4. Chop potatoes, cook in boiling water for 10min, drain, transfer to dish and season with oil, salt, pepper, vinegar, thyme, finely chopped cucumber (optional: drizzle some salad cream). Refrigerate only after potatoes have cooled down
  5. Chop fresh lettuce, baby tomatoes, cucumber, beetroot and mix with vinegar and honey
