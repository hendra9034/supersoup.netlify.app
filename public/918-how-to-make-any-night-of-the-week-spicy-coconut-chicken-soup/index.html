The ingredients needed to make spicy coconut chicken soup:
  1. Get 200 grams vermicelli noodles
  2. Get 2 tsp peanut oil
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp red curry paste
  4. Make ready 500 chicken thigh fillets, sliced
  5. Get 2 1/2 cup water
  6. Take 400 mil light coconut milk
  7. Make ready 1 Broccoli florets
  8. Get 1 cup bean sprouts
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves
  10. Take 1 sliced red chilli
  11. Make ready 1 lime which is to serve

Steps to make spicy coconut chicken soup:
  1. Place noodles in a heatproof bowl. Cover with boiling water. Soak for 5 min until tender. Drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large saucepan on medium. Add curry paste and cook, steering, for 1 min until fragrant. Add chicken and cook for 4 to 5 min.
  3. Blend in water and coconut milk. Simmer for 5 min. Add broccoli (or baby bok choy or choy sum, trimmed, quartered, if you prefer)and simmer for another 2 to 3 min
  4. divide noodles among serving bowls. Ladle soup over. Top with beansprouts, coriander leaves and Chilli.serve with lime wedges
