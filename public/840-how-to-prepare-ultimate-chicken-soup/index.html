  1. Make ready 1 chicken carcass (leftover from a roast, if not much is leftover or the carcass is small freeze and use 2-3)
  2. Prepare 2 onions (no need to peel just halved)
  3. Take 2 bay leaf
  4. Make ready 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  5. Make ready 6 peppercorns
  6. Make ready 1 leftover roast chicken
  7. Prepare 2 handful broken vermicelli

  9. Prepare 1 boiling water
  10. Prepare salt
  11. Get 4 garlic cloves
