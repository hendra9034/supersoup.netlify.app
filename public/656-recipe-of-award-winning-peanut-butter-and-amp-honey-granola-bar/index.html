The ingredients needed to make Peanut butter & honey granola bar:
  1. Prepare 200 g peanut butter
  2. Make ready 200 g organic honey
  3. Make ready 100 g oats
  4. Take 200 g fruit granola
  5. Make ready Chocolate for drizzle top (optional)

Instructions to make Peanut butter & honey granola bar:
  1. Add peanut butter and honey to a microwave bowl and microwave 3 minutes
  2. Add granola and oats to a mixing bowl (extra nuts or dry fruit can be added)
  3. Mix honey and peanut butter to granola and oats, mix well.
  4. Line a baking tray with cling film and add mixture, spread evenly. Apply melted chocolate drizzle if you fancy to tip
  5. Put baking tray in fridge to set, cut and enjoy with a cuppa
