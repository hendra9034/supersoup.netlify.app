  1. Get 2/3 Cup Mozzarella Cheese
  2. Take 1/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  3. Take 1 & 1/3 Cups Tapioca Flour
  4. Get 1/2 Tsp Salt
  5. Prepare 2/3 Cup Milk
  6. Make ready 1/3 Cup Oil

Steps to make Brazilian Cheese Bread (Pão de Queijo):
  1. Grate 2/3 Cup Mozzarella Cheese (85g)
  2. Grate 1/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese (85g)
  3. Place the Cheeses aside
  4. Pour 1 & 1/3 Cup Tapioca Flour into a mixing bowl (170g)
  5. Pour 1/2 Tsp Salt into the mixing bowl
  6. Set the Tapioca Flour aside
  7. Pour 2/3 Cup Milk into a sauce pan (160ml)
  8. Pour 1/3 Cup of Oil into the sauce pan (80ml)
  9. Bring it to a boil
  10. Once its boiling, pour it into the Tapioca Flour mix and whisk until combined
  11. Crack one egg into the final mixture and whisk until combined
  12. Pour in the Grated Cheeses and whisk until combined
  13. Shape it into balls and place it on baking paper on a tray
  14. Bake at 200 Degrees Celsius for around 15-20 Minutes until lightly golden brown and puffed up
