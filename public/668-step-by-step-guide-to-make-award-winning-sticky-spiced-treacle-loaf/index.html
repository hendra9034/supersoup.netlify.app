The ingredients needed to make Sticky spiced treacle loaf:
  1. Get 150 g butte
  2. Take 175 g black treacle
  3. Prepare 150 g brown soft sugar
  4. Prepare 250 g plain flour or whole meal flour
  5. Make ready 1 tsp soda
  6. Take 1 tbsp mixed spice
  7. Prepare 2 eggs, beaten
  8. Make ready 75 ml whole milk
  9. Make ready 3 pieces stem ginger in syrup. Chopped plus 4tbsp syrup

Instructions to make Sticky spiced treacle loaf:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180c and prep 900g loaf tin.
  2. Place the butter,treacle and sugar in small saucepan and gently heat to melt.
  3. Mix flour,soda and mixed spice into a bowl.make a well in the centre and add the eggs,then incorporate the flour using a wooden spoon and after slowly add the treacle mixture to make smooth batter.
  4. Add the milk and chopped stem ginger and 2tbsp syrup then pour into the prepared loaf tin.
