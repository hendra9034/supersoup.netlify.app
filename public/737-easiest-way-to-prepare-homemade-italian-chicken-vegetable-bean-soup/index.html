The ingredients needed to make Italian Chicken Vegetable Bean Soup:
  1. Prepare olive oil
  2. Take garlic – chopped
  3. Make ready black pepper
  4. Make ready thyme
  5. Make ready fennel seed
  6. Get large onion – diced 1/4 inch
  7. Get large red bell pepper – diced 1/4 inch
  8. Get large carrots – diced 1/4 inch
  9. Make ready celery – diced 1/4 inch
  10. Take flour
  11. Make ready chicken stock
  12. Make ready diced tomatoes
  13. Prepare fresh rosemary finely chopped
  14. Make ready cooked chicken, diced 1/2 inch (rotisserie chicken is real good)
  15. Take white beans, cooked or canned,drained
  16. Take fresh spinach (take off the stems) or 1/2 cup frozen spinach
  17. Get salt
Steps to make Italian Chicken Vegetable Bean Soup:
  1. In a large stock pot add the oil and lightly brown the garlic then add the vegetables and turn to lower heat.
  2. Sweat the vegetables and spices over a medium low heat for 8 minutes covered with a lid.
  3. Dust with the flour and mix in to make the compound roux.
  4. Add the chicken stock, tomatoes, rosemary and diced chicken. Bring to a boil and turn down to a simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the bean and simmer 10 more minutes, then add the spinach.
  6. Adjust seasoning and serve.
