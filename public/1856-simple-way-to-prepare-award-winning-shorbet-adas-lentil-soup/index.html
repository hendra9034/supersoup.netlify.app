The ingredients needed to make Shorbet adas (lentil soup):
  1. Take 250 gm yello lentils
  2. Get 80 gm rice
  3. Get 1 stick celery
  4. Make ready 1 carrot
  5. Make ready 1 big onion
  6. Prepare Turmeric, red chilli powder and salt for taste
  7. Take 3 green chillies

Steps to make Shorbet adas (lentil soup):
