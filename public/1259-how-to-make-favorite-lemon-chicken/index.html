The ingredients needed to make Lemon Chicken:
  1. Make ready 600 g Chicken Thigh Fillets
  2. Get 1 clove Garlic *grated
  3. Make ready 1 small piece Ginger *grated
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon White Pepper
  6. Get 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  7. Make ready 1 Egg *whisked
  8. Take 1 tablespoon Plain Flour
  9. Take 3 tablespoons Potato Starch OR Corn Starch
  10. Take Oil for frying
  11. Make ready Lemon Sauce
  12. Prepare 3 tablespoons Lemon Juice *You should get 3 tablespoons juice from 1 Lemon
  13. Get 3 tablespoons Chicken Stock OR Water
  14. Make ready 2-3 tablespoons Sugar *alter the amount to suit your taste
  15. Take 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  16. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Soy Sauce
  17. Get 1 & 1/2 teaspoons Potato Starch OR Corn Starch
Instructions to make Lemon Chicken:
  1. Cut Chicken into chunky bite-size (about 4cm) pieces. Combine Garlic, Ginger, Salt, White Pepper, Soy Sauce in a bowl, add Chicken pieces, massage, and marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Add Egg and mix well, then add Plain Flour and Potato Starch, and mix well.
  3. Heat the Oil to 170-180ºC and deep fry the chicken. *Note: For the crunchier result, I often fry them twice. After I finished frying all Chicken pieces, fry them again. The second frying won’t take long.
  4. Place all the Lemon Sauce ingredients in a small saucepan. Alter the amount of Sugar depending on the sourness of the Lemon Juice. Heat the sauce over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens.
  5. Arrange the Chicken pieces on a serving plate, pour the sauce all over, and enjoy.
