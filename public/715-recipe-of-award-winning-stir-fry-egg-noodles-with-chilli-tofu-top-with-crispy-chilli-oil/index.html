Steps to make Stir Fry Egg Noodles With Chilli Tofu Top With Crispy Chilli Oil üçú üå∂:
  1. Blanched egg noodles in hot water for 4-8 minutes. Once noodles softened, drained and place in a plate or bowl.
  2. On a medium heat pan or wok, add vegetable cooking oil then garlic, fry for a min until garlic golden.
  3. Add all the vegetable in and quick stir fry. Add tofu and fry for another 1-2 minutes.
  4. Mix the sauce base together in a mixing bowl, pour them on to the pan or wok mix well. Add noodles in and stir well.
