The ingredients needed to make Spinach Soup:
  1. Make ready 6 cup spinach
  2. Get 1 can parsnip greens
  3. Take 1 can fire roasted tomatoes
  4. Prepare 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  5. Make ready 1 can white baby potatoes
  6. Prepare 1 cup lentils, cooked
  7. Make ready 1 cup tofu, cubed
  8. Get 1 onion, chopped
  9. Prepare 6 tbsp garlic, minced
  10. Get 1 lemon, juiced
  11. Prepare 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
