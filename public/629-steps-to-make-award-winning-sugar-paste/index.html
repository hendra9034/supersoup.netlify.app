The ingredients needed to make Sugar paste:
  1. Take 60 ml cold water
  2. Prepare 20 g powdered gelatine
  3. Prepare 125 ml liquid glucose
  4. Take 15 g glycerine
  5. Prepare 1 kg icing sugar

Steps to make Sugar paste:
  1. Soak the gelatine in a bowl with the water
  2. Gently melt the gelatine over a bain marie
  3. Add the glucose and glycerine and mix until smooth and runny allow to cool
  4. Slowly add this to the icing sugar whist mixing all the time
  5. Work the dough and wrap it and rest it for a short time
  6. Dust the work surface with icing sugar and use to make flowers or to cover a cake
