The ingredients needed to make lentil soup:
  1. Prepare 1 cup orange lentil
  2. Get 1 head onion chopped
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  4. Take 2 tsp salt
  5. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  6. Get 2 large potatoes cubed
  7. Make ready 2 large carrots cubed
  8. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
  9. Prepare 5 cup chicken stock

Steps to make lentil soup:
  1. sweat the chopped onions in the olive oil after adding salt until translucent
  2. add the potatoes, carrots, chicken stalk and lentils and leave to simmer until lentils are well done and fork goes through vegetables easily
  3. add cumin and pepper
  4. then use a hand mixer to blend it until no lumps of vegetables are left and soup is smooth …serve hot with a pinch of parsley if u want
