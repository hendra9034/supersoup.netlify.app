The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Venison Veg Soup:
  1. Get 1 lb. (4) Deer Chop Loin Medallions
  2. Take 1 large onion, chopped
  3. Make ready 2 whole carrots, chopped disks
  4. Take 1 whole celery heart, leaves and all, plus one stalk. Chopped
  5. Take 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  6. Prepare 3 garlic cloves, pressed
  7. Get 1 lb Frozen Mixed Veg (P's & C's, corn, green and lima beans.)
  8. Prepare 1 can (14.5 oz) petitedice tomatoes
  9. Get 32 oz box of Beef Stock
  10. Take 40 oz. V-8
  11. Take 1/2 tsp, each, ground sea salt and pepper
Instructions to make Crockpot Venison Veg Soup:
  1. Lay the 4 venison chops in the bottom of the crock-pot.
  2. Add all the rest of the ingredients and stir, not disturbing the medallions.
  3. Set on high crockpot setting. Let go for 8 hours, and then indefinitely on low.
  4. Remove and shred the venison, add back.
