The ingredients needed to make Leek and Potato Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 leeks trimmed and cut in thin rounds
  2. Prepare 1 onion sliced
  3. Make ready 4 medium potatoes peeled and cubed
  4. Make ready 2 celery stalks
  5. Take 4 cups chicken stock or water
  6. Prepare 1 knorrox cube
  7. Get 1/4 cup milk
  8. Take 1/4 cup fresh cream
  9. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  10. Make ready to taste Salt
  11. Prepare 1-2 green chillies (optional)
  12. Prepare 1 tbs butter
  13. Make ready 1 tbs olive oil
  14. Prepare 1 clove garlic chopped
  15. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  16. Take 1/2 tsp thyme (optional)
  17. Take 1 tsp lemon juice
Instructions to make Leek and Potato Soup:
  1. Heat butter and oil in pot, then add chopped leeks, sliced onion and garlic
  2. Allow to soften then add potatoes and celery stalks
  3. Stir together & add the bay leaf, thyme, green chillies and black pepper
  4. Next add the stock and salt to taste
  5. Turn the heat down to low
  6. Allow to simmer for 20 minutes or until the potatoes are soft
  7. Remove bay leaf and celery stalks and discard
  8. Blend the mixture to a smooth consistency
  9. Put the soup back in the pot, add the cream and milk and adjust the seasoning
  10. You can add the lemon juice to perk the flavour
  11. Simmer for a few minutes to reach the consistency of your preference
  12. Serve with warm toasted bread or croutons
