The ingredients needed to make Seaweed Chicken Soup/Sop Ayam Rumput Laut:
  1. Take 200 gr boneless chicken
  2. Make ready 3 garlic
  3. Take 2 packets seaweed
  4. Take 1 tofu
  5. Take 1.5 tsp salt
  6. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Prepare 150 gr beans vermicelli/beans thread
  8. Prepare 1 carrot
  9. Get 7 cups of water
