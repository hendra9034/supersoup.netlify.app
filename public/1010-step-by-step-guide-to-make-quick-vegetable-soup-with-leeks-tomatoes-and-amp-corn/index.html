The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Soup with Leeks, Tomatoes & Corn:
  1. Prepare 1.5 onions, quartered
  2. Take 1 stalk leek, chopped
  3. Get Olive oil
  4. Take 5-6 ripe tomatoes, quartered
  5. Get 2 large potatoes, peeled + cubed
  6. Take 2.5 corn on cob, husked + halved
  7. Prepare 10 cups/ + water
  8. Prepare Salt to taste
  9. Take 1-2 bay leaves
