The ingredients needed to make Winter Lentil Soup:
  1. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Prepare 4 leeks sliced in half moons
  3. Prepare 4 large tomatoes
  4. Prepare 2 Sweet potatoes peeled and cut
  5. Take 1 bunch kale chopped
  6. Get 1/2 cup Brown lentils
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  8. Prepare 1 salt and pepper
