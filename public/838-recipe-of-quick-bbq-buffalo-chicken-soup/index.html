The ingredients needed to make BBQ Buffalo Chicken Soup:
  1. Take 1 Cooked Rotisserie Chicken
  2. Prepare 1 can Family Size - Cream of Chicken Soup
  3. Take 6 cup Milk
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup Hot Sauce
  5. Make ready 1 cup BBQ Sauce
  6. Take 1 12 oz. bag of Medium Egg Noodles (optional)
  7. Get 8 oz Sour Cream
Instructions to make BBQ Buffalo Chicken Soup:
  1. Boil egg noodles.
  2. Remove meat from chicken.
  3. Mix milk, chicken, soups, and sauces in a large pot and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add sour cream and stir until completely dissolved.
  5. Strain egg noodles and add to the soup, then serve.
