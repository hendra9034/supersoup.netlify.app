The ingredients needed to make Cheesy spinach cabbage gnocchi soup:
  1. Prepare 1 pack potato gnocchi (store brought)
  2. Make ready 1 bowl baby spinach (shredded)
  3. Make ready 1 bowl red cabbage (sliced)
  4. Take 1 cup whole milk
  5. Make ready 5-6 garlic pods (neatly chopped)
  6. Make ready 1/2 stick butter
  7. Take 2 tbsp italian seasoning
  8. Take 2 tbsp crushed peppercorns
  9. Take 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
