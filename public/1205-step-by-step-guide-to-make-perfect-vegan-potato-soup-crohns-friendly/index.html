The ingredients needed to make Vegan Potato Soup (Crohns-friendly):
  1. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Get 1 onion diced
  3. Make ready 2 medium carrots
  4. Make ready 6 potatoes
  5. Take Pinch nutmeg
  6. Make ready Salt and pepper to taste
  7. Get 4 cups vegetable broth
  8. Take 2 bay leaves
  9. Take 3 cloves garlic
Steps to make Vegan Potato Soup (Crohns-friendly):
  1. Heat oil in a large pot and add the onion. Saute for 3 minutes
  2. Add the garlic, carrots, potatoes and all spices and saute for another minute
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bay leaves and bring g soup to a boil
  4. Let simmer for 20 minutes
  5. Take out bay leaves and blend with an immersion blender until smooth. You have have crohns, you may want to strain it. (Don't worry it will still be thick enough)
  6. Serve!
