  6. Get Seasoning
  7. Take 1.5 Tablespoons Chilli bean sauce (Toban Dian)

  9. Get 1 teaspoon Ground white pepper

Instructions to make Twice-cooked pork:
  1. Cook the pork belly within water and some rice wine until the pork is completely cooked. Also boil the dried tofu and cabbage until they turn slightly softer.
  2. Get a frying pan and add 1 teaspoon of cooking oil. Fry the pork belly slices until they get coloured.
  3. Add in the dried tofu slices.
  4. Push the pork and tofu aside and then add in the chilli bean sauce. Mix them well.
  5. Add in garlic slices and the spring onion (the white part).
  6. Add in the cabbage and other seasoning ingredients.
  7. Finally sprinkle the spring onion (the green part) and mix them properly. Enjoy!!
