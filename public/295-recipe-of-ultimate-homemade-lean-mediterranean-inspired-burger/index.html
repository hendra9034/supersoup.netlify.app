The ingredients needed to make Homemade Lean Mediterranean Inspired Burger:
  1. Make ready Burger patty
  2. Get 500 g 3% minced beef
  3. Prepare 1 white onion
  4. Take 1 clove garlic
  5. Take 1 tbsp dried thyme
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  7. Take Salt
  8. Get Pepper
  9. Prepare Oil
  10. Get Mayonnaise
  11. Take 3 tbsp low fat mayonnaise
  12. Make ready 6 sunblused tomatoes
  13. Make ready 1 small bunch of fresh basil
  14. Take Salt
  15. Get Pepper
  16. Get Toppings
  17. Take 1 ball of low fat mozzarella
  18. Prepare 2 fresh vine tomatoes
  19. Make ready 1 cup shredded lettuce
  20. Prepare 4 brioche buns
Steps to make Homemade Lean Mediterranean Inspired Burger:
  1. To make the burger, dice your onion and garlic and gently soften in a pan with a little oil. Allow to cool
  2. Add your minced beef, softened onions/garlic, thyme, olive oil to a mixing bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Combine thoroughly to form a firm mixture.
  4. To form your burger patty’s, separate the mixture into 4 portions (Around 130g) and roll into a ball. Press firmly between your hands to form your patty.
  5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes
  6. For the mayonnaise naise, finely chop your basil and tomatoes and add to the mayonnaise with a little seasoning to taste.
  7. Cook your burgers in a hot griddle pan, occasionally turning to form a criss cross effect. Once cooked, allow to rest and top with the mozzarella.
  8. Toast your bun and begin to construct your burger with as much or as little filling you desire.
