The ingredients needed to make Mince Teriyaki:
  1. Take 300 grams Beef Mince
  2. Make ready 1 Union, chopped
  3. Make ready 3 Garlic, chopped
  4. Prepare 1 Inch Ginger, sliced
  5. Make ready 1 Tbsp Sweet Soy Sauce
  6. Make ready 1 Tsp Shaoxing Wine / Chinese Cooking Wine
  7. Prepare as needed Spring onion
  8. Prepare Pinch salt
  9. Take Pinch pepper
  10. Get Pinch sugar

Steps to make Mince Teriyaki:
  1. Brown the chopped union and ginger
  2. One you smell the flavor aroma from the union and ginger, put the mince into the pan
  3. When you see the mince getting brown, put the chopped garlic in and the rest of the ingredients
  4. Taste it and add what’s needed (salt or sugar or pepper), the last put the spring onion over.
  5. Dish up into hot steam white rice, I use sushi rice.
