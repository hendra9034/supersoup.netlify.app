The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Chorizo Jambalaya:
  1. Get 1 onion
  2. Get 1 celery stick
  3. Get 1 Bell pepper
  4. Take 4 Chicken thighs
  5. Get Handful Chorizo
  6. Prepare Salt and pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tsp smoked paprika
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp thyme
  10. Get 1 tsp oregano
  11. Prepare 2 clove minced garlic
  12. Prepare 1 Tbsp tomato puree
  13. Get 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  14. Get 1 can chopped tomatoes
  15. Prepare 450 ml chicken stock
  16. Get 2 cups long grain rice
Steps to make Chicken and Chorizo Jambalaya:
  1. Finely chop onions and celery. Dice peppers and put all to the side.
  2. Cut and season the chicken and cut the chorizo. Add chicken to a large dutch oven pan with olive oil and leave for 6-8 mins on a medium high heat, add the chorizo about a quarter way through. After 6-8 mins Chicken may not be fully cooked through at this point and this is ok - it'll be white. Remove them and set aside ensuring you keep the oil.
  3. Add a tbsp more olive oil to the pan and add the vegetables to the pan for 6 mins to soften.
  4. Then add meat to the mixture again and add all the powder spices. Add the minced garlic and Worcestershire sauce. Then add the tomato puree and leave for a few mins before adding the canned chopped tomato.
  5. Add the chicken stock and mix. Add the rice, mix for a bit then cover and leave for 20 minutes.
