The ingredients needed to make Scalloped Potatoes with a twist:
  1. Get 5 Golden Russet potatoes peeled and thinly sliced
  2. Prepare 5 tbs butter (hold 1 tbs for onions)
  3. Make ready 1 small onion sliced like you would do onion rings but thin
  4. Take 4 tbs flour
  5. Get 4 tbs mushroom soup
  6. Prepare 2 1/2 cups shredded blended cheese
Steps to make Scalloped Potatoes with a twist:
  1. In a saute pan, cook onion in butter till they start to separate and are tender and translucent and set side in a bowl. Season to taste. Place half the sliced potatoes in a greased 1 quart dish.
  2. Add butter in a pan until melted, now add flour, milk and mushroom soup stiring frequently.
  3. Cover with half layer the sauce, half layer of onions and 1 cup of cheese. Repeat layers.
  4. Top with remaining 1 1/2 cup of cheese.
  5. Bake covered at 425°F for 35 minutes. Uncover and bake 30 minutes more or till potatoes are tender. Let stand 5 minutes. Serves 4.
  6. Optional: On the last layer, after the onions, I cut up 2 tbs of extra butter into quarter pieces and place on top of the onions and then add cheese.
