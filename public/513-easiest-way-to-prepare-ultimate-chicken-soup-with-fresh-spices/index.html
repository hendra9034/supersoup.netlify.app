The ingredients needed to make Chicken Soup with Fresh Spices:
  1. Prepare 1 union, (quartered)
  2. Take 1 tomato (quartered)
  3. Prepare 1 carrot (Cut into cubes)
  4. Make ready 1 Potato (cut into cubes)
  5. Make ready 2 bunch Chinese Celery
  6. Get 5 Chicken Thigh
  7. Take 1 inch ginger
  8. Get 3 cloves
  9. Prepare 1/4 nutmeg
  10. Take 1 cardamon pod
  11. Take 2 cm cinnamon sticks
  12. Make ready 1/4 tsp white pepper
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  14. Take 1 bunch Coriander (for garnish)
Steps to make Chicken Soup with Fresh Spices:
  1. Heat cooking oil in a stockpot. Stir-fry chicken first for two mins then add onion, carrots, potatoes and close the lid. Cook for another 5 mins till slightly brown.
  2. Add 1 liter water into the stockpot
  3. Combine all the spices into a muslin bag or a tea-strainer and drop into the stockpot.
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Let boil and add celery and tomato into the soup. Scoop up the floating stuff on the soup
  6. Simmer for 15 minutes or till the vegetables soften. Remove the spices
