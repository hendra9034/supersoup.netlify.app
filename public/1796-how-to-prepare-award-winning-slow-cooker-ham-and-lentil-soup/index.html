The ingredients needed to make Slow-cooker Ham and Lentil soup:
  1. Make ready 1 lb pre-cooked spiral ham
  2. Prepare 1/2 lb dried split green peas
  3. Get 1/2 lb dried lentils
  4. Prepare 1 cup carrots, finely shredded
  5. Prepare 4 clove garlic
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbsp thyme
  7. Make ready 1 tsp basil
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp rosemary
  9. Prepare 32 oz chicken broth

Steps to make Slow-cooker Ham and Lentil soup:
  1. Dice ham into 1" cubes.
  2. Add all ingredients to slow-cooker.
  3. Cook on low for 8-10 hours.
  4. Stir, salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Raise heat to high, cook for 2 hours stirring occasionally.
