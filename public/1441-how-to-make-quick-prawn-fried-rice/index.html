The ingredients needed to make Prawn Fried Rice:
  1. Get Prawns
  2. Take 2 tbsp butter
  3. Get 12 pcs Prawns
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Prepare 1/8 tsp salt
  6. Prepare Rice
  7. Take 1 medium red onion (crushed)
  8. Make ready 3 cloves garlic (crushed)
  9. Take 2 tbsp chilli paste
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  11. Take 3 drops Maggi Liquid Seasoning
  12. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Make ready 1 egg
  14. Prepare 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots/corn/peas)
  15. Take 2 bowls cooked rice

Instructions to make Prawn Fried Rice:
  1. Heat up butter in pan.
  2. Mix turmeric powder & salt with prawns.
  3. Fry prawns until it changes colour.
  4. Remove prawns & put aside.
  5. Using the same pan, sauté onion & garlic.
  6. Add chilli paste & oyster sauce, & let them cook for a while.
  7. Add Maggi seasoning & salt.
  8. Crack & scramble an egg.
  9. Lastly, add vegetables & rice. Mix well.
