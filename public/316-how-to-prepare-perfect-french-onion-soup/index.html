The ingredients needed to make French onion soup:
  1. Get 1 tbsp butter
  2. Get 1 cup chopped onion
  3. Get 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
  4. Get 1 tsp maida/all-purpose flour
  5. Prepare 1 tsp garlic
  6. Get to taste Salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  8. Take 1 tsp Oregano
  9. Make ready 1 tsp chilli flakes
  10. Make ready 3-4 cup vegetable stock
  11. Take as needed Toasted bread
  12. Get 3 tsp Cheese
  13. Get as needed Cream for garnishing

Instructions to make French onion soup:
  1. First heat up a pan then add butter
  2. Next add garlic and saute for few second then add chopped onion
  3. Now also add mushrooms and cook both till it become light golden in colour
  4. Then add maida and cook it properly then add veg stock or hot water add all spices as per taste and allow it to boil till it thick
  5. Now add cheese on bread and bake it till it melt then serve it on top of soup and garnish with cream and enjoy.
