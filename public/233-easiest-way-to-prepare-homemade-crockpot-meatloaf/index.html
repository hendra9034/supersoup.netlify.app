The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Meatloaf:
  1. Make ready 2 lb ground beef
  2. Make ready 1 package onion soup mix
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1 cup bread crumbs
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup ketchup
  6. Take 4 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  7. Take 1/4 piece a small onion
  8. Prepare 6 peeled potatoes
  9. Take Salt and paper
  10. Prepare 1/4 ketchup held back
Instructions to make Crockpot Meatloaf:
  1. Place all ingredients in a bowl together. Except potatoes and salt and pepper.
  2. Mix all ingredients together form into a loaf.
  3. Line crockpot with foil. Then place loaf into crockpot.
  4. Place peeled potatoes with salt and pepper on them in crockpot around the loaf. Cook on high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Abb 1/4 ketchup to top of loaf serve and enjoy!!
