The ingredients needed to make Crockpot potato soup:
  1. Get 1/2 pack Bacon
  2. Prepare Potatoes 6 large
  3. Make ready Chives 3 to 4 or more if u wish
  4. Prepare 1 box Chicken stock
  5. Take Salt, pepper
  6. Prepare Heavy cream 8 oz to 10oz depends on your preference
  7. Prepare Cheddar cheese as much as u wish
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup Butter
  9. Make ready 3 cloves Garlic

Steps to make Crockpot potato soup:
  1. Melt butter n seasoning in pan with a little of the broth
  2. Peel cut wash potatoes add chives pepper n salt garlic well minced
  3. Add potatoes chives broth about 1/2 the carton
  4. Cut n defrost cook bacon till crisp y it will turn softer from crockpot keep some out if u wish to top with it pat w a napkin n add to soup mix
  5. Add bacon and additional broth leave about 1/4 in carton
  6. Add shreeded cheese about 1/2 cup at a time u till desired amount is reached Add cream about 20 to 30 mins before serving cook u till warm on low or warm setting stirring every 10 mins once it is warm if you wish for it to be more soupy add remaining broth. If u wish for extra cheese or thick add more cream or cheese to your liking
  7. Top w additional cheese chives bacon salt pepper enjoy
