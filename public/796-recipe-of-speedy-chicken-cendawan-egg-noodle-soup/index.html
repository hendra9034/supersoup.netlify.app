The ingredients needed to make Chicken Cendawan Egg Noodle Soup:
  1. Get Chicken thigh part
  2. Take Pickles
  3. Take salt
  4. Get Cendawan(cheesy mushroom soup) powder
  5. Make ready round egg noodle
  6. Take basil dried leaves
  7. Take of crouton
  8. Make ready Hot water
Instructions to make Chicken Cendawan Egg Noodle Soup:
  1. Marinate chicken with 1 pinch of salt and pickles.
  2. Dissolve cendawan powder in a hot water.
  3. Heat dissolved cedawan in a casserole and let it boil.
  4. Add the chicken. Wait until chicken is tender.
  5. Add egg noodle. Let it boil for 2mins.
  6. Serve in a bowl, sprinkle some basil leaves and put some crouton.
