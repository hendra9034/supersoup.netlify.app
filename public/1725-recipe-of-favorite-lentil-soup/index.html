The ingredients needed to make Lentil Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 cup sliced carrots
  2. Take 2 cup red yellow or orange peppers
  3. Take 1 1/3 cup Chopped or sliced garlic
  4. Make ready 2 cup Chopped onion
  5. Prepare 1 can whole peeled tomatoes
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 lb lentils
  7. Take 4 cup chicken, beef, or veggie stock
  8. Get 4 cup water

Steps to make Lentil Soup:
  1. sauté carrots, tomatoes, onion, garlic, and peppers in 1/2cup tomato juice (from can) and 1 cup stock
  2. add spices as preferred:I like extra granulated garlic, non-salt seasoning, black ground pepper, fresh jalapeños, and red pepper flakes
  3. boil lentils until al dented. You do not want them mushy!
  4. Combine lentils and veggies- let simmer one hour then serve!
