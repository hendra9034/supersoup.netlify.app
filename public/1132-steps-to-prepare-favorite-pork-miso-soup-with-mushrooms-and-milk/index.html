The ingredients needed to make Pork miso soup with mushrooms and milk:
  1. Make ready 100 g pork shouldre
  2. Take 3 fresh Shiitake mushrooms
  3. Get 60 g Shimeji mushrooms
  4. Take 40 g carrot
  5. Take 5 cc vegetable oil
  6. Make ready 60 g Japanese leek
  7. Get 1/2 fried tofu
  8. Take 1 1/2 tablespoon sake
  9. Get 100 ml soup broth
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 tablespoons miso
  11. Take 300 ml milk

Instructions to make Pork miso soup with mushrooms and milk:
  1. Cut the pork shoulders into 2-3cm pieces. Remove the tip of the stem from the shiitake mushrooms. Slice the remaining part. Remove the tip of the stem from the shimeji mushrooms and separate them. Slice the carrot into quarter-rounds. Cut the Japanese leek diagonally into 2-3cm long pieces.
  2. Pour boiling water over fried tofu to drain the excess oil and cut ito into rectangles.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a flying pan and fry the pork. Add the mushrooms and carrots, mix well and add sake, soup broth, and fried tofu. Add 1 tablespoon of miso and boil until carrots are tender.
