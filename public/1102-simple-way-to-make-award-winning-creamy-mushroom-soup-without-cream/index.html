The ingredients needed to make Creamy Mushroom Soup WITHOUT Cream:
  1. Get 1/2 Organic Onion
  2. Take 3 Cups Organic Mushrooms
  3. Get 1 Medium Organic Potato
  4. Make ready 2 Cloves Garlic
  5. Take 3 Cups Organic Mushroom Broth or Water
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Organic Oil
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Organic All-Purpose Flour
  8. Take Pinch Parsley
  9. Get Salt & Pepper
Instructions to make Creamy Mushroom Soup WITHOUT Cream:
  1. Chop and cook potato and set it aside
  2. Add Olive Oil in a large saucepan over medium heat
  3. Add Garlic & Onion to the pan and cook for 3 minutes or until onion is tender
  4. Add mushrooms and cook for another 3 minutes, set 1 tbsp mushrooms aside for later
  5. Add Flour, Salt, and Pepper
  6. Add Mushroom Broth or water and bring it to boil
  7. Add cooked and chopped potato to the soup and cook for another 2 minutes
  8. Put soup into the blender and blend for 3 minutes on high
  9. Add Pinch of Parsley and 1 tbsp mushrooms on top and serve
