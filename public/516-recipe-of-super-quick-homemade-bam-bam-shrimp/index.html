The ingredients needed to make Bam Bam Shrimp:
  1. Get Battered Shrimp
  2. Take 1/4 cup coconut oil
  3. Prepare 1 large egg white
  4. Take 1/4 cup potato starch
  5. Make ready 1 tsp sea salt
  6. Take 2 lb shrimp
  7. Prepare 2 green union
  8. Prepare 1 pinch sesame seeds
  9. Get –Sauce—
  10. Get 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp honey
  12. Get 1 tbsp lemon juice
  13. Get 1 tsp puri puri chili
  14. Make ready 1 tsp chili powder
  15. Prepare 1 pinch sea salt
  16. Prepare 1 pinch white pepper
Steps to make Bam Bam Shrimp:
  1. Combine the sauce ingredients in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. In a wok or skillet, heat the coconut oil over medium-high heat until shimmering, about 5 minutes.
  3. Whip the egg white until frothy, then add the starch and salt and stir to combine into a batter. Toss the shrimp in the batter to coat.
  4. Add the coated shrimp to the wok in batches and fry until golden brown, about 5 minutes per batch, then place on paper towels to drain.
  5. Toss the shrimp with the sauce and green onions, then garnish with sesame seeds and serve.
