The ingredients needed to make Seaweed Soup with Sliced Zucchini and tender beef:
  1. Prepare 400 g Shabu beef
  2. Get 2 tbsps dried wakame (Rehydrate in water)
  3. Get 1 large sheet Chinese seaweed
  4. Get 2 dried shiitake mushrooms
  5. Prepare 2 Zucchini
  6. Prepare Mushroom Dashi
  7. Get 1 tbsp mirin
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  9. Prepare 1.5 tbsps minced garlic
Instructions to make Seaweed Soup with Sliced Zucchini and tender beef:
  1. Make mushroom Dashi by soaking dried shiitake mushrooms in water overnight.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp sesame oil on a non-stick pan, stir-fry zucchini till cooked. Add in beef slices and cook till no longer pink.
  3. Add in wakame and shiitake mushrooms. Fry for about 5 mins or till most of the moisture have evaporated off.
  4. Add in mushroom Dashi and all other seasonings. Bring to a boil and then simmer over low heat for 10-15 mins. Enjoy!
