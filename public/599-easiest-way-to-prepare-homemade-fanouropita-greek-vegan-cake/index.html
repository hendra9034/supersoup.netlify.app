The ingredients needed to make Fanouropita - Greek vegan cake:
  1. Take 300 g olive oil
  2. Get 300 g sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  4. Get 1 tsp ground cloves
  5. Prepare 300 gr fresh orange juice
  6. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 125 g raisins
  8. Get 125 g walnuts (ground)
  9. Make ready 500 g flour

Instructions to make Fanouropita - Greek vegan cake:
  1. In a big bowl mix the oil with the sugar until it changes color. Add the cinnamon and ground cloves cloves and keep mixing.
  2. Mix the orange juice with the baking soda (be careful as it foams a lot) and add it to the bowl.
  3. Add the raisins and walnuts. Add the flour and mix well until you have a nice batter.
  4. Put the batter in an oiled pan and bake (180ºC) for 30-45 min, depending on your oven and the size of pan you used. I usually use a 25cm round pan.
  5. One of the traditional ways to decorate this cake is this: we cover the cake with a crochet lace doily (all greek grandmothers have tons of them), sprinkle with lots of icing sugar and remove the doily.
