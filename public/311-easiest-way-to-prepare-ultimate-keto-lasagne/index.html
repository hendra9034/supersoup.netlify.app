The ingredients needed to make Keto Lasagne:
  1. Prepare 750 g Minced Beef
  2. Get 2 Aubergines
  3. Prepare 1 Onion
  4. Make ready Cube Beef Stock
  5. Prepare 1 Tube of Tomato Paste
  6. Take 1 Tub Ricotta or Mascarpone
  7. Prepare 2 Cups Mature Grated Cheese
  8. Take 2 Cups Grated Mozzerella
  9. Take 1 Cup Parmesan
  10. Take 1 Egg
  11. Make ready Salt and Pepper
  12. Prepare Olive Oil
  13. Prepare Crushed Garlic or Garlic Powder
Instructions to make Keto Lasagne:
  1. Preheat Oven to 180 - Slice Aubergines, brush with Olive oil, season and bake for 20 Minutes
  2. Fry the Beef with Onions until beef is browned. Then add the seasoning, Garlic, Stock cube and Tomato Paste. Remove from heat.
  3. Whisk the Ricotta or Mascarpone with the egg and half of the parmesan
  4. Layer the Lasagne: (Note - I drop spoonfulls of the Rocotta/Mascarpone Mixture on top of the Aubergine layer) - 1/2 Beef - 1/2 Aubergine - 1/2 Ricotta/Mascarpone mixture - 1/2 Grated Cheese - 1/2 Mozzerella - Remaining.. - Beef - Aubergine - Ricotta/Mascarpone mixture - Grated Cheese - Mozzerella - Parmesan
  5. Bake for 30 - 40 Minutes
