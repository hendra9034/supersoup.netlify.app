The ingredients needed to make Creamy chicken and rice soup:
  1. Prepare large chicken breast
  2. Get Carrots cut into small cubes
  3. Make ready small onion dices
  4. Prepare chicken broth or 6 cups water and 8 chicken bullion cubes
  5. Take milk
  6. Get cream cheese
  7. Make ready oil
  8. Prepare frozen peas
  9. Prepare rice uncooked
  10. Prepare Salt and pepper
  11. Get flour mixed with 1/4 cup of milk

Steps to make Creamy chicken and rice soup:
  1. Cook chicken breast until long longer pink in middle. Shred into small pieces
  2. Put oil, onions and carrots in large pot. Cook until onions are soft.
  3. Add in shedded chicken, broth or bullion cubes and water. Bring to a boil the simmer for 15 min.
  4. Add in milk, cream cheese, peas and rice. Turn up to medium heat and stir until cream cheese melts.
  5. Mix flour with 1/4 cup of milk and add to pot simmer again for 15 min.
  6. Add in salt and pepper to taste. Once rice is soft enjoy.
