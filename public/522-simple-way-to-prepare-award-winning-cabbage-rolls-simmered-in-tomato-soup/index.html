The ingredients needed to make Cabbage Rolls Simmered in Tomato Soup:
  1. Get leaves Cabbage
  2. Take Mixed ground beef and pork
  3. Get Onion
  4. Make ready Egg
  5. Make ready Panko
  6. Take Salt and pepper
  7. Make ready ☆Canned crushed tomatoes
  8. Make ready ☆Water
  9. Get ☆Soup stock granules
  10. Take ☆Tomato ketchup
  11. Make ready ☆Salt and pepper
  12. Get of each Dried parsley
Instructions to make Cabbage Rolls Simmered in Tomato Soup:
  1. Peel off each cabbage leaf carefully, remove the stem, lightly boil, and let cool.
  2. Finely chop the onions, cover with plastic wrap, microwave for about a minute at 500w, and let cool.
  3. In a bowl, mix the ground meat, onions from Step 2, eggs, panko, and salt and pepper until it all sticks together.
  4. If the cabbage from Step 1 is large, use 1 leaf. If it is small or has holes, stack 2-3 leaves together.
  5. Add Step 3 on top of Step 4, and roll the meat filling tightly in the cabbage. Secure it with a toothpick to keep it from falling apart.
  6. In a pot, mix the ingredients marked with ☆, turn the heat to medium and add in the cabbage rolls, and remove the scum.
  7. Cover the pot with aluminum foil and boil for 30 minutes over low heat.
  8. Remove the toothpick and serve in bowls, garnish with dried parsley, and you are done .
  9. These are cabbages rolls made with lots of broth. Add bacon to the leftover broth and boil, and eat as tomato soup for a nutritious and delicious treat!
