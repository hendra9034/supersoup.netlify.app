The ingredients needed to make Pot Cabbage and Chicken Wing Tips Soup:
  1. Make ready Cabbage
  2. Prepare Chicken wing tips
  3. Get Water
  4. Take Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Pot Cabbage and Chicken Wing Tips Soup:
  1. Cover the wings with plenty of salt and let it sit for 15 minutes, then pat dry the excess water. Cut the cabbage into eighths with the core.
  2. Snuggle the cabbage around the wing tips, add water until everything is submerged.
  3. Bring to a boil over high heat, skimming off the scum. Next, remove the lid and simmer on low for 15 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste and it's done!
