The ingredients needed to make KUNG PAO BEEF w/ RICE:
  1. Make ready 2 sirloin steaks
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp cornstarch
  3. Get 1 tbsp ketchup
  4. Get 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp ginger paste
  6. Get 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  7. Get 1 tsp chilli sauce
  8. Get 1 tsp sesame oil
  9. Take 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  10. Prepare 1 green pepper
  11. Get 1 medium white onion
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp cashew nuts
  13. Prepare 1 sliced red chilli
  14. Take 225 g boiled rice
  15. Prepare 1 carrot sliced
  16. Take 50 ml water
  17. Prepare 1 tsp vegetable oil

Steps to make KUNG PAO BEEF w/ RICE:
  1. Slice your steak and season with cornflour
  2. Make your sauce by mixing ketchup, rice wine vinegar, soy sauce, chilli sauce, sesame oil and water and mix
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a wok on a high heat, add chilli flakes, ginger, for 10 seconds
  4. Add green pepper, onion, carrot and cook for 1 minute
  5. Add steak and coin for 1 minute
  6. Boil rice for 10 minutes
  7. Add your sauce to your steak with red chilli and simmer for 8 minutes
  8. Add cashew nuts to sauce and cook for 1 minute
  9. Serve
