The ingredients needed to make Salt and Vinegar Crisps:
  1. Prepare 4 small potatoes
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  3. Get 1 tablespoon water
  4. Get 1 tablespoon salt
  5. Get 6 tablespoons white vinegar
  6. Take 1 tablespoon oil
Instructions to make Salt and Vinegar Crisps:
  1. Wash and dry the potatoes.
  2. To a bowl add the salt.
  3. Balsamic vinegar.
  4. Water.
  5. White vinegar.
  6. Mix until salt is dissolved.
  7. Add the oil and mix again.
  8. Set aside. Use a mandoline to cut the potatoes thinly. You can also use a knife if you are very careful.
  9. After slicing all of the potatoes pat them dry with a paper towel.
  10. Add the slices in the mixture of vinegar and set aside for 45 minutes.
  11. After 45 minutes lay the potatoes on a baking tray.
  12. Put in the preaheated oven at 220Â°C for 15 minutes.
  13. Enjoy!
