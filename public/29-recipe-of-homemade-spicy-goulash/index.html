The ingredients needed to make Spicy goulash:
  1. Make ready 8 Cumberland sausages
  2. Make ready Pack smoked bacon
  3. Take 750 ml chicken stock
  4. Get 250 g grated Parmesan
  5. Get 1 large onion
  6. Get 2 red chillies
  7. Make ready 3 tbs paprika
  8. Prepare 3 bay leaves
  9. Make ready 2 tbs tomato paste
  10. Get 2 tbs garlic paste
  11. Get 1 tbs salt and pepper
  12. Prepare 1 tbs lemon juice
  13. Take 1 knob butter
  14. Make ready 1 beef oxo cube

Instructions to make Spicy goulash:
  1. Place French oven on medium heat and turn oven to 140c - Separate sausages & trim fat from bacon - Cut onion & chillies in half and slice finely - Dice bacon and bacon fat (keep separate)
  2. Render bacon fat in French oven then add sausages and diced bacon to brown - Add onion and chillies to caramelise - Add garlic paste & tomato paste and cook off
  3. Once browned, add paprika, salt, pepper, oxo cube and cheese - Mix through until cheese dissolves and you have a paste
  4. At this stage, add bay leaves and chicken stock - Put lid on, let it come to a simmer and transfer to oven
  5. Cook in oven for 2 hours at 140c
