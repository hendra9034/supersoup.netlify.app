The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup with steak salad:
  1. Prepare French Onion Soup
  2. Take 6 small Sweet Onions
  3. Get 1/2 cup butter
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  5. Get 1 tsp minced garlic
  6. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  7. Prepare 4 cup Beef broth
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Worsteshire Sauce
  9. Get Soup. OPTIONAL!
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup Cabernet
  11. Take Soup Garnish
  12. Prepare 6 oz grated parmesean cheese
  13. Take 1 loaf French baguette
  14. Get Salad
  15. Make ready 8 oz baby spinach
  16. Prepare 2 oz grated parmesean cheese
  17. Prepare 12 grape tomatoes
  18. Take 12 oz Beef Sirloin steak
Instructions to make French Onion Soup with steak salad:
  1. Peal and cut off the onion ends. Cut the onions in half and with the onion now laying flat side down, thinly slice it. Place onion slices in a large sauce pan.
  2. Add butter, EVOO, garlic, bay leaf to the onions and saute until onion is translucent and slightly browned.
  3. Optional: Once step 2 is complete, add Cabernet and bring back to a simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add beef broth and worsteshire sauce to simmering onions.
  5. Bring to a slow simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. OR: pour all ingredients into a slow cooker:Medium-low setting for 6 hours.
  6. while soup is simmering, grill steak to preferred temperature and season to taste.
  7. Fill four bowls with soup and sprinkle 3 tablespoons of grated parm cheese into each. (My family loves about twice this amount of parm, but some families may not want that much cheese)
  8. Cut toasted baguette and place on plate to dip into soup.
  9. plate spinach and garnish with 1 tablespoon parm cheese on top and 3 grape tomatoes cut in halves. Drizzle EVOO and balsamic vinegarette on top. Add four steak slices.
