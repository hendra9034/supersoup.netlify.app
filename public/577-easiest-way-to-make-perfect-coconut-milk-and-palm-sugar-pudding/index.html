The ingredients needed to make Coconut milk and palm sugar pudding:
  1. Take 160 g palm sugar / gula jawa / gula malaka cut into chunks
  2. Get 3 pieces pandan leaves, tied into a bundle
  3. Prepare 700 ml water
  4. Take 1 packed agar agar (I used swallow brand agar agar)
  5. Prepare 200 ml coconut milk
  6. Get Pinch salt
