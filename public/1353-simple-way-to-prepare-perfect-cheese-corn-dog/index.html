The ingredients needed to make Cheese Corn Dog:
  1. Get 80 g Bread Flour,
  2. Get 80 g Rye Flour,
  3. Get 8 g Caraway Seeds,
  4. Prepare 66 g Granulated Sugar,
  5. Make ready 7 g Sea Salt,
  6. Get 8 g Baking Powder,
  7. Make ready 25 g Cornmeal,
  8. Make ready 80 g Eggs Lightly Whisked,
  9. Make ready 145 g Whole Milk,
  10. Get Peanut / Grapeseed / Sunflower / Canola Oil, For Deep Frying
  11. Make ready 8 Mozzarella Cheese Sticks,

Steps to make Cheese Corn Dog:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, add in bread flour, rye flour, caraway seeds, sugar, salt, baking powder and cornmeal. - - Give it a quick mix. - - Add in eggs and milk. - - To measure eggs, whisk the eggs and measure with a weighing scale. - - Fold to combine well.
  2. The batter should be thick and not runny. It is okay to have a few small lumps. - - Transfer the batter into a tall cylinder. - - Add about 4 inches of oil into a dutch oven. - - Turn the heat up to medium-high, temperature 375 fahrenheit or 190 degree celsius. - - While the oil is heating up, insert popsicle sticks into the cheese sticks. - - Don't rush and take your time, as the cheese sticks are pretty fragile.
  3. Once the oil is heated, dunk a cheese stick into the batter. - - Swirl about 1/2 portion of the cheese corn dog in the oil for about 10 seconds, before dropping it entirely into the oil. It will float almost immediately. - - This is to prevent the dog from sticking at the bottom. - - Keep flipping for even browning.
  4. Once golden brown, remove from heat and drain off excess oil on a wire cooling rack or on a plate lined with kitchen paper. - - Repeat the process with the remaining cheese sticks. Do not crowd. Do it one by one or in batches. - - Serve immediately.
  5. I like to serve mine with some kewpie mayo, nori flakes and sriracha powder. - - You can serve it however you desired. - - You can also use the batter to make corn dogs with some sausages or hot dogs. Be sure to use the same procedures as stated above.
