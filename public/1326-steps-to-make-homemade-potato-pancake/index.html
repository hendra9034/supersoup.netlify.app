The ingredients needed to make Potato Pancake:
  1. Take 1 baking potato (3 medium size potatoes)
  2. Take 25 g corn starch
  3. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  4. Prepare olive oil
  5. Make ready miso sauce (miso + mirin + water)
Instructions to make Potato Pancake:
  1. Cut a baking potato into three pieces
  2. Wrap each piece with wrapping film
  3. Warm the potatoes up in a microwave until a spit (or folk) can easily pass through, about 4-5min in 600w
  4. Let the potatoes cool down for a while and put them into a plastic bag (preferably one with zipper)
  5. Push the potatoes a few times and add corn flour and salt into the bag
  6. Mash up the contents by squashing with hands until it gets smooth 1. Shape into small pieces with hands
  7. Put olive oil into a pan and cook until the both sides get colored and become crispy
  8. It’s nice to eat as it is if seasoned with enough salt - if too lightly seasoned, you can have with a sauce like miso sauce
