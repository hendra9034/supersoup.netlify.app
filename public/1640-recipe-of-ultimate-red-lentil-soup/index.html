The ingredients needed to make Red lentil soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup red lentils
  3. Take 1 clove garlic
  4. Get The juice of half a lemon
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  6. Take 2 cups broth or water

Steps to make Red lentil soup:
  1. In a sauce pan put the oil and the clove of garlic in medium heat.
  2. Meanwhile, rinse the lentils well until the water comes out clean.
  3. Add the lentils at the saucepan and also the lemon juice until it evaporates completely. Then add the tomato sauce and mix.
  4. Next, add the 2 cups of water and bring to boil. Once it starts boiling, let it simmer for 15 to 20 minutes (it should look like in the second pic).
  5. Blend the soup with a hand blender or with any other blender you have until it has a creamy consistency.
