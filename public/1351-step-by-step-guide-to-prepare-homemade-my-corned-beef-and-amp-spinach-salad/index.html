  4. Get Some iceberg crisp lettuce
  5. Prepare 1 inch piece cucumber diced
  6. Get 2 inch square piece of Corned beef chopped
  7. Get 2 tbls Feta cheese diced
  8. Get Salt and Pepper to taste
  9. Make ready About 2 tsp blue cheese dressing over the top
