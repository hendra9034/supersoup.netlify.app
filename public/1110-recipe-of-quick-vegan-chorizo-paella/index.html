The ingredients needed to make Vegan Chorizo Paella:
  1. Make ready 6 Vegan Chorizo Sausages (Linda McCartney)
  2. Take 1 Onion
  3. Get 1 Pepper
  4. Get 3 Cloves Garlic
  5. Get 350 g Paella Rice
  6. Take 75 g Tomato puree (1/3 of a tube)
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Moroccan Spices
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp paprika
  9. Take 700 ml Vegetable Stock (1 or 2 stock cubes)
  10. Prepare 2 Cups Peas
  11. Prepare 1/2 lemon juice
  12. Prepare Handful coriander
Instructions to make Vegan Chorizo Paella:
  1. Cook the sausages in the oven according to the packaging (usually 20mins). Meanwhile dice the onion, pepper and garlic.
  2. Add the diced veg to an oiled pan on medium heat and cook for 10 mins until the onion browns. Boil the kettle to make 700ml of vegetable stock (normally 2 cubes)
  3. When the sausages finish cooking, slice them up and set aside for later.
  4. Add the paella rice to the pan and cook for a further 5 mins. Then mix the tomato puree, Moroccan spices and paprika
  5. Next, add 400ml of the vegetable stock and stir into the rice. Keep adding water slowly as the water is absorbed until the rice is soft to eat
  6. Mix in the frozen peas, lemon juice, coriander and slices of chorizo and cook for a further 5 mins
  7. Serve up and enjoy!
