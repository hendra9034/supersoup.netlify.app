The ingredients needed to make Bok Choy Soup with Chick Peas:
  1. Prepare 1 Bok Choy, rinsed and sliced
  2. Prepare Olive oil
  3. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  4. Take 1 green pepper, chopped
  5. Take 1 (12 ounce) bag petite baby cut carrots
  6. Get 6 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Make ready 4 red potatoes, cut into quarters
  8. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Get 1 tablespoon Herb De Provence
  10. Prepare 2 can chicken peas (15.5 ounce) undrained
  11. Get 2 containers Vegetable Stock (32 ounce)
  12. Make ready 2 cups egg noodles
