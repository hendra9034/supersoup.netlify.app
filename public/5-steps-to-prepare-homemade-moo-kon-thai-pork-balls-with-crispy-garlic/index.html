The ingredients needed to make Moo Kon - Thai pork balls with crispy garlic:
  1. Get 150 g Minced pork or chicken
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp plain flour
  3. Take 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  4. Get 3 tbsp finely chopped onions
  5. Make ready 2 -tbsp finely chopped spring onions
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp fishsauce
  7. Take Pinch sugar
  8. Take 1-2 clove garlic, finely chopped
  9. Get 1 tbsp finely chopped coriander root or you can use coriander stalk instead
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp ground white pepper
  11. Get Crispy garlic : 4-5 clove garlic, finely chopped (for fry crispy garlic to top up) and some vegetable cooking oil
Instructions to make Moo Kon - Thai pork balls with crispy garlic:
  1. Pound coriander roots (or stalk), garlic and pepper corn together in pestle and mortar.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add minced chicken or pork, flour, oyster sauce, onions, spring onions, fishsauce, pinch of sugar and the coriander paste that you pound on no.1 in. Mix it well.
  3. Form the mixture into small pingpong ball size. Leave it in the fridge for 15 min to set.
  4. To make crispy garlic, add about 3-4 tbsp in a medium high small saucepan or pan, add finely chopped garlic in and use wooden spatular stir until garlic turn crisp. Turn heat off.
  5. On another pan, add about 2-3 tbsp of vegetable cooking oil on medium heat, add chicken or pork balls in and fry for about 5-8 min then turn it over. Keep turn it all round until they cooked evenly
  6. Serve your Thai pork (or chicken) balls top with crispy garlic. Perfect with jasmine rice and sriracha sauce.
