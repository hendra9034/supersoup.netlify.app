The ingredients needed to make Chicken Katsu Curry with Corriander Rice:
  1. Prepare Katsu Curry Sauce
  2. Get 1 medium white onion
  3. Get 1 medium carrot
  4. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  5. Prepare 2 cm fresh ginger
  6. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala, turmeric, medium curry powder
  7. Take 2 tbsp plain flour
  8. Get 600 ml boiling water
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp mango chutney
  10. Take Salt
  11. Prepare Pepper
  12. Make ready Olive oil
  13. Make ready Chicken
  14. Make ready 2 medium chicken breasts
  15. Make ready 250 ml low fat buttermilk
  16. Prepare 2 tbsp medium curry powder
  17. Take 2 crushed garlic cloves
  18. Prepare 100 g panko breadcrumbs
  19. Prepare Olive oil
  20. Get Corriander Rice
  21. Prepare 150 g long grain basmati rice
  22. Get 400 ml water
  23. Get Small bunch of corriander
  24. Make ready Salt
Instructions to make Chicken Katsu Curry with Corriander Rice:
  1. Finely dice the onion, garlic, ginger and soften in a pan with a little olive oil.
  2. One softened, add one finely diced medium carrot and combine until soft.
  3. Add a small bunch of finely chopped corriander stalks and combine
  4. Add the turmeric, garam masala and curry powder and toast for 1 minute.
  5. Add the flour and cook out for 2 minutes on a low heat.
  6. Gradually add 600ml of boiling water to the pan, stiring until the sauce begins to thicken.
  7. One all the water is added, bring the sauce to the boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
  8. Stir in the mango chutney and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. One the sauce is thickened. Set aside.
  10. Butterfly the chicken breasts and add to a bowl with the buttermilk, curry powder and garlic. Combine fully, cover and marinade for 2 hours.
  11. Once ready, remove the chicken from the buttermilk and cover in panko breadcrumbs making sure the whole chicken is covered.
  12. Add the rice to a pan with 300ml of water and salt. Bring to the boil and simmer for 10 minutes. Turn off the heat and cover until all the liquid is absorbed and the rice is cooked.
  13. In a hot pan, add enough oil to cover 1/4 of the chicken breast and gently fry on both sides until golden brown and cooked through to 75 degrees.
  14. Finely slice the chicken breasts and top with the katsu sauce.
  15. Finely chop the corriander and add to the rice. Mix well and serve alongside the chicken.
