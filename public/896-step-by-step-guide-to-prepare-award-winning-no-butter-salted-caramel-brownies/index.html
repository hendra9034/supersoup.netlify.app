The ingredients needed to make No Butter Salted Caramel Brownies:
  1. Make ready 120 ml vegetable oil
  2. Take 200 g caster sugar
  3. Get 1/4 teaspoon salted caramel flavouring
  4. Prepare 2 eggs
  5. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  6. Get 65 g plain flour
  7. Make ready 40 g cocoa powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon Mardon salt
Steps to make No Butter Salted Caramel Brownies:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°c
  2. Mix the oil and sugar until well blended
  3. Add the eggs and flavouring; stir until just blended
  4. Mix all the dry ingredients together.
  5. Combine the dry mixture with the oil mixture.
  6. Add some chocolate chips/chunks if desired.
  7. Pour into a greased/lined 9x9 pan. (Improvise if necessary!)
  8. Bake for roughly 20 minutes, check the centre of the brownie using a cocktail stick. If the mix is still liquid return to the oven until cocktail stick is semi-clean
  9. Cool completely before cutting. Sprinkle additional flakes of Maldon salt for extra mouth watering goodness!
