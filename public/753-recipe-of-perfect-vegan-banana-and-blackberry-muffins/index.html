Steps to make Vegan Banana and Blackberry Muffins ğŸŒğŸ§ ğŸŒ±:
  1. Preheat oven to 220c. Mash the banana and coconut sugar together. Add the butter and combine with a whisk. Sift flour and baking powder into mix - adjusting if required so that you have a fairly thick cake batter consistency.
  2. Gently fold through the blackberries. Spoon the mixture into muffin cases and bake for around 20 minutes (depending on size of muffins). Muffins should be golden on top and a fork should be clean when the muffin is tested. Enjoy warm!
