The ingredients needed to make Split Pea, Bacon & Potato Soup:
  1. Make ready 6 oz bacon
  2. Prepare 1 medium onion
  3. Make ready 2 clove garlic
  4. Take 1 lb split peas
  5. Get 8 cup chicken broth
  6. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  7. Prepare 1 pepper to taste
  8. Take 1 1/2 lb potato
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
Steps to make Split Pea, Bacon & Potato Soup:
  1. Cut bacon, add to large pot, cook till brown and crispy
  2. Add diced onions and minced garlic to pot
  3. Wait 5min add split peas, chicken broth, pepper
  4. Add pealed 1in Potato cubes
  5. Put lid on pot
  6. Set burner to high
  7. Boil, then simmer for 45 min
  8. Check peas are soft
  9. Blend to wanted thickness
  10. Garnish with shredded cheese or green onions
