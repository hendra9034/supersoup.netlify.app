The ingredients needed to make French onion soup:
  1. Get 3 lbs yellow onion
  2. Make ready 1 table spoon minced garlic
  3. Get 2 table spoon oil or butter
  4. Take 1 cup Dry wine, Sherry (optional)
  5. Take 6 cups beef stock
  6. Take 2-4 bay leaves
  7. Take 1 tablespoon Thyme
  8. Take to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Take French bread slice and toasted
  10. Make ready Provolone
  11. Get Scallion or parsley for added color

Steps to make French onion soup:
  1. Cut onions pole to pole. Trim off root and core. Cut pole to pole in 1/4 inch slices
  2. Heat oil on low. Add onions and garlic until onions are soft and translucent. Pinch of salt will help sweat the onions.
  3. Deglaze pan with wine. Allow wine to evaporate a bit.
  4. Add bay leaves and beef stock. Bring to a low boil
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
  6. Ladle soup into oven safe bowl. Top with french bread and cheese.
  7. Broil until cheese is melted and started to brown.
  8. Add chopped scallions or parsley for plating. And enjoy
