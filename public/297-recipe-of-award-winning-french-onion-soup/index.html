The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Prepare 8 -10 large onions, sliced
  2. Get 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  3. Get 10 1/2 oz 6 cans condensed beef broth
  4. Take 1 1/2 tsp worcestshire sauce
  5. Get 3 bay leaves
  6. Prepare 10 slice french bread toasted
  7. Make ready 1 grated parmesan and/or shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps to make French Onion Soup:
  1. saute onions in butter until crisp-tender. transfer to crock pot
  2. add broth, worcestershire sauce, and bay leaves
  3. cover. cook on low 5-7 hours, or until onions are tender. discard bay leaves
  4. ladle into bowls. top with slice of bread and some cheese
