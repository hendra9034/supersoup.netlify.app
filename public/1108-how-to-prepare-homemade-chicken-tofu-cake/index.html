The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tofu Cake:
  1. Prepare 350 g hard type Tofu
  2. Make ready 150 g minced chicken
  3. Get 2 tablespoon starch or rice flour
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare colorful vegetables
Instructions to make Chicken Tofu Cake:
  1. Drain tofu for a while. Mix tofu, salt, chicken and starch. Today I used glutinous rice flour. Starch, flour, rice flour, tapioca powder are also ok. 1. Mix with hand. And put it in the tray.
  2. Cut your colorful vegetables and decorate with them.
  3. Steam it in pot for 30 minutes at medium flame covering the lid with cotton cloth.
  4. Enjoy tofu cake with your favorite sauce, lemon soy sauce, sweet chili sauce etc.
