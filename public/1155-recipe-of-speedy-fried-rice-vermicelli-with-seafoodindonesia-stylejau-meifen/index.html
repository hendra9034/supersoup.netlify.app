  1. Prepare 100 gr rice vermicelli,100gr carrots,150gr cabbage, spring onion
  2. Prepare 100 gr lobster meat,150gr squid
  3. Get 1 piece small Onion slice it,3pieces garlic chop finely
  4. Make ready 1 tbs sweet sauce,1tbs pepper,1/2tbs sesami oil,1tsp salt
