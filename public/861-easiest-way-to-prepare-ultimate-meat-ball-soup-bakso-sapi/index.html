The ingredients needed to make Meat Ball Soup (Bakso Sapi):
  1. Make ready 750 gr mince beef
  2. Prepare 150 gr tapioca starch
  3. Make ready 1 egg white
  4. Take 5 garlic (mashed)
  5. Make ready 5 garlics (fried then mashed)
  6. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  9. Prepare 200 gr ice cube
  10. Make ready Soup:
  11. Get 200 gr beef frame
  12. Take 1 bunch choy sum or bokcoy (cut up)
  13. Make ready 2 spring onions (chopped)
  14. Get 5 garlics (fried then mashed)
  15. Take 1 tsp salt
  16. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  17. Make ready 1/2 tsp pepper
  18. Get 1 pot of water
  19. Take Additional: (optional)
  20. Get Vermicheli
  21. Get Yellow noodle
  22. Prepare Fried onion
  23. Take Fried dumpling
  24. Get Steamed stuffed dumpling
  25. Make ready Tofu (fried or steamed)
  26. Prepare Chicken or beef tofu
Instructions to make Meat Ball Soup (Bakso Sapi):
  1. Grind mince beef with ice cube, place it into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add mashed garlics, fried and mashed garlics, salt, sugar, peper, and tapioca starch, mix well.
  3. Boil water in a pot, make balls from beef mixture with 2 spoons, when the balls float means they are done, take them out of the pot.
  4. For the soup: boil water with beef frame for 15-20 mins, add fried and mashed garlics, salt, sugar, pepper, choy sum or bokcoy, and spring onion.
  5. Add additional ingredients if you like.
  6. Perfect to consume on cold or rainy day.
