The ingredients needed to make Slow cooker Split pea soup:
  1. Take 1 Tblsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 2 onions
  3. Prepare 2 + cloves garlic
  4. Get 1 lb split peas
  5. Get 1/2 lb bacon ends
  6. Get 2 cups pork broth (with pork)
  7. Take 1 cup white wine
  8. Take 4 cups water
  9. Take Small sprig rosemary
  10. Prepare Several sprigs thyme
  11. Prepare salt
  12. Prepare Pepper
  13. Take 4-6 carrots

Instructions to make Slow cooker Split pea soup:
  1. Coat sides and bottom of slow cooker, cook onions on high. Add garlic when onions become transparent (about 1 hour) and cook until fragrant (about 20 mins?)
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients (except carrots) and cook on low for 8 hours. Add carrots and cook an additional 4 hours.
