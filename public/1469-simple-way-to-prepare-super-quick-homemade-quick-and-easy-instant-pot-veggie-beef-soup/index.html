The ingredients needed to make Quick and easy instant pot veggie beef soup:
  1. Make ready 1 lb ground beef
  2. Take 1 small yellow onion
  3. Prepare 1 Tbsp minced garlic
  4. Make ready 1 (32 oz) box of beef broth
  5. Prepare 1 lb. Frozen mixed vegetables
  6. Make ready 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
  7. Make ready 1 (4 oz) can stems and pieces mushrooms
  8. Take 2 bay leaves
  9. Take 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  10. Take to taste Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Quick and easy instant pot veggie beef soup:
  1. Turn instapot to saute mode. While its heating chop onion and mince garlic.
  2. When instapot is hot. Brown hamburger with onion and garlic.
  3. Add all other ingredients to pot and stir
  4. Set instapot to manual setting with high pressure for 5 minutes.
  5. Let pressure release naturally for 5 minutes. Then release the rest of the pressure by turning it valve. (Caution the steam will be hot)
