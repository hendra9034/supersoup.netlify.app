The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Minestrone Soup (GF/Vegan):
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  3. Prepare 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Prepare 3 celery stalks
  5. Take 2 large carrots, cubed
  6. Get 2 cups green beans, cut to bite size
  7. Take 1 zucchini, cubed
  8. Take 1 can white bean (rinsed, drained) cannellini or Great Northern
  9. Take 2 , 18 oz jars of diced tomatoes (jovial is my favorite)
  10. Prepare 6 cups veggie broth
  11. Make ready 1-1/2 cup elbow GF rice pasta (jovial is my favorite)
  12. Prepare 1-1/2 tsp basil
  13. Get 1 tsp tyme
  14. Get 1 tsp oregano
  15. Prepare 1 tsp sea salt
  16. Get 1/2 tsp crushed black pepper
Instructions to make Instant Pot Minestrone Soup (GF/Vegan):
  1. Saute (5 minutes or until golden brown) onions, garlic and olive oil in instant pot
  2. Prepare and add remaining ingredients to instant pot, cover and set to pressure cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Quick release when done and serve.
