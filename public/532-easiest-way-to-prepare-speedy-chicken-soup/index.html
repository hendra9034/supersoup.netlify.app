The ingredients needed to make chicken soup:
  1. Take 3/4 tbsp butter
  2. Take 1 pinch salt
  3. Make ready 1 pinch pepper
  4. Get 2 can mixed vegitables
  5. Prepare 3 can chicken broth
  6. Make ready 1 dash sage
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup union - diced
  8. Take 1 italian seasoning - to taste
  9. Get 2 can white mean chicken in water
  10. Make ready 1/2 packages wide egg noodles

Steps to make chicken soup:
  1. add butter and union to a pan with a pinch of salt and in a pot start to cook egg noodles
  2. let the unions lose color, but not brown
  3. once lost color, add chicken, water and all
  4. add pepper, sage, and Italian seasoning. make it strong because the noodles will dilute the taste later
  5. simmer
  6. add mixed vegetables and broth
  7. raise heat to a boil. add cooked noodles
  8. reduce to a simmer for 5-10 minutes
  9. serve
