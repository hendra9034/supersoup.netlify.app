Instructions to make Mom's ginger butternut squash soup:
  1. chop the onions
  2. peel and mince the fresh ginger
  3. in a large soup pot saute the onion and ginger in the oil until the onion is clear
  4. peel, seed, and chop the apple
  5. peel, seed, and cut into cubes the squash
  6. add all ingredients to the soup and then bring to a boil
  7. simmer until the squash and Apple's are tender
  8. puree in a blender until smooth. salt and pepper to taste.
