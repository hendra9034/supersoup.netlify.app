The ingredients needed to make Chicken noodle soup:
  1. Get 1 lb chicken (bone in and in peices)
  2. Make ready 1 package baby carrots
  3. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  4. Prepare 6 cup cold water
  5. Take 2 cups stock (veg or chicken)
  6. Make ready 1 onion
  7. Make ready 1 stick celery or 2 ice cubes of celery stock
  8. Prepare 1 package Egg noodles
