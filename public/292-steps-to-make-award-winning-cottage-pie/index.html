The ingredients needed to make Cottage Pie:
  1. Prepare 500 g minced beef
  2. Make ready 200 ml water
  3. Prepare 1 large carrot
  4. Get 1 celery stalk
  5. Prepare 1 small onion
  6. Prepare beef stock pod / cube
  7. Make ready frozen peas
  8. Make ready 1 tsp tomato paste or tomato ketchup
  9. Get Masshed potoates (white or purple vitelotte)
  10. Make ready 50 g cheddar cheese
  11. Prepare 1-2 cloves garlic
Instructions to make Cottage Pie:
  1. Chop the onions, carrots and celery into small squares and sweat them on a low heat for around 20 minutes. Add the garlic to sweat for a few minutes near the end.
  2. Remove the softened vegetables from the pot and brown the mince.
  3. Once the mince is browned return the vegetables to the pot with the mince and add the stock pod, water and tomato puree/sauce and simmer for around 30 minutes.
  4. Heat the oven to around 180 degrees celsius.
  5. Spread the mashed potato over the top of the mince and vegetables.
  6. Grate the cheese and sprinkle on top of the mash.
  7. Put the pot into the oven for 30 minutes and once the cheese is nice and melty take it out and serve.
