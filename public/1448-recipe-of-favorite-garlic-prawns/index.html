The ingredients needed to make Garlic prawns:
  1. Get 20 medium size prawns
  2. Take 3 tsp corn flour
  3. Make ready 4 tsp plain flour
  4. Get 1/3 tsp chilli flakes
  5. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  8. Take ice cold water
  9. Get Few cloves of garlic chopped
  10. Take 1 tbsp Sliced or diced onion

Instructions to make Garlic prawns:
  1. Mix flour, corn flour, salt,chilli flakes, garlic powder, turmeric and coriander powder add ice cold water just enough to make little bit thick batter but not too thick, batter should be thick enough to cook or hold on the prawns
  2. Fry them in hot oil
  3. In a fry pan fry chopped garlic along with diced onion,add more chilli flakes or chopped chilli, when garlic becomes lightly brown add cooked prawns mix or just put on top of prawns
