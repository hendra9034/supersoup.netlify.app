The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Chilli with Loads of Beans:
  1. Take 1 red onion
  2. Prepare 4 medium cloves of garlic
  3. Make ready 3 tsp Mexican spices
  4. Get 200 g Vegetarian Mince
  5. Prepare 1 tin Kidney Beans
  6. Make ready 1 tin Black Beans
  7. Take 1 tablespoon tomato puree
  8. Get 3 tsp coriander
  9. Take 25 g dark chocolate
  10. Make ready 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  11. Get 1 vegetable stock pot
  12. Prepare Couscous
  13. Make ready 50 g Cheddar or parmesan cheese
Steps to make Vegetarian Chilli with Loads of Beans:
  1. Gather together your ingredients. Then realise you've forgotten some so get them too.
  2. Finely chop the red onions and sweat them for around 10 minutes. Then add the grated garlic.
  3. Add in the spices and mix them with the onions and garlic mixture.
  4. Add the vegetarian mince and mix with the garlic, onion and spice mixture.
  5. Throw in the tinned tomatoes, beans, stock pot, tomato puree, coriander and set to simmer on the stove top for around 40 minutes till the beans are nice and tender. Towards the end add in the chocolate and let it melt and mix it through. Let it simmer for a few minutes more to allow the chocolate to thicken the liquid.
  6. Serve with rice or couscous and and top with cheese (we like a bit of cheddar or parmesan).
