The ingredients needed to make Roast Pork:
  1. Get 1 kg pork ribs
  2. Make ready 1 tsp Five spice powder
  3. Get Half tsp white pepper
  4. Get Half tsp sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp chicken powder
  6. Take 1/3 tsp salt
Instructions to make Roast Pork:
  1. Mix all the ingredients.
  2. Then rub it in and out of the pork.Leave for an hour to marinate.
  3. Then roast in.pre heated oven for 250c for 30–45 mins.
  4. Serve
