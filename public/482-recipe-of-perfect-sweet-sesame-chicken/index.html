Instructions to make Sweet sesame chicken:
  1. Prepare the sauce and set aside.
  2. Combine all of the marinade ingredients and mix thoroughly. Make sure it is enough to coat the chicken.
  3. In a pan, add oil over high heat and deep fry the chicken.
  4. In another pan, heat up the sauce until thicken. Pour sauce over the chicken and mix well for 2 mins.
  5. In a clean small pan over low heat. add sesame seeds and cook for 1 min.
  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds and spring union over chicken and serve.
