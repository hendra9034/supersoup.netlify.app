The ingredients needed to make Hamburger Gravy & Mashed Potatoes:
  1. Make ready 1 lb ground meat
  2. Make ready 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  3. Get 1 can chicken broth
  4. Take Half a box of instant mashed potatoes or make your own
  5. Take 1 small yellow onion
  6. Take 2 small green onion
  7. Take 1 teaspoon parsley
  8. Prepare 1 small can sliced mushrooms
  9. Make ready Butter
  10. Get Sour cream
  11. Take Salt
  12. Prepare Pepper

Steps to make Hamburger Gravy & Mashed Potatoes:
  1. Brown your ground meat with the green and yellow onion. Drain grease. Return to pan and add in soup and broth. Simmer over medium heat for 20 mins.
  2. Next add butter, mushrooms, parsley, salt, & pepper. Mix well, then fold in 2-3 tablespoons of sour cream.
  3. Continue to simmer while preparing the mashed potatoes. When ready, add potatoes to bowl and top with meat sauce! Serve & Enjoy!
