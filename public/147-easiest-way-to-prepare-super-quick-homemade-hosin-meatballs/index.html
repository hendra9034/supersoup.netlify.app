The ingredients needed to make Hosin Meatballs:
  1. Make ready 12 meatballs
  2. Prepare 2 pak choi
  3. Prepare 2 medium carrots
  4. Get 1 red pepper
  5. Get 1 red onion
  6. Take 160 g mangetout
  7. Make ready 300 g egg noodles
  8. Get 5 tbsp hoisin
Instructions to make Hosin Meatballs:
  1. Peel and slice the carrots.
  2. Cut the onion into thin wedges.
  3. Cut the pepper into chucks the same size as the carrots.
  4. Cut the pak choi into large slices seperating the green leaf from the white stalks.
  5. Add the meatballs to a large frying pan without oil and cook for 7 minutes or until all browned. Keep turning throughout.
  6. Add the onions, carrots, peppers and pak choi stalks to the pan and keep stirring for 3 minutes.
  7. Add the egg noodles and stir well for 2 minutes.
  8. Add the mangetout and pak choi leaves to the pan for another 2 minutes.
  9. Add the hoisin and stir completely for 1 minute.
  10. Serve.
