The ingredients needed to make Yoghurt, Honey & Walnut Muffins:
  1. Make ready 55 g dairy free spread
  2. Prepare 5 tablespoons honey
  3. Get 250 ml dairy free natural yoghurt
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Make ready 65 ml lemon juice
  6. Get 310 g gluten free self raising flour
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  8. Take 60 g walnuts, chopped

Instructions to make Yoghurt, Honey & Walnut Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC
  2. Place the dairy free spread and honey in a small saucepan - Heat gently until melted
  3. In a large bowl stir together the flour, xanthan gum and walnuts - In another bowl whisk together the natural yoghurt, egg and lemon juice - Pour this and the honey mixture into the dry ingredients
  4. Mix until well combined - Spoon into a greased or lined 12 hole muffin tin
  5. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes - Allow to cool before serving
