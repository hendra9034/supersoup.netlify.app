Instructions to make â˜…Colorful Potato Saladâ˜…:
  1. Peel and cut potatoes, and boil until soft.
  2. When the potatoes are tender, drain and mash. and let it cool.
  3. When it become cool, mix lemon juice, mayonnaise and minced onion and salt as taste. This is the Basic Potato salad.
  4. Boil the Broad beans and peel it.
