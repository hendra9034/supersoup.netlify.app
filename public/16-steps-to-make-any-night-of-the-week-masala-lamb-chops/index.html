The ingredients needed to make Masala Lamb Chops:
  1. Get 12 lamb chops
  2. Prepare 4 Tbsp Thick Yogurt
  3. Make ready 4 Tbsp Mustard oil
  4. Make ready 1 Lemon juice
  5. Make ready Salt to taste
  6. Prepare 1 Tbsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  7. Make ready 1 Tbsp Cumin & Coriander seed roasted & crushed
  8. Take 1 tsp Red chilli powder or to taste
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Garam masala
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Ginger & Garlic paste
  11. Get 1 tsp Red chilli flakes
  12. Make ready 1 onion thinly slice
  13. Prepare 2 Green chilli
  14. Take Fresh Coriander for garnish
  15. Prepare 4-5 Tbsp Vegetable oil for frying

Steps to make Masala Lamb Chops:
  1. Wash all chops well and dry. - Take a bowl add yogurt, mustard oil, lemon juice and all spices mix well and make a thick paste then add lamb chops and coat well cover and keep in refrigerator about 1 hour or up to 4 fours.
  2. Heat up oil in nonstick pan add onion and fry till nice light golden brown add lamb chops fry 8-10 mins add green chillies fry more 1-2 mins then add 1/4 cup boiled water cover pan with lid and simmer slow flame till all chops nice and tender.
