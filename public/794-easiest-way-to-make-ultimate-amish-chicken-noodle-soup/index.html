Steps to make AMISH CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP:
  1. Place water in a large soup pot. Add the raw chicken, saltand pepper. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes. Remove the chicken and let cool.
  2. Add the onion and celery and bring to a boil, boil for 10 minutes and add thecarrots, bring back to a boil and add the chicken bouilion. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Dice the cooled chicken. Add to the pot, add additional water to get back to 1 1/2 gallons
  4. Bring to aboil and add the noodles. Turn the heat off and let stand for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve or place in contaiers to freeze
