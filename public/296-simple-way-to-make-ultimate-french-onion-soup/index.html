The ingredients needed to make French onion soup:
  1. Make ready 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  2. Get 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  3. Prepare 4 large sweet onions
  4. Get 4 cup beef broth
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup red wine
  6. Get 1 tsp cornstarch
  7. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  8. Get 1 clove garlic
  9. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  10. Get 1 loaf french baguette cubed and toasted or curetons
  11. Make ready 8 oz swiss or gruyere cheese

Instructions to make French onion soup:
  1. In a large sauce-pan over low-medium heat, melt butter and oil, add thinly sliced onion, garlic and bay leaf
  2. saute covered slowly until translucent and golden about 20-25 minutes stirring occasionally
  3. add broth and bring to slow simmer, reduce heat and add wine.
  4. cook covered on low heat for 30 minutes
  5. add little water to cornstarch and add slowly to soup just to thicken very slightly
  6. remove bay leaf
  7. position rack 6-8" under boiler
  8. ladle soup into bowls and mound bread cubes over soup cover with cheese.
  9. place soup bowls on a cookie sheet for easier handling
  10. broiled 2-3 minutes or until cheese is browned and crusty careful not to burn
