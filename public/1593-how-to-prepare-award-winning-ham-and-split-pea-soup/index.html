The ingredients needed to make Ham and Split Pea Soup:
  1. Get 2 T Butter
  2. Take 1/2 Onion (diced)
  3. Take 2 Celery ribs (diced)
  4. Make ready 3 Cloves Garlic (sliced)
  5. Make ready 1 qt Chicken Stock
  6. Prepare 2 1/2 C Water
  7. Prepare 1 lb Dried Split Peas (rinsed)
  8. Make ready 1 lb Ham (cubed)
  9. Take 1 Bay Leaf
  10. Prepare to taste Salt and Pepper
Instructions to make Ham and Split Pea Soup:
  1. Cook onion, celery, and garlic in butter over med-low heat until onions are translucent (not brown)
  2. Add remaining ingredients. Stir together. Simmer slowly, stirring occasionally, until peas are tender and soup is thickened (about 1 hour & 15 min)
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
