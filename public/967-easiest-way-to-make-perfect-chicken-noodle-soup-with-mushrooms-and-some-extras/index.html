The ingredients needed to make Chicken noodle soup with mushrooms and some extras:
  1. Take 2 cups roasted chicken (left over or store)
  2. Get 2 tablespoons butter
  3. Get 1 whole onion
  4. Prepare 2 green onion stalks
  5. Take 1 bouillon cube
  6. Prepare 1 Tbsp pepper
  7. Get 1/2 Tbsp adobo seasoning
  8. Get 6 whole allspice
  9. Make ready 1 pinch cardamom (optional)
  10. Take 1/2 cup mushrooms (your choice)
  11. Get 1 can chicken broth
  12. Make ready 2-4 cups water
  13. Get 2 cups egg noodles
  14. Get Boiled eggs ( I made 4)
  15. Make ready Seasonings:
  16. Prepare cilantro
  17. Prepare ketchup
  18. Make ready soy sauce
  19. Prepare hot sauce

Instructions to make Chicken noodle soup with mushrooms and some extras:
  1. Take roasted chicken and 2 tablespoons of butter and let that simmer together, stirring it.
  2. Then add adobo, garlic, pepper, cardamom, green onions, onions, bouillon cube, stir then add chicken broth and water.
  3. Let that come to a boil then add noodles turn heat back down to med. P.S. make sure eggs are boiling as well
  4. Let it cook till noodles are done and serve.
  5. I add 1 egg, cilantro, soy sauce, ketchup, hot sauce and more green onions.
  6. Most call this yaka mein but i just call it chicken soup with mushrooms and some extras. As with any recipe you can switch up the seasonings to your liking but those other ingredients I wouldn't.
