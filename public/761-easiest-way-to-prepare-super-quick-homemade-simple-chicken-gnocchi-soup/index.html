The ingredients needed to make Simple Chicken Gnocchi Soup:
  1. Make ready unsalted butter
  2. Take olive oil
  3. Get small onion - finely chopped
  4. Get garlic - minced
  5. Get all purpose flour
  6. Get low sodium chicken broth
  7. Get half & half
  8. Take dried thyme
  9. Prepare celery seed
  10. Prepare of a rotisserie chicken (white and dark meat) - chopped
  11. Prepare (packed) fresh spinach - roughly chopped
  12. Prepare package gnocchi
  13. Take salt and pepper
Instructions to make Simple Chicken Gnocchi Soup:
  1. Heat a 2 quart sauce pan over medium heat. Add butter and oil. As soon as butter melts add onion and garlic. Sautée until onion is translucent, stirring often.
  2. Wisk in flour. Cook 2 minutes, wisking frequently.
  3. Wisk in chicken broth, half & half, thyme, celery seed, and a pinch of salt. Bring to a simmer, wisking frequently. Simmer until thickened. About 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in chicken, gnocchi, and spinach. Simmer 5 minutes more, stirring often. Taste, adjust seasoning as desired. Serve with crusty bread. Enjoy!
  5. See notes below ⤵
  6. Note1: This makes a hearty, thick soup. If you would like your soup a little thinner you can add up to 1 more cup of chicken broth.
  7. Note2: If you want to cut some of the fat you can replace 1 cup of half & half with milk. You could go with all milk, but then it wouldn't have the same richness.
