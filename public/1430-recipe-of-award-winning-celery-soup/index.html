The ingredients needed to make Celery soup!!:
  1. Get 6 celery stalks
  2. Prepare half large onion
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup Flour
  4. Make ready 11/2 cup Milk
  5. Prepare 11/2 cup cup Chicken broth
  6. Make ready salt
  7. Take Pepper
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup Butter

Steps to make Celery soup!!:
  1. Take your sauce pan add 1/3 cup of butter melt.
  2. Then take your celery and onion and sautee till they are very soft
  3. Now add your 1/3 cup of flour and whisk until all flour is not noticed any longer.
  4. Now take your 11/2 cup of milk and whisk all together till smooth.
  5. Now add your 11/2cup of chicken stock.
  6. And finishing touch some salt and pepper.give one last good stir and simmer just a few minutes.
  7. Let cool !! and eat.
