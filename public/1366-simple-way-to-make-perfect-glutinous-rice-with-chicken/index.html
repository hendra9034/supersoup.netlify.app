The ingredients needed to make Glutinous Rice with Chicken:
  1. Make ready Glutinous rice (soaked over night)
  2. Take 1.5 tbsp corn flour
  3. Make ready Chinese sausage (thinly sliced)
  4. Take Marinate (overnight)
  5. Get Boiled Eggs
  6. Make ready Mushrooms
  7. Get 2 strips Pork belly (cut into slices)
  8. Prepare 3 Chicken legs/thighs (deboned, cut into thin bite size)
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  10. Take 1 tsp Soya sauce
  11. Get 1 tsp Oyster sauce
  12. Make ready 1 tsp brown sugar
  13. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp shao xing wine
  15. Make ready 1 tsp cooking oil
  16. Take sprinkle of white pepper
  17. Get Sauce For the rice
  18. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  19. Take 1 tsp soya sauce
  20. Make ready 1 tsp sesame oil
  21. Get 1/2 tsp white pepper
  22. Prepare 1/2 tsp brown sugar
  23. Get 3 tbsp lard
  24. Prepare 3 tbsp mushroom water
  25. Get 1 tbsp oyster sauce
Instructions to make Glutinous Rice with Chicken:
  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for overnight.
  2. Soak the mushroom in warm water for 10 mins, reserve the water.
  3. Marinate all the ingredients (including mushroom) and mix them all well.
  4. Then remove the mushroom from the marinate, add corn flour to the marinate. Marinate over night.
  5. Next day. Remove the water and use just enough water to cover the rice. Steam the rice for 15 mins on heat 7.
  6. Prepare the boil egg (medium heat with lid cover cook for 10 mins).
  7. Once the rice is steamed then add the sauce ingredients to the rice and mix well.
  8. Prepare a few small rice bowl, add marinated chicken, pork, egg, mushroom and Chinese sausage at the bottom, topping with the rice, press it down.
  9. Place the bowl in the steamer and before you steam them, add 1 tbsp of mushroom water onto each rice bowl.
  10. Steam the rice for 30 mins on high heat.
