The ingredients needed to make Tomyam Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready A 📌
  2. Make ready 3-4 c cold rice
  3. Make ready 2 eggs - lightly beaten
  4. Prepare 1 shallot - thinly sliced
  5. Make ready 1 small brown onion - finely chopped
  6. Make ready 1 C sliced fish ball or fish cake
  7. Get (Or use #prawns, #squid, #chicken, chopped #veggies for #vegetarian)
  8. Prepare Frozen mixed veggies (optional)
  9. Get 1 tbsp tomyam paste
  10. Prepare 5-6 Keffir lime leaves
  11. Make ready B. Seasonings
  12. Take 1 tsp Chicken seasoning powder/ bouillon powder (or use as needed)
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp - 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  14. Get Notes:
  15. Take Add few tablespoons of crushed bird eyed chilies for extra heat
Instructions to make Tomyam Fried Rice:
  1. Prep all ingredients. Cut, chop & mince.
  2. Heat enough cooking oil in a deep pan. Add beaten eggs and scramble until just set. Push aside. Next sauté shallot, onions & mixed veggies (if using) stir to combine well. Add a tbsp of tomyam paste. Stir again until aromatic.
  3. Add keffir lime leaves & your preferred #protein (diced #chicken/#prawns/#squid/#fishball/#fishcake). Cook until done. Add the rice & toss well with the rest of the ingredients. Cook over high heat. Keep stirring for few minutes (4-5mins) until rice is nicely coated and dried.
  4. Serve with fried egg (or sunny side up egg) and few slices of cucumber
