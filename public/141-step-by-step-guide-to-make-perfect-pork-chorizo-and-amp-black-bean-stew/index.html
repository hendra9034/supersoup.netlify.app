The ingredients needed to make Pork, Chorizo & Black Bean Stew:
  1. Take 250-300 g Pork Belly *cut into chunky pieces
  2. Make ready 1 Chorizo Sausage *thickly sliced
  3. Take 100 g thick slice Bacon *rind removed, diced
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon Oil *I used Canola Oil
  5. Get 1 Onion *finely chopped
  6. Prepare 1-2 cloves Garlic *finely chopped
  7. Take 1 canned (400g) Diced Tomatoes
  8. Prepare 1 cup Chicken Stock OR Stock of your choice
  9. Take 1 Bay Leaf
  10. Prepare 1 canned (*400g) Black Beans *drained
  11. Prepare Salt & Black Pepper
  12. Get Cooked Rice to serve

Steps to make Pork, Chorizo & Black Bean Stew:
  1. Heat Oil in a pot over medium heat, cook Pork, Chorizo and Bacon. When nicely browned, add Onion and Garlic, and stir for a few minutes.
  2. Add canned Tomatoes, Chicken Stock (OR Stock of your choice) and Bay Leaf, cover with a lid, and simmer over low heat for 30 to 60 minutes.
  3. Add drained Black Beans, season with Salt & Black Pepper, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the soup is thickened.
  4. Serve with freshly cooked Rice.
