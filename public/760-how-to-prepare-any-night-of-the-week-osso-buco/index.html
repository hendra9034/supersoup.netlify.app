The ingredients needed to make Osso Buco:
  1. Take 1 kg beef shanks
  2. Make ready 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  3. Take 1 bay leaf
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp whole cloves
  5. Make ready flour for dredging
  6. Get 1 small carrot, diced
  7. Prepare 1 stalk celery, diced
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato paste
  9. Make ready salt and pepper to taste
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  11. Prepare 1 cup white wine
  12. Get 3 cups chicken/vegetable stock or water
Steps to make Osso Buco:
  1. Place the rosemary, thyme, bay leaf, and cloves inside an empty tea bag or wrap inside a cheesecloth tied with twine. This is the bouquet garni.
  2. In a saucepan, heat oil on medium high heat until smoking. Dredge the beef shanks in flour and fry on all sides until browned, about 3 minutes per side, and set aside.
  3. In the same saucepan, add carrot and celery. Season with a bit of salt, and saut√© until soft, about 8 minutes. Add tomato paste, and mix well.
  4. Return beef shanks and add white wine, reducing liquid to half, about 5 minutes. Add bouquet garni and 2 cups stock/water and reduce heat to low. Let simmer until the meat is tender, about 1¬Ω to 2 hours, turning shanks periodically and adding more stock/water as needed so that the liquid remains about ¬æ up the shanks.
  5. Remove the shanks from the pot to a serving plate. Remove and discard bouquet garni. Pour juices and sauce from the pot over the shanks and serve, topped with gremolata or chimichurri.
