The ingredients needed to make Spicy Beef and Cabbage Soup:
  1. Prepare ground beef
  2. Take onion, chopped
  3. Get cabbage, chopped
  4. Make ready kidney beans, 16 oz., drained
  5. Prepare water
  6. Prepare tomato sauce
  7. Take ground cumin
  8. Make ready salt
  9. Prepare ground black pepper
  10. Take beef bouillon cubes
  11. Make ready crushed red pepper flakes
  12. Prepare Worcestershire sauce
  13. Take diced tomatoes
Steps to make Spicy Beef and Cabbage Soup:
  1. brown ground beef and onion in large pot.
  2. drain fat after beef is browned.
  3. add in rest of ingredients.
  4. bring to boil.
  5. simmer until cabbage is tender, usually 30 to 45 minutes.
