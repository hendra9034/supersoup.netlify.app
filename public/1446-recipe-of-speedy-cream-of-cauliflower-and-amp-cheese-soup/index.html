The ingredients needed to make Cream of Cauliflower & Cheese Soup:
  1. Take 1 head cauliflower
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Take 1/2 red onion diced
  4. Get 1 tsp minced garlic
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  6. Get 4 1/2 cup chicken stock
  7. Make ready 4 sprigs thyme chopped
  8. Get 1 cup shredded cheese (any type) I used double cheddar
  9. Take Fresh ground pepper
  10. Prepare Dash salt
  11. Get Chili peppers (Optional for a kick)
  12. Make ready Chives (Optional for color)

Steps to make Cream of Cauliflower & Cheese Soup:
  1. Prep your cauliflower into small pieces removing leaves & stock
  2. Pour olive oil in medium pot with garlic and saute for few minutes then add chicken stock, heavy cream & cauliflower and simmer
  3. Add chopped thyme, pepper & salt then bring pot to a small boil to fully cook cauliflower pieces
  4. When fully cooked use a blender/ immersion blender to cream up the soup then add shredded cheese stir until melted then serve
