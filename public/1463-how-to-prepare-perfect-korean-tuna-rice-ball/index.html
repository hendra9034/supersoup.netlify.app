The ingredients needed to make Korean tuna rice ball:
  1. Make ready 2 bowls sticky rice （Glutinous rice or Japanese rice）
  2. Get 1 can tuna
  3. Get 4-6 spoons seasoned seaweed
  4. Make ready 2 spoons mayo
  5. Get 1 spoon sesame oil
  6. Prepare 4 spoons frozen sweet corn
  7. Take 4 spoons frozen green beans
  8. Prepare 2 spoons soy sauce
  9. Get 1 spoon salt
  10. Take 1 spoon black pepper
