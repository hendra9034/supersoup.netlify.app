Instructions to make Simple French Onion Soup:
  1. Boil water in large pot, add bouillon cubes and Worcestershire sauce (maybe 5-6 splashes or a couple of table spoons)
  2. Thinly slice onions into into half rings.
  3. Separated large skillet melt 1-2 teaspoons butter. Add small portion of onions, cook until browned. Repeat butter and onions until entire batch nicely browned and wilted.
  4. Add browned onions to boiling beef broth pot. Turn down heat and simmer for 4 hours.
  5. Add crustini bread slice, thin mozarella slice. Brown in oven or serve as is… Mmm
