  1. Take 1 kg split peas

  3. Make ready 1 kg barley
  4. Get 1 cube chicken stock
  5. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
  6. Prepare 250 ml brown onion powdered soup
  7. Prepare 125 ml hearty beef powdered soup
  8. Get To taste ground pepper
  9. Prepare To taste salt
