The ingredients needed to make Taco Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 lb ground beef
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Make ready 1 tbs minced garlic
  4. Get 1 lb bag frozen corn
  5. Make ready 1 can rotel
  6. Prepare 1 can black beans
  7. Take 1 can diced tomatoes
  8. Make ready 2 can diced green chillies
  9. Prepare 2 can red Enchilada sauce
  10. Get 1 Tbs tomato paste
  11. Get 6 cup beef broth reduce sodium
  12. Make ready 2 taco season pack
  13. Get Optional toppings
  14. Make ready Shredded cheese
  15. Take Sour cream
  16. Get jalapeños
  17. Get Sliced avocado
  18. Prepare Corn chips
  19. Get Tortilla chips
Steps to make Taco Soup:
  1. Brown ground beef, drain off fat, return to pot. Toss in onions and cook until tender, add garlic, cook for a few more mins.
  2. Add all other ingredients to the pot. Cook for about 30 mins.
  3. Serve with your favorite toppings, Cheese, sour cream etc. Goes well with a side of scoop style tortillas, or scoop style corn chips for soup scooping!
