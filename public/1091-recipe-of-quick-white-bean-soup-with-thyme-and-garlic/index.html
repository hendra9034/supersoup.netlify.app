The ingredients needed to make White Bean Soup with Thyme and Garlic:
  1. Make ready 2 (15.5 oz) cans Great Northern Beans
  2. Prepare 1/2 onion (about 1/4 cup diced)
  3. Prepare 5 small peeled carrots (chopped)
  4. Make ready 2 small celery stalks (chopped)
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup olive oil
  6. Prepare 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  7. Take 2 sprigs fresh thyme, pull the leaves off the stem
  8. Make ready Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions to make White Bean Soup with Thyme and Garlic:
  1. Heat pan on medium heat. Add 1/4 cup of olive oil. Saute onions until shimmering.
  2. Add celery and carrots. Cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and thyme. Cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  4. Drain and add white beans. Cook for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
