The ingredients needed to make Bok Choy Prawn Spring Roll:
  1. Prepare 6 sheets Filo pastry
  2. Prepare 300 g Fresh prawn
  3. Prepare 1 Bok Choy
  4. Take 1 Spring onion
  5. Make ready 1 Egg
  6. Take Ginger 1 small chunk
  7. Prepare 2 Tablespoons Corn flour
  8. Take Seasoning
  9. Get 5 g Sugar
  10. Take 8 g Salt
  11. Make ready 1 g White pepper powder
  12. Get 2 Tablespoons Rice wine
  13. Take Batter (for sealing)
  14. Make ready 15 g Plain flour
  15. Prepare 4-5 teaspoons Water

Steps to make Bok Choy Prawn Spring Roll:
  1. Use kitchen roll to dry the prawns a bit. Put the prawns, egg and all seasoning ingredients in the food processor and blend them well.
  2. Add in the corn flour and mix well.
  3. Add in minced ginger, chopped Bok choy and spring onion. Mix them all together.
  4. Make the batter for sealing. Take a sheet of filo pastry and place some prawn filling. Fold and roll pastry up and then smear the batter to seal the end.
