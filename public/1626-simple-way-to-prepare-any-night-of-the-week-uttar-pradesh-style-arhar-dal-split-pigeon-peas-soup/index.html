The ingredients needed to make Uttar Pradesh Style Arhar Dal Split pigeon peas soup:
  1. Get 1 cup Arhar/ tur dal
  2. Take 1 onion,thin sliced
  3. Prepare 1 tomato/ deseeded & small diced
  4. Get 2 whole dry red chillies
  5. Prepare 1 tsp mustard seeds
  6. Get 1/4 tsp asafoetida
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  8. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  10. Get 1 tsp ginger paste
  11. Take to taste Salt
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  13. Make ready 1 tsp mustard oil
  14. Get 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Steps to make Uttar Pradesh Style Arhar Dal Split pigeon peas soup:
  1. Wash & soak the arhar/toor dal.
  2. Put it into pressure cooker & add salt, turmeric powder & water.
  3. Cook it in for 3-4 whistles.Let the pressure settle down.
  4. Add 1 tsp of mustard oil in a kadai.Once the oil is hot, add whole dry red chillies,mustard seeds, asafoerida & let them crackle.
  5. Add the onions & fry until it becomes translucent.
  6. Add ginger paste, tomatoes & cook for 2 mints till the raw smell of ginger goes away.
  7. Add salt,sugar, turmeric powder, red chlli powder,dhania powder & give a nice mix.
  8. Add the boiled dal, water according to your preferable consistency & let it boil.
  9. Sprinkle chopped coriander leaves,lemon juice & switch off the flame.
  10. Transfer the Utter Pradesh style Arhar Dal into a serving bowl & serve hot with steamed rice, pickle,papad & enjoy.
