The ingredients needed to make Chicken Soup:
  1. Make ready Chicken Carcass
  2. Prepare Chicken Breast
  3. Get Bunjut Sup Tulang
  4. Prepare Potatoes
  5. Get Carrot
  6. Get Ripe Tomato
  7. Take Stack of Green Onions
  8. Make ready Little Bit of Cilantro
  9. Take Salt
  10. Get Chicken Stock
  11. Get Water
  12. Take Cooking Oil
  13. Prepare Shallots
  14. Get Garlic Cloves
  15. Prepare Ginger
Steps to make Chicken Soup:
  1. Put Shallots, garlics and ginger together into blender.
  2. Heat up cooking oil into the pan. Add the Blended ingredients. Stir until it smell good.
  3. Pour water into the pan. Stir for little bit then add potatoes and carrot. Wait until the water boiled.
  4. Add chicken, tomato, salt, Sup Tulang packet, chicken stock and stir. Cook until the potatoes tender.
  5. Add Green Onions and cilantro. Ready to eat!
