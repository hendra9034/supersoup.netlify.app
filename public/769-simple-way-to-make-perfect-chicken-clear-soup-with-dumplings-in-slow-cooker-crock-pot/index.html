The ingredients needed to make Chicken Clear Soup with Dumplings in Slow Cooker (Crock pot):
  1. Make ready chicken broth
  2. Take chicken filet, cut into pieces
  3. Get shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  4. Make ready carrots, peeled and sliced
  5. Take chopped celery
  6. Take salt
  7. Take Crushed black pepper
  8. Take garlic powder (or garlic paste)
  9. Make ready dumplings:
  10. Make ready flour
  11. Make ready parsley flakes
  12. Get salt
  13. Get water
Steps to make Chicken Clear Soup with Dumplings in Slow Cooker (Crock pot):
  1. Combine all ingredients (except for dumplings) in a slow cooker. Stir well. Cook HIGH. My slow cooker is small and the soup should be ready in a couple of hours, but please adjust the timing to your slow cooker.
  2. After a couple of hours, make the dumplings. Combine all ingredients. It should form a thick paste.
  3. Spoon the paste to the soup, make sure the spooned parts do not touch each other. Cover again.
  4. After thirty minutes or so, your soup is ready!
