The ingredients needed to make French onion soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  2. Take 2 tbsp butter
  3. Take 3 onions, thinly sliced
  4. Take 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp flour
  7. Take 4 cup beef broth
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup white wine
  9. Prepare 8 slice crusty french bread
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup swiss cheese

Steps to make French onion soup:
  1. cook onions in oil & butter over med. heat until soft. add sugar & salt & cook for 15 min. stir in flour & cook 3 min.
  2. stir onion mixture, broth & wine in slow cooker & cook on low for 6-8 hrs.
  3. toast bread, sprinkle with cheese & broil until bubbly. serve soup topped with bread.
