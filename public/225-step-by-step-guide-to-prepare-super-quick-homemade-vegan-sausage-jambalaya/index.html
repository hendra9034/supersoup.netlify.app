The ingredients needed to make Vegan sausage jambalaya:
  1. Make ready sunflower oil
  2. Make ready 6-8 vegan sausages (I like the chorizo-style sausages for this dish, but any kind is fine), chopped into pieces about 2 cm long
  3. Get 1 large onion
  4. Prepare 1 tsp minced garlic (or 2 chopped cloves)
  5. Take 2 tbsp tomato puree
  6. Take 3 tbsp cajun spice mix (or use the spices listed below)
  7. Get 1 tbsp brown sugar
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
  9. Make ready 1-2 peppers, any colour
  10. Make ready 4 small / medium carrots
  11. Get 500 ml vegetable stock
  12. Get 2 cups long grain rice
  13. Take 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  14. Take 1 tin red kidney beans
  15. Get Other vegetables if you like. A double-handful of frozen sweetcorn adds some nice colour, for example
  16. Prepare Cajun spice mix
  17. Get 2 tsp chilli powder (mild for me!)
  18. Get 1 tsp cumin
  19. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  20. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  21. Prepare 1 tsp sweet paprika
  22. Prepare 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  23. Take 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  24. Make ready 1 tsp oregano
  25. Make ready 1 tsp coriander leaf

Steps to make Vegan sausage jambalaya:
  1. Heat the oil in a large casserole dish over a low heat. Add the sausages. Fry gently while you chop the onion. Then add the onion, garlic and tomato puree. Cook for a few minutes to soften the onion.
  2. Now stir in the spices, sugar and lemon juice. Let that fry gently while you chop the pepper and carrots. Add the pepper, carrots, and a little stock to keep the mixture moist. Cook that for a few minutes while you measure out the rice, open the cans and drain / rinse the beans.
  3. Add the rice, tomatoes, beans and any other vegetables you'd like to use. Stir. Pour over the stock. Cover the pan and leave to simmer for 20 to 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
