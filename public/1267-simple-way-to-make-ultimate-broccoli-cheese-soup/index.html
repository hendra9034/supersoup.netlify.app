The ingredients needed to make Broccoli cheese soup:
  1. Get 4 cup chicken broth
  2. Get 1 cup water
  3. Make ready 1 cup Half and half
  4. Take 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  5. Take 1/2 cup minced onion
  6. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper
  7. Get 4 cup broccoli florets
  8. Get 2 cup grated cheddar cheese

Steps to make Broccoli cheese soup:
  1. Add broccoli and simmer for 15-20 minutes, until broccoli is tender but not soft.
  2. In a large saucepan, whisk together chicken broth, water, half and half, flour, onion and pepper. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring often, then reduce heat to low.
  3. Stir in cheese and heat until melted completely. Remove from heat at once.
