The ingredients needed to make T.G.I.F Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Take 4 cup Chicken Broth
  2. Prepare 1 cup Water
  3. Take 1 cup Half n Half
  4. Make ready 5 slice Cheese
  5. Prepare 12 cup Flour
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Dried Onion
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp Pepper
  8. Get 4 cup Broccoli Florets
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup Shredded Cheese (garnish)
Steps to make T.G.I.F Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Combine broth, water, half n half, cheese, flour, onion and pepper in large pot.
  2. Whisk to combine and to break up any lumps of flour, then turn heat to med/high.
  3. Bring soup to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  4. Add broccoli to soup and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until broccoli is tender but not soft.
