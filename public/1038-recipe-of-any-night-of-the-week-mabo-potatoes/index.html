The ingredients needed to make Mabo Potatoes:
  1. Get 500 g Potatoes *peeled and cut into bite-size pieces
  2. Make ready 1 tablespoon Oil
  3. Make ready 250-300 g Pork Mince
  4. Prepare 1 clove Garlic *finely chopped
  5. Make ready 1 small piece Ginger *finely chopped OR grated
  6. Prepare 4 Spring Onions *finely chopped, save some for topping
  7. Take Sauce
  8. Get 1 tablespoon Dark Colour Miso
  9. Prepare 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon Sugar
  11. Make ready 2-3 teaspoons Toban Djan (Chili Bean Sauce)
  12. Take 2 tablespoons Sake (Rice Wine)
  13. Prepare 1 cup Chicken Stock *OR 1 cup Water & 1 teaspoon Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  14. Prepare 1 tablespoon Sesame Oil
  15. Take 1 tablespoon Potato Starch

Steps to make Mabo Potatoes:
  1. Peel Potatoes, cut into bite-size pieces, cook in the salted Water until soft, then drain.
  2. Place all the Sauce ingredients in a mixing bowl and mix well. This has to be done before you start cooking Pork Mince.
  3. Heat Oil in a large frying pan or a wok, cook Pork Mince, Garlic, Ginger and Spring Onions, breaking up the mince.
  4. Before you add the sauce to the pan, mix it well again. Then pour in the sauce and bring it to the boil and let it thicken.
  5. Add the drained Potato and gently mix and combine. Sprinkle with the saved Spring Onion and serve.
