The ingredients needed to make Fish red curry dumplings with wood ear mushroom:
  1. Prepare 150-200 g white meat fish
  2. Get 20 cm Spring onion
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon red curry paste
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon nampula
  5. Prepare 4 green beans
  6. Get 2 big wood ear mushroom
