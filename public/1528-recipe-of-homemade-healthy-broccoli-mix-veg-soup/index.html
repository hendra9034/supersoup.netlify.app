The ingredients needed to make Healthy Broccoli Mix Veg Soup:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup carrot chopped
  2. Get 1/2 cup capsicum chopped
  3. Get 4 Garlic cloves chopped
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup onions chopped
  5. Get 2 cups Broccoli broken into small florets
  6. Make ready 1/4 th cup sweet corn kernels
  7. Get 1/4 th cup French Beans cut diagonally (small)
  8. Take 2 cups vegetable broth (by boiling 1 cup mixed vegetables like potato, cabbage, onion, capsicum with a pinch salt and water and sieving them)
  9. Prepare 1/4 th teaspoon sugar(optional)
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper powder
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon Olive oil
  12. Prepare 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  13. Get 1 teaspoon fresh cream(optional)
  14. Take 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  15. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon or to taste Salt
Instructions to make Healthy Broccoli Mix Veg Soup:
  1. Heat olive oil in a wok on high heat. - Add garlic and onions and stir fry for a few a minute.
  2. Add all carrot, capsicum, french beans, broccoli, corn kernels and stir fry for 2 minutes on high flame.
  3. Add the vegetable broth to the vegetables while stirring continuously.
  4. Add black pepper, vinegar, oregano sugar and salt.
  5. Mix well and add 1 cup of water. - Cook for 2-3 minutes and bring to a boil.
  6. Sprinkle some oregano, according to choice and enjoy hot and healthy soup
