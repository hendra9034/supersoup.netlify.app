The ingredients needed to make Balti Chicken:
  1. Get 1 kg chicken, skinned
  2. Prepare 2 union, sliced
  3. Take 1 tomatoes, halved and sliced
  4. Make ready 1 small cinnamon stick
  5. Prepare 2 black cardamon pods
  6. Take 4 black peppercorns
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  8. Get 1 tsp ginger pulp
  9. Take 1 tsp garlic pulp
  10. Get 1 tsp garam masala
  11. Get 1 tsp chili powder
  12. Take 1 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 2 tbsp plain natural yogurt
  14. Get 4 tbsp lemon juice
  15. Prepare 1 bunch chopped coriander
  16. Prepare 2 green chilies, chopped.
Instructions to make Balti Chicken:
  1. Heat the oil in the wok. Throw in the unions and fry until they are golden brown.
  2. Add the tomatoes and stir well.
  3. Add cinnamon stick, cardamoms, peppercorns, cumin seeds, ginger, garlic, garam masala, chili powder and salt. Lower the heat and cook for 5 mins.
  4. When cooked put half the ingredients into blender to make into puree.
  5. Add the chicken pieces, cover the wok with lid. Check occasionally and stir fry for at least 7 mins or until the spice mixture has completely penetrated the chicken pieces.
  6. Add the yogurt in.
  7. Lower the heat and cover the wok with lid. Cook very gently for about 20 mins.
  8. Finally, add lemon juice, green chilies and coriander and slices of pepper and serve at once.
