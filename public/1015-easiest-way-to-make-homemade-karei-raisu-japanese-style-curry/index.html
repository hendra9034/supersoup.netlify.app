The ingredients needed to make Karei Raisu (Japanese style curry):
  1. Prepare 4 tbsp butter
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. Get 1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced into half- moons
  4. Get 500 g boneless fatty beef cut into 3/4 inch pieces
  5. Take 2 medium carrots, peeled and roughly chopped into irregular 3/4 inch pieces
  6. Prepare 1 big potato, peeled and roughly chopped into 3/4 inch pieces
  7. Make ready 1 block/ 220 g Golden curry (Japanese curry mix)
  8. Prepare 9 hard boiled quail eggs
  9. Make ready 1 tsp kosher salt
  10. Prepare 2 cups Japanese rice (Short grain white rice)
Steps to make Karei Raisu (Japanese style curry):
  1. Heat the butter and oil in a wok over medium high heat until the butter melts and bubbles. Add the onion and cook, stirring occasionally until it’s translucent and slightly wilted, about 5 minutes. 1. And the carrot and potato and stir well. 1. Add the beef and cook, stirring occasionally just until the pieces are no longer pink on the side, about 5 minutes. 1. Add 660 ml water. Bring the water to the boil over high heat, skim off any froth that appears on the surface, then reduce the heat to maintain a strong simmer. 1. Cook, stirring occasionally until the beef very tender, about 15 minutes.
  2. Add the curry paste and salt and continue cooking, stirring and scarping the bottom frequently for 10 minutes more. 1. Add quail eggs. 1. Divide the rice among shallow bowls, spoon on the curry and serve immediately. 1. To store, cool to room temperature and keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
