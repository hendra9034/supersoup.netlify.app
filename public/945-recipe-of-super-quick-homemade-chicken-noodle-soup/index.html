The ingredients needed to make Chicken noodle soup:
  1. Take 2 chicken breast
  2. Take 2 tbsp butter
  3. Get 1 1/3 cup water
  4. Get 1/2 bag baby carrots
  5. Make ready 1 stalk celery
  6. Prepare 1 carton vegetable or chicken broth I use vegetable
  7. Prepare 1/4 of onion more or less to taste
  8. Get Salt pepper to taster
  9. Make ready 1/4 tbsp Italian seasoning
  10. Take 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  11. Get 1/2 tsp tomato basil seasoning
  12. Take 3 cups water
  13. Make ready 4-5 lasagna noodles

Instructions to make Chicken noodle soup:
  1. After you boil chicken breast rinse off and cut into small chunks add 2 tbsp of butter and allow it soak up
  2. Add 1/2 cup of broth or enough to cover chicken bring to boil
  3. Thinly slice carrots and add to mix bring to boil add salt and pepper
  4. Thinly slice celery stalk and add onions add 1 1/3 cup water bring to boil
  5. Add all remaining seasonings here bring to boil
  6. Add noodles and remaining broth I like lasagna noodles broke up small for this soup or homemade style either way boil add remaining water cook till noodles are soft the long they sit the more flavor absorbs. enjoy
