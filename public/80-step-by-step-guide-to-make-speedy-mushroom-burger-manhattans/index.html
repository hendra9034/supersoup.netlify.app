The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Burger Manhattans:
  1. Make ready 1 lbs ground beef
  2. Get 2-3 cups mushroom gravy
  3. Get 1 pkt dry onion soup mix
  4. Make ready Splash Worcestershire sauce
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup bread crumbs
  6. Get 3 large potatoes
  7. Prepare 1 egg
  8. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Get 8 oz mushrooms
Steps to make Mushroom Burger Manhattans:
  1. Mix your ground beef, egg, onion soup mix, and Worcestershire sauce well and form into 4 equal patties.
  2. Skin and dice your potatoes and put them in a pot of water to boil, boil until soft.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the burgers in a skillet until done, saute the mushrooms,cover with gravy and cover with a lid to simmer.
  4. Mash your potatoes.
  5. Put a slice of bread on each plate, a scoop of potatoes on the bread, a burger patty on the potatoes, and cover with mushroom gravy.
  6. Serve hot.
