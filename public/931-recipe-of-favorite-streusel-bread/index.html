Steps to make Streusel Bread:
  1. Same dough making as cream cheese bread bun - (see recipe)
  2. Divide dough into even parts
  3. De - gas the dough and roll it into rectangular shape
  4. Brush one third part with melt butter
  5. Lay some pork floss on the buttered area.
  6. Cut the rest into strips
  7. Roll it up, bend and placed into a buttered muffin tray. - Put a tray of hot water on the bottom of the oven to make oven steamy and warm - Place them into oven for second raise - After raise, brush with egg wash
  8. Place the muffin tray at the low to medium rack. - Bake in 180 degrees for 25 minutes - And ready.
