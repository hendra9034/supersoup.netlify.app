The ingredients needed to make Tofu and Chinese leaf stir-fry:
  1. Prepare 1 whole block of tofu (firm)
  2. Get 1 whole Chinese leaf cabbage
  3. Get 3 tablespoons light soy sauce
  4. Prepare 2 heaped tablespoons of garlic and ginger paste
  5. Take 1 teaspoon pure sesame oil
  6. Get Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  7. Get Half a teaspoon of corn flour
  8. Prepare 1 heaped teaspoon of tomato ketchup
  9. Take 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  10. Get 2 diced onions
  11. Make ready Some chopped coriander

Steps to make Tofu and Chinese leaf stir-fry:
  1. Fry the tofu in some oil to make it slightly crispy, make sure to turn it so that both sides are crispy. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Then remove the tofu and set it aside. Start frying the onion, after a few minutes add the garlic and ginger paste.
  3. When softened add the Chinese leaf, I like to separate the stalk from the leaf as this takes longer to cook. Stir Fry the stalk for 2 minutes then proceed to add the leaf part
  4. Mix together the soy sauce, vinegar, corn flour, ketchup and sesame oil with a splash of water and then add to the cabbage. Check the seasoning and add salt and pepper accordingly. Add the tofu back in.
  5. Stir Fry for another few minutes and add fresh coriander just before serving.
