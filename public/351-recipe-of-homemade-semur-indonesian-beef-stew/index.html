The ingredients needed to make Semur (Indonesian Beef Stew):
  1. Take Meatballs
  2. Make ready 400 g minced beef
  3. Get 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tsp nutmeg
  6. Get 1/2 tsp white pepper
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy)
  8. Prepare Broth/soup stock
  9. Take 6 cups water
  10. Get 3 cubes beef bouillon
  11. Prepare 2 large tomatoes, or 3 for medium size tomatoes (chopped)
  12. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  13. Take 1/2 tsp white pepper
  14. Take 1/2-3/4 tbsp salt
  15. Make ready 3 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy)
  16. Take 2 small glass noodles (80g dried)
  17. Take Sides
  18. Prepare Carrots (steamed/boiled)
  19. Take Greenbeans (steamed/boiled)
  20. Get Fried/baked potatoe chips

Steps to make Semur (Indonesian Beef Stew):
  1. Put all meatballs ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Make some round meatballs and put aside.
  2. Put all soup stock ingredients in a pan (except the glass noodle), put it on a stove and bring it to boil.
  3. Add in meatballs to the broth. Once meatballs are cooked (float to the surface), add in the glass noodles and cook for another 5-10 mins. Taste the broth and adjust to your liking.
  4. Served your meatballs soup with steamed veggies and chips. You can cook the potatoes with the meatballs if you like, but I prefer mine cooked separately. I think it adds more depth to the dish.
