The ingredients needed to make Russian Cabbage Soup:
  1. Make ready 16 oz Package of sausage, spicy works best but any will do
  2. Take 1 medium onion
  3. Prepare 1 small cabbage
  4. Make ready 64 oz Bottle of spicy Bloody Mary mix. For less spice use v8
  5. Make ready 1 as needed Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Russian Cabbage Soup:
  1. Rough chop the onion brown it with the sausage
  2. Chop the cabbage add it and the Bloody Mary mix to the pot bring it to a boil
  3. Reduce to a simmer and cook until cabbage is soft.
  4. Salt and pepper to taste
