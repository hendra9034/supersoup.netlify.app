The ingredients needed to make Egyptian lentil soup:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup red lentil
  2. Get 1 tomato
  3. Take 1 potato
  4. Get 1 carrot
  5. Get 1 onion
  6. Get 2 cloves garlic
  7. Get Salt to your taste
  8. Get 1/2 tea spoon pepper
  9. Prepare 1/2 tea spoon turmeric
  10. Prepare 1/2 tea spoon curry
  11. Make ready 1 tea spoon cumun

Steps to make Egyptian lentil soup:
  1. First soak the lentil in water and leave it for 1 hour, then wash it very well
  2. Cut the tomato, potato, carrot, onion, and put all together with the lentil in a pot full of water and add the cloves
  3. Leave to boil until all ingredient are very soft
  4. Add all the seasoning
  5. Mix all together with hand mixture
  6. You can add water again if you find it very heavy
  7. Now it’s ready to serve with some crunchy Lebanese bread
