The ingredients needed to make Mediterranean Rice & Vegetable Loaf:
  1. Get 1 Onion, finely chopped
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil + 1 tbsp for vegetables
  3. Prepare Pinch saffron
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  5. Take 2 garlic cloves, crushed or finely grated
  6. Get Pinch chilli flakes
  7. Make ready 1 tin tomatoes, chopped (see tip)
  8. Make ready 3-4 sundried tomatoes in oil, drained and finely chopped
  9. Get 400 g risotto or paella rice
  10. Get 1 couple of good pinches of salt
  11. Make ready Freshly ground black pepper
  12. Prepare 1.2 litres hot chicken or vegetable stock
  13. Make ready 1 tsp dried basil
  14. Make ready Splash sushi seasoning vinegar or wine vinegar + 1 tsp sugar
  15. Take 1 bag frozen Mediterranean vegetables mix
  16. Get 1 smidge of Butter, for greasing the tin
  17. Get 125 g provolone piccante or pizza mozzarella or mixture of them
  18. Take 100 g roasted red peppers, peeled (see tip)
  19. Prepare 60 g /2 tbsp toasted pine nuts

Steps to make Mediterranean Rice & Vegetable Loaf:
  1. Saute the onion in the 2 tbsp olive oil with the Saffron in a large pan for 10 minutes on a low heat.
  2. Add the fennel seeds, garlic and chilli flakes and cook for another minute or two before adding the sundried tomatoes, tinned tomatoes and the rice.
  3. Increase the heat and cook for 5 minutes, simmering to reduce liquid. Season well.
  4. Have the stock simmering in a separate pan.
  5. Stir the rice and add a ladle of stock. Stir again. Once absorbed, repeat until all stock is used.
  6. Check the seasoning (and that the rice still has a little bite) by tasting. Add the dried basil, then remove from heat and, allow to cool.
  7. Heat oven to 180C.
  8. In a wok or large frying pan, add a tbsp of olive oil. Stir fry the vegetables until all liquid has disappeared and the vegetables are cooked through and becoming golden.
  9. Butter a 2 lb loaf tin and lay a strip of greaseproof paper along the bottom.
  10. Press 1/3 of the rice mixture into the bottom of the loaf tin.
  11. Then put in half the vegetables, peppers, half the pine nuts, followed by a layer of the cheese.
  13. If using a large tin you may be able to repeat step 11. Either way, finish with a layer of rice.
  14. Place the loaf tin in the oven and bake for 30 minutes, until golden brown. Remove and carefully run a knife around the edge, then turn out onto a serving plate.
  15. Slice to serve, eating it either hot or cold.
