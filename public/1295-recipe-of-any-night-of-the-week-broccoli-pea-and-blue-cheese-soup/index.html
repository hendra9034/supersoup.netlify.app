The ingredients needed to make Broccoli, Pea and blue cheese soup:
  1. Prepare 2 cups broccoli including the stems
  2. Prepare 2 cm wedge of blue cheese I used gambozola
  3. Get 1/2 cup peas
  4. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 1 chopped clove of garlic
  6. Take salt and white pepper to season
  7. Make ready pinch bicarbonate of soda
  8. Prepare 1 chicken or veg stock cube
