The ingredients needed to make My Portuguese Style Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 cups basmati rice
  2. Take 2 1/4 cups cold water
  3. Get 1 red onion diced
  4. Take 1/2 juice of a lemon
  5. Make ready 1 red chilli diced
  6. Make ready 1 chicken stock cube
  7. Take 2 tbsp tomato puree
  8. Take 1 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Get 1 tsp cumin
  10. Get 2 tsp smoked paprika
  11. Prepare 1 tsp oregano
  12. Get 2 tsp garlic powder
  13. Take 1 tsp parsley
  14. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  15. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
Steps to make My Portuguese Style Rice:
  1. Its very easy chop veg
  2. Put rice, stock cube, tomato puree, water, seasoning and veg into a pot mix well, bring to a boil then cover with a tight fitting lid and cook for 10 mins
  3. Once cooked allow to steam for 5 mins,then mix well serve….. enjoy
