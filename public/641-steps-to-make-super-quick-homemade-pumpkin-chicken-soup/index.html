The ingredients needed to make 南瓜鸡汤 Pumpkin Chicken Soup:
  1. Get 鸡肉 Chicken (500g)
  2. Prepare 南瓜 Pumpkin (300g)
  3. Make ready 老姜 Old ginger
  4. Take 枸杞子 Goji berries
Instructions to make 南瓜鸡汤 Pumpkin Chicken Soup:
  1. 南瓜切小块 Cut pumpkin into cubes
  2. 把南瓜，鸡肉，老姜，枸杞子加入压力锅 Put pumpkin, chicken, old ginger and goji berries into pressure cooker
  3. 加小撮盐和两碗水 Add some salt and two bowls of water
  4. 煮20分钟 Cook for 20mins
