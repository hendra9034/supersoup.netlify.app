The ingredients needed to make Jade corn soup and stuffed paneer corn patra:
  1. Make ready for Soup
  2. Get 250 gm Spinach purée
  3. Get 2 tablespoon carrot chopped
  4. Take 1 tablespoon green Beans
  5. Take 2 tablespoon american corn
  6. Get 250 ml vegetable stock
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon butter
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon garlic chopped
  10. Prepare as per test Salt
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon white paper powder
  12. Make ready 1 tablespoon Conflour

  3. Next add spinach purée sauté 2 minit then add vegetable stock and boil it
