The ingredients needed to make Tomato carpaccio with spring onion and ginger salsa:
  1. Get 2 cm piece of ginger, peeled and roughly chopped (10 g)
  2. Take 3 spring onion, very finely sliced (45g)
  3. Make ready 40 ml sunflower oil
  4. Prepare 2 tsp good quality sherry vinegar
  5. Prepare 400 beef tomatoes, sliced 2mm thick
  6. Get 1/4 green chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  7. Get 1 1/2 tbsp finely shredded coriander
  8. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  9. Get Flaked sea salt

Instructions to make Tomato carpaccio with spring onion and ginger salsa:
  1. Put the ginger and 1/2 tsp of flaked salt into a pestle and mortar and crush to a fine paste. Transfer to a bowl with the spring onions and stir to combine.
  2. Put the oil into a small pan and place in a low heat until just warm; you don’t want it to heat too much. Pour over the spring onions and add 1 tsp of vinegar. Stir together and set aside.
  3. Lay the tomatoes on a large platter, with the slices slightly overlapping. - Season with 1/4 tsp of flaked salt and drizzle over the remaining tsp of vinegar.
  4. Spoon the spring onion and ginger salsa evenly over the tomatoes (use your hands to better effect), scatter over the chilli and coriander and finish with the olive oil. Enjoy
