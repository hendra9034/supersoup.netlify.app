The ingredients needed to make Buttery dinner rolls:
  1. Take 400 gram plain flour
  2. Get 1 cup warm milk
  3. Get 1/4 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup butter
  5. Take 1/4 cup yogurt
  6. Take 7 gram yeast
  7. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  8. Get 5 gram butter pieces for filling
  9. Take Milk for brushing

Instructions to make Buttery dinner rolls:
  1. In a bowl add warm milk, sugar and yogurt and mix well. - Add flour, instant yeast and salt. Mix until you form a dough. - Cover and let it rest for 20 minutes. - After 20 minutes add butter and mix until all incorporated. 1. Knead, pound and fold the dough until smooth and elastic. This will take about 8 minutes. - Place it in an oiled bowl and cover for 90 minutes or until double in size.
  2. Deflate the dough by punching out the air. - Flatten the dough and divide into 18 equal pieces. Make round balls and cover for 15 minutes. Flatten the dough and fill with chilled butter pieces and pinch the seams to seal.
  3. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish and cover for 30 minutes. - Brush the rolls with milk. - Bake in a preheated oven for 18 to 20 minutes at 180 C - Let it cool for 5 minutes and brush the rolls with melted butter.
