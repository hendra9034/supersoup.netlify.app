The ingredients needed to make My broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. Take 1 and 1/2 bricks of velveeta cheese
  2. Prepare 1/2 large onion diced
  3. Prepare 1 and 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  4. Prepare 4-5 crowns of broccoli
  5. Prepare 6 cups chicken broth
  6. Get 2 tablespoons butter
  7. Get 2 cups cheddar cheese
  8. Prepare 1 cup heavy cream

Steps to make My broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. Saute the onions and garlic in the butter until onion is soft, put into crock pot.
  2. Add 6 cups of chicken broth to crock pot on high temp.
  3. Once broth is hot add broccoli, diced small. Cook until broccoli is soft, time depends on size of broccoli.
  4. Add velveeta cheese and cheddar cheese to pot. Stir occasionally until cheese is completely melted. Then turn to low temp.
  5. Add heavy cream to soup to help thicken soup and add flavor. If soup is not thick enough mix corn starch and water then add till soup reaches desired consistency.
  6. I have also added shredded carrots to the soup to change it up. This recipe took 2 cups of carrots.
