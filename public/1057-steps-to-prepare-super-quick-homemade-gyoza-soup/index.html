The ingredients needed to make Gyoza Soup:
  1. Take 4 in piece of kombu
  2. Get 6 shitaki mushrooms, fresh or dried
  3. Prepare 6 cups water
  4. Prepare 1/2 a sweet onion sliced
  5. Get 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  6. Get 1 carrot, cut in sticks
  7. Get 2-3 inch piece of japanese daikon, cut in sticks
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp tamari or shoyu
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp mirin
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp sake
  11. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  12. Prepare Ground pepper, to taste
  13. Get 6-8 Shizo leaves, chopped
  14. Get 1 brown beech mushroom, trimmed and broken in bite sized pieces
  15. Get 1/2 of 1 baby bok choy, washed and chopped
  16. Get 1 large handful of garland, washed and chopped
  17. Prepare 6-8 frozen vegetable gyoza
  18. Make ready 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
Steps to make Gyoza Soup:
  1. Bring water to a low boil in a dutch oven or a large saucepan. Add kombu and shitaki mushrooms. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add onions, carrot, daikon and garlic. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Add tamari, mirin, sake, salt pepper and sesame oil.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 mins.
  5. Add garland, bok choy, beech mushroom and Shizo leaves. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add frozen gyoza. Cover and simmer 5 minutes. Turn heat off and let rest 5 minutes. Add scallions if desired. Add hondashi if needed.
  7. Serve in large noodle bowls.
