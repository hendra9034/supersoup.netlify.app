Steps to make Simply Grilled Sirloin Steak ðŸ¥©:
  1. Take the steaks out of the fridge and open the packaging to allow them to rest at room temp for ten minutes.
  2. Heat the grill pan nice and hot.
  3. Lightly oil the meat then mix the seasonings in a small bowl and rub them on both sides of the steak.
  4. Put the steak on the grill pan and cook for half of your cooking time (this will depend on how you like your steak and how big your steak is).
  5. Turn over only once and cook on the other side for the rest of the time you calculated. 1. Allow the steak to rest for 10 minutes before serving.
