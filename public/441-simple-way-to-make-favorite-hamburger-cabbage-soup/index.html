The ingredients needed to make Hamburger Cabbage Soup:
  1. Take Cabbage Chopped
  2. Prepare Chopped Celery
  3. Take Large Onion Diced (White)
  4. Make ready Beef Broth (32oz)
  5. Make ready Pepper
  6. Make ready ground beef
  7. Prepare tomato sauce (15 oz)
  8. Prepare Brown Sugar
  9. Take Ketchup
  10. Get Worcestershire Sauce
Steps to make Hamburger Cabbage Soup:
  1. Brown meat with 1/2 diced onion. Drain when browned.
  2. Put beef broth in pot with celery and rest of onion. Boil until tender.
  3. Once tender add all remaining ingredients except cabbage. Bring to boil. Then simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. After simmering add cabbage. Simmer for additional 10 minutes.
  5. Let cool at least 10 minutes before eating.
