The ingredients needed to make Dhal Lentil Soup:
  1. Get 8 oz dry lentils
  2. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  3. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  5. Take 3 oz diced onions
  6. Prepare 1/4 oz Dahl Masala Powder (cumin, coriander, paprika, cayenne, garam masala)
Steps to make Dhal Lentil Soup:
  1. Simmer lentils in water with salt and turmeric until mushy
  2. In skillet, heat oil, add onions and cook until clear. Add spice mix and fry until aromatic, about 1 minute
  3. Remove from heat and stir into cooked lentils. Serve hot
