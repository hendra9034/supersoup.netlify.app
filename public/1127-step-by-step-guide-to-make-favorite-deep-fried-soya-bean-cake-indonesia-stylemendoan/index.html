The ingredients needed to make Deep fried soya bean cake Indonesia style(mendoan):
  1. Take 400 gr soya bean cake cut into thin sliced
  2. Make ready 120 gr all purpose flour
  3. Take 20 gr rice flour
  4. Take 2 stalks spring onion chop finely
  5. Take 1/2 piece egg
  6. Prepare 1/4 sdt turmeric powder
  7. Make ready 1/4 salt
  8. Take 1/4 sdt pepper
  9. Prepare 200 ml water
  10. Prepare 400 ml oil for deep fried
