The ingredients needed to make Lentil & Potato Curry:
  1. Make ready 2 cups rice
  2. Prepare 250 g water
  3. Take Vegetable stock
  4. Take 75 g lentils
  5. Take Handful Mushrooms
  6. Make ready 1 x Onions
  7. Take 2 tablespoons mild curry powder
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  9. Make ready 500 g new potatoes, cut in half
  10. Prepare 4 tablespoons tinned coconut cream (I used 200ml milk)
  11. Make ready 400 g tin of chopped tomatoes

Steps to make Lentil & Potato Curry:
  1. In a saucepan of boiling water, cook the potatoes for 15 minutes until tender
  2. In a separate saucepan, heat the oil over a medium heat then fry the onions and curry powder for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the lentils, tomatoes & stock to the pan - cover and allow to simmer for 15 minutes
  4. When the potatoes have cooked, add them to the curry, along with the coconut cream or milk but milk makes sauce more runny - cook uncovered for a further 10 minutes, until the curry is thick and creamy
  5. Now serve with rice (which I used with the rice cooker and added turmeric to change its colour to yellow)
