The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  2. Take 6 each chicken bouillon cubes
  3. Make ready 5 cup water
  4. Get 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  5. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 3 cup milk
  7. Make ready 1 each pepper to taste
  8. Get 1 each 1 lb brick of Velveeta
  9. Prepare 2 each 10 oz box chopped Broccoli
  10. Prepare 1 each onion, chopped
  11. Prepare 1 box 1 lb box of Orzo

Instructions to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. cook onions in oil lightly to Brown
  2. add water (5-6 cups), bouillon cubes, garlic powder, salt and broccoli and cook for about 10 min
  3. cook orzo pasta according to package directions separately
  4. add orzo, milk and cheese, cook over low heat until cheese is melted and soup thickens.
