The ingredients needed to make Fresh from the oven - Chicken Sausage Roll, Garlic Bread and Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Make ready TangZhong (A)
  2. Prepare 15 g bread flour
  3. Prepare 65 ml water
  4. Make ready The main dough (B)
  5. Get 250 g bread flour
  6. Get 75 ml milk
  7. Make ready 40 g caster sugar
  8. Get 2 g Salt
  9. Prepare 1 egg
  10. Take 40 g Butter
  11. Make ready 3 g active dry yeast (dissolve in 2 tbsp of water)
  12. Get Others (C)
  13. Prepare 4 sausages, 10cm length for the sausage Rolls
  14. Take Egg wash, sesame seeds and butter to brush and glaze the bun
  15. Get Cinnamon, sugar and butter for the Cinnamon Butter
  16. Get Chopped garlic, parsley and butter for the garlic Bread
Steps to make Fresh from the oven - Chicken Sausage Roll, Garlic Bread and Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Prepare the TangZhong. Mix the bread flour and water (A) in a small pan, heat it slowly over low heat. When the slurry starts to become thicker, you can draw lines while stirring it. When this happens, remove it from heat.
  2. Dissolve the yeast with 2 tbsps of milk and 1 tbsps of sugar, and wait for five minutes until the yeast is activated.
  3. Mix all the ingredients in (A) and (B) except the butter with the electric mixer (use a dough hook) until it becomes anelastic mass.
  4. Add the butter, continue mixing until the butter is thoroughly absorbed.
  5. Cover, let it proof for 40 minutes or until it doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough. Divide into 3 portions.
  7. Roll out 1 portion out and cut it into 4 pieces. Roll it into balls and set aside to further proof for 30 minutes. Roll out the dough to form a long robe, about 3.5 to 4 times the length of the sausage.
  8. Branch the sausages in boiling hot water, and remove then part dry. - Looped the dough rope around the sausage. Tuck in both ends under the sausage.
  9. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it proof for 20 minutes. Apply some egg wash on the surface. Arrange on a lined baking tray.
  10. For the Cinnamon Rolls, Roll out the second portion on a floured surface till about 1 cm thick. Brush over with the Cinnamon butter. Then Roll it into a spiral log. Cut along the log to Form flat spiral discs measuring 2 cm thick. Arrange on a lined baking tray.
  11. For the garlic bread, use the third portion of the dough to form 2 to 3 elongated bread rolls and flatten slightly. Make several diagonal slits on the upper surface of the bread. Apply the garlic butter over the top generously. Arrange on a lined baking tray.
  12. Bake at 190째C/375째F for 20 minutes or until the crust turns golden brown.
