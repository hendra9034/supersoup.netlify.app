The ingredients needed to make Grandma's Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Prepare 5 Lb. Whole Chicken
  2. Prepare 2 Celery Sticks- cut 1 in big chunks, mince other
  3. Take 2 Medium Carrots- cut 1 in big chunks, mince other
  4. Make ready 1 Large Red onion, quarter half of it, and mince the other half
  5. Take 8 large Garlic Cloves or the whole head If your a garlic lover
  6. Make ready Half bunch of Parsley
  7. Prepare 2 Bay Leaves
  8. Make ready A Few Sprigs Fresh Thyme
  9. Make ready 1 Sprig Fresh Rosemary
  10. Prepare 2 Fresh Sage Leaves
  11. Prepare 1/2 Lemon, Quartered
  12. Get 2 TBS Butter
  13. Get 1 TBS. Black Pepper
  14. Prepare 1/8 Cup salt (I use Himilayan Pink Sea Salt)
  15. Make ready 1/2 LB. Homemade Noodles
  16. Get Optional- 1 Tbsp. Red Pepper Flakes

Instructions to make Grandma's Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Put the chicken in a large pot and fill with water until chicken is covered. Add 1 stick of celery(the big chunks) and the 1 carrot stick (big chunks) plus the rest of the ingredients except noodles. Bring to boil, then reduce to simmer. Put the lid on and check after an hour and 15 mins.
  2. Insert a thermometer into deepest part of breast until the internal temperature reaches 160. (Not 165) it will come to temperature after resting for 10 mins.
  3. At this point, if your chicken has reached temperature, remove the chicken and place on a large cutting board. Strain and SAVE the amazing broth you just made. Picture below is after shredding chicken and before straining. I added the bones back to broth and simmered for another 30 mins.
