The ingredients needed to make Vegetable soup with Naan bread:
  1. Prepare 2 medium potatoes peeled and diced
  2. Get 1 large carrot peeled and diced/sliced
  3. Prepare 2 medium baby marrows sliced
  4. Get 1 medium onion chopped
  5. Get 1 chicken stock
  6. Take 1 packet Knorr vegetable cup s soup
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon curry powder
  8. Take 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  9. Make ready Pinch salt or more when needed
