The ingredients needed to make Misomato Soup:
  1. Take 2 Tomatoes, roasted, peeled, chopped
  2. Get 3 Tbsps White Miso Paste
  3. Make ready 8-10 Green Beans, chopped
  4. Get 4 Small Garlic cloves
  5. Make ready 1 Potato, cut into cubes
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp Tomato sauce or paste
  7. Make ready Cube Chicken stock or Chicken Bouillon
  8. Take 2 Tbsps Neutral Oil
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 liters water
  10. Get 1 Bay Leaf
