The ingredients needed to make Cabbage Soup in Crockpot:
  1. Make ready 1 head cabbage, chopped
  2. Make ready 1 lb carrots, sliced
  3. Get 1 onion, chopped
  4. Get 4 Garlic cloves, chopped
  5. Prepare 5 celery stalks, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes
  7. Make ready 1/2 Tbs Italian Seasoning
  8. Take 1 Tsp Paprika
  9. Get 1 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
  10. Prepare 1 (32 oz) carton beef broth
  11. Take 8 grinds salt
  12. Make ready 8 grinds Pepper
  13. Prepare Cayenne to taste (optional)
