The ingredients needed to make Split Pea Soup (crockpot):
  1. Prepare 1 meaty Ham bone
  2. Make ready 1 lb bag dry split Peas
  3. Get 6 cups Water
  4. Make ready 1 large Onion, chopped
  5. Take 4 stick Celery, sliced
  6. Get 5 or 6 Chicken bullion cubes
  7. Take 1 tsp Thyme, Garlic powder and Black pepper, each
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Red pepper
  9. Prepare 1 bag mini Carrots

Steps to make Split Pea Soup (crockpot):
  1. Slice meat from ham bone, remove visible fat. Add bone and meat to crockpot.
  2. Add 6 cups water, and rest of ingredients except carrots.
  3. Cover and cook on high 5 hrs.
  4. Meanwhile cook carrots separately until soft.
  5. After 5 hrs remove bone from pot, break up big chunks of meat, then add cooked carrot.
  6. Serve with bread & butter.
