The ingredients needed to make Rice with Salami, Soy sauce, and Mashed Egg:
  1. Get 2 Eggs
  2. Prepare 1 Salami Sausage
  3. Take Some Rice
  4. Get 1 Tea Spoon Soy Sauce
  5. Get 1 ml Oil
Steps to make Rice with Salami, Soy sauce, and Mashed Egg:
  1. First, cut the salami into pieces.
  2. Turn on the cooking stove to high heat and let it heat up.
  3. Add some oil and pour the salami chunks.
  4. Then make the mashed egg and pour it in too.
  5. Pour the rice.
  6. Now add the soy sauce, then cook for 5 mins, and while you're waiting, you still need to stir them around.
  7. Finally, turn off the cooking stove and serve it on a plate.
