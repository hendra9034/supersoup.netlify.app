The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Pizza:
  1. Make ready 250 GM's flour
  2. Make ready Half cup water
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  4. Make ready 7 grams dry yeast
  5. Prepare Mozzarella cheese or a vegan substitute
  6. Prepare Tomato paste
  7. Prepare Toppings
  8. Get 2 tablespoons sugar
  9. Prepare Cooking oil

How to make easy vegetarian pizza at home. This is very easy, simple and Tasty. You can also make this in a thava or pan. Vegetarian pizza doesn't have to be boring!

Steps to make Vegetarian Pizza:
  1. In half cup of warm water, mix sugar and yeast. Cover it for 5-7 minutes or until bubbles start appearing.
  2. Mix the flour and salt. Knead the dough with this yeast water and 2 tablespoon oil.
  3. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes in a bowl. Keep it somewhere warm, near a kettle does the trick.
  4. Divide in 3 equal parts. Use a rolling pin to spread it flat.
  5. Preheat oven at 230 degrees C.
  6. Transfer the rolled pizza base on a pizza tray.
  7. Spread tomato paste evenly on the base. Add toppings, cover it with cheese.
  8. Cook it on the oven at 200 degrees for 14-15 minutes. Check if the cheese has melted and the base looks golden brown.
