The ingredients needed to make Feel Better Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  2. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Get 2 cup carrots, sliced
  4. Take 2 cup celery
  5. Make ready 1 large onion, chopped
  6. Prepare 1 lb boneless chicken chopped into 1" cubes
  7. Prepare 8 cup chicken stock
  8. Get 1 tsp taragon
  9. Prepare 1 tsp savory
  10. Prepare 1 tsp chicken seasonings
  11. Prepare 8 oz egg noodles
  12. Prepare salt
  13. Make ready black pepper

Steps to make Feel Better Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Saute carrots, celery, and onion with the butter and olive oil in a large stock pot till onion is translucent.
  2. Add chicken and saute until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken stock and spices. Simmer on low for about 1 hour.
  4. Add egg noodles and cook until noodles are done.
