The ingredients needed to make Toad In The Hole:
  1. Prepare 10-12 good quality pork or beef sausages
  2. Prepare 225 g plain flour, sifted
  3. Prepare 3 eggs
  4. Get 300 ml full fat Milk
  5. Get 1 tbsp lard or other fat + 2 tbsp olive oil
  6. Make ready Salt to taste
  7. Prepare Ground black pepper to taste
  8. Make ready Onion Gravy
  9. Take 1 dollop of butter
  10. Make ready 2 small onions, sliced thinly
  11. Take 1 beef stock cube
  12. Make ready 500 ml water
  13. Take 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  14. Prepare 1/2 tbsp plain flour
  15. Get 1 tsp light brown sugar

Instructions to make Toad In The Hole:
  1. Heat the oven to 200c.
  2. First to make the batter, whisk the eggs and milk together, then add the flour and season with salt and pepper. Leave to rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan or oven tray in the oven. Then add the fat/oil and place back in the oven. Oil should be piping hot so leave in the oven to heat for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Carefully add the sausages to the hot oil, and brown on all sides by turning them as needed, but don't cook through.
  5. Ensuring the oil is piping hot carefully pour the batter over and around the sausages (batter should start curling and cooking around the edges if the oil is hot enough).
  6. Cook for 30-35 or until cooked through, batter should have risen and be nice and golden brown on top.
  7. Serve with choice of vegetables or mash and Gravy. I served mine with carrot and potato mash and boiled sweetcorn topped with onion gravy.
  8. To make the gravy: First dissolve the stock cube in the water. Next heat the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat and add the onions, fry until soft. Then add the flour and stir until its fully absorbed. Add the mustard and sugar.
