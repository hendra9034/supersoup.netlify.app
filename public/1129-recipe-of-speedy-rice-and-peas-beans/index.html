The ingredients needed to make Rice and Peas! (Beans):
  1. Get 200 grams long grain rice
  2. Take 400 ml coconut milk
  3. Make ready 1/2 tbsp all spice
  4. Get 100 ml water
  5. Prepare 6-8 spring onions
  6. Take Salt and pepper
  7. Prepare 1 can kidney beans
