The ingredients needed to make Pot Roast:
  1. Take 3-4 lb pot roast
  2. Take 1 box Lipton Obion Mushroom recipe soup and dip mix
  3. Take salt
  4. Prepare pepper
  5. Prepare 1 lb carrots
  6. Take 8-10 idaho potatoes
Instructions to make Pot Roast:
  1. Preheat oven to 300.
  2. Salt and pepper roast in pan
  3. Empty both liptons packets into bowl and add hot water. Whisk to mix.
  4. Pour mixture over roast and add arm water until roast is almost covered.
  5. Cover roast with tin foil and put in oven for 5 hours.
  6. Wash and peel carrtsand potatoes and add to roast pan.
  7. Increase oven temp to 350 and cook for an additional hour to hour and 15 minutes.
