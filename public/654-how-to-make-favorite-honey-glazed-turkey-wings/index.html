The ingredients needed to make HONEY GLAZED TURKEY WINGS 蜜汁烤火鸡翼:
  1. Get 3 lb turkey wings, wing tips removed
  2. Prepare 4 large cloves garlic, minced
  3. Get 1/2 cup liquid honey
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp soy sauce
  5. Make ready 3 tbsp sunflower oil
  6. Get 3 tbsp lemon juice
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp chilli powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Get 1 tsp pepper

Steps to make HONEY GLAZED TURKEY WINGS 蜜汁烤火鸡翼:
  1. In a deep baking dish or large resealable plastic bag combine garlic, chilli powder, honey, soy sauce, oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper and ginger. Add Turkey wings and marinate overnight.
  2. Remove wings from marinade and place in large baking pan; reserve marinade. Bake uncovered, in 400ºF (200ºC) oven for 45 to 55 minutes. Drain off excess fat.
  3. In small saucepan, bring marinade to a boil. Boil gently for 3 to 4 minutes or until slightly thickened; pour over wings. Increase oven to 475ºF (240ºC), bake wings for 15 minutes or until sticky and golden brown, turning several times.
  4. Remove from oven and let wings rest on rack to cool slightly, 5 minutes. Serve with your desire side dishes.
