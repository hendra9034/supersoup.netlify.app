The ingredients needed to make Schezwan Fried Rice:
  1. Get 500 grms Rice
  2. Take 250 grms Chicken
  3. Make ready 500 grms Mix vegetables (beans, carrots, peas, corns)
  4. Prepare 1 Red Bell paper
  5. Make ready 10-12 Kashmiri Red Chillies
  6. Get 10 Garlic
  7. Get 1 inch Ginger
  8. Get Onion 1 medium
  9. Prepare 1 Tbsp Ketchup
  10. Prepare 2 Tbsp Soya sauce
  11. Take 2 Tbsp Vinegar
  12. Make ready 1 Tsp Sugar
  13. Take 1 tsp Schezwan paper or crushed black paper..
  14. Make ready Salt to taste
  15. Get Oil
Steps to make Schezwan Fried Rice:
  1. First make Schezwan sauce. For that first soak Kashmiri chillies in warm water for 20-30 minutes. Then blend it with little water to make a paste.
  2. Saute chopped ginger and garlic till it's aromatic.
  3. Add chopped Onion into it and saute till light brown.
  4. Add vinegar, soya sauce.
  5. Now add sugar, salt and ketchup in it.
  6. Now add blended paste of kashmiri Red Chillies in it
  7. Mix it and add a little water to cook it properly. Then add crushed black paper.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes and Schezwan sauce is ready.
  9. Soak rice in water for 15 minutes.
  10. Boil 12 glasses of water. When it's steaming hot add rice in it. Add 2 tbsp of salt also and cook till 80% means al dante.
  11. Now in a separate pan saute small chunks of chicken in 1tbsp of oil.
  12. Add vegetables in chicken. Saute all together
  13. Now add Schezwan paste in it. Mix well and cook on low flame till chicken is tendered. - Since it's boneless,it won't take long to be tendered.
  14. Once chicken is tendered, adding al dante rice in it. Mix well and let it cook on low flame for 15 minutes.
  15. The yummy treat is ready to serve. Sprinkle some green onion for extra fine touch and…. Viola ❤️
