The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken Noodle soup:
  1. Get 2 carrots/diced cubes
  2. Prepare 5 stick celery/diced
  3. Take 6 mushrooms/diced
  4. Prepare 4 clove garlic/crushed
  5. Get 1 tsp cracked black pepper
  6. Get 1 tsp mixed herbs
  7. Prepare 2 stick spring onion/sliced
  8. Prepare 1/2 packages san remo fettuccine/broken in half or - 1 packet of 2minute noodle broken up.
  9. Get 1 whole cooked chook/meat removed from bones and diced.
  10. Make ready 3 packages continental stock pot chicken/dissolved in 6 cups of boiling water.
  11. Make ready 2 cup boiled water
  12. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  13. Get 1 french bread stick/sliced
Steps to make Easy Chicken Noodle soup:
  1. add olive oil to a large soup pot and heat.
  2. once oil is hot add carrots, celery, mushrooms, onion, garlic, pepper, mixed herbs and cook for 5mins.
  3. add chicken to pot and break it down to desired amout.
  4. add chicken stock and boiled water,bring to the boil.
  5. turned down to a simmer add spring onions, cook for a further 30mins with lid on or until vegies are soft.
  6. once 30mins is up add broken pasta cook for a further 15mins with lid on stirring every 5mins.
  7. serve with fresh french stick bread sliced up to dunch…
