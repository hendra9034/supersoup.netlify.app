The ingredients needed to make Tomato soup:
  1. Make ready 4big size red tomato
  2. Prepare 1 onion
  3. Prepare 1 carrot
  4. Take 1 bay leaf
  5. Take 1 teaspoon garlic chopped
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon corn flour(optional)
  7. Make ready 2 teaspoon oil butter mix
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Take 1 teaspoon sugar
  10. Take to taste Salt
  11. Take 4/5 slices bread
  12. Take 1/2 tsp Basil leaves

Instructions to make Tomato soup:
