The ingredients needed to make Beef Lasagne:
  1. Get Beef Mince
  2. Make ready 800 g beef mince
  3. Get 1 Red, green and yellow bell pepper
  4. Prepare 1 onion
  5. Take 1 carrot
  6. Take 1 tomato
  7. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  8. Make ready 1 Tbsp dried mixed herbs
  9. Get Salt & pepper to taste
  10. Make ready Cheese Sauce
  11. Get 50 g butter
  12. Take 50 g flour
  13. Make ready 500 ml milk
  14. Get 100 g mozzarella cheese
  15. Get 1 tsp white pepper
  16. Get Salt to taste
  17. Take Others
  18. Prepare 2 packets lasagna sheets
  19. Get 100 g Mozzarella cheese (grated)
Steps to make Beef Lasagne:
  1. Gather all ingredients that need to be preped
  2. Dice up all the veggies to about the same size. Saute the onion, carrot and tomato together for about 5 mins. Add the bell peppers and fry for another minute.
  3. Add in the mince and garlic. Fry until the mince is browned. Season with mixed herbs, salt and pepper. Reduce the heat, cover and let cook for 5 mins.
  4. In a saucepan, melt the butter and add the flour to make a roux. Cook out the flour for a few minutes.
  5. Slowly pour in the milk to the roux while stirring continuously to avoid any lumps. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens.
  6. Season sauce with white pepper and salt. Add the mozzarella cheese and mix until it's fully melted.
  7. Once the beef mince and cheese sauce are ready it's now time to assemble the lasagna in an oven proof dish.
  8. Put a layer of the cheese sauce, followed by the lasagne sheets and then a layer of the beef mince. Continue these layers until you reach the top of the dish making sure to end with a layer of cheese sauce.
  9. Once the lasagne has been layered sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Bake lasagna at 160 degrees for about 20mins or until the top has browned. Once baked, serve while hot.
