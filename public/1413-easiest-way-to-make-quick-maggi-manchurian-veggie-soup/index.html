The ingredients needed to make Maggi Manchurian Veggie Soup:
  1. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon butter
  2. Take 2-3 garlic cloves
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon soya sauce
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon chilli sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon vinegar
  7. Make ready 2 bowl cooked rice water
  8. Take 3-4 tablespoons Maggi
  9. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
Steps to make Maggi Manchurian Veggie Soup:
  1. Take a pan and add 1/2 teaspoon butter, add 2-3 grated garlic cloves, 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger and fry till golden brown now add 1tablespoon soya sauce, 1/2teaspoon chilli sauce and 1tablespoon vinegar.
  2. Now mix them for 1min and now add 2bowl cooked rice water and boil, now add the Maggi veggie masala and let boil now add 3-4 tablespoons Maggi noodles and 1/2 teaspoon salt and boil until Maggi is cooked.
  3. Serve Hot Enjoy!
