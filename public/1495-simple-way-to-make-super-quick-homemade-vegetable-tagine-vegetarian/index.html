The ingredients needed to make Vegetable tagine - vegetarian:
  1. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Take 1 onion, peeled and chopped
  3. Take 2 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  4. Get 1 tsp ground ginger
  5. Take 1 tsp ground turmeric
  6. Take Generous pinch saffron
  7. Get 1 piece cassia bark/ 1 cinnamon stick
  8. Take About 500-600g vegetables eg squash, carrot, sweet potato,
  9. Prepare 300-400 ml veggie stock
  10. Get 10 or so prunes - chop some, leave rest whole
  11. Take 1 preserved lemon - just the skin, finely chopped
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp honey
  13. Take 1 handful green olives
  14. Get 1 (400 g) can chickpeas, drained
  15. Prepare handfuls Fresh coriander - couple of
  16. Take Blanched or flaked almonds - toasted

Instructions to make Vegetable tagine - vegetarian:
  1. Heat the oil in a pan (with a lid for later). Add onions and fry til soft.
  2. Add garlic and spices. Cook for a couple of minutes
  3. Add the vegetables, stick, prunes and preserved lemon skin. Bring to a simmer, cover and simmer for about 30 mins - until the vegetables are soft.
  4. Add the honey, olives and chickpeas. Stir through.
