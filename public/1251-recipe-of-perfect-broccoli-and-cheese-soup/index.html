The ingredients needed to make broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. Get base
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  3. Get 1 yellow onion, diced
  4. Prepare 32 oz frozen broccoli
  5. Make ready 1 lb shredded cheddar cheese
  6. Get 1 cup water
  7. Prepare 2/3 cup corn starch
  8. Take 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  9. Take 2 tbsp seasoning salt, plus more to taste
  10. Make ready 1 tsp each black and white pepper
  11. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  12. Make ready 1 quart half and half

Steps to make broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. Melt butter over medium heat
  2. Add onion and cook until onion is soft, stirring frequently
  3. Add broccoli, olive oil, and half and half
  4. Add seasoning salt and pepper.
  5. Bring to boil, reduce heat to medium low and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring
  6. Reduce heat to low and stir in cheese, continuing to stir until cheese is melted and evenly distributed among the soup
  7. Stir in garlic powder
  8. In another bowl, slowly add cornstarch to water, stirring
  9. Add mixture to soup slowly, stirring until an even consistency is achieved
