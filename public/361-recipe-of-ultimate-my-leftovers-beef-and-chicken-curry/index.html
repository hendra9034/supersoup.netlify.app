The ingredients needed to make My leftovers beef and chicken curry:
  1. Make ready 1 tub curry powder
  2. Prepare 2 pints cold water
  3. Prepare Leftover beef chopped
  4. Prepare Leftover chicken
  5. Take 2 carrots sliced
  6. Prepare 2 onions sliced
  7. Take 1 pepper sliced
  8. Get Handful mushrooms diced
  9. Take 1 cup frozen peas
  10. Prepare Cooked rice to serve
