The ingredients needed to make 3 colour fussili pasta:
  1. Make ready 350 grms tri coloured fussili pasta
  2. Make ready 250 grms mixed frozen vegetables (corn, peas, beans, carrots)
  3. Prepare 2 cloves garlic
  4. Take 1/2 (1 tsp) crushed black pepper
  5. Prepare 1/2 (1 tsp) white paper powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 (1 tsp) crushed red chillies
  7. Get 1/2 tsp onion granules
  8. Make ready 1/2 chicken stock cube
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp hot sauce
  10. Prepare 1 Tbsp vinegar
  11. Prepare 1 Tbsp light soya sauce
  12. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Take Handful of mozzarella or CheddarCheese
  14. Prepare 1 Tbsp oil

Instructions to make 3 colour fussili pasta:
  1. First boil pasta to al dante. Al dante is you cook something only 90%.
  2. For boiling, boil water. Then add salt and oil in water. We add oil so that pasta won't get stick with each other.
  3. Now in a pan, add oil. When it's less hot add crushed garlic in it. Saute it.
  4. Add frozen vegetables. I am adding corns, peas, beans, carrots to match the colours of pasta.
  5. Add crushed black pepper, white paper powder, and crushed red chillies one by one.
  6. Now add onion granules and mix it thoroughly.
  7. Adding stock cubes and mix it well.
  8. Add vinegar, soya sauce, and hot sauce. Give it a mix.
  9. Now adding ketchup, salt, and cream in it. While adding cream, turn the flame slow.
  10. Now adding boiled pasta and mix it. Don't turn off the flame.
  11. Now add cheese as a final ingredient and mix well till it is melt.
  12. Our tricolour fussili pasta is ready to serve… Viola ❤️
  13. Note - If you don't have onion granules, skip it. - If you have an oregano, add it in the end. - Always add cream or yogurt in your recipes on low flame. - Boil pasta as per instructions on the packet.
