The ingredients needed to make Salmon, Kimchi & Avocado Rice Bowl:
  1. Get 60-80 g Salmon Sashimi *OR very fresh Salmon, bones removed and cut into small slices
  2. Get 1/2 teaspoon Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce) *OR Gochujang (Korean Spicy Miso)
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon Sesame Oil
  4. Get 1 teaspoon Soy Sauce
  5. Take 1 small clove Garlic *optional, grated
  6. Get 3-4 tablespoons Kimchi *cut into small pieces if large
  7. Make ready 1/4-1/2 Avocado *cut into small pieces
  8. Get 1/2 Spring Onion *finely chopped
  9. Prepare 1 serving Cooked Rice *warmed
  10. Get Toasted Sesame Seeds

Instructions to make Salmon, Kimchi & Avocado Rice Bowl:
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce), Soy Sauce and Sesame Oil. Add grated Garlic if you like it. Then add Salmon Sashimi, mix to combine.
  2. Add Kimchi, Avocado and Spring Onion (save some for topping), and gently combine.
  3. Half fill a bowl with warm Cooked Rice and cover it with the Salmon mixture. Sprinkle with the saved finely chopped Spring Onion and Toasted Sesame Seeds, and enjoy.
