The ingredients needed to make Thick Split Peas Soup:
  1. Take 1 kg split peas
  2. Get 3 grated Tomatoes
  3. Take 3 Onions
  4. Get 3 All peppers full (yellow, green, red)
  5. Get Spoon of each Ginger, garlic & dhana
  6. Make ready to drizzle Olive oil
  7. Get 500g Mince
  8. Take to serve Noodles or cooked spaghetti
  9. Take 1 cube chicken stock
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoons tomato sauce
  11. Take 2 tablespoons mayonnaise

This soup gets its creaminess from the peas, instead of milk or cream. It uses a ham bone to infuse extra savory flavor while it cooks. Instructions to make Thick Split Peas Soup:
  1. Boil split peas until they look like a smooth soft porridge and I add 1 chicken stock for taste
  2. Make tomato grave. Fry onion, peppers, garlic, ginger & dhana. Add tomatoes, chicken stock and your favorite spice. Add a bit of soup to make it thick.
  3. Make mince meat. Fry onion, all peppers, garlic, ginger and dhana. Add mince once cooked add half chicken stock and steak and chop spice. Add 2 spoons of tomato sauce and mayonnaise.
  4. Add all in big pot and add thick vegetable soup. Add your cooked spaghetti cut into small small bits.
  5. Serve with Dombolo
