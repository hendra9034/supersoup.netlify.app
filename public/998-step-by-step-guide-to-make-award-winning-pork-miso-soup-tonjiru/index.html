The ingredients needed to make Pork Miso Soup (Tonjiru):
  1. Take 400 g daikon
  2. Get 2 carrots
  3. Prepare 1 large onion
  4. Take 400 g shabu pork collar
  5. Prepare 1 vegetable dashi packet (Kayanoya)
  6. Get 3 heaped tbsps shiro miso
  7. Get 2 tbsp minced ginger
  8. Get 2 tbsps sesame oil
Steps to make Pork Miso Soup (Tonjiru):
  1. Fry sliced pork with sesame oil till no longer pink.
  2. Add in 1 chopped onions. Cook till brown. Then add in 2 carrots, 1 daikon. Coat well with oil.
  3. Add in leeks, water and 1 dashi packet. Mix well. Bring to a boil and then let simmer for 15 mins till vegetables are soft. We kept this in the fridge to let flavours develop from here.
  4. Before serving, bring pot to a boil. Then lower heat, add in minced ginger and miso and simmer for 5 mins. Serve with chopped spring onions. 1.
