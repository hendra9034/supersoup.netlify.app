The ingredients needed to make Sweet Chilli Chicken w/ Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breasts cut into strips
  2. Get 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp cornflour
  4. Take 2 tsp salt
  5. Take 2 tsp minced garlic
  6. Make ready 4 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  7. Get 1 tsp lime juice
  8. Prepare 50 ml water
  9. Take 225 g boiled eice
  10. Prepare 1 egg
  11. Get 20 g frozen peas
  12. Prepare 2 tsp black pepper
  13. Get 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  14. Take 2 spring onions, sliced

Instructions to make Sweet Chilli Chicken w/ Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Cut chicken and season with salt and cornflour
  2. Fry in oil until golden and crisp and set aside
  3. In a pan, simmer on a low heat chilli sauce, garlic and water until it thickens
  4. Boil rice for 10 minutes and then fry in soy sauce for 30 secinds
  5. Add peas, egg, and mix
  6. Add chicken to your sweet chilli sauce and cook for 2 minutes on a low heat
  7. Serve
