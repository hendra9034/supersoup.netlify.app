The ingredients needed to make French onion soup:
  1. Take Soup
  2. Take 1/4 cup butter
  3. Prepare 6 medium-sized onions, peeled, sliced
  4. Get 6 clove of garlic, peeled, sliced
  5. Get 1 bay leaf

  11. Prepare Topping
  12. Make ready 4 slice sourdough bread, toasted
  13. Take 1 cup gruyere cheese, grated
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions to make French onion soup:
  1. Melt butter in heavy, large sauce pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, garlic, bay leaf and thyme spring and saute until very tender and brown, about 25 minutes.
  3. Add 1/4 cup wine to deglaze pot every 5 minutes for 20 minutes (total onion cooking time is 45 minutes).
  4. Stir in Dijon mustard.
  5. Add white wine and simmer until reduced to a syrup consistency, about 3 minutes.
  6. Stir in vegetable stock. Simmer 20 minutes.
  7. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  8. ————— To assemble ——————–
  9. Preheat broiler.
  10. Ladle soup into overproof bowls.
  11. Top each with slice of toast and grated cheese.
  12. Broil until cheeses melt and bubble.
