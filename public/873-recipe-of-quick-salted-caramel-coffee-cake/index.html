The ingredients needed to make Salted Caramel Coffee Cake:
  1. Prepare Coffee Cake
  2. Make ready 1 egg (70gm with shell)
  3. Prepare 70 gm Flour
  4. Prepare 70 gm Castor Sugar
  5. Prepare 70 gm Unsalted Butter
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Milk
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Vanilla Essence
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp Coffee Essence
  10. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  11. Get Salted Caramel
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup Castor Sugar
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  14. Get 3 tbsp Water
  15. Take 1 tsp Coarse Salt
Steps to make Salted Caramel Coffee Cake:
  1. Mix all the ingredients of the coffee cake together and put it in the oven that is preheated at 180°C for 25 minutes or till skewer come out clean. Set cake to cool on cooling rack.
  2. Boil sugar and water in a pot on medium heat for about 8 minutes or till sugar turns amber in colour. Remove from heat and stir in heavy cream slowly. Be careful of the hot steam in the process. Add salt into caramel mixture or after dripping it on the cake.
