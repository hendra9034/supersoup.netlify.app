The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 head broccoli
  2. Make ready 2 cans chicken broth or 2 cubes chicken bouillon in 2 cups water
  3. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  4. Get 1 stick butter
  5. Get 1/2 cup flour
  6. Make ready 2 cups milk
  7. Take 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

Instructions to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Cook broccoli and drain.
  2. Saute onion in butter, add flour and cook 5 minutes. Add chicken broth, milk, cheese and broccoli. Cook 15 minutes.
