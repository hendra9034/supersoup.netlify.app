The ingredients needed to make Chinese sweet corn and cabbage soup:
  1. Prepare 500 gm cabbage
  2. Make ready 2 handful sweet corn (kernels peeled, save the cob)
  3. Get as needed (Vegetables stock)
  4. Get 100 gm shitake Mushrooms(rough chop)
  5. Take 100 gm yellow onions (diced)
  6. Get 50 gm chinese calery(rough chop, stems and leaves separated)
  7. Prepare 30 gm garlic (rough chop)
  8. Get 100 gm reddish (rough chop)
  9. Take 100 gm carrot(rough chop)
  10. Prepare 3-4 tbsn cooking oil
  11. Get As needed water (6-8cup)
  12. Get (Seasoning)
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  14. Make ready As needed salt(1.5tbsn)

Steps to make Chinese sweet corn and cabbage soup:
  1. Stock:-in a large pot, heat cooking oil until hot. Stir fry the onions, garlic and chinese celery stemps until fragrant.
  2. Then add and mix well the Mushrooms, raddish and carrots. Stir fry for about 2minutes. Then add water and bring it to a boil.
  3. Soup:-once boiling, add the cabbages, sweet corn kernel and the cobs. Cook for 30minutes.
  4. After that discard the cobs add chinese celery leaves and serve.
  5. To gate a more potent soup, countinue cooking for another 30 minutes.
