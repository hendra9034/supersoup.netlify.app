The ingredients needed to make CARBONADA, CHILEAN BEEF SOUP. JON STYLE:
  1. Get 3 tbsp. vegetable oil
  2. Get 2 cups diced beef
  3. Get 1/2 cup diced yellow onions
  4. Get 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  5. Get 1/4 tsp. cumin powder
  6. Take 1/4 tsp. ground pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tsp. paprika
  8. Prepare 1 tsp. fresh oregano or ½ dry
  9. Get 1 3/4 qt. water
  10. Prepare 2 beef bouillon without MSG
  11. Prepare 1 tsp. salt
  12. Get 3 tbsp. rice
  13. Take 2 1/2 cups diced red potatoes
  14. Make ready 1 cup diced carrots
  15. Prepare 1 1/2 cups diced butternut squash
  16. Make ready 1 cup corn kernels
  17. Make ready 1 1/2 cups string beans, cut in small pieces
  18. Get 1/2 cup green peas
  19. Make ready 1 tbsp. cilantro, chopped
