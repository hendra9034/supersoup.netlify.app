The ingredients needed to make Beef In Black Bean Sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 rump steak
  2. Make ready 3 tsp cornflour
  3. Get 4 tbsp light soy sauce
  4. Make ready 4 tbsp dark soy sauce
  5. Make ready 1 tsp white pepper
  6. Take 100 ml water
  7. Take 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  10. Take 200 g salted black beans
  11. Make ready 1 tsp crushed garlic
  12. Prepare 1 tsp grated ginger
  13. Make ready 1 green bell pepper
  14. Get 1 sliced carrot
  15. Prepare 1 chopped medium onion
  16. Take 120 g basmati rice
  17. Get 1 tsp sesame oil

Instructions to make Beef In Black Bean Sauce:
  1. Slice steak into strips and marinade in 1 tsp of cornflour, half of the light and dark soy sauce, and white pepper for 20 minutes.
  2. Get your rice boiled in a pan and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Make your sauce by mixing water, oyster sauce, the remaining of light and dark soy sauce, salt and pepper and the rest of your corn flour.
  4. Heat 1 tsp of sesame oil in a wok and fry your steak strips for 3 minutes turning regularly and set aside.
  5. Crush your black beans slightly and get them in the same wok, with your prepared black bean sauce, pepper, onion and carrot and cook through.
  6. Add your steak and and cook until your sauce thickens.
  7. Serve with your cooked rice.
