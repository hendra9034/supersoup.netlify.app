The ingredients needed to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 1/4 cups dried split peas (1 standard 16oz bag)
  2. Prepare 6-8 cups water
  3. Make ready 6 cups vegetable broth
  4. Make ready 2 onions, thinly sliced
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 pinch dried marjoram
  8. Make ready 2 garlic cloves, minced
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  10. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  11. Make ready 1 potato, diced
  12. Make ready 3 large carrots, diced
  13. Make ready 3 stalks celery, chopped

Steps to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. Simmer peas in water for 2 minutes and then let soak for 1 hour.
  2. Add vegetable broth, onion, salt, pepper, marjoram, garlic, cumin, and bayleaf. Cover, bring to boil, then simmer for 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add celery, carrots, and potatoes. Cook slowly, for 1 hour, until vegetables are tender and soup is very thick when stirred.
  4. Serve with fresh bread!
