The ingredients needed to make Macaroni Shell Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 pack macaroni shell (small)
  2. Prepare 1 can cream
  3. Get 1 can evaporated milk
  4. Take Butter (optional)
  5. Get Cooking oil
  6. Get 3 cloves garlic
  7. Prepare 1 small onion minced
  8. Make ready 5 pcs hotdog
  9. Take 2 pcs burger patty(optional)
  10. Make ready 5 glass Water
  11. Get Chicken
  12. Prepare Chicken broth
  13. Prepare Carrots
  14. Get Potato
  15. Take Bell pepper
  16. Make ready Salt
  17. Take Pepper
  18. Get Lettuce or cabbage (optional)
Instructions to make Macaroni Shell Soup:
  1. Prepare all the ingredients, cut all the vegetables in small pieces, make the chicken boiled and use the broth later.
  2. In a casserole, saute garlic and onion with butter and cooking oil, add the meat, hotdogs and chicken.
  3. Add the chicken broth then macaroni shell, wait until its boiled.
  4. Add vegetables, cream, evaporated milk and cheese
  5. Use salt and pepper to taste
