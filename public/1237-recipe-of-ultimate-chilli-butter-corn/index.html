The ingredients needed to make Chilli Butter Corn:
  1. Get 2-3 Corn Cobs *cut into 1/4 or 1/3
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons (30 g) Butter
  3. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  4. Get 1/2 tablespoon Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce)
  5. Take Extra Chilli Flakes *optional
Steps to make Chilli Butter Corn:
  1. Bring a pot of salted water to the boil. Add corn and cook 5 minutes, then drain. *Note: Steaming is another good method.
  2. In a large heat-proof bowl, melt Butter in the microwave. Add Soy Sauce, Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce) and Extra Chilli (*optional), and mix well.
  3. Add drained Corn and mix to combine.
