The ingredients needed to make Walnut & Ginger Flapjacks:
  1. Prepare 400 g/4 cups rolled oats
  2. Take 200 g/1 cup golden syrup/light corn syrup
  3. Make ready 200 g/ 2 cups light brown soft sugar
  4. Make ready 200 g /1 1/2 sticks butter (see tip)
  5. Make ready 100 g/ 3/4 cup crystallised ginger, chopped
  6. Take 100 g/ 3/4 cup walnuts, chopped

Steps to make Walnut & Ginger Flapjacks:
  1. Melt the butter in a medium to large saucepan with the golden syrup and sugar.
  2. Once everything is melted and combined, like a caramel, take off the heat.
  3. Stir in the oats, with the walnuts and ginger and give it a good mix together.
  4. Lay the mixture out on a 6 inch by 8 inch baking sheet lined with greaseproof paper.
  5. Flatten down with a spatula (if you rub it with oil first it will prevent stickiness).
  6. Bake in an oven preheated to 170C for 20-25 minutes, or until it is golden around the edges.
  7. Remove from the oven and immediately score out the portions with a knife.
  8. Leave to cool completely before cutting through scored lines and dividing into servings.
  9. Store in an air tight tin, box or biscuit tin.
