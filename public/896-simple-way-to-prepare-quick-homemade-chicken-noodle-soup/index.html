The ingredients needed to make Homemade chicken noodle soup:
  1. Prepare soup
  2. Prepare 4 chicken breast
  3. Get 3 veggies of your choice
  4. Get 2 tbsp chicken boullion
  5. Take noodles
  6. Prepare 2 cup flour
  7. Get 3 egg yolks
  8. Take 1 whole egg
  9. Get 2 tbsp cold water
  10. Make ready 1 tsp salt

Instructions to make Homemade chicken noodle soup:
  1. Cut up and brown chicken in large pot.
  2. Add water(about half way fill the pot) and boullion to taste.
  3. Add desired veggies
  4. For the noodles
  5. Combine egg yolks, whole egg, salt and water and beat together.
  6. Add flour to mixture and combine all ingredients.
  7. Roll dough to about 1/8" flat. Cut to desired length and width.
  8. Add to boiling soup and cook until desired texture.
  9. ENJOY!!
