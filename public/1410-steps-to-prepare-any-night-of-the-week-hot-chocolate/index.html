The ingredients needed to make Hot Chocolate:
  1. Make ready 15 g unsweetened cocoa powder
  2. Get 50 g granulated sugar
  3. Get pinch salt
  4. Get 50 ml water
  5. Prepare 600 ml milk
  6. Take 1 tsp corn starch
  7. Prepare 30 ml rum (optional)
Steps to make Hot Chocolate:
  1. Warm up the milk and set aside.
  2. Mix the corn starch with a spoon of water and set aside.
  3. In a small saucepan, bring the water to boil.
  4. Add the cocoa powder, sugar and salt, and stir until smooth.
  5. Pour in the hot milk while stirring constantly.
  6. Keep stirring and add the corn starch.
  7. Continue stirring over low heat until it thickens up a bit.
  8. Serve immediately. Add rum to taste. Enjoy!
