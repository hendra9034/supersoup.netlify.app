The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup-HCG:
  1. Get 1 onion, thinly sliced
  2. Take 3 clove garlic, minced
  3. Get 1 tsp Stevia
  4. Prepare 2 cup Beef broth
  5. Prepare 1 salt & pepper

Instructions to make French Onion Soup-HCG:
  1. Sauté onion with garlic for 10 mins.
  2. Stir in stevia, cook for 10 min.
  3. Add beef broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to simmer, cover and cook for 20 min.
  5. Add salt & pepper to taste.
