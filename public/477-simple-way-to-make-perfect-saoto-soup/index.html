The ingredients needed to make Saoto soup:
  1. Get Broth water (bouillion water)
  2. Prepare broth cubes + onions + garlic + pimento + bay laves + laos + lemongrass
  3. Prepare Chicken breast (kipfilet)
  4. Prepare Fried unions (gebakken uitjes)
  5. Make ready bean sprouts (tauge)
  6. Take Fried chips (mini frietjes chips)
  7. Prepare Deep fried noodles (gebakken mie)
  8. Get Boiled eggs (gekookte eieren)
  9. Take Fresh parley (verse peterselie) of celery
  10. Make ready hot sauce = soya sauce sweet and sour + hot peppers

Instructions to make Saoto soup:
  1. For the broth water 5 liter water in the pan. Heat it up and than put all the ingredients for the broth water in the water.
  2. Boil the chicken breast in hot water. fluff it into pieces and then fry in a pan to taste. season it with pepper, salt and a little bit of sweet soya sauce.
