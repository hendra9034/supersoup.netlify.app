The ingredients needed to make chicken noodle soup:
  1. Get 1 packages chicken any parts
  2. Prepare 2 can carrots
  3. Prepare 1/2 celery
  4. Prepare 2 can broth
  5. Take 3 quart water
  6. Prepare 1/4 salt
  7. Get 1/4 pepper
  8. Take 1/2 clove garlic and onions
  9. Take 1 pinch rosemary
  10. Take 1 large pot

Instructions to make chicken noodle soup:
  1. heat pot with 1/4 oil
  2. add chicken cook till done
  3. add onions, garlic, salt, pepper, and rosemary to chicken let mix for a few min
  4. add broth and water tp pot
  5. add remaing ingredients
  6. bring to boil then simmer about 20 min
  7. serve with sweet rolls
