The ingredients needed to make Ham & Bean Soup:
  1. Get 1 pound Northern beans
  2. Make ready 1 large ham shank
  3. Prepare 4 cloves garlic minced
  4. Prepare 1 medium stalk celery
  5. Make ready 2 cups shredded carrots
  6. Prepare 7 cups chicken broth
  7. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp dried parsley
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp celery salt
  10. Take 1 tbsp dried minced onion
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp mustard powder

Steps to make Ham & Bean Soup:
  1. Rince and drain beans and add to crockpot
  2. Add 1 large ham hock or shank
  3. Add all other ingredients and cook on low for 7 hours
  4. Take ham shank out and discard bones and cartilage
  5. Add meat back to soup and add more ham if necessary. I used leftovers, cook for another 30 minutes
