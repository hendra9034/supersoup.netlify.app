The ingredients needed to make My Gemuse Mix:
  1. Get Onion 1 large size
  2. Prepare Tomatoes 1 medium
  3. Prepare Tomato Purée Half cup
  4. Take Salt 1 tbs
  5. Make ready Turmeric 1 tbs
  6. Get Red Chilli power 0.5 tbs
  7. Make ready Coriander power 0.5 tbs
  8. Make ready Garam Masala 0.5 tbs
  9. Take 100 gm Corn
  10. Make ready 50 gm Pea
  11. Prepare 50 gm Paprika
  12. Prepare Chilli 1 medium size
  13. Get Garlic 1 medium size
  14. Get Ginger crushed 1 tbs
Steps to make My Gemuse Mix:
  1. Fry garlic and ginger in 1 tbs of cooking oil. Cook until golden brown
  2. Add tomtaoes and tomato purée until the tomatoes are soft. This takes about 5min.
  3. Add all the spices at once, except Garam Masala. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add all vegetables at once. Mix properly. You can add half cup water if needed.
  5. Let the excess water vaporise. Cook for about 10 minutes on medium heat.
  6. Add garam masala on top. Mix well and serve with white rice.
