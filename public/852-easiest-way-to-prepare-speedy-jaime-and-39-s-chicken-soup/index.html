The ingredients needed to make Jaime's Chicken Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 whole chicken
  2. Take 2 medium carrots
  3. Prepare 1 packages egg noodles
  4. Take 2 tbsp seasoned salt
Instructions to make Jaime's Chicken Soup:
  1. Boil chicken and carrots (sliced), debone, save water
  2. Add chicken and carrots into water from boiling chicken, add seasoned salt and bring to boil.
  3. Add egg noodles and cooked to desired tenderness (al dente is best)
