The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Dango Dumplings:
  1. Take 50 g Pumpkin *pureed
  2. Make ready 100 g Shiratamako (Japanese Glutinous Rice Flour)
  3. Take 1 pinch Salt
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup Warm Water *Do not add all of it at once
  5. Get Mitarashi Sauce, Condensed Milk OR Sweetened Cream to serve
  6. Get Mitarashi Sauce
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon Sugar
  9. Get 3 tablespoons Water
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon Potato Starch OR Corn Starch Flour
Steps to make Pumpkin Dango Dumplings:
  1. Place ‘Shiratamako’ in a mixing bowl. Add pureed Pumpkin and Salt, then gradually add warm Water, NOT all at once, and knead to achieve the firmness of the dough same as firmness of your earlobe. (*This is traditionally taught.) *Note: You don’t need to add all water. The dough should be dry to the touch.
  2. Make about 2cm balls. Today I made 20 balls.
  3. Cook them in plenty of rapidly boiling water. When the Dango Dumplings are floating, cook a few more minutes, then drain and place them in icy cold water to cool.
  4. Enjoy with your favourite sauce such as Mitarashi Sauce, Condensed Milk, Sweetened Cream, OR etc.
  5. *Mitarashi Sauce: Combine all the ingredients in a small heat-proof bowl, heat in the microwave and bring to the boil, and mix well. You can cook it in a small saucepan until the sauce thickens.
