The ingredients needed to make Balsamic Braised Beef:
  1. Prepare 800 g stew/diced bee
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Get 3 carrots
  4. Get 1 red bell pepper
  5. Take 2 garlic cloves
  6. Take 60 ml balsamic vinegar
  7. Take 2 tbs brown sugar
  8. Take 240 ml beef stock
  9. Take 3 tbs tomato puree
  10. Get Pinch dried thyme
  11. Make ready Pinch dried parsley
Steps to make Balsamic Braised Beef:
  1. Chop the carrots, onion and peppers and add them to a pan.
  2. Mince the garlic then add that. Cook for about 2 minutes or until fragrant.
  3. Add in the beef next and brown.
  4. Add these cooked ingredients into the slow cooker.
  5. Add the stock, balsamic vinegar, sugar, tomato puree and the dried herbs. Stir everything together.
  6. Cook on high for at least 4 hours or low for 6.
  7. Whilst the slow cooker is going prepare your choice of potato and veg.
  8. Plate everything up and enjoy!
