The ingredients needed to make Alton Brown's Lentil Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 cup onion, chopped
  2. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Get 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup finely chopped celery
  5. Get lb rinced and picked lentils
  6. Take 2 tsp kosher salt
  7. Get 1 cup peeled and chopped tomatoes
  8. Take 2 quart chicken or vegetable broth
  9. Get 1/2 tsp freshly ground coriander
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp freshly ground cumin
  11. Get 1/2 tsp freshly ground grains of paradise

Instructions to make Alton Brown's Lentil Soup:
  1. Place olive oil in large pot and set over medium heat
  2. Once the oil is hot, add onion, carrot, celery, and salt and sweat until onions are translucent, approximately 6-7 minutes.
  3. Add lentils, tomatoes, broth, coriander, cumin and grains of paradise and stir to combine.
  4. Increase heat to high and bring just to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover and cook at low simmer until the lentils are tender, approximately 35-40 minutes.
  6. (Optional) using a stick blender, puree to your preferred consistency.
