The ingredients needed to make homemade chicken noodle soup:
  1. Take 1 packages boneless skinless chicken breast - thawed and diced
  2. Take 1 large package egg noodles
  3. Get 2 tbsp salt
  4. Get 2 tbsp pepper
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp garlic powder
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp onion powder
  7. Get 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  8. Get 1 1/2 cup Optional sunflower seeds-shelled

Steps to make homemade chicken noodle soup:
  1. Put chicken breast into 8 quart pot, fill with water to about 2-3 inches from the top.
  2. Boil at least 4 hours, until there is a good natural broth. Just make sure to check it occasionally, so that the broth doesn't boil off completely. If it starts getting low just add more water until its back at the starting level.
  3. Once you've finished boiling the chicken add your seasonings. I always make the broth without first so it keeps the natural flavor and it is just that much stronger.
  4. Add the shelled sunflower seeds. skip this step if not using them
  5. Boil an additional 10 minutes, then add noodles. Boil 7-10 minutes more, or until noodles are tender.
  6. ENJOY THE SOUP OF YOUR LABOR!!!
