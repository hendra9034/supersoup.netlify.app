The ingredients needed to make Turkish Sausage & Eggs (Sucuklu Yumurta):
  1. Prepare 600 g Turkish sausages (cut to 1cm-thick slices)
  2. Take 1 Tbs butter
  3. Prepare 3 eggs

Steps to make Turkish Sausage & Eggs (Sucuklu Yumurta):
  1. In a pan on medium heat, place the slices of Turkish sausages in the pan. Spread the slices out evenly.
  2. Add butter into the pan. Let it melt and maneuver the pan gently to let the melted butter spread across the pan.
  3. After 2 minutes, use a spatula to flip the slices of sausages over. The sausages should have turned slightly brown.
  4. Crack in the 3 eggs. Cook them till the yolk is slightly runny, as you would want to dip some of the yolk with bread.
  5. When the eggs have been cooked. Serve fresh in pan! - - Eating tip: Dip slices of bread into the pan and soak up the oil. The oil is flavourful as it has been infused with juices from the sausage.
