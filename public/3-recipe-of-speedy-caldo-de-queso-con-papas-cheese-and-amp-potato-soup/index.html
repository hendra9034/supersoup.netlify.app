The ingredients needed to make Caldo de queso con papas (cheese & potato soup):
  1. Make ready 2 cans Chicken Broth
  2. Take 1 12-14 oz. can of Evaporated Milk
  3. Take 1 (14 oz.) can of diced green chiles
  4. Take 1 tbsp oil
  5. Take 6-8 large potatoes peeled and cubed
  6. Prepare 1 bay leaf or ground pepper (to taste)
  7. Make ready 1 medium onion diced
  8. Make ready 1 cup celery and leaves diced
  9. Take 1 can or 14 oz of water
  10. Take Shredded Mexican blend or pepper jack cheese
Instructions to make Caldo de queso con papas (cheese & potato soup):
  1. Sauté onion and celery in oil
  2. Add chicken broth, water, bay leaf or pepper, and potatoes. Boil until potatoes are cooked
  3. Add in evaporated milk, diced green chiles, and shredded cheese stir until cheese is melted throughout
  4. Top with additional shredded cheese and green onions (if desired)
