The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Soup (Margaret):
  1. Prepare 16 oz broccoli-cauliflower frozen vegetables
  2. Get 2 each carrots
  3. Get 2 each or 3 potatoes
  4. Take 2 can cream of chicken soup
  5. Get 2 can chicken broth (or 4 cups)
  6. Take 6 oz velveeta cheese, to taste
  7. Get 1 Grandma adds onions.
Instructions to make Broccoli Cheese Soup (Margaret):
  1. Cut and peel vegetables.
  2. Cook all vegetables over stove. Drain water.
  3. Add soup and broth. Cook until creamy and well-mixed.
  4. Add velveeta. Heat until melted and well-mixed.
