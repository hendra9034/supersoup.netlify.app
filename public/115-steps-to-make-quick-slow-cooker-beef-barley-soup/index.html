The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Beef barley soup:
  1. Make ready 1.5 lbs chuck roast
  2. Make ready 1.5 lbs potatoes peeled and cubed 1/2 inch
  3. Prepare 2 carrots peeled and cubed
  4. Make ready 1 onion diced
  5. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
  6. Get 2 tbsp tomato paste
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp better than bouillion
  8. Get 2 tsp Worcester sauce
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Take 1/4 tsp pepper
  11. Make ready 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  12. Make ready 2 bay leaf
  13. Take 6 cups beef broth
  14. Make ready 2/3 cup barley
  15. Get Option - mushrooms or other vegetables
  16. Make ready oil of choice
Instructions to make Slow Cooker Beef barley soup:
  1. Brown beef cubes in oil 1-2 minutes per side on med-high heat
  2. Add beef and all ingredients to slow cooker and cook for 4-5 hours
  3. Fill liquid to the top or check if more is require later.
