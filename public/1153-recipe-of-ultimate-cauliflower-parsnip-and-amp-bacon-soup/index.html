The ingredients needed to make Cauliflower Parsnip & Bacon Soup:
  1. Take 3 Bacon rashes (diced) + 2 more slices for the garnish
  2. Get 1 brown Onion (diced)
  3. Prepare 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  4. Make ready 1 Tablespoon fresh Thyme
  5. Get 1 cup Vegetable stock
  6. Get 250 mls Water
  7. Make ready 6 Parsnips (diced)
  8. Make ready 140 g lactose free plain yoghurt

Instructions to make Cauliflower Parsnip & Bacon Soup:
  1. Heat a saucepan over medium heat. Add bacon, onion & garlic, thyme & cook, stirring for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the stock & water to the pan. Increase heat to high, bring to the boil, reduce heat & simmer on medium heat until parsnip is cooked & tender. Set aside for 10 minutes to cool slightly.
  3. With a handheld blender blitz until smooth. Add to bacon and onion mix, cook through for a further 2 minutes.
  4. Garnish with yoghurt & extra bacon.
  5. Note: for a variation I often blitz it all together, and garnish with a tablespoon of crispy bacon.
