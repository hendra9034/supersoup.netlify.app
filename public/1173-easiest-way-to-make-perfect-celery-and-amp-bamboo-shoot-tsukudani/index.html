The ingredients needed to make Celery & Bamboo Shoot ‘Tsukudani’:
  1. Get 150 g Celery Stalks and Leaves
  2. Prepare 1 can (*225g) Bamboo Shoot *Today I had 132g Bamboo Shoot after drain
  3. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon Sesame Oil
  4. Get 1 pinch Chilli Flakes *optional
  5. Take 1 tablespoon Sugar
  6. Make ready 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  7. Take 2 tablespoons Sake (Rice Wine) OR Water
  8. Make ready 1 sachet (2.5 g) ‘Katsuobushi’ Bonito Flakes
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon Toasted Sesame Seeds

Instructions to make Celery & Bamboo Shoot ‘Tsukudani’:
  1. Wash Celery Leaves and Stalks, and thinly chop up. Drain canned Bamboo Shoot, rinse, drain again, and cut into small pieces.
  2. Heat Sesame Oil in a frying pan or saucepan over medium heat and cook Celery, Bamboo Shoot and Chilli Flakes. When Celery is cooked, add Sugar, Soy Sauce and Sake (Rice Wine), and keep cooking, mixing well, until sauce is gone.
  3. Add ‘Katsuobushi’ (Bonito Flakes) and Toasted Sesame Seeds, and combine well. Enjoy with freshly cooked Rice.
