The ingredients needed to make Wisconsin Split Pea Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 lb Dried split peas
  2. Get 2 1/2 quart Water
  3. Make ready 1 Meaty hambone (or one cup chopped cooked ham)
  4. Make ready 1 cup Chopped onion
  5. Get 1 cup Each: diced carrot, celery and potato
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Parsly
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Pepper
  8. Get 1/4 tsp Each: garlic salt and marjoram
  9. Make ready 1 Salt
Instructions to make Wisconsin Split Pea Soup:
  1. Bring peas, ham and water to boil in large soup pot. Lower to a simmer and cook, covered 1 hour 30 minutes, Stirring frequently.
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients, bring to simmer. Cover and simmer 15-20 additional minutes, until veggies are tender.
  3. If using ham bone, remove from soup, remove ham from bone. Chop ham and return to soup.
