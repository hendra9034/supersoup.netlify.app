The ingredients needed to make Keto Creamy Broccoli & Cheese Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 Head Broccoli Chopped
  2. Prepare 2 Cups Chicken Broth
  3. Make ready 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  4. Make ready 1 Tsp Ground Black Pepper
  5. Take 1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
  6. Prepare 1 Cup Medium/Mature Cheese
  7. Prepare to taste Salt
Instructions to make Keto Creamy Broccoli & Cheese Soup:
  1. Steam or boil broccoli until slightly softened.
  2. Blend broccoli with all other ingredients until mostly smooth.
  3. Pour into a large pan and heat to a simmer.
  4. Stir in the cheese. And season to taste.
