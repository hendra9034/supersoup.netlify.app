The ingredients needed to make Beef Steak with Herbs and Spices:
  1. Take 500 gm beef steak
  2. Get 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  3. Take 1 tsp turmeric powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  6. Make ready to taste Salt
  7. Get 1 tsp light soya sauce
  8. Prepare 1 tsp fresh lime juice
  9. Take Hand full of fresh herbs (I have used rosemary & coriander)
  10. Make ready 3 tbsp garlic butter
Steps to make Beef Steak with Herbs and Spices:
  1. Marinate the steak with all the spices for 1 hour. Then in a skillet pan add butter and put the steak in the middle of the pan.
  2. Cook each side for 5 minutes. Keep the heat in medium low temperature. Then take in a plate or serving dish and rest it for 5-7 minutes. Now slice it with a sharp knife. Enjoy ☺️
