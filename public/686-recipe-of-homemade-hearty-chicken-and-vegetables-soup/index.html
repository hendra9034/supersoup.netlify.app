The ingredients needed to make Hearty Chicken and Vegetables soup:
  1. Make ready x chicken breast
  2. Make ready large can of sweet corn
  3. Take x garlic cloves
  4. Make ready Sping onions
  5. Prepare Yellow bell pepper
  6. Prepare Red cabbage
  7. Get Dryed tomatoes
  8. Make ready x medium heat chilli
  9. Make ready heaped tbsp Corn flour
  10. Make ready Soya sauce
  11. Take Black pepper
  12. Prepare x Chicken stock cubes
  13. Prepare chicken gravy granules
  14. Get Water
  15. Make ready Cooking cream
Steps to make Hearty Chicken and Vegetables soup:
  1. Fry up the chicken and dice up vegetables
  2. Add to the pot and add water and stock. Throw in pepper and garlic
  3. Throw in sweet corn
  4. Bring to boil and add soya sauce to taste and stock cubes… I also added chicken granules
  5. After 30 mins…. whisk it all up… not too smooth then add a good gulp of cream…..
  6. Serve…. enjoy….
