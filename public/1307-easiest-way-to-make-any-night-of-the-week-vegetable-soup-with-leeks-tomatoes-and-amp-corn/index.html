The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Soup with Leeks, Tomatoes & Corn:
  1. Prepare 1.5 onions, quartered
  2. Get 1 stalk leek, chopped
  3. Make ready Olive oil
  4. Make ready 5-6 ripe tomatoes, quartered
  5. Get 2 large potatoes, peeled + cubed
  6. Get 2.5 corn on cob, husked + halved
  7. Make ready 10 cups/ + water
  8. Make ready Salt to taste
  9. Make ready 1-2 bay leaves
Steps to make Vegetable Soup with Leeks, Tomatoes & Corn:
  1. In a large stock pot, sweat onions and leeks. Add potatoes, tomatoes, corn and remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil. Add water as needed to adjust consistency. Simmer for approximately 45 mins on low-med.
