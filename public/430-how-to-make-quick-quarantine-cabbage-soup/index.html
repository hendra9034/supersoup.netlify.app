The ingredients needed to make Quarantine Cabbage Soup:
  1. Get 3 Tbs olive oil
  2. Prepare 1 white onionchopped
  3. Take 1 bunch celery chopped
  4. Take 4 russet potatoes chopped
  5. Make ready 6 small carrots chopped
  6. Prepare Half a bag of cauliflower pearls
  7. Make ready 1 head cabbage chopped
  8. Get 64 oz Chicken Stock
  9. Prepare Half teaspoon of Wylers Chicken Powder
  10. Prepare 1 Caldo de Pollo chicken Bouillon
  11. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  12. Get 1 can stewed tomatoes
  13. Prepare 2 cups water
Steps to make Quarantine Cabbage Soup:
  1. Heat oil and cook potatoes, onion and carrots for about 5 min.
  2. Add everything else and cook on medium heat for 30 minutes.
  3. Eat.
