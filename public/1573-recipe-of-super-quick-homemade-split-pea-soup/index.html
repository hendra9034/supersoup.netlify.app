The ingredients needed to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. Take 2 cup chicken broth
  2. Make ready 1 cup dry split peas (rinsed & drained)
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 lb meaty pork hocks or meaty ham bone
  4. Take 1/4 tsp dried marjoram crushed
  5. Get 1 bay leaf
  6. Take 1/2 cup chopped carrot
  7. Get 1/2 cup chopped celery
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped onion

Homemade Split Pea Soup, just like mom made growing up! This hearty, protein packed, comforting soup is made with tender split peas, flavorful ham and fresh vegetables. Pea soup takes time to make, but mostly cooks unattended. You can start it on a weekend when you're around the house, early in the afternoon, and make enough for a few days' worth of leftovers.

Instructions to make Split Pea Soup:
  1. In large saucepan combine broth, split peas, ham hock, marjoram, bay leaf, 2 cups of water & dash of pepper.
  2. Bring to boiling, reduce heat, cover & simmer for 1 hour. Stirring occasionally
  3. When cool enough, cut meat off bones. Return meat to saucepan. Stir in carrots, celery & onion.
  4. Return to boiling, reduce heat, cover & simmer for 20-30 min. (til veggies are tender)
