The ingredients needed to make Fried Tortilla Nachos:
  1. Get 8 flour tortillas Tomato and herb or (flour, corn, whole wheat or multigrain) choice
  2. Get cooking oil spray
  3. Take sea salt or kosher salt
  4. Prepare Oil for frying
  5. Prepare Salsa or Any your Favourite dips
Instructions to make Fried Tortilla Nachos:
  1. Pour oil in a heavy skillet; heat oil over low to medium heat. - Cut each tortilla into 8 equal wedges with a sharp knife or pizza cutter. 1. Working in batches, gently arrange tortillas in the hot oil using a slotted spoon; cook, frequently turning tortillas with the slotted spoon, until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove nachos with slotted spoon and place a paper towel-lined plate to drain. - Serve immediately with salsa or your favourite dips. - Enjoy !!!
