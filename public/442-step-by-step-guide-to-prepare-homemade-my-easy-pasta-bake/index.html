Instructions to make My easy pasta bake:
  1. Cook pasta as instructed on package
  2. Brown mince
  3. Add veg cook for 10 mins
  4. Drain pasta but reserve 1 cup of pasta water add mince and veg to pasta
  5. Pour into oven dish top with cheese and bake for 30 minutes
