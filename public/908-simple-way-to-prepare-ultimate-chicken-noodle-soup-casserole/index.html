The ingredients needed to make chicken noodle soup casserole:
  1. Take 1 packages egg noodle
  2. Get 2 can Campbells cream of chicken
  3. Prepare 3 large chicken breasts
  4. Take 1 cup cooked carrots
  5. Get 1 cup water
  6. Make ready 1 packages Ritz crackers
  7. Take 2 cup shredded cheese

Steps to make chicken noodle soup casserole:
  1. First boil the chicken until cooked thoroughly while you are also cooking your noodles
  2. Preheat oven to 350° degrees.
  3. Shred chicken
  4. Then in a greased glass baking dish, I use 9x13, combine cooked noodles, shredded chicken, cooked carrots, cream of chicken and mix
  5. Next add the water to break it up a bit and keep from being so thick, add more if needed.
  6. After mixing everything together, top with shredded cheese and crumbled ritz crackers
  7. put in oven and bake until cheese is melted and the top is a golden brown
