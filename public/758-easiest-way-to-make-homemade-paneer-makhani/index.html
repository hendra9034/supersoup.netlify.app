The ingredients needed to make Paneer Makhani:
  1. Make ready 2 tablespoons Oil
  2. Make ready 2 chopped tomatoes
  3. Get 3 cloves
  4. Get 1 cinnamon stick
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cardamon powder
  6. Prepare 12 cashew nuts
  7. Take 4 green chillies
  8. Take 3 cloves garlic
  9. Take 1 inch ginger
  10. Get 2 tablespoon butter
  11. Make ready Cumin seeds
  12. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  14. Make ready 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  15. Make ready 1 teaspoon jeera powder
  16. Prepare Sprinkle of sugar
  17. Make ready Squirt of tomato ketchup
  18. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon kasturi methi
  19. Make ready 250 g paneer
  20. Make ready Hot boiling water
  21. Take 1 teaspoon garam masala
Instructions to make Paneer Makhani:
  1. I bought chopped paneer so I just opened the pack and covered this in hot boiling water so the paneer stays soft.
  2. Add oil to a pan then add tomatoes cloves cinnamon ginger garlic chillies cardamon powder and cashew nuts and mix well till the tomatoes are soft and mushy. Then wait for this to cool down.
  3. Then I made a paste of these ingredients to make a paste. And left this aside.
  4. Then in the same pan I added butter and cumin seeds and let these pop I then added the paste be careful as it spits. Then add salt chilli powder turmeric powder and jeera powder sugar and ketchup and a teaspoon of garam masala. Mix all this well then drain the paneer and add this to the sauce let this simmer for about 10 minutes. Then sprinkle kasturi methi on top enjoy with chapattis or rice ðŸ˜Š
