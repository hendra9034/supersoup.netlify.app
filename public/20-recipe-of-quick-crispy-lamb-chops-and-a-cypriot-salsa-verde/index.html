The ingredients needed to make Crispy lamb chops and a Cypriot salsa verde:
  1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp sesame seeds
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  3. Prepare 2 garlic cloves
  4. Prepare 1 lemon
  5. Take 6-9 lamb cutles
  6. Take Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  7. Take 1 green chilli
  8. Prepare 1/2 (1 bunch) flat leaf parsley
  9. Take 1/2 (1 bunch) mint
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp capers
  11. Get 4 anchovy fillets
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  13. Make ready 2-4 flatbreads

Steps to make Crispy lamb chops and a Cypriot salsa verde:
  1. Toast the sesame and cumin seeds in a small dry pan and leave to one side.
  2. Peel the garlic cloves. Finely grate one of them into a shallow mixing bowl (big enough to hold all the lamb cutlets) and finely grate in a the zest of the lemon.
  3. Squeeze in the lemon juice, drizzle in a little extra virgin olive oil and season well.
  4. Add the lamb, toss to coat, cover and leave in the fridge to marinate until needed (the longer you can leave it the better.
  5. Halve and deseed the chilli and finely chop on a large chopping board with the remaining garlic clove.
  6. Pick the herb leaves and add to the board along with the capers and anchovies. Chop everything together finely.
  7. Spoon it all into a mixing bowl and stir in the red wine vinegar and enough extra virgin olive oil to loosen.
  8. Place a griddle pan on a high heat. Toast the flatbreads till nicely charred, then tear them and lay over a large wire rack 1.
