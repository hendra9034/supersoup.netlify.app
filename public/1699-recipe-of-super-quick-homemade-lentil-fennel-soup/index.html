The ingredients needed to make Lentil Fennel Soup:
  1. Take 1 Carrots
  2. Make ready 1 Celery
  3. Get 1 Onion
  4. Get 1 Fennel
  5. Make ready 1 Lentils
  6. Prepare 1 Garlic
  7. Take 1 Bay leaf
  8. Take 1 Terragon
  9. Get 1/2 cup Tomato sauce
  10. Make ready 1 Salt & Pepper
  11. Prepare 3 cup Chicken stock
Instructions to make Lentil Fennel Soup:
  1. Sautee chopped carrots, celery, onion, & fennel in olive oil. 10 min
  2. Add garlic, salt & pepper
  3. Add chicken stock, lentils, bay leaf & terragon, bring to a boil
  4. Reduce to simmer 15 min until lentils are tender
