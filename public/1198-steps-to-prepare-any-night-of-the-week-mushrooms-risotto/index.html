The ingredients needed to make Mushrooms risotto:
  1. Make ready 1 red onion, midsize
  2. Get 5 garlic clovers
  3. Take 3 tablespoon fresh parsley
  4. Get 100 g dried mushrooms (Cantharellus lutescens)
  5. Make ready 200 g round rice
  6. Get 100 ml Olive oil
  7. Make ready Mushroom sauce (optional)
  8. Take 1 pinch salt
  9. Take 100 ml white wine

Steps to make Mushrooms risotto:
  1. Chop the onion, garlic and parsley
  2. Wash and hydrate the mushrooms
  3. Heat a flat pan with olive oil and fry the onion, garlic and parsley at high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the mushrooms and keep frying for another 5 minutes
  5. Add the white wine to the pan, mix everything and let the wine evaporate 1. Boil the rice and strain but don't wash it after that
  6. Add the rice and mix it with the rest of the ingredients. - (Optional) Add the mushrooms sauce to give the right consistency to the dish and increase the mushroom taste. - (Optional 2) Sprinkle some goat cheese on top after serving, in fits very well the earthy flavour of the mushrooms.
  7. Serve and enjoy!!
