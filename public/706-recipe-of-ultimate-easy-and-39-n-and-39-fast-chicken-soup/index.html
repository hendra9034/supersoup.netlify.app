The ingredients needed to make Easy 'n' Fast Chicken Soup:
  1. Take Chicken breast
  2. Get Onion
  3. Make ready Carrot
  4. Get Green beans (Frozen)
  5. Prepare Potato
  6. Take Salt
  7. Take Black pepper
  8. Make ready Chicken
Instructions to make Easy 'n' Fast Chicken Soup:
  1. Prepare ingredients (I forgot to add a potato here!) and cut them into small pieces
  2. In large pan, boil chicken until fully cooked
  3. Add chopped onion, carrots, potatoes, 1/2 frozen green beans
  4. Add salt/black pepper/1 chicken cube and stir until everything is cooked
  5. Enjoy!
