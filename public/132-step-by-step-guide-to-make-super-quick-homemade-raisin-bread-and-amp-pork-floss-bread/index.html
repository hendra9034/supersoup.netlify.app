The ingredients needed to make Raisin bread & Pork floss bread:
  1. Get 250 grams high gluten flour
  2. Prepare 3 grams dry yeast
  3. Get 50 grams sugar
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Take 40 grams margarine
  6. Make ready 120 ml fresh milk
  7. Get 4tbs pork floss
  8. Prepare 3tbs raisin
  9. Take 1 egg yolk for brush the top of the bread
