The ingredients needed to make Red Yam Potato Cream Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 onions
  2. Get 1 Red Yam potato
  3. Make ready 1 Potato
  4. Make ready 1 Big carrot
  5. Get 2 Red Chillies
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp oil (corn, sunflower or olive)
  7. Prepare 2 tsp coriander seeds
  8. Take 1 Spring of dill on top
  9. Get 300 g. Condensed chicken broth (Campbell's or any)
  10. Make ready 250 g. Water
Instructions to make Red Yam Potato Cream Soup:
  1. Dice onions, potatoes, carrot, Chillies.
  2. Add coriander seeds, oil, mix all the ingridients.
  3. Pour condensed chicken broth and water.
  4. Mix again and leave in a slow cooker for 1 hour (slow cooking mode)
  5. In 1hr. Pour your soup into mixer and mix hard while it is hot. Serve on a plate with a spring of dill on top.
