The ingredients needed to make Honey and mustard pork chops:
  1. Make ready 4 pork chops
  2. Get 1 tblspn sunflower oil
  3. Make ready 2 tablspn butter
  4. Get 1 tablspn grain mustard
  5. Prepare 1 tablspn honey
  6. Get 2 tablspn soy sauce
  7. Get 2 tablspn cold water
  8. Prepare 2 tspn sugar
  9. Prepare 2 tspn lemon juice
  10. Make ready 1 tspn minced garlic
  11. Get 1/2 tspn dried thyme
  12. Make ready 1 tspn pepper
  13. Take to taste Salt

Steps to make Honey and mustard pork chops:
  1. Pat dry the pork chops and season with salt and pepper of each side of the pork chop.
  2. In a smal bowl, combine soy sauce,mustard, garlic,sugar lemon juice, honey and water. Whisk till everything is combined and set aside.
  3. Next heat the pan and add sunflower oil,then place the pork chops. Cook them for 3 minutes each side.
  4. Remove the chops from the pan, add butter to the same pan, let it melt and add thyme, and the sauce made at stage 2. Whisk everything and immediately add back the pork chops.
  5. Use a tablespoon to pour some of the sauce on top of the chops. Repeat the process for 5 minutes and set aside to serve.
