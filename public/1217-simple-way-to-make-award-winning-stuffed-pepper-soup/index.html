The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Pepper Soup:
  1. Get 2 lbs Ground Beef
  2. Take 6 cups Water
  3. Get 28 oz. Tomato Sauce
  4. Make ready 2 cups Diced Green Peppers
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup Packed Brown Sugar
  6. Prepare 2 Tsp Salt and Pepper each
  7. Make ready 2 Tsp Beef Granules
  8. Take 2 cups Cooked Long Grain Rice
Steps to make Stuffed Pepper Soup:
  1. In a large Pot Brown Beef- Drain
  2. Add next 8 ingredients.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat. Simmer uncover about 30 minute.
  4. Hint: can add 1 - 1 1/2 cups uncooked Rice along with the other ingredients.
  5. Corn Bread goes great with the soup.
