The ingredients needed to make Cuban Beef Picadillo:
  1. Get 250 g Minced Beef
  2. Prepare 1/2 an onion diced
  3. Make ready 200 g fresh tomatoes peeled and chopped
  4. Get 2 Cloves Garlic minced
  5. Take 1/2 tbls Cumin
  6. Make ready 1/4 tbls Oregano
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Chilli Powder
  8. Make ready 10 Stuffed small green olives
  9. Prepare 15 g Sultanas
  10. Make ready 2 tbls Tomato purèe
  11. Prepare 125 g Potatoes diced into sqaures
  12. Take 1/2 cup Beef Stock
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp Red Pepper flakes
  14. Make ready 1/4 courgette sliced
  15. Make ready 1 little Black pepper
  16. Take Some freshly chopped Corriander
Instructions to make Cuban Beef Picadillo:
  1. Add the minced beef, onion and garlic to a large frying pan and cook until browned.
  2. Add the fresh tomatoes and cook until they break up.
  3. Add the potatoes, Cumin, chilli powder and stir to mix in.
  5. Add the peppers, olives and sultanas, tomato paste, Balsamic vinegar and stock.

  8. Stir in the courgette for the last few minutes of cooking.
  9. Season with pepper as the olives are salty you should not need any salt.
  10. Add the fresh Corriander on top and serve. I served mine with a Mediterranean rice mix.
