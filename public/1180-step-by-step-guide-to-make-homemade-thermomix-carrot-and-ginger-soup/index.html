The ingredients needed to make Thermomix: carrot and ginger soup:
  1. Make ready 1/4 onion
  2. Take 3 tbsp butter, softened
  3. Get 3 cups water
  4. Take 3-4 carrots (depending on size), peeled
  5. Make ready 1 tsp ginger powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup heavy cream (whipping cream)
  7. Take to taste salt and pepper
  8. Make ready To serve:
  9. Make ready to taste cubed Monterey Jack cheese (optional)
  10. Prepare cream
  11. Prepare dried parsley

Instructions to make Thermomix: carrot and ginger soup:
  1. Place onion and butter in the mixing bowl, process 20s/0/5 to get the mushy mix pictured. Then cook 10m/250/1
  2. In the meantime, peel your carrots and cut them in medium-sized pieces.
  3. Add water, carrots, ginger, cream, salt and pepper to the bowl, and cook 20m/200/2
  4. Once cooked, blend 1m/0/5-9 (start at speed 5 and gradually increase to 9)
  5. If you want, you can put some cubed Monterey Jack cheese in the plates…
  6. Serve and garnish with cream and dried parsley. Enjoy!
