The ingredients needed to make My Mexican Inspired Pork + Beef Salsa on a Jacket Potato:
  1. Take 250 g pork and beef minced meat
  2. Get 1/4 onion diced
  3. Prepare 1 tbls Mexican spice
  4. Take 1/2 Cup kidney beans red
  5. Make ready 1/2 Cup loose corn
  6. Get 4 tbls passata
  7. Make ready 2 jars mixed salsa sauce
  8. Get 1/2 yellow bell pepper chopped
  9. Get 1/2 tsp paprika
  10. Prepare 3 tbls chipotle sauce
  11. Make ready 1 Cup cheese grated
  12. Take 1 heaped tbls Sour cream mix chopped chives in it
Steps to make My Mexican Inspired Pork + Beef Salsa on a Jacket Potato:
  1. Heat up 1 tsp oil and add the onions then add the Mexican spice mix and paprika. Stir in.
  2. Next add the pork and beef minced meat fry and stir in until all meat is a grey colour. Then add the corn.
  3. Next add the red kidney beans and mix in. Next the chipotle sauce.
  4. Next add the 2 jars of salsa sauce and stir in and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Meanwhile cook your jacket potato and grate the cheese ready.
  6. Next add the passata and stir in. Bring back to the boil then turn low and simmer for 15 minutes stirring often.
  7. Add the cooked potato to the serving dish and cut on top a cross but not right through the potato. Add salt if you need to.
  8. With a ladle scoop out 2 ladle full of mex mix and pour over the potato
  9. Next add about 4 tbls of cheese on top then add to microwave for 30 secounds to melt it a little.
  10. Add the tbls of the sourcream and chive mix and serve and enjoy.
