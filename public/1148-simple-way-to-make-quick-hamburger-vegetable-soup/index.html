The ingredients needed to make Hamburger Vegetable Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
  2. Take 1 chopped onion
  3. Get 2 stalks celery chopped
  4. Make ready 2-3 carrots peeled and chopped
  5. Prepare 2 large potatoes
  6. Prepare 6-8 cups vegetable broth
  7. Take 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp dried basil
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp onion powder
  10. Prepare 1 can green beans
  11. Make ready 1 can corn
  12. Make ready 1 can 14.5 diced fire roasted tomatoes
  13. Take Salt & peper to taste
  14. Take 1/2 garlic powder
  15. Prepare Olive oil
  16. Prepare 4-6 garlic cloves
  17. Prepare Worcestershire
Steps to make Hamburger Vegetable Soup:
  1. In a large pot on medium high sauté onions, garlic and celery. Add hamburger meat and Worcestershire to taste.
  2. Add vegetable broth diced potatoes, carrots,fire roasted tomatoes and all seasonings. Cook for 30 minutes or until potatoes are done.
  3. Drain corn and green beans add to soup cook another 10 minutes! Make some corn bread and Enjoy!
