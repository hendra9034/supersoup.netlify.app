The ingredients needed to make Rice potatoes and mussels:
  1. Take 500 gr mussels (fresh or frozen)
  2. Get 500 gr tomatoes
  3. Get 500 gr potatoes
  4. Get 300 gr rice
  5. Make ready 2 onions
  6. Take Grated pecorino cheese
  7. Get Parsley,garlic salt pepper olive oil

Rice has a longer shelf life than potatoes and it holds its nutrients for longer after harvest, so choose freshly-picked potatoes whenever possible to maximize their nutrient content. Steps to make Rice potatoes and mussels:
  1. Mince a large handful of parsley with 2 garlic cloves,dice the tomatoes and cut onions.
  2. Put a layer of onions in a saucepan and sprinkle them with half the chopped garlic and parsley
  3. Arrange about 250 g of chopped tomatoes and a handful of pecorino cheese
  4. Add salt, place a layer of potato slices in the bowl and spread the rice over it
  5. On the rice place the mussels sprinkle with the remaining chopped garlic and parsley, chopped tomatoes and all the sliced ​​potatoes.
  6. Sprinkle the preparation with olive oil and cover with plenty of water put the pan in the oven(170°) coock for about 35 min.
