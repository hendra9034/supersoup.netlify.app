The ingredients needed to make Weeknight One Pot Goats Cheese Pasta:
  1. Get 300 g rigatoni pasta
  2. Prepare 250 g baby spinach
  3. Make ready 150 g goats cheese
  4. Prepare 50 g pine nuts
  5. Prepare 2 vegetables stock cube
Instructions to make Weeknight One Pot Goats Cheese Pasta:
  1. Boil the kettle.
  2. Make 700ml of stock.
  3. Cube the goats cheese.
  4. In a large frying pan or wok add the pasta, goats cheese and stock. Set on a high heat.
  5. Add the spinach and keep on high until wilted.
  6. Once wilted set heat to medium. Add salt and pepper. Leave until pasta is cooked.
  7. Serve with pine nuts and a sprinkle of pepper.
