The ingredients needed to make Pork rib soup with daikon:
  1. Take 500 g pork ribs
  2. Get 1 medium size daikon(~10 inches long), cut into bite-sized pieces
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup dried mushroom, soaked for 15 min, dried and cut into half before use
  4. Make ready 4 garlic cloves
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp black pepper, coarsely grinded
  6. Get 3 coriander roots, coarsely chopped
  7. Prepare 1 cup pork broth

Instructions to make Pork rib soup with daikon:
  1. In a pot or a large saucepan, add pork ribs and water. Simmer with low-to-medium heat for 20-30 min. Use a fine mesh sieve to skim foam and fat.
  2. Add chopped daikon, mushroom, garlic cloves, coriander roots, black pepper, soy sauce.
  3. Cook until the daikon turn soft and translucent(around 30 min). Add salt to taste.
  4. Serve with steamed rice.
