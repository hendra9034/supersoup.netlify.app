The ingredients needed to make Corn Muffins:
  1. Take 2 small boxes of corn muffin mixes
  2. Take 2 large hen eggs
  3. Prepare 250-300 ml pasteurised cow milk
  4. Take vegetable oil to grease muffin baking tray
Instructions to make Corn Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to gas mark 6. Beat hen eggs in a large mixing bowl and then add the milk - add the packets of corn muffin mix - stir and then allow the mix to rest for 4 minutes.
  2. Grease the muffin baking tray - could put it in the oven for a short while and then add 2 measuring tablespoons in each of the baking tray's holes - of the corn muffin mix and then bake for 20 minutes. Use the end of a metal dessert spoon too loosen around all edges and then rest on a cake rack.
