The ingredients needed to make Penne Beef Ragu:
  1. Get 180 g penne pasta
  2. Make ready 240 g beef mince
  3. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  4. Prepare cube Beef stock
  5. Prepare 1 pinch chilli flakes
  6. Make ready Drizzle of balsamic vinegar
  7. Make ready 20 g rocket
  8. Take 30 g Monterey Jack cheese
  9. Take 1 ciabatta roll
  10. Make ready 1 TBS oil
  11. Make ready 100 ml water
  12. Get 390 g tinned tomatoes

Instructions to make Penne Beef Ragu:
  1. Pre heat grill to highest level and put pasta on to boil.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add the beef. Cook for 5 to 6 minutes until browned. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Peel and grate or use garlic press. Add half of the garlic to the mince and add the tomatoes. Stir well
  4. Add the stock and water along with a pinch of chilli flakes. Add in the cooked pasta and give everything a good stir to fully combine.
  5. For the garlic bread cut the bread in half. Spread on the remaining garlic and grate the cheese and sprinkle over. Grill until cheese melts.
