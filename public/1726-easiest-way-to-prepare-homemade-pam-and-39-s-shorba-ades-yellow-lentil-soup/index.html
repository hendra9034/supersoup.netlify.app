The ingredients needed to make Pam's Shorba Ades (Yellow Lentil Soup)…:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup yellow lentil

  3. Get 1 small onion cut it half

  7. Take 4 cup chicken or vegetables stocks

Steps to make Pam's Shorba Ades (Yellow Lentil Soup)…:
  1. wash the lentil drain set aside…wash and cut vegetables put it a pot with stock bring to boil.when d veg is half cook add the lentil continue cooking untill mushy and the veg is soft.
  2. pour it in the blender….blend to smooth about 1min.pour it back in a pot.bring to boil(you can add more stock if it is too thick)season with salt and pepper and the soup spices.
  3. stir constantly on low heat add parsley/corriander.
  4. serve hot with the splash of lemon juice…enjoy:-)
