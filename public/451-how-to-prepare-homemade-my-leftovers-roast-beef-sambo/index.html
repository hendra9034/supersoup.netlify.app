The ingredients needed to make My leftovers roast beef sambo:
  1. Make ready 2 bread slices
  2. Prepare 2 Thin slices of roast beef
  3. Take 3 tsp Mayonnaise
  4. Take 1 scallion sliced
  5. Make ready 1 tomato sliced
  6. Get 1 Sliced cucumber
  7. Take 3 tsp butter
