The ingredients needed to make #Breakfast#Brunch#LunchMy Sausage Onion & Cheese Slice Bagel:
  1. Make ready 2 Cooked Sausages sliced down the center
  2. Make ready 1 Slice Cheese
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Chopped spring onion + White onion
  4. Take Some Rocket lettuce mixed with Cress
  5. Take 1 tsp Spicy Mayonaise (Optional)
Instructions to make #Breakfast#Brunch#LunchMy Sausage Onion & Cheese Slice Bagel:
  1. Cook the sausage then cut them through to make 4 slimmer sausages
  2. Toast the bagel butter optional
  3. Add the sausages
  4. Next add the cheese slice
  5. Then add the spring onion
  6. Then the rocket and cress
  7. Lastly add the spicy mayo on the top slice of the bagel. If you want the mayo plain don't add the spice mix in the mayonaise(sweet chilli + a pinch of red pepper flakes)
  8. Add the Top and Enjoy.
