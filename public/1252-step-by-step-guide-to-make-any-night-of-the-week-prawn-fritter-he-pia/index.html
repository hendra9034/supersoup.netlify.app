The ingredients needed to make Prawn fritter (he pia):
  1. Get 7 chopped big prawns
  2. Make ready 1 egg
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons plain flour
  4. Take 1 tablespoon corn flour
  5. Take 1 tablespoon chopped shallot
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon chicken powder
