The ingredients needed to make Lentil and Spinach Soup:
  1. Take Soup
  2. Get 2 tbsp Olive oil
  3. Prepare 1 large onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 2 clove garlic, minced
  5. Get 2 cup uncooked lentils
  6. Get 4 cup vegetable broth
  7. Take 4 cup water
  8. Get 1 fresh rosemary, 2-3 in sprig
  9. Make ready 7 oz fresh spinach, chopped
  10. Prepare 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  11. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions to make Lentil and Spinach Soup:
  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion & garlic; cook until onion is soft, about 5 minutes, stirring often.
  2. Add lentils, broth, water, and rosemary, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered until lentils are tender, about 20 minutes.
  3. Add spinach and tomatoes, and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove rosemary sprig. Stir in pepper.
