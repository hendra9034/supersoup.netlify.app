Steps to make Cabbage Soup:
  1. Saute onion, carrot, celery in oil until translucent. Add garlic, cook for 1 min.
  2. Add chopped cabbage, tomato, and spices. Stir to combine.
  3. Add remaining ingredients. Add enough water to cover the vegetables.
  4. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer until vegetables are cooked through, stirring occasionally.
