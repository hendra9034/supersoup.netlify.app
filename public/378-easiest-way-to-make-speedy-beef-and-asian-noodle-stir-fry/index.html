The ingredients needed to make Beef and Asian Noodle Stir Fry:
  1. Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
  2. Prepare Green and / or red pepper finely sliced
  3. Make ready 2 sirloin beef fillet thinly sliced
  4. Get Udon Noodles
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  6. Take 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  8. Make ready 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  11. Take Pinch chilli flakes
Steps to make Beef and Asian Noodle Stir Fry:
  1. Whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, chilli flakes, brown sugar
  2. Add oil to a pan and brown the beef and add peppers
  3. Cook the noodles in boiling water for a couple of minutes
  4. Add the noodles and soy sauce mixture to the beef and serve immediately
