The ingredients needed to make Slow cooked beef ragu:
  1. Prepare 250 g beef mince
  2. Prepare 400 g chopped tomatoes
  3. Get 400 g water
  4. Get 240 g tagliatelle
  5. Make ready 1 tsp minced garlic
  6. Prepare 1/2 diced white onion
  7. Prepare 1 diced carrot
  8. Get 1 diced celery stick
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato purée
  10. Get 1 beef stock cube crushed
  11. Make ready 100 ml red wine
  12. Get 2 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 2 tsp pepper
  14. Make ready 2 tsp dried rosemary
  15. Take 1 tsp dried thyme
  16. Take Grated parmesan for serving

Instructions to make Slow cooked beef ragu:
  1. Fry your mince until brown for approximately 7 minutes
  2. Throw all of your ingredients apparat from your tagliatelle in the slow cooker and leave on low for 8 hours
  3. Boil your tagliatelle for 10 minutes
  4. Mix everything together and serve with grated parmesan
