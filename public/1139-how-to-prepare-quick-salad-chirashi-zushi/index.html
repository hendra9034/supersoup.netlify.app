The ingredients needed to make Salad Chirashi-zushi:
  1. Make ready Sushi Rice
  2. Make ready 2 cups (*180ml cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  3. Take 50 ml Rice Vinegar
  4. Prepare 40 g (about 40ml) Sugar
  5. Prepare Slightly less than 1 teaspoon (4g) Salt
  6. Prepare Other Ingredient Suggestions
  7. Get 1. Protein
  8. Prepare Ham
  9. Take Cooked Prawns
  10. Take Smoked Salmon
  11. Take Sashimi (Fresh Raw Fish *e.g. Tuna, Salmon, etc.)
  12. Get Edamame Beans
  13. Take Eggs (Thin Omelette Strips, Dry Scrambled, Boiled, etc.) (see recipe)
  14. Prepare 2. Vegetables
  15. Make ready Lettuce
  16. Take Cucumber
  17. Take Carrot *blanched in salted water
  18. Prepare Cherry Tomatoes
  19. Make ready Avocado
  20. Take Snow Peas OR Sugar Snap Peas *blanched in salted water
  21. Prepare Asparagus *blanched in salted water
  22. Get 3. Sauce
  23. Make ready Japanese Mayonnaise & Soy Sauce
  24. Prepare *Note: You may wish to add some Wasabi
Instructions to make Salad Chirashi-zushi:
  1. Cook Rice with slightly less amount of water to achieve firmer texture. Make Sushi Vinegar by mixing Rice Vinegar, Sugar and Salt.
  2. When Rice is cooked and steamed, pour the Sushi Vinegar over the HOT rice, then empty it into a large mixing bowl. Mix it well gently and thoroughly, fanning the rice with a fan or piece of cardboard to help the rice cool quickly. Cover the Rice with a damp cloth to stop the Rice drying out.
  3. When the Rice is cooled, mix with other ingredients. Some ingredients are more suitable for topping. Decorate Sushi Salad as you like. Mix Japanese Mayonnaise with some Soy Sauce, and drizzle it over OR serve with the sauce.
