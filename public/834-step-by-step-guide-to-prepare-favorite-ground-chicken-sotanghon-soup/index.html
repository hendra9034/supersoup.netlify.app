The ingredients needed to make Ground chicken sotanghon soup:
  1. Get 3 teaspoon vegetable oil
  2. Make ready 3 cloves garlic
  3. Get 3 pieces scallion
  4. Prepare 2 servings sotanghon or cellophane noodles
  5. Take 1 teaspoon pepper
  6. Take 2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  8. Prepare 1/2 kg ground chicken
  9. Take 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  10. Get 3 cups water

Instructions to make Ground chicken sotanghon soup:
  1. Saute garlic and scallion in vegetable oil.
  2. Add the ground chicken, cook until it turns to brown color.
  3. Put in oyster sauce
  4. Then soy sauce and pepper
  5. Add 3 cups of water
  6. And lastly put the sotanghon noodles in and let cook for 3 minutes. Serve. Good for 2-3 persons
