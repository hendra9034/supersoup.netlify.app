The ingredients needed to make 🥞🍚Pastry Cream🍚🥞:
  1. Take 1 & 1/3 cup (330g) Whole milk
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup + 2 tbsp (90g) Sugar
  4. Prepare 2 & 1/2 tbsp corn flour
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  6. Get Pinch salt
Instructions to make 🥞🍚Pastry Cream🍚🥞:
  1. In a pan, combine the sugar and corn flour. - - Add the milk and lightly beaten eggs, and bring to a simmer.
  2. Whisk constantly and cook until the cream starts to thicken. - - Remove from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. - - Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate.
