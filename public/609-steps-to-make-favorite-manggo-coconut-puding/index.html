The ingredients needed to make Manggo Coconut Puding:
  1. Make ready 800 grams mango flesh, puree
  2. Prepare 1100 ml liquid milk
  3. Take 500 ml coconut milk
  4. Get 200 grams sugar (reduce if the mango is too sweet)
  5. Make ready 2 packs plain agar-agar swallow powder
Instructions to make Manggo Coconut Puding:
  1. Mix the agar-agar powder, sugar, liquid milk and coconut milk.  Cook while stirring until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the mango flesh.  Keep stirring until it starts to boil.  Lift.
  3. Pour into the mold.  Allow it to come to room temperature and then refrigerate until set.
