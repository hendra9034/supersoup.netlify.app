The ingredients needed to make Cabbage vegetable soup:
  1. Make ready 1 small onion
  2. Make ready 2 whole bell peppers
  3. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  4. Make ready 4 cups chopped cabbage
  5. Get 1 cup diced carrots
  6. Prepare 1 cup green beans
  7. Prepare 1 can diced tomatoes 28oz

  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon Tyme

Instructions to make Cabbage vegetable soup:

  2. Add carrots, cabbage, green beans cook additional 5 mins
  3. Stir in pepper, broth, tomatoes, bay leaves and seasoning simmer for 8 mins
  4. Add rest ingredient for 5 more mins
  5. Remove bay leaves before serving it
