Instructions to make Meatball soup:
  1. Line a baking sheet with foil and lightly oil it with olive oil
  2. Mix all meatball ingredients by hand until combined.
  3. Form 1 inch meatballs and space evenly on the baking sheet. Broil on high for 6 minutes. Remove from oven and set aside.
  4. Heat oil in a large pot
  5. Add chopped vegetables and minced garlic and cook on med-high heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add broth, tomatoes, and spices. Cook on med-high heat for 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in meatballs and pasta. Cook for 10 minutes.
  8. Turn off heat, remove from stovetop, cover and let sit for 15-30 minutes.
  9. Serve with chopped parsley
