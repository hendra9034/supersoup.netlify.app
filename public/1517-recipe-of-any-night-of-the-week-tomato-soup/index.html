The ingredients needed to make Tomato Soup:
  1. Get 2 tbsp Butter
  2. Make ready 2 Bay leaves
  3. Make ready 4 chopped tomatoes
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp All purpose flour
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Sugar
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Make ready to taste Salt
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup Fresh cream

Steps to make Tomato Soup:
  1. In a pan add 1tbsp of butter. To it add bay leaves. Sauté for couple of seconds. Then add chopped tomatoes.
  2. Sauté for couple of minutes and then add 1/2cup water.
  3. Let it cook till the tomatoes become soft and mushy.
  4. Let the tomatoes cool down completely.
  5. Once the tomatoes cool down, take out the bay leaves and grind the tomatoes.
  6. Strain the tomatoes to get rid of any skin.
  7. Take a pan and add butter to it, then add all purpose flour. Cook it for a couple of minutes and then add tomato purée to it. Add sugar, salt, black pepper to it. Cook it for 3-5 minutes and then add fresh cream to it.
  8. Enjoy hot!!!!
