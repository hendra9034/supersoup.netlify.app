The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Minestrone Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 carrots
  2. Take 1 onion
  3. Take 1 small celery root (celeriac)
  4. Make ready 2 zucchini
  5. Get 1 yellow bell pepper
  6. Make ready 1 pack diced tomatoes
  7. Make ready 1 can red beans
  8. Make ready salt
  9. Get dried vegetable bouillon
  10. Get olive oil
Instructions to make Vegetarian Minestrone Soup:
  1. Cut the carrot (into slices), onion, and celery into bigger pieces and cook it with water until half soft. When it starts boiling lower the heat and cook it slowly under the lid.
  2. Clean bell pepper and zucchini cut it. Once the root vegetable is half soft add bell pepper and zucchini and cook until they are soft but not mushy.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, dried bouillon, and canned beans. Season it with salt and olive oil. Heat it for a little while and the soup is ready to serve. Bon appetite!!!
