The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Spring Onions Fritters:

(Palak aur Hari Pyaz ke Pakore)::

  1. Take 2 cups spinach
  2. Make ready 150 g spring onions chopped
  3. Get 1 onion cut into sliced
  4. Get 1 potato cut into thin strips (Julienne)
  5. Prepare 2-3 green chilies chopped
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup mint & coriander leaves
  7. Take 1/3 tsp salt or to taste
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  9. Get 1 & 1/2 cup gram flour (besan) 2 tbsps more if needed)
  10. Get 3 Tbsp rice flour
  11. Take 1 tsp corn flour
  12. Take 1 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  13. Prepare Oil for deep frying
