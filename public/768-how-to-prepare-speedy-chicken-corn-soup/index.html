The ingredients needed to make Chicken Corn Soup:
  1. Make ready water or chicken or vegetable broth or stock
  2. Get corn drained and rinsed
  3. Get soy sauce
  4. Prepare Chinese cooking wine (optional)
  5. Make ready corn starch mixed in 1 tbsp of water
  6. Take egg lightly beaten
  7. Take cubed or shredded cooked chicken OR
  8. Get small cubed uncooked chicken
  9. Prepare Salt and pepper
  10. Take Chopped scallion
Steps to make Chicken Corn Soup:
  1. Heat wok or sauce pan over high heat. (IF USING COOKED CHICKEN, SKIP TO STEP 3)
  2. If using uncooked chicken, place in wok and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add the first 5 ingredients to wok and bring to a boil.
  4. Lower heat and cook for 5 minutes or until soup thickens. Taste for seasoning and add salt and pepper if necessary.
  5. Turn off heat and drizzle in egg while stirring (This will cook the egg and thicken soup further).
  6. If using pre-cooked chicken, add to soup now and garnish with chopped scallion.
