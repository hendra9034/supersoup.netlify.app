Steps to make Homemade steak and mashed potato with garlic and pepper sauce:
  1. Season the steak with sea salt, black pepper, and olive oil. Let it sit for at least 10 minutes
  2. Put the pan on a high heat. Use a thick pan to ensure the heat is consistent. Once the pan is hot enough, drizzle olive oil
  3. Put the steak onto the pan and let it sits for 3 minutes. Don't touch!
  4. Put on the garlic and thyme just to add flavour. Bask the steak briefly.
  5. Flip the steak, ant wait for 3 minutes again. Bask it with the butter, garlic and thyme. After that, take out the steak and let it sits in a room temperature for 10 minutes.
  6. While the steak is cooking, make the sauce by heat butter in the saucepan. When the butter is melted, put the mushroom. Mix well until golden brown.
  7. Add the double cream, and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Boil the corn and green bean.
  9. Serve the steak with mashed potato, creamy mushroom sauce, and the veggies.
