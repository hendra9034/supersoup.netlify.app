The ingredients needed to make Caldo De Pollo (Chicken Soup):
  1. Make ready Chicken Drumsticks
  2. Get 2 Roma Tomatoes (sliced to liking)
  3. Prepare Baby Carrots
  4. Make ready 4 Large Potatoes diced large
  5. Get 2 Zucchini diced large
  6. Take 4 tbsp Chicken Bouillon
  7. Take 2 tbsp Tomato Bouillon
  8. Make ready Salt to taste
  9. Take 2-3 stems of cilantro
  10. Make ready 1 cup Rice
Steps to make Caldo De Pollo (Chicken Soup):
  1. Wash chicken and place in a large pot with 4 quarts of water. Let cook for about 25 minutes, until mostly cooked but not completely. Take foam off top and dispose.
  2. Add Chicken bouillon and tomato bouillon. Add diced potatoes, zucchini, and carrots. Cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Add tomato and rice and cook for another 20 minutes or until rice is cooked through. Add salt if needed. Add cilantro stems at the last minute and turn off heat. (Rice amount can be increased or decreased depending on personal preference, I prefer a lot of rice)
