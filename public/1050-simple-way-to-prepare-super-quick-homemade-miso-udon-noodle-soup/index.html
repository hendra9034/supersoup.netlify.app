The ingredients needed to make Miso Udon Noodle Soup:
  1. Prepare 1.5 oz miso paste
  2. Prepare 6-7 cups water
  3. Make ready 4-6 shiitaki mushrooms
  4. Prepare 1 udon noodle block, cooked
  5. Make ready 1 tofu block, cooked + cubed
  6. Prepare 1 nori sheet, strips
  7. Take spring onions, chopped
Steps to make Miso Udon Noodle Soup:
  1. In a pot, whisk miso and water together and bring to a boil. Add shiitaki mushrooms and tofu.
  2. Divide cooked udon into bowls. Pour in soup. Garnish with nori and green onions.
