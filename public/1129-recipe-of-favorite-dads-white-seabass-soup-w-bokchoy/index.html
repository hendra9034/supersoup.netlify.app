The ingredients needed to make Dad’s White Seabass Soup w/Bokchoy:
  1. Get 1 lb white seabass cut into steaks
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp white miso
  3. Make ready Salt & pepper to taste
  4. Take Chicken bouillon to taste
  5. Prepare Bokchoy
  6. Take 4 garlic cloves; minced
  7. Take 1 knob ginger; thinly sliced rounds
  8. Make ready 4 green onions; 3 in pieces or chopped
Instructions to make Dad’s White Seabass Soup w/Bokchoy:
  1. In a pot of water, add ginger, garlic, and onion and bring it to a boil.
  2. Season the soup with salt & pepper and chicken bouillon cube. - - Feel free to substitute with white pepper.
  3. Turn heat to low. Add in your fish and use a tea strainer to stir in the miso into the soup.
  4. Lastly add your bokchoy. Simmer until cooked and enjoy with rice!
