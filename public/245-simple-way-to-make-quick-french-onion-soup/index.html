The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Take 2 Lbs. Onions
  2. Make ready 4 cloves Garlic
  3. Get Splash White Wine
  4. Get 4 Tbs. Butter
  5. Get Few Shakes of Worcestershire Sauce
  6. Prepare Squirt of Mustard
  7. Get 2 Pinches Oregano
  8. Get 2 Pinches Parsley
  9. Prepare 3 Tbs. Flour
  10. Make ready French Bread
  11. Get Cheddar Cheese
  12. Make ready 1.5 Liters Beef Broth
  13. Make ready Black Pepper
  14. Make ready Salt
  15. Get 1 Bay Leaf
Instructions to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Half, peel and dice onions. Place in a large bowl. Peel and dice garlic, reserve in another bowl.
  2. Heat butter on medium low heat. Add in onions and season. Stir occasionally.
  3. Slowly cook these onions. When the onions are browning nicely, add in the garlic. Cook until browned. Add a healthy slash of white wine. Cook out the alcohol.
  4. After onions are looking caramelized, add in the flour. Stir and cook a few more minutes. They should be looking very appealing by now.
  5. Add in bay leaf, oregano, parsley stock, Worcestershire sauce and a squirt of mustard. Stir, cover, reduce heat to low.
  6. Make four little mounds of cheese and place them on a pan. Turn the heat on medium and allow them to melt. When they’re brown around the edges, turn off the heat. 1. Serve with bread and cheese. A sandwich is never a bad idea.
