Steps to make Pizza with Spring Roll Pastry:
  1. Pile up 3 spring roll pastries with spreaded beaten egg in between
  2. Spread ketchup with the sides left as it is
  3. Put any ingredients you like
  4. Grate cheese and sprinkle on the top
  5. Heat in an oven for 10min with 200℃
  6. When the cheese melts and the sides of the pastry become crunchy, it’s ready to eat!
