The ingredients needed to make Lentil Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup lentils
  2. Take 4 cups water
  3. Make ready 1/2 onion
  4. Get 3 cloves garlic
  5. Get 1 orange's zest
  6. Prepare 2 tomatoes
  7. Get 1 tsp tomato paste
  8. Get 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  9. Take 3/4 tea-cup olive oil
  10. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  11. Get 1 tsp pepper
  12. Take 1/2 tsp oregano
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp rosemary

Instructions to make Lentil Soup:
  1. Cover lentils with enough water that there is 1in of water above lentils and bring to a boil for 5 minutes.
  2. Chop onions, crush garlic, and blend tomatoes.
  3. Drain lentils in a collander.
  4. Fry onions and garlic for a few minutes, then add lentils.
  5. After stirring, add tomatoes, tomato paste, and the 4 cups of water.
  6. Add seasoning and orange zest.
  7. Bring to boil and simmer on medium/low for 30 minutes.
  8. Add vinegar right before serving.
