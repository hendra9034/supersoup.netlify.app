The ingredients needed to make Hot Sausage & Potato Soup:
  1. Take 1 lb Hot Sausage
  2. Take 1 yellow onion (diced)
  3. Get 5 russet potatoes (pennies)
  4. Make ready 2 large cloves garlic (grated)
  5. Take 48 oz low-sodium chicken broth
  6. Prepare 1 bunch kale (1 inch chopped)
  7. Make ready Dash salt
  8. Prepare Dash ground thyme
