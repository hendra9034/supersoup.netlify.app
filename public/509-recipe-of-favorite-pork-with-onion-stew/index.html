  2. Prepare 1 tsp parsley
  3. Get 1 tsp thyme
  4. Make ready 1 bottle Guinness
  5. Prepare 1 cup tomato sauce
  6. Take 2 cups water
  7. Make ready 1 cup chopped potatoes
  8. Prepare 1 cup chopped carrots
  9. Get 2 cups chopped unions
