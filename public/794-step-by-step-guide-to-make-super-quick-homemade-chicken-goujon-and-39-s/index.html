Instructions to make Chicken Goujon's:
  1. Place enough Vegetable Oil in a non-stick Wok or Wok for deep frying, Place a cube of White bread in the cold Vegetable Oil within the Wok and when the cube of bread becomes crisp and golden brown then get it out of the hot Vegetable Oil and then turn the hob down too the lowest setting.
  2. Wash Chicken Breasts under the cold water tap and then chop into dice's - Place diced raw damp chicken breasts into the seasoned flour and then gradually add some raw diced chicken too the beaten eggs and then place into the large Mixing Bowl with the Panko Breadcrumbs in (could make your own Breadcrumbs with the use of a Food Processor).
  3. Fill up the Wok with your raw Goujon's and then turn up the hob too the highest setting and then deep fry them for 5 minutes, place the hot cooked Goujon's into a large bowl with Kitchen Roll underneath and then allow to rest for no longer than 5 minutes and then enjoy with either Shop bought Salad Cream or Shop bought Tomato Ketchup Sauce.
