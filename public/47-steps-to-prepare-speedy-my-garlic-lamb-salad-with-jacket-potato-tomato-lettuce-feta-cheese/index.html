The ingredients needed to make My Garlic Lamb Salad with jacket Potato, tomato, lettuce, Feta cheese:
  1. Get Some Garlic + Rosemary lamb I cooked earlier
  2. Get 3 tbls Chopped iceberg lettuce
  3. Take 1 Vine Tomato sliced
  4. Prepare 1 potato cooked in the skin then crisped
  5. Take 1 tbls Feta cheese diced
  6. Take 1 tbls diced sweet pepper diced
  7. Take 1 tbls Soft Cheese
  8. Take 1 tbls mixed lettuce
  9. Prepare Salt
  10. Make ready 2 tbls Yogurt and Mint sauce
Instructions to make My Garlic Lamb Salad with jacket Potato, tomato, lettuce, Feta cheese:
  1. Cook the jacket Potato first. Then add the Soft cheese
  2. Add the lettuce and the sweet bell pepper.
  3. Add Tomatoes, then add the cooked potato,then add the cheese and lamb and Toasted Garlic bread.
  4. Add all the rest around add the salt and then finish with the yogurt and meat sauce
