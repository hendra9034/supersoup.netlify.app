The ingredients needed to make Red Lentil And Bacon Soup:
  1. Take 1 cup Red lentils
  2. Get 3 slice Smoked Bacon, diced
  3. Take 1/2 medium Sweet onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 4 clove Garlic, smashed and sliced
  5. Make ready 1 Parsnip, peeled and chopped
  6. Take 1 box Mushroom broth
  7. Get 1 cup Water
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Paprika
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  10. Get 1 pinch Salt
  11. Take 2 Baby bell mushrooms (for garnish)
Instructions to make Red Lentil And Bacon Soup:
  1. In a medium pot, crisp the bacon on medium heat.
  2. Then add the parsnip and onion. Cook together for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Next add the garlic and cook about 3 minutes more.
  4. After adding the broth and water, stir in the lentils.
  5. Allow to simmer for 30-40 minutes stirring occasionally.
  6. Once the soup has thickened up, combine the paprika, turmeric and salt.
  7. Pour all the contents into a blender and liquify for 10 seconds.
  8. Transfer to bowls and serve hot. Garnish with a few mushroom slices or a squiggle of ketchup.
