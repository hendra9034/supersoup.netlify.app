The ingredients needed to make Rava upma:
  1. Get 1 cup rava
  2. Take 2 tbsp oil
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp mustard seed
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp chana dal
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp urad dal
  6. Get 1/2 cup rosted peanut
  7. Get 1 small chopped unions
  8. Get 2 chopped green chilli
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp curry leave
  10. Prepare as needed coriander leave
  11. Take to taste salt
  12. Make ready as needed water

Instructions to make Rava upma:
  1. Heat a pan add rava and rosting the rava.
  2. Heat a pan in 2 tbsp oil second add a mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, fry till they begin to brown a bit
  3. Immediately then add chopped onion, chopped green chilli and being to fry now add curry leave, rosted peanut saute for a minute
  4. Then add water and add the salt as required, coriander leave and boil the water and add the rosted rava and mix it,
  5. And cook 2 minutes and after a 2 minutes then switch off the flame. Rava Upama is ready! Enjoy! !!
