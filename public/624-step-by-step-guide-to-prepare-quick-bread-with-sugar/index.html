Instructions to make Bread with sugar:
  1. Get a loaf of bread from the baker's.
  2. Clear and clean your kitchen countertop, wash your hands thoroughly and take the bread out of its bag.
  3. Place it on the countertop, lengthwise.
  4. Use a knife to cut thing slices (3/4 of a cm).
  5. Spread your slices on a plastic plate, its size depending on the amount of slices that you have.
  6. Fill a cup with tap water.
  7. Dip your fingers in the water and then sprinkle the slices of bread to slightly wet them.
  8. Using a teaspoon sprinkle the slices with sugar.
  9. Then, dipping your fingers in the water once more, sprinkle some water over the sugar.
  10. Your slices of bread are ready for you to enjoy.
