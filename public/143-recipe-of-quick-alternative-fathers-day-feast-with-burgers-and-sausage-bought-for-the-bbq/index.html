The ingredients needed to make Alternative Father’s Day Feast with Burgers and Sausage Bought for the BBQ !:
  1. Prepare Meat Balls
  2. Prepare 4 pack mince burgers
  3. Take 2 cloves garlic diced or 1 tbsp of squeezy garlic
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  5. Take 6 Sausages
  6. Get 2 tbsp flour
  7. Prepare Salt and pepper
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp hot paprika
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp oregano or other dried herbs
  10. Prepare Sausage Rolls
  11. Get 14 sausages good quality
  12. Get 1 tbsp sweet chilli jam
  13. Get 1 tbsp whole grain mustard
  14. Make ready Chopped parsley
  15. Get 2 packets puff pastry
  16. Make ready 2 eggs beaten
  17. Make ready Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Alternative Father’s Day Feast with Burgers and Sausage Bought for the BBQ !:
  1. Take the burgers out of the packet and place in a large bowl. Cut the end of the sausage and squeeze them out of their skins into the bowl too!
  2. Add the spice, garlic and herbs and mix really well with your hand or fork.
  3. Put the flour on a small plate dab you palm in!
  4. Using a ice cream scoop, take out the mixture and mould into a ball with your floury hand. Set aside!
  5. This makes 18 meatballs! Put the oil into a large no stick frying pan, heat it to a medium high heat, place in the meatballs. Keep turning them until they are cooked all the way through, lower the heat and keep them warm on a low heat.
  6. Make the BBQ Sauce! It’s in my recipes! Recipe is so easy and made in a few mins!
  7. Making the Sausage rolls! - Heat your oven to 200 degrees
  8. Take 3 bowls and split the sausages into 3 bowls and squeeze them out of their skins! Add the chilli jam to one bowl, mustard to another and parsley to the third! Sprinkle with salt and pepper and mash up with a fork!
  9. Roll out the pastry, add the sausage mix to the edge of the pastry, turn over into the middle then crimp the edges with a fork, cut into equal sizes.
  10. In a bowl beat the eggs and brush over the beaten eggs! Repeat another 2 times! I’ve kept the remaining pastry to make puff pastry twists later.
  11. Place on a lined baking tray and cook for 20 - 25 mins until crispy on the top and bottom! Serve !
