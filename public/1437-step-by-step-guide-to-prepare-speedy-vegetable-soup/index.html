The ingredients needed to make Vegetable soup:
  1. Make ready 2 medium potatoes or 3 small potatoes
  2. Get 2 zucchinis
  3. Get 2 cups butternut squash
  4. Get 1.5 cups carrots
  5. Prepare 3 chicken bouillon
  6. Prepare 1 frozen garlic
