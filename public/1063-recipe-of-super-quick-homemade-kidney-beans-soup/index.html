The ingredients needed to make Kidney Beans Soup:
  1. Prepare 450 g Light Red kidney Beans
  2. Take 935 g Stew Meat
  3. Take 1 kg Beef Marrow Bones
  4. Make ready 7 Big - Medium size Potatoes (Chopped)
  5. Make ready 1 Onion (diced)
  6. Make ready 6 Carots (Chopped)
  7. Make ready 1 stalk Celery (Chopped)
  8. Take 3 Stalk Green Onion (Chopped)
  9. Make ready Salt (as you needed)
  10. Prepare White pepper (as you needed)
  11. Get 1 Tbsp Nutmeg
  12. Take 3 Tbs Butter
  13. Make ready 5 Tbsp Beef / Chicken Powder
  14. Prepare 4 Liter Water
Instructions to make Kidney Beans Soup:
  1. Pre-soak your beans the night before cooking. In a stainless steel pot, cook the beans and beef marrow bones on medium heat with the lid tilted open. Cook it for one hour or until the beans are soft and tender. Then, add the stew meat, potatoes and carrots. Continue to cook the soup until the potatoes and carrots become soft.
  2. While your beans and vegetables are cooking, sautÃ© the onion in a frying pan with butter until it becomes light brown. Then add it into the soup and season the soup with beef/chicken powder, nutmeg, salt and pepper as preferred. Lastly, garnish with green onion.
