The ingredients needed to make Not a Bolognese:
  1. Make ready 1 large onion, diced
  2. Take 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. Make ready 2 tsp garlic puree
  4. Get 650 g beef mince (12% fat)
  5. Get 2 tbsp tomato puree
  6. Make ready 2 tsp chilli puree or chilli powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 jar red chilli pesto
  8. Take 2 anchovies
  9. Get 250 ml red wine (optional)
  10. Prepare 250 ml beef stock
  11. Get 1 tsp marmite (yeast extract)
  12. Get Salt and pepper to taste
  13. Prepare 2 tsp dried Italian herbs
  14. Get 400 g tin chopped tomatoes
  15. Prepare Small punnet baby button mushrooms, halved
  16. Make ready Small punnet cherry tomatoes, halved

Instructions to make Not a Bolognese:
  1. Fry onion in oil for 5 mins on low heat until translucent.
  2. Add garlic puree and fry for 2 minutes on low heat.
  3. Add beef mince and fry until browned on medium heat.
  4. Add tomato and chilli purees, fry on medium heat for 2 minutes.
  5. Add red pesto and anchovies, fry on medium heat for 2 minutes.
  6. (Optional) add red wine, reduce for approximately 5 minutes.
  7. Add beef stock, reduce for approximately 5 minutes.
  8. Add marmite, salt and pepper any dried Italian herbs.
  9. Add chopped tomatoes, cherry tomatoes and mushrooms. Stir well and cook on low heat until tomatoes have disintegrated and liquid has evaporated.
  10. Turn off heat and cover until serving. This dish is best cooked in advance and reheated when needed.
  11. Serve with fresh pasta of choice or jacket potatoes, with plenty of fresh grated parmesan cheese and garlic bread.
