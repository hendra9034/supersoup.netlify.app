The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Rice One-Pot (St Helena Plo):
  1. Take 450 g Chicken (drumstick, wing or breast is best)
  2. Take 1 onion (diced)
  3. Take 4 Potatoes (Chopped)
  4. Get 1/2 small White Cabbage (Chopped) Optional
  5. Make ready 2 Medium Carrots (Chopped)
  6. Take 1/2 small butternut squash or pumpkin (chopped) Optional
  7. Take 1-2 tbsp Medium Curry
  8. Take 225 g white basmatti Rice
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp Cooking Oil
  10. Get 1 tsp Sugar
  11. Prepare dash tomato ketchup
  12. Make ready dash Worcestershire Sauce
Instructions to make Chicken and Rice One-Pot (St Helena Plo):
  1. Heat oil and fry Onion until starting to brown.
  2. Add curry powder, sugar and a dash of tomato sauce. Cook for about a minute.
  3. Add chicken and coat in the curry paste. Add enough water to cover the Meat. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add chopped vegetables. And add sufficient water to cover all ingredients. Cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the rice and season to taste with salt and a dash of Worcestershire sauce to taste. Cover and bring back to a boil, simmer gently for 12-15 minutes (depending on rice recommended cooking times). Stirring occasionally. Add a little more water if needed and cook until almost all the water has evaporated and the meat and vegetable are all cooked.
