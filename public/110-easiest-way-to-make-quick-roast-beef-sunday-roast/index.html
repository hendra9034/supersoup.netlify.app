The ingredients needed to make Roast Beef Sunday Roast:
  1. Take 1 small beef joint
  2. Get 4 sprigs rosemary
  3. Take 100 g carrot
  4. Get 100 g swede
  5. Prepare 1 knob butter
  6. Take 1 tsp English mustard
  7. Prepare 20 g cauliflower
  8. Take 30 g cheese
  9. Take 50 g potatoes
  10. Make ready 8 cocktail sausages
  11. Prepare 4 rashers streaky smoked bacon
  12. Take 2 potato croquets
  13. Get 80 g stuffing mix
  14. Make ready 10 g piccalilli
  15. Take 6 stems broccoli
  16. Prepare 3 tsp salt
  17. Take 3 tsp pepper
  18. Take 4 tbsp flour
  19. Make ready 8 tbsp milk
  20. Make ready 1 egg
  21. Prepare 3 tbsp goose fat
  22. Make ready 300 ml beef stock
Instructions to make Roast Beef Sunday Roast:
  1. Season your beef with salt and pepper, roast for 40 minutes with rosemary springs and leave to rest for 20 minutes
  2. Whisk egg, flour and milk and pour into Yorkshire pudding trays and cook for 25 minutes. Don’t open the oven
  3. Boil your carrot and swede for 20 minutes and mash with butter and mustard
  4. Par boil potatoes and cauliflower for 5 minutes, get goose fat hot in the oven and get your potatoes in there for 40 minutes turning regularly, sprinkle cheese on cauliflower and get them in there for the same amount of time
  5. Wrap sausages with bacon and get in the oven for 20 minutes
  6. Mix your stuffing into balls and get in the oven for 20 minutes
  7. Get potato croquets in the oven for 20 minutes
  8. Boil broccoli for 7 minutes
  9. Get the juice from the beef in a pan with beef stock, rosemary and flour and whisk in the hob, simmer until slightly thickened for the gravy
  10. Serve everything with piccalilli on the side
