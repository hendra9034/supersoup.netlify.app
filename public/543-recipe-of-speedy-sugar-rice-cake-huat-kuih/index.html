The ingredients needed to make Sugar Rice Cake (Huat Kuih):
  1. Take 130 garam cooked white rice (cold)
  2. Get 80 garam castor sugar
  3. Take 300 ml water
  4. Make ready 150 garam rice flour (100% rice)
  5. Take 1 tsp instant yeast
  6. Prepare 2 tsp vegetable oil
  7. Get Food color (not compulsary)
Instructions to make Sugar Rice Cake (Huat Kuih):
  1. Blend Cooked rice and Sugar with water in blender into a thick smooth paste. Transfer to a bowl.
  2. Add in Rice flour. Mix well without lumps. The mixed rice mixture should be smooth and thick. (Add 1 or 2 tsp rice flour to thicken the mixture if needed)
  3. Add in 2 tsp instant yeast. Mix well. Cover the bowl. Let it ferment for 1 to 2 hours.
  4. After fermenting process, add 2 tsp oil and mix well. Cover for 15minutes.
  5. Mix for less than 1 minute and fill in the moulds. Boil water in steamer pot. Place the moulds on the steamer tray/plate. Steam for 20 minutes on high flame.
