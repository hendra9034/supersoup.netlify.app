The ingredients needed to make Creamy curry noodle soup, extra hot:
  1. Take 1 cup mushroom
  2. Make ready 2 big radishes
  3. Get 1 small Diced squash
  4. Make ready 1 cup Diced potatoes
  5. Get Fresh parcely, rosemary and thyme as needed (or dried)
  6. Get 1 table spoon ketchup
  7. Prepare 2 jalapenos, finely chopped
  8. Prepare Salt and pepper as needed. Taste to like
  9. Make ready 2 table spoons spicy curry spice, or taste to like
  10. Make ready 1 table spoon mixed abido spice, or taste to like
  11. Get Approximately 50 grams of Cream cheese
  12. Prepare Approximately 1 liter of water

Instructions to make Creamy curry noodle soup, extra hot:
  1. Fry the mushrooms
  2. Put oil in pan and mix with flour until it becomes a big ball
  3. Pour in water slowly and mix until they fuse together, and becomes liquid
  4. Add the potatoes and let boil for a 5 minutes. Add a more water as soup thickens.
  5. Add rest of vegetables add spices.Mix together
  6. Add jalapeno and the pack of noodles. Break the noodles before adding
  7. Add the cream cheese and let summer until the cheese is completely melted.
  8. Serve and enjoy. - - The soup taste extra good serves with bread
