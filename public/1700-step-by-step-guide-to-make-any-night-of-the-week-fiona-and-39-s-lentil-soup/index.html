The ingredients needed to make Fiona's lentil soup:
  1. Make ready 1 large brown onion
  2. Get 1 tbsp mustard seeds
  3. Take 1 tbsp cumin
  4. Get 1 tbsp turmeric
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp minced garlic
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  7. Make ready 250 grams split red lentils
  8. Get 1 large grated carrot
  9. Make ready 400 grams tinned chopped tomatoes
  10. Prepare 400 ml low fat coconut milk
  11. Make ready 500 ml chicken stock (more if needed)
  12. Take 1 tsp Sugar (to taste)

Instructions to make Fiona's lentil soup:
  1. Fry onion in olive oil with spices
  2. When onion is soft add grated carrot fry until soft
  3. Add lentils tomatoes chicken stock and coconut milk simmer for 30-45mins
  4. Check your consistency
  5. Blend/blitz until liking eg lumpy or smooth
  6. Eat with crusty bread xxxx
  7. Add a teaspoon of sugar to taste if needed
