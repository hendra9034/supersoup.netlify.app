The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Brownie with Salted Caramel Drizzle:
  1. Prepare 200 g dark chocolate, chopped
  2. Prepare 200 g salted butter
  3. Get 3 medium eggs
  4. Make ready 100 g caster sugar
  5. Make ready 100 g brown sugar
  6. Take 100 g plain flour
  7. Get 40 g cocoa powder
  8. Prepare 100 g milk chocolate chunks
  9. Make ready 100 g dark chocolate chunks

Instructions to make Chocolate Brownie with Salted Caramel Drizzle:
  1. Preheat oven to 160°C and line a 8x8 inch baking tray with greaseproof paper
  2. Melt butter and dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl in the microwave until melted. Leave to cool to room temperature.
  3. In a seperate bowl, whisk together the eggs & sugars for a few minutes until pale. Then pour the cooled chocolate into the eggs and fold together carefully
  4. Once combined, sift in the cocoa powder, plain flour and the chocolate chunks. Stir to combine then pour into the prepared tin. Top the brownies with nuts if you like. Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes
  5. Once cooled drizzle with salted caramel and Enjoy!…
