Steps to make 30 minute chicken tortilla Soup:
  1. Start by dicing and preparing all ingredients set aside
  2. Now heat stock pan add oil, onions, celery and carrot sweat down for about 3-4 minutes, add in diced garlic and garlic salt, now add in diced chicken cook down for about 10 minutes fill with water as desired add in zucchini bring to a boil.
  3. Now after 7 minutes or so add in pasta and all can beans and corn, taste for seasoning as desired.. cook till pasta is as desired. I do about 10 minutes
  4. Now garnish with your desired toppings ! Enjoy
