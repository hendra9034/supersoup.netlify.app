The ingredients needed to make Healthy detox chicken soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbs olive oil, divided
  2. Take 1-1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  3. Make ready to taste Kosher salt & black pepper
  4. Prepare 1 onion-diced
  5. Make ready 2-3 carrots peeled & diced
  6. Make ready 1/2 zucchini-diced
  7. Take 4 cloves garlic-minced
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  9. Get 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  10. Get 8 cup chicken stock
  11. Take 1-3 c water
  12. Get 2 bay leaves
  13. Prepare 1/2 box favorite small pasta
  14. Make ready 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  15. Prepare 1 bunch kale (removed from stems & cut)
  16. Take 1 (15 oz) canelli beans, drained
  17. Get 1 lemon

Steps to make Healthy detox chicken soup:
  1. Heat 1 tsp of olive oil in a stockpot or dutch oven over medium heat. Season chicken with s&p. Add chicken to stock pot and cook about 2-3 minutes per side, set aside. Add remaining 1 tbs of olive oil to stock pot. Add onion & carrots, cook stirring occasionally 3-4 minutes. Add garlic & zucchini, cook about 3minutes. Stir in thyme & oregano, cook 1 minute.
  2. Whisk in chicken stock & bay leaves. Bring to a boil. Stir in pasta, rosemary & chicken. Reduce heat & simmer 10-12 minutes. Stir in kale & beans until kale wilts..anout 3-4 minutes.
  3. Fetch out bayleaves & rosemary sprig (rosemary will naturally fall off).
  4. Squeeze lemon & mix in. Season with s&p. Mix.
