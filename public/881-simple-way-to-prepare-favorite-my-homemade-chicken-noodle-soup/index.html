The ingredients needed to make My Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 whole chicken
  2. Make ready 2 cups carrots
  3. Take 1 cup celery
  4. Prepare 1 potato (diced)
  5. Make ready 4 bullion cubes
  6. Get to taste Kosher salt
  7. Take to taste course black pepper
  8. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  9. Make ready 1 onion
Steps to make My Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Cut up extra veggies for soup after, straining the ones you cooked with broth.
  2. When chicken is done strain, into another pot then, let chicken cool down and shred.
  3. In a large pot add water and, everything else.
  4. Add the other veggies and, let simmer till they 're tender an serve.
