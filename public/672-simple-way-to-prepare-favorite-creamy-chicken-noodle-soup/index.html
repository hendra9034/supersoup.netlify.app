The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Get cooked chicken (can use rotissiere cicken)
  2. Make ready can condensed cheese soup
  3. Take chicken broth
  4. Make ready milk
  5. Prepare celery thinly sliced or chopped
  6. Take carrots thinly sliced or chopped
  7. Get packet of powdered ranch dressing mix
  8. Make ready package real bacon bits
  9. Make ready shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Prepare packaged uncooked egg noodles
Steps to make Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. In a large kettle combine chicken, cheese soup, chicken broth, milk, chopped celery, sliced carrots and bacon bits. bring to a boil, reduce heat and let simmer & cook for about 30 minutes or until vegtables are soft n tender.Stir in egg noodles and cook about 5 minutes or till noodles are tender. Serve with your favorite bread. and can top with more cheddar cheese.
