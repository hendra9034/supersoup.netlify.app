The ingredients needed to make Leftover Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Take 1 Rotisserie chicken (plain or garlic)
  2. Make ready 6 quarts Water
  3. Make ready 6 Chicken bullion cubes
  4. Take 5 sticks Celery chopped finely
  5. Get 1 medium Onion or 1 tbsp of dried onion
  6. Get 1 tbsp Minced garlic
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp Salt
  8. Take 3/4 tbsp Cumin
  9. Get 1/2 tsp Ginger
  10. Get to taste Salt and Pepper
Instructions to make Leftover Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. In an 8 quart pot place water and chicken and bring to a boil. Heat the chicken until the meat falls away from the bone
  2. Remove the pot from the heat and remove the bones from the broth
  3. Add the remaining ingredients to the broth and bring back to a boil for 10 min.
  4. Turn down to a low heat for at least a few more hours.
  5. Serve plain or over rice or noodles.
