The ingredients needed to make Honey-glazed char siu rice bowl:
  1. Take 500 g Pork belly or Pork shoulder
  2. Make ready 2 Spring onion
  3. Prepare 1 small chunk Ginger
  4. Make ready 4-5 cloves Garlic

  6. Get 2 Star anise

  9. Take Boiled water
  10. Make ready Seasoning
  11. Make ready 4 Tablespoons Char siu sauce
  12. Take 2 Tablespoons Fermented bean curd sauce
  13. Prepare 1 Tablespoon Soy sauce

Instructions to make Honey-glazed char siu rice bowl:
  1. Pan fried pork, spring onion, garlic and ginger until all meats turn into golden brown on both sides.
  2. Add in boiled water and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add in all seasoning, bay leaf and star anise. Stew for 20-25 minutes.
