The ingredients needed to make Delicious Tomato Soup:
  1. Get 13 tomatos
  2. Get 3 tbsp tomato puree
  3. Prepare 2 leeks
  4. Make ready 3 carrots
  5. Prepare 1 large union
  6. Get 1 tbsp butter
  7. Make ready 2 cubes instant broth/bouillon
  8. Get 1 pinch pepper
  9. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  10. Take 1 garlic clove

Steps to make Delicious Tomato Soup:
  1. Cut the carrots, union, leeks and garlic. Stove with the butter.
  2. Boil some water, cut a cross in the skin of the tomatoes and poach them for about half a minute, until you can see the skin coming loose. peel the tomatoes and cut them in pieces.
  3. Stir the vegetables and add some of the poaching water of the tomatoes to allow the vegetables to boil until they are soft.
  4. Add the two cubes of bouillon and the tomato puree to the vegetables.
  5. Add the tomatoes and let the entire thing simmer for twenty minutes on a low heath.
  6. taste and season!
  7. Puree everything together and enjoy
