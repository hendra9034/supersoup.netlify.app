The ingredients needed to make Mum’s Sweet and Sour Pork:
  1. Prepare 1 packet sweet and sour pork
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp plum sauce
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp ketchup
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp chilli
  5. Get 1 tbsp light sauce
  6. Take 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  7. Take 1 tbsp sesame oil
  8. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  9. Prepare 1/2 plain flour 1/2 corn flour
  10. Make ready 3-4 pcs butter biscuits
Instructions to make Mum’s Sweet and Sour Pork:
  1. Marinate pork with light sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar and butter biscuit. Refrigerate
  2. Mix plum sauce, ketchup and chilli sauce.
  3. Coat marinated pork in flour (mix in plastic bag). Put in freezer for 15-30 mins.
  4. Deep fry marinated pork with medium heat, increase fire and refry everything.
  5. Chop some onions, stir fry with a little bit of oil. Lower fire and heat sauce till boil, add fried pork. Off fire and mix well.
