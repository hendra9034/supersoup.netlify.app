The ingredients needed to make Chicken soup:
  1. Make ready 4 cup water
  2. Prepare 4 Chicken piece
  3. Get 1/2 cup mixed vegetables
  4. Take 1/2 onion, chopped
  5. Prepare 2 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Get 1 salt
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp vinegar
  8. Take 1 chili sauce
  9. Get 1/2 cup noodles

Steps to make Chicken soup:
  1. Add the first 6 ingredients in a container with closed lids n boil it for an hour
  2. Now remove the cooked chicken and remove the bones. Shred the chicken and put it back in the soup
  3. Add noodles and boil it for another 15 minutes
  4. Add vinegar and chili sauce.
