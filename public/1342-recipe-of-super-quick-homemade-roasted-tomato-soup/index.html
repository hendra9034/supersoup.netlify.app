  1. Prepare 6 tomatoes
  2. Get 3 tbsp olive oil

Steps to make Roasted tomato soup:
  1. Mix 1st 5 ingredients and roast for 20 minutes
  2. Sautee onions in oil of choice until soft and browning
  3. Add carrots garlic and celery and sautee
  4. Add all remaining ingredients to slow cooker. (plus all vegetables)
  5. Cook for 3-4 hours. (I do less if I'm busy and just as good.)
  6. Cool and blend.
