The ingredients needed to make Spicy Corned Beef Hash with Poached Egg:
  1. Take 6 slices West Country Corned beef
  2. Take 3-4 potatoes
  3. Make ready 4 cherry tomatoes thinly sliced
  4. Prepare 1 stick celery very finely sliced
  5. Take Pinch chilli flakes (depending on how hot you like it)
  6. Prepare Cooked peas
  7. Prepare 2 onions finely chopped
  8. Get 2-3 eggs
  9. Prepare Salt & Pepper for seasoning
  10. Get Oil
Instructions to make Spicy Corned Beef Hash with Poached Egg:
  1. Boil the potatoes whole and with the skins on for about 15 minutes or until soft to touch. - Leave to go cold then cut into chunks. - Heat the pan and with a bit of oil and sauté the potatoes until golden and crispy and tip onto kitchen paper.
  2. Wipe the pan, add a drop more oil and fry the onions gently until they take on a bit of colour, add the celery, peas, tomatoes and a pinch of chilli flakes. - - Add the potatoes and the corned beef and warm through. Check for seasoning and serve with a poached egg on top.
