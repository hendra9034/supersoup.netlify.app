The ingredients needed to make My 3 INGREDIENT CARAMEL POPCORN:
  1. Prepare 1 cup popcorn
  2. Take 1 cup margarine or butter
  3. Take 2 cups brown sugar

Instructions to make My 3 INGREDIENT CARAMEL POPCORN:
  1. Pop the popcorn and put aside in a bowl to cool
  2. Melt butter or margarine in a pot
  3. Add the sugar and begin to stir occasionally. - The butter will form a liquid and almost look like it's separating, keep mixing until it's combined
  4. Continue stirring the mixture until bubbles begin to form and there is a light brownish colour
  5. Once there seems to be a darker colour. - - Very quickly and carefully remove the caramel mixture and pour into the popcorn. - - Please do not keep a second longer on the heat or in the pot, otherwise the caramel will be bitter and you cannot enjoy that popcorn. So WORK FAST - - Note: the bowl of popcorn should be 3/4 full or less to allow space for mixing the caramel and popcorn together.
  6. Mix the caramel into the popcorn and make sure it's well combined and well coated - - Be careful as you work as the caramel is very hot and can burn you.
  7. Once mixed, put caramel in a tray and leave to air dry.
  8. Once dry, break into pieces and it's ready to serve ! - - Happy Cooking and Dining
