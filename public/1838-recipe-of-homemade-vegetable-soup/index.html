The ingredients needed to make vegetable soup:
  1. Prepare 1 curry powder
  2. Make ready 1 leek
  3. Take 3 tsp powdered stock
  4. Get 1 cup red lentils
  5. Get 1/2 pumpkin chopped
  6. Take 1 liter water
  7. Get 2 cup chopped fresh vegetables

Instructions to make vegetable soup:
  1. slice leek and fry in oil till soft
  2. add curry powder to taste
  3. add next 5 ingredients and simmer for half hour
  4. mash together
  5. add extra vegetables & more water as required
  6. boil till tender & season to taste. puree or leave chunky.
