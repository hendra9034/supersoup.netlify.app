The ingredients needed to make Curried Lentil Soup:
  1. Get 1 large Garlic Clove
  2. Take 1/3 inch. Peeled Fresh Ginger Stick
  3. Take 1/2 small Bulb Fennel Cored/Diced
  4. Take 1 small Carrot Peeled/Diced
  5. Get 1 small Parsnip Peeled/Diced
  6. Take 1 large Shallot Cut in half
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Curry Powder
  9. Prepare 1 cup Brown Lentils picked over and rinsed
  10. Prepare 1 quart Chicken or Veg. Broth
  11. Get 1 Kosher Salt/Pepper to taste

Steps to make Curried Lentil Soup:
  1. Pulse garlic/ginger in food processor until chopped. Add fennel/carrots/parsnips/shallot and pulse until coarsely chopped.
  2. Melt 2tbs. of the butter in a sauce pan over medium-high heat. Add the chopped veg. and cook stirring until softened, about 3min.
  3. Add the curry powder and cook, stirring, until the curry powder is fregrant, about 30sec.
