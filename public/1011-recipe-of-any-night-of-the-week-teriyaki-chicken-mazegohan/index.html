The ingredients needed to make Teriyaki Chicken Mazegohan:
  1. Take 2 cups (*180ml cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  2. Get 1 teaspoon Oil *Today I used Sesame Oil
  3. Get 300-400 g Chicken Thigh Fillets *cut into 2-3cm pieces
  4. Get Salt
  5. Make ready 1 small piece Ginger *OR 1 clove Garlic, grated
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon Mirin
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon Sugar
  9. Make ready Toasted Nori, Spring Onion OR Toasted Sesame Seeds for topping
  10. Prepare Shichimi (Japanese Chilli Spice Mix) OR Ground Chilli *optional
Instructions to make Teriyaki Chicken Mazegohan:
  1. Wash Rice and cook as normal.
  2. In a small bowl, combine grated Ginger (OR Garlic), Soy Sauce, Mirin and Sugar.
  3. When Rice is cooked, heat Oil in a frying pan over medium high heat, cook Chicken pieces and lightly season with Salt. Add the sauce and cook for a few minute or until the sauce thickens.
  4. Pour the Chicken and the sauce over the cooked rice and mix to combine evenly.
  5. Sprinkle with some Shichimi (Japanese Chilli Spice Mix) and your favourite topping. Today I added Toasted Nori.
