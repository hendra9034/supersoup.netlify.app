The ingredients needed to make Home Made Chicken Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 Whole Chicken
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Carrots
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup Celery
  5. Prepare 1 packages Noodles
  6. Take 1 tsp Salt
  7. Get 1 tsp Pepper
  8. Take 1 can Pettie diced tomatoes
Steps to make Home Made Chicken Soup:
  1. Place chicken in large pot with the onion cut in half (onion is for taste). You can dice a onion if your family likes onion.
  2. Fill pot with water, so it covers the top of the chicken. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Boil the chicken for 30 - 40 min.
  4. While chicken is boiling, cut up carrots and celery.
  5. After chicken is done, remove from pot and set aside to cool. Add carrots and celery to broth to cook. Add can of tomatoes.
  6. Once chicken is cool remove from bone. Add the chicken back to broth with the package of noodles.
  7. Cook soup until noodles are done. Then enjoy!
  8. You may have to add more salt and peper for your taste. May have to add some chicken broth, depending on your noodles and how much broth you like.
