The ingredients needed to make Veg french onion soup:
  1. Make ready 4 big onions
  2. Take 5 cups brown stock(veg)
  3. Take 2 tsp all-purpose flour
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp sugar
  5. Take 1 tbsp mustard sauce
  6. Take 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp vinegar
  8. Take To taste Salt

Steps to make Veg french onion soup:
  1. Boil 7 cups water in pan add celery, french beans, tomatoes & carrots. Keep boiling till water reduce to 5 cups. Then strain, brown stock is ready.
  2. Peel, wash & slice onion evenly. Heat oil & butter in deep pan add sliced onions & sugar saute on high flame for 5 minutes, stir constantly.
  3. Now cook onions on slow flame for 30 minutes, stir occasionally. Now add all-purpose flour mix well, saute for 5 minutes.
  4. Now add pepper powder, mustard sauce & salt, mix. Add brown stock and cook on high flame for 10 minutes. Add cheese & boil till cheese melts. Serve with cheese toast.
