The ingredients needed to make Tuna Flakes Spirals (Spiral Pasta):
  1. Get 500 g Spirals Pasta
  2. Prepare 1/3 C Olive Oil
  3. Make ready 1 big brown onion - finely chopped
  4. Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
  5. Make ready 5 (more) bird eyed chillies - halved & crushed
  6. Prepare 1 C frozen mix veggies (corn, carrot & peas)
  7. Get 4 cans small tuna flakes - plain or in olive oil
  8. Take 2 tsp - Chicken seasoning powder or chicken or chicken bouillon
  9. Make ready 1/4-1/2 tsp White Pepper Powder/ground pepper
  10. Take As needed - Mixed Herbs (see pic)

Instructions to make Tuna Flakes Spirals (Spiral Pasta):
  1. Prepare all ingredients. Cook pasta according to packet instructions. Reserve 1/4-1/3 c pasta liquid.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan. Saut√© garlic, onion and chillies for a minute or until onion is translucent. Next, add mixed-veggies. Stir to mix everything well. Add tuna flakes and seasonings (chicken seasoning powder & pepper powder). Stir well for another minute.
  3. Next, Stir in pasta. Add the reserved liquid if your pasta is too dry. Mix well. Add pinches of mixed herbs & adjust seasonings accordingly. Serve warm.
