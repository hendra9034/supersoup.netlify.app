The ingredients needed to make Chicken vegetable soup with gnocchi:
  1. Make ready 2 chicken breast
  2. Prepare 3 chicken bouillon cubes
  3. Make ready 3 carrots (chopped)
  4. Make ready 2 celery sticks (chopped)
  5. Make ready 1 zucchini (chopped)
  6. Prepare 1 yellow squash (chopped)
  7. Get 1 small yellow onion (chopped)
  8. Prepare 1 can spinach
  9. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  10. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon rosemary
  11. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  12. Make ready 1 teaspoon black pepper
  13. Make ready 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 ounces)
  14. Get 1 pound gnocchi

Instructions to make Chicken vegetable soup with gnocchi:
  1. Add eight cups of water to a pot and bring to boil with chicken bouillon. Place chicken in boiling water and boil for 10 minutes.
  2. Chop carrots, celery, zucchini, yellow squash, and onion while chicken is boiling. Remove chicken from pot and set aside.
  3. Add carrots, celery, zucchini, yellow squash, and onion to boiling broth. Lower temperature, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Cut chicken while vegetables are simmering. Add chicken, spinach, oregano, rosemary, salt, pepper, and tomatoes to pot. Replace lid and simmer another 10 minutes.
  5. Add gnocchi to pot and simmer additional 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool for 5 minutes. Enjoy.
