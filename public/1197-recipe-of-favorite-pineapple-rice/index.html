The ingredients needed to make Pineapple Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 tablespoon ghee
  2. Get 2 tablespoon oil
  3. Make ready 1 cup fresh pineapple cubed
  4. Prepare 1 cup mixed capsicum finely chopped
  5. Take 1 finely chopped onion
  6. Get 2 cloves garlic finely chopped
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon chilli paste
  9. Take 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup cashews
  11. Take 1/4 cup raisins
  12. Take 2 cups cooked basmati rice
  13. Get 1/2 lime juice
  14. Take Handful if coriander leaves
  15. Get Salt to taste
  16. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  17. Prepare 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  18. Get 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  19. Get 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  20. Make ready leaves Few curry
Steps to make Pineapple Rice:
  1. Heat ghee in a wide bottom pan and fry the cashews until lightly golden. Remove from ghee and set aside.
  2. In the same pan add oil, cumin seeds, mustard seeds and curry leaves. Once it starts fluttering add ginger, garlic, green chilli paste and onions. Sauté until the onion is translucent. Add in pineapple and capsicum and cook until pineapple is caramelised and liquid evaporated. It should take about 3 minutes. Add in cashews, raisins,salt, turmeric, chilli powder and garam masala.
  3. Add the rice to the pan and stir to combine. Cook until the rice is hot, stirring occasionally. Add half chopped coriander leaves and mix well. - Squeeze the juice of lime and mix. - Transfer in a serving dish and garnish with coriander leaves.
