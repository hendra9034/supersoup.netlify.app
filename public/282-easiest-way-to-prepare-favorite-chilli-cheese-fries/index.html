The ingredients needed to make Chilli cheese fries:
  1. Prepare 500 g lean beef mince
  2. Take 400 g chopped tomatoes
  3. Prepare 400 g kidney beans
  4. Get 1 large white onion
  5. Make ready 1 red pepper
  6. Prepare 2 tsp minced garlic
  7. Prepare 4 tbsp tomato purée
  8. Prepare 150 ml beef stock
  9. Prepare 1 tsp oregano
  10. Prepare 2 tsp ground coriander
  11. Take 2 bay leaves
  12. Get 2 tsp cumin
  13. Get 2 tsp chilli powder
  14. Prepare 2 tsp smoked parpika
  15. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  16. Take 1 tsp pepper
  17. Prepare 900 g potato
  18. Take 120 g cheese
  19. Make ready Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions to make Chilli cheese fries:
  1. Fry your mince until brown and put in the slow cooker
  2. Throw in every ingredient apart from potato and cheese and slow cook on low for 7 hours
  3. An hour before the chilli is ready, cut your potato into fries and par boil in salted water for 4 minutes
  4. Steam fry your fries and get them in the oven at 200c for 45 minutes turning regularly
  5. Serve your fries with your slow cooked chilli and top with cheese, and freshly diced coriander for garnish
