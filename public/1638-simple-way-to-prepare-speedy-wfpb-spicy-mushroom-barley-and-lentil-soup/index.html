The ingredients needed to make WFPB Spicy Mushroom Barley and Lentil Soup:
  1. Prepare Med. Onion chopped
  2. Take 2 celery stalks sliced
  3. Make ready 2 carrots sliced
  4. Take 2 jalapenos sliced seeds left intact
  5. Prepare 16 oz sliced mushrooms
  6. Make ready 2 cloves garlic minced
  7. Prepare 4 cups kale
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp rosemary
  9. Take 1/2 tsp oregano
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp basil
  11. Prepare 2 bay leaves
  12. Get 1 tsp ground turmeric
  13. Take 1 tsp crushed chipotle chili (or red pepper)
  14. Get 1/2 cup pearl barley
  15. Take 1/2 of brown lentils
  16. Prepare 6 cups low sodium vegetable broth
