The ingredients needed to make Spicy Chicken in Coconut milk sauce:
  1. Take 2 tsp minced garlic
  2. Take 2 Tbsp minced juicy ginger
  3. Get 3 Tbsp curry powder (hot or medium hot according to preference)
  4. Make ready 2 tsp coriander powder
  5. Get 250 ml (whole can) full fat coconut milk
  6. Take 1/4 cup desiccated coconut
  7. Get 4 large chicken fillets cut into bite size pieces
  8. Prepare Juice of one medium lemon
  9. Take 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  10. Prepare 1 medium onion (preferably white)
  11. Prepare Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions to make Spicy Chicken in Coconut milk sauce:
  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil on medium in a shallow pan and sauté the onions until golden but not burnt
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir. And as it gets fragrant, add all the spices and desiccated coconut and keep stirring until everything sticks together
  3. Add another 2 tablespoons of oil and raise the heat to medium-high. Immediately add the chicken, season with salt and pepper and cook stirring until the chicken starts to brown (4 to 5minutes). Ensure it does not cook all the way through as it will become tough
  4. Pour in the lemon juice, milk, and sugar. Stir and cook until sauce is a little thickened
  5. Enjoy with rice or naan
