The ingredients needed to make Thai Green Curry w/ Duck:
  1. Prepare 1 duck breast
  2. Get 140 g jasmine rice
  3. Prepare 200 ml coconut milk
  4. Make ready 2 spring onions
  5. Make ready 5 Birdseye red chilli
  6. Take 1 Pureety Thai Green Curry seasoning
  7. Get 75 g bamboo shoots
  8. Make ready leaves Basil

Instructions to make Thai Green Curry w/ Duck:
  1. Fry your duck skin side down for 5 minutes and finish in the oven for 12 minutes at 180c
  2. Get coconut milk in a wok and add the Thai green curry seasoning and add bamboo shoots
  3. Simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Boil your jasmine rice in a pan for 12 minutes
  5. Serve everything with some basil, red chilli and spring onion as garnish
