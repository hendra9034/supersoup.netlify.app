The ingredients needed to make Get Well Chicken Noodle soup:
  1. Get egg noodles
  2. Take boneless skinless chicken breast
  3. Prepare Celery
  4. Make ready Carrots
  5. Prepare Onion
  6. Prepare Parsley
  7. Take Cilantro
  8. Prepare Garlic
  9. Get Salt and pepper
  10. Get Worcestershire sauce
  11. Prepare Accent seasoning
  12. Take Ms.Dash seasoning
  13. Prepare Chicken broth
  14. Take Chicken stock
Instructions to make Get Well Chicken Noodle soup:
  1. Simply….grill chicken breast.
  2. Put both broth & stock in pot, with all seasonings, sauce & noodles
  3. Same time….chop & dice all veggies, & chicken breast (once completely cooked).
  4. Add everything together & let simmer for 1/2 hour or more….the longer….the more flavor it seems.
  5. Serve to the sick….or just enjoy on a cold night…..(did somebody say, Netflix & Chill?)
