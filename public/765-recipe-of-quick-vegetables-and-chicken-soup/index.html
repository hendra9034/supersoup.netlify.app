The ingredients needed to make Vegetables and chicken soup:
  1. Prepare 1 red onion
  2. Make ready 2 small potatoes
  3. Make ready 6-7 small zucchini
  4. Take 1/4 k frozen broccoli
  5. Take 2 small carrots
  6. Prepare 3 thin fillets chicken
  7. Get 1-2 tsp minced garlic
  8. Prepare Salt, pepper, spices -
  9. Make ready 1.2 L hot water

Instructions to make Vegetables and chicken soup:
  1. Without adding any oils, butter. Stir on medium heat, chopped chicken, chopped onion, garlic with black pepper, cumin, salt, cinnamon, bay leaves, 1/8 tsp chili powder (tiny pinch)
  2. After a few minutes of sautéing add chopped potatoes and carrots, let cook covered for about 8-10 minutes on low heat. Stirring.
  3. Add hot water and broccoli and zucchini, let boil on low heat for another 10-15 minutes or until the water and vegetables have combined into a colourful broth.
