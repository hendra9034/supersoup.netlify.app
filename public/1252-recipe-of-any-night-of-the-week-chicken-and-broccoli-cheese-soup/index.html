Instructions to make chicken and broccoli cheese soup:
  1. Boil 1lb of chicken. Strain the water then use two forks to separate chicken to desired size. Using a large pot, place the chicken into 9 cups of water.
  2. Bring 9 cups of water and chicken to a boil stir in entire contents of Mrs.Grass soup cartons.
  3. Add 1lb of fresh or frozen broccoli to your soup.
  4. Add 1/2 - 1 box of Velveeta cheese depending on your preferences (cut into portions for a quicker melt). Stir frequently until cheese is evenly melted. Let cool then serve.
