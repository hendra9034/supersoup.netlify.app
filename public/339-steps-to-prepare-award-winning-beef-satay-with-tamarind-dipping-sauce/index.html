Steps to make Beef satay with tamarind dipping sauce:
  1. To marinate the beef, mix in the oil and all the other ingredients until all ingredients are well combined
  2. Cut the beef into 1 to 1 1/2 inch cubes (or however you would prefer your beef satay to be)
  3. In a bowl, combine the marinade and beef until all beef pieces are thoroughly coated with the marinade. Leave aside for at least 30 minutes for the flavours to combine
  4. Skewer the beef satays and keep aside to grill
  5. To make the tamarind dipping sauce, in a sauce pan, add the tamarind pulp, water and the rest of the ingredients while stir over medium heat.
  6. Continue stirring the tamarind sauce until it reaches the consistency you prefer (use less or more water). I prefer to heat the ingredients together for it to combine well.
  7. In a hot grill pan, heat the cooking oil and begin grilling the beef satays until cooked to your liking.
  8. Serve warm with the tamarind dipping sauce and a sprinkle of fresh chopped cilantro.
