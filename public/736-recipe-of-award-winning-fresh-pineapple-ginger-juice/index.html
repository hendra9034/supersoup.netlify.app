The ingredients needed to make Fresh Pineapple Ginger Juice:
  1. Take 1 small pineapple cut into cubes
  2. Prepare 1 Tbsp ground ginger root
  3. Make ready 1 cup water
  4. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Get Juice of half a lemon
Instructions to make Fresh Pineapple Ginger Juice:
  1. Mix ginger, pineapple, water, sugar. and boil with frequent stirring until fruit is soft. Let stand to cool
  2. Sieve through a strainer, pushing through all the juice
  3. Add lemon and stir the juice to fully incorporate
  4. Refrigerate or serve with ice and extra pineapple pieces. Garnish with mint and enjoy!
