The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Chicken Rice:
  1. Make ready 1 cup rice (~ 120g)
  2. Prepare 250 g chicken breast, cubed
  3. Prepare 5 fresh shiitake mushroom, cubed (if using dried mushroom, soaked till soften)
  4. Prepare 1tbsp dried shrimp, chopped small bits
  5. Get 5 shallots, thinly sliced
  6. Make ready Water (to boil rice)
  7. Get Cooking oil
  8. Make ready Marinade
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp pepper
  10. Get 1 tsp salt
  11. Prepare 2 Tbsp light soy sauce
  12. Get 1 Tbsp dark soy sauce
  13. Make ready 1/2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  14. Prepare 1 Tbsp Chinese cooking wine
  15. Prepare Optional Add-on
  16. Make ready 1 portion (~3cmx5cm) salted fish, fried and cut into small cube
  17. Make ready 2 Chinese sausage, sliced
Steps to make Mushroom Chicken Rice:
  1. Marinade chicken for about 30 to 60 minutes
  2. Add cooking oil in pan. Fry shallots till golden brown. Add in dried shrimp and sausage (optional) when shallot starts browning.
  3. Add in mushroom. Stirfry for a bit for the mushroom flavour to release.
  4. Add in chicken without the marination sauce. Reserve the marination sauce for the next step. Stir fry till chicken is partially cooked.
  5. Add in rice and fry the rice for a bit. Arrange the rice evenly on the pan
  6. Add some water into the reserved sauce to dilute. Pour them into the pan. Top up with more water until rice is fully covered.
  7. Once the water starts boiling, lower the heat and boil for about 30minutes until rice is cooked
  8. Rice can be served with a vegetable dish and dinner is complete
  9. Rice can be garnished with spring onion
