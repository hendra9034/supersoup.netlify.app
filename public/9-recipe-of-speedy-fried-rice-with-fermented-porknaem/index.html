The ingredients needed to make Fried rice with fermented pork(Naem):
  1. Prepare 1 cup Steamed rice
  2. Get 1/2 cup Fermented pork
  3. Take 1/2 Onion, finely chopped
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Vegetable oil
  6. Take 1 tbsp Oyster sauce for seasoning
  7. Prepare 1-2 Spring onion, finely chopped

Instructions to make Fried rice with fermented pork(Naem):
  1. Prepare all the ingredients. Add the oil to the nonstick saucepan(or Wok). Use high heat during cooking.
  2. Sauté the onion for 2-3 min or until it tender and translucent.
  3. Add garlic and fermented pork to the pan. Stir fry for 2-3 min.
  4. Add egg and scramble it with all the ingredients. Keep the egg mildly undercooked.
  5. Add steamed rice. Keep stirring or tossing the pan until all the ingredients mix well and become dry.
  6. Season with oyster sauce.
  7. Before you turn off the gas, sprinkle the spring onion.
