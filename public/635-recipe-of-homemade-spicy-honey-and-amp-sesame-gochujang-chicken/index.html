The ingredients needed to make Spicy Honey & Sesame Gochujang Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breasts, butterflied in half and scoured with lines,
  2. Prepare 1 white onion, chopped
  3. Make ready For the marinade:
  4. Take 4 tbsp light soy sauce,
  5. Take 2 heaped tsp garlic puree paste,
  6. Take 2 tbsp shaoxing wine,
  7. Prepare 1 tsp sesame oil,
  8. Take 1 pinch white pepper,
  9. Take For the honey sesame gochujang sauce:
  10. Take 2 heaped tbsp gochujang paste,
  11. Get 2 tbsp runny honey,
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp light soy sauce,
  13. Make ready 2 inches fresh ginger, chopped,
  14. Make ready 3 tsp sesame seeds, extra for garnish,
  15. Make ready 1 tsp sesame oil,
  16. Make ready Cooking oil for frying
  17. Prepare To garnish:
  18. Take Red or green fresh sliced chilli,
  19. Prepare Spring onion,
  20. Make ready Sesame seeds
Instructions to make Spicy Honey & Sesame Gochujang Chicken:
  1. Mix together the marinade ingredients in a bowl and place the butterflied chicken into it. Leave in the fridge for at least 30 mins.
  2. Mix together the honey sesame gochujang sauce ingredients and set aside. Heat up around 2tbsp oil in a wok and add in the white onions. Cook over a medium heat until just becoming translucent.
  3. Take the marinaded chicken and place into the wok. Fry over a medium heat, turning occasionally until browned and cooked all the way through.
  4. Take the honey sesame gochujang sauce and pour it into the wok. Cook it until it bubbles, thickens and reduces down a little and the chicken is coated in the sauce. Remove from the heat.
  5. Serve up and sprinkle with sesame seeds then garnish with fresh chilli and spring onion. See picture for serving suggestion. Enjoy! :)
