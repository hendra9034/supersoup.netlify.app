The ingredients needed to make Potato Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 lg carrot diced
  2. Make ready 2 lbs potatoes peeled and diced
  3. Take 1 (500 g) media crema
  4. Make ready 4 cups milk
  5. Get 1/2 c bacon
  6. Make ready 1 lg onion diced
  7. Prepare 2 cloves garlic
  8. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  9. Take 2 tbsp flour

Steps to make Potato Soup:
  1. Saute bacon, diced onions, garlic in large stock pot until bacon is cooked and onions are clear.
  2. Add diced potatoes and carrots and enough water to cover. Bring to boil and cook until potatoes are almost done but not mushy.
  3. Mix flour with milk. Add milk and media crema to boiling potatoes. Add salt and pepper to taste. Simmer until desired consistency.
