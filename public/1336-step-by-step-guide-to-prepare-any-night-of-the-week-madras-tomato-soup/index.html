The ingredients needed to make Madras tomato soup:
  1. Get 6 medium sized tomatoes
  2. Make ready 1 cup shredded fresh coconut
  3. Make ready 1 cup diced carrot
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup onion
  5. Get 1 tsp butter
  6. Make ready 2 tsp sugar
  7. Take black pepper and salt per taste

Instructions to make Madras tomato soup:
  1. Saute onion and carrot in butter until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes, coconut and two cups of water. Add sugar, salt and pepper.
  3. Pressure cook for 10 minutes or one whistle.
  4. Cool this. Blend with a hand blender or mixer. Strain and serve hot with bread croutons.
