The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooked Lamb Madras w/ Rice:
  1. Prepare 400 g diced lamb
  2. Take 1/2 tbsp mustard seeds
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  4. Get 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  6. Prepare 1 diced red chilli
  7. Take 2 tsp minced garlic
  8. Take 2 tsp minced ginger
  9. Make ready 400 g chopped tomatoes
  10. Get 250 ml chicken stock
  11. Take 3 bay leaves
  12. Make ready 1 medium white onion, chopped
  13. Make ready 500 g boiled rice
  14. Make ready Chilli flakes for garnish
  15. Get Coriander for garnish
Steps to make Slow Cooked Lamb Madras w/ Rice:
  1. Throw everything in your slow cooker apart from rice, chilli flakes and coriander and cook on low heat for 7 hours
  2. Boil rice
  3. Serve everything with garnishes
