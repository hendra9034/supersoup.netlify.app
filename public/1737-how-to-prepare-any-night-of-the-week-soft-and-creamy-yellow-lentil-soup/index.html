The ingredients needed to make Soft and Creamy Yellow Lentil Soup:
  1. Take 2 cups yellow lentils
  2. Prepare 1 onion, finely chopped
  3. Take 2 tablespoons rice
  4. Take 1/4 teaspoon curry
  5. Take salt to taste
  6. Prepare vegetable oil
Instructions to make Soft and Creamy Yellow Lentil Soup:
  1. Put the lentils in a pot and cover with 5 cups of water. Bring to a boil.
  2. After 8 minutes of boiling, add in the rice, curry and salt, and leave to cook for another 8 minutes.
  3. In the mean time, fry the onion in vegetable oil. When the lentils are cooked, add in the fried onion and leave to boil for 5 minutes.
