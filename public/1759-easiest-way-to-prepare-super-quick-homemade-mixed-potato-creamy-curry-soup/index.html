The ingredients needed to make Mixed Potato Creamy Curry Soup:
  1. Get 2 tsp curry powder
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  3. Take 1 bay leaf
  4. Make ready 2 large potatoes
  5. Prepare 1 cup lentils
  6. Get 1 cup rice
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp coconut oil
  8. Prepare 150 grams portuguese sausage
  9. Get 1 cup pre soaked lima beans
  10. Prepare 2 tsp salt
Instructions to make Mixed Potato Creamy Curry Soup:
  1. Add everything in the pot.
  2. Fry ingredients, specially onions, for 5 minutes.
  3. Add enough water to cover.
  4. Pressure cook for 20 minutes.
