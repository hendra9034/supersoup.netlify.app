The ingredients needed to make Simple Slow Cooker Chicken and Vegetable Soup:
  1. Prepare chicken breast
  2. Take 12-16oz bag of frozen mixed vegetables
  3. Make ready low sodium chicken broth
  4. Take cold water
  5. Take oregano
  6. Get basil
  7. Make ready garlic powder
  8. Take thyme
  9. Take bay leaves
  10. Take Ground black pepper for taste
Steps to make Simple Slow Cooker Chicken and Vegetable Soup:
  1. Mix the spices together. Set to the side.
  2. Dice up the chicken into small pieces. Put into the slow cooker.
  3. Add the vegetables to the slow cooker.
  4. Pour the water and stock over the chicken and vegetables.
  5. Finally, add the spices and stir together. Then place the bay leaves on top of the soup. Put the lid on top, turn the temperature to low and set the timer to 8 hours.
  6. Enjoy!
