The ingredients needed to make My Outdoor Easter Nibbles is Feta cheese & Asparagus mix:
  1. Make ready Some baby corn cooked
  2. Take 1 Bunch Asparagus Grill the Asparagus in Garlic butter
  3. Take 1 knob Butter
  4. Take 1/4 Tsp Garlic Granuals
  5. Take Some Spinach
  6. Make ready Some iceberg lettuce
  7. Prepare 1 Off the vine Tomato
  8. Make ready 1/2 Avocado sliced
  9. Prepare 8 Stuffed Olives
  10. Make ready 2 in x 1inch piece of Feta cheese cubed
  11. Take 1/2 Red sweet Pepper sliced thin
  12. Prepare Some Cucumber sliced thin
  13. Make ready 1 tbls red sliced onion
  14. Make ready 1 tbls white sliced onion
Instructions to make My Outdoor Easter Nibbles is Feta cheese & Asparagus mix:
  1. Mix the garlic in the butter on a tray and grill turn them over after 2 minutes - ( I mixed the garlic butter then spread it on foil and then rolled the asparagus in it, then put under the grill.
  2. Boil the baby corn
  3. Slice all the veg long lengths
  4. On a long serving dish add the Spinach at the end then add the Asparagus in the middle.
  5. Next add all the veg lengthways following the spinach. Add them all then add the stuffed olives here and there.
  6. Add a little squirt of lemon juice and the balsamic vinegar very sparingly as it is strong.
  7. Serve as a help yourself Salad out in the sunshine.
