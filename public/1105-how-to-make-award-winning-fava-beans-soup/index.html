The ingredients needed to make Fava beans soup:
  1. Get 3 tbsp oil
  2. Prepare 2 carrots; chopped in cubes
  3. Prepare 2 celery sticks; chopped
  4. Make ready 3 garlic cloves; thinly chopped
  5. Get 1/2 onion; chopped
  6. Make ready 1 tsp cumin
  7. Make ready 1 bunch parsley; chopped
  8. Make ready 300 gr dry fava beans
  9. Prepare 5 cups hot water
  10. Get salt
  11. Get Optional: 1 dry pasilla pepper
  12. Take Optional: olive oil for drizzling

Steps to make Fava beans soup:
  1. Rinse the fava beans a few times and then keep them soaking in water for at least two hours.
  2. Heat the oil in a pot and cook the onions until soft and transparent. Add the chopped carrots and celery and cook for 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Add the rinsed fava beans, mix them and cook for 3 more minutes. If using pasilla pepper, ad half of it chopped.
  3. Add the hot water, mix, bring to a boil and then lower to simmer for about 1 hour or until the consistency is as desired. If needed, add more hot water.
  4. Add the salt to season (do not add salt until the fava beans are fully cooked), turn the heat off and add almost all the parsley and mix. Serve and add a little of extra parsley on top and optionally, a drizzle of olive oil and pasilla pepper slices.
