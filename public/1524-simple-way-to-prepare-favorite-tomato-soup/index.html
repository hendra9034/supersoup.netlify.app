The ingredients needed to make Tomato soup:
  1. Prepare 4 piece Medium sized tomato
  2. Take 1 tsp Black salt
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  4. Take 1/4 tsp Green chilli chopped
  5. Take 1/2 inch chopped ginger
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic chopped
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon Carrot chopped
  8. Prepare 1 potato chopped for thickness (optional)
  9. Get 1 tablespoon Butter
  10. Take 1 Bay leaf
  11. Get 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  12. Prepare 1 tablespoon Onion chopped
