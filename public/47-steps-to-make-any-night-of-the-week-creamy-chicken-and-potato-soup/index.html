The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken and Potato Soup:
  1. Take 4 cups boneless skinless chicken breast cooked and cut up
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  3. Prepare 1 Onion
  4. Take 1-2 cups chopped carrots
  5. Get 1/2 Cup flour
  6. Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
  7. Prepare 6 cups chicken broth
  8. Take 4 cups milk
  9. Get 5-6 potatoes peeled and cut into bit size peices
  10. Get 3 cups cheese
  11. Take 2 tsp thyme
  12. Prepare to taste Salt & Pepper
Instructions to make Creamy Chicken and Potato Soup:
  1. Melt butter in a large pot and at veggies and garlic. Saute till fragrant.
  2. Add flour to create a roux. Slowly stir in the chicken broth and milk till combimed.
  3. Then stir in the thyme, salt/pepper and potatoes let simmer for about 10 min.
  4. Once potatoes are almost ready add your chicken and cheese then let simmer till potatoes are soft then serve and enjoy üòäüëç
