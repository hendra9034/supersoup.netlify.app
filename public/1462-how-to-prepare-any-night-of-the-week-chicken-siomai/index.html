The ingredients needed to make Chicken Siomai:
  1. Make ready Siomai wrappers
  2. Make ready 250 g ground chicken
  3. Prepare 1 egg
  4. Take 4 tbsp tapioca flour
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp corn starch
  6. Prepare 1 chopped carrot, medium size
  7. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1 tsp pepper
  9. Prepare 3 garlic cloves
Steps to make Chicken Siomai:
  1. Siomai fillings: Mixed all ingredients except the siomai wrappers.
  2. Fill the wrappers with the filling.
  3. Steam the siomai for about 20-30 minutes.
