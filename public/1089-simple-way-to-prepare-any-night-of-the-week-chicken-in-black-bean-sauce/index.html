Instructions to make Chicken in black bean sauce:
  1. Marinade chicken in 1 tsp cornflour, half of the light and dark soy sauce, and white pepper for 20 minutes
  2. Cook your chicken in a pan for 10 minutes turning regularly and set aside
  3. Get your rice boiled in a pan of boiling water for 10 minutes
  4. Make your sauce by whisking water, oyster sauce, the remaining light and dark soy sauce, salt & pepper and the rest of the cornflour
  5. Heat 1 tsp of sesame oil in a wok and fry your black beans, crush them a little, and add your black bean sauce, carrot, onion and around onion and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Add your chicken back to the sauce and cook until sauce thickens
  7. Serve with your rice and sliced red chilli for garnish
