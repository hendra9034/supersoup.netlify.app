The ingredients needed to make Tomato soup with cashew:
  1. Prepare Olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Take 2 pieces garlic
  4. Prepare Bay leaves, new spice, oregano, nutmeg
  5. Take 500 ml vegetable broth
  6. Take 4 boiled peeled tomatoes (or can)
  7. Prepare 1 cup soaked cashew nuts
  8. Get Basil
  9. Get Salt, pepper
