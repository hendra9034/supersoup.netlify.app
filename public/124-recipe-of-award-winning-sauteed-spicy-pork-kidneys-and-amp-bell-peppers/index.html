The ingredients needed to make Sauteed Spicy Pork Kidneys & Bell Peppers:
  1. Prepare Green, red, yellow bell peppers 1/2 piece each colour
  2. Get 2 pieces Pork kidneys
  3. Get Ginger,
  4. Get 4 piece garlic chop finely
  5. Prepare 1 tbs sesame chili oil
  6. Get 3 tbs cooking wine,
  7. Get 1/2 tbs oysters sauce
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  9. Take 1 tsp cornstarch mix with 1tbs of water

Instructions to make Sauteed Spicy Pork Kidneys & Bell Peppers:
  1. Slice the Bell peppers & ginger
  2. Cut the Pork kidneys into half the cut way the inside (red thing) clean well.
  3. Cut pork kidneys after cut soak in cold water for 10 minutes then drain well
  4. Sauteed the ginger until fragrant add in the pork kidneys then add the cooking wine & oysters sauce sauteed 2 minutes take out put a side
  5. Sauteed the garlic until fragrant add bell peppers add salt & 4tbs water cover for 3 minutes.
  6. Boil the water after boiling add Pork kidneys cook for 30second then take out soaked in cold water change the waters when the water turn into red colour
  7. Then add in the Pork kidneys, sesame chili oil & cornstarch sauteed around 10 second take out put in the plate.
  8. Ready to serve😋😋 - - Notes:if don't like spicy skip the sesame chili oil.
