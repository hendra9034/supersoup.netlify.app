Steps to make Sweet Corn Soup

#mycookbook:

  1. Add 1/2 cup of corn kernels with 1/2 cup of water in a blender and blend into a fine paste
  2. Heat 1 & 1/2 cup water in a saucepan
  3. Add in all the vegies and Mix well.
  4. Add in the corn paste, 1 tablespoon sugar, salt as per taste and pepper.
  5. Mix well and cook for 6-8 minutes.
  6. Take out the froth/ foam and discard it.
  7. Add few drops of water to 1 tablespoon of corn flour and make a slurry.
  8. Add it gradually to the soup, and watch it thicken.
  9. Turn off the flame and add in a tablespoon of butter and mix well.
  10. Serve hot.
