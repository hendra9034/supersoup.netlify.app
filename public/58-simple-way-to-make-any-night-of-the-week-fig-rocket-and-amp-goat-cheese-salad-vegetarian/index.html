The ingredients needed to make Fig, rocket & goat cheese salad #vegetarian:
  1. Prepare 200 g rocket / ruccola
  2. Prepare 100 g - 150g soft and smooth goat cheese
  3. Take 4-6 fresh figs, sliced into wedges
  4. Get 1 handful chopped or flaked almonds
  5. Make ready For Dressing:
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup virgin olive oil
  7. Get 1-2 tbs balsamic vinegar
  8. Make ready 2 tsp soy sauce
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp chilli powder (optional)

Instructions to make Fig, rocket & goat cheese salad #vegetarian:
  1. Wash the rocket and dry the leaves. Cut the figs into wedges about 6-8 per fig, depending on the size
  2. Mix the rocket, fig and almonds in a bowl.
  3. In a separate cup, mix the ingredients for the dressing.
  4. Add the dressing to the bowl and mix well. Crumble the goat cheese into the bowl and give it a light stir. As vinegar wilts the rocket a bit, best is to add dressing right before serving.
