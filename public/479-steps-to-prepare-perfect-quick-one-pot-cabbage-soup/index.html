The ingredients needed to make Quick One Pot Cabbage Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 Cabbage
  2. Take 10 stalks of celery ~ chopped
  3. Prepare 1 large onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 2 cans of sliced carrots
  5. Make ready 2 cans of tomato sauce
  6. Make ready 2 cans of beef broth
  7. Take 2 cans diced tomatoes with green chili's
  8. Get 1/2 bag of frozen green beans
  9. Make ready 2 large Bell pepper chopped
  10. Take 2 cup water
  11. Prepare 1 garlic powder
  12. Prepare 1 ground black pepper
  13. Get 1 salt
  14. Make ready 1 large stock pot
  15. Make ready optional
  16. Take 1 lb ground turkey
  17. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp cooking oil
Instructions to make Quick One Pot Cabbage Soup:
  1. In a large stock pot add your oil and turkey to brown season to taste
  2. After your meat has browned and left a little flavor at the bottom remove from the pot into a separate clean dish
  3. Add onions first then cabbage and the rest of your ingredients with juices
  4. Cover and bring to a boil and leave for about 10 mins
  5. Add water if needed
  6. Stir reduce heat to medium cook until vegetables are tender add meat and eat
