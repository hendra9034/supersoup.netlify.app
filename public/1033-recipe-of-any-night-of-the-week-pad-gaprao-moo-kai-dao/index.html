The ingredients needed to make Pad Gaprao Moo Kai Dao:
  1. Get 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  2. Take 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  3. Get 2 Teaspoons fish sauce
  4. Get 1.5 teaspoons sugar
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoons water
  6. Make ready 250 grams mince pork
  7. Get 1/2 small brown onion
  8. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  9. Prepare 2 small red chilli
  10. Prepare 1 red chilli
  11. Get 100 grams green beans
  12. Prepare 1 handful thai basil leaves
  13. Make ready 1 egg
  14. Take 1 bowl steam rice

Steps to make Pad Gaprao Moo Kai Dao:
  1. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, sugar and water for the sauce.
  2. Stirfry sliced onion, chopped garlic first, add the pork mince, add the mix sauce then the green beans and red chilli. Add the basil last when it's almost ready.
  3. Fried the egg with generous amount of oil then wait until you see it golden on the edge. Splash some oil on the top layer of the egg to seal the top part of the egg without overcooking it before you take the egg out.
