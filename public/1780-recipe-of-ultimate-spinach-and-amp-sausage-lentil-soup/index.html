The ingredients needed to make Spinach & Sausage Lentil Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 pound smoked turkey sausage
  2. Get 1 cup dried brown lentils, rinsed
  3. Get 1 can (15 ounces) cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
  4. Take 1 carton (32 ounces) reduced-sodium chicken broth
  5. Get 1 cup water
  6. Get 1 can (14-1/2 ounces) fire-roasted diced tomatoes, undrained
  7. Take 6 cups fresh spinach (about 4 ounces)
  8. Make ready Crumbled goat cheese

Instructions to make Spinach & Sausage Lentil Soup:
  1. Slice turkey sausage into bite sized pieces.
  2. Put all ingredients except the goat cheese in the crock pot and cook on low for 8 hours.
  3. Serve hot with 1 Tbsp of goat cheese.
