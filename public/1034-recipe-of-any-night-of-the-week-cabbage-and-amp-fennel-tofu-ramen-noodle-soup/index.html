The ingredients needed to make Cabbage & Fennel Tofu Ramen Noodle Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 small cabbage head, sliced in strips
  2. Make ready 3 medium green onion, chopped
  3. Make ready 1 cup dehydrated carrot slices
  4. Make ready 3 celery ribs, sliced or chopped
  5. Prepare 1 small red onion sliced philly or french cut
  6. Take 2 small or 1 large fennel root sliced french style cut
  7. Get 1 (16 oz) package firm tofu, chopped
  8. Get 2 packs beef ramen noodles
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon black currant balsamic vinegar
  10. Take 2 tablespoons blood orange fused olive oil
  11. Make ready 1 can progresso french onion soup
  12. Prepare Salt
  13. Get Pepper
  14. Prepare Garlic powder

Steps to make Cabbage & Fennel Tofu Ramen Noodle Soup:
  1. Begin by boiling water for your ramen noodles and making them. Set aside. Meanwhile, lightly sautee your fennel, onions, and cabbage with some of the olive oil in a large frying pan or wok.
  2. Next add frying pan ingredients to a large sauce pot. Mix in carrots & celery. Add French onion soup, ramen noodle flavor packets, and seasonings in desired amounts. Cover and begin to simmer.
  3. Cube and pan fry tofu with olive oil, chopped green onion, balsamic vinegar, and seasonings.
  4. Finally add noodles and tofu with onions to the mixture in the large pot. Cover and continue to simmer another 15-20 minutes.
  5. Use chopsticks, fork, or spoon! Serve & Enjoy!
