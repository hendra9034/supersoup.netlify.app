The ingredients needed to make Macaroni Shell Soup:
  1. Prepare macaroni shell (small)
  2. Make ready cream
  3. Make ready evaporated milk
  4. Prepare Butter (optional)
  5. Prepare Cooking oil
  6. Get garlic
  7. Prepare small onion minced
  8. Prepare hotdog
  9. Get burger patty(optional)
  10. Get Water
  11. Make ready Chicken
  12. Get Chicken broth
  13. Make ready Carrots
  14. Prepare Potato
  15. Make ready Bell pepper
  16. Get Salt
  17. Make ready Pepper
  18. Take Lettuce or cabbage (optional)
Steps to make Macaroni Shell Soup:
  1. Prepare all the ingredients, cut all the vegetables in small pieces, make the chicken boiled and use the broth later.
  2. In a casserole, saute garlic and onion with butter and cooking oil, add the meat, hotdogs and chicken.
  3. Add the chicken broth then macaroni shell, wait until its boiled.
  4. Add vegetables, cream, evaporated milk and cheese
