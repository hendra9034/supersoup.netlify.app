Steps to make My Marinated Chilli pork loin steaks with Red Pepper.#Mainmeal:
  1. Add the marinade to the steaks and leave marinating for a couple of hours.
  2. Add to a non stick pan and fry for 1 minute on each side. Then turn down the heat and cook on low for 10 minutes, turning often.
  3. Add to the serving plate Romaine lettuce the heart its crisp then the large Cress.
  4. Next add on top Celery, Red onion and Tomatoes
  5. Next the Spring onion and Gherkins.
  6. Black Pepper, a little dash of sweet Chilli and a little Dash of mayonaise then a sprinkle of Red pepper flakes.
  7. Add 2 Slices of lime and squeeze a tiny amount of lime juice all over.
  8. Next add the pork loins and the juice off them next to the salad.
