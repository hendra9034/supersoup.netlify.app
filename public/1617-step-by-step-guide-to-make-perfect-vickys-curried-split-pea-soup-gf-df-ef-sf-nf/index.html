The ingredients needed to make Vickys Curried Split Pea Soup, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  2. Prepare 1 carrot, chopped
  3. Prepare 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  4. Get 3 tbsp sunflower oil
  5. Take 2 tbsp tomato puree / paste
  6. Get 1 tbsp medium curry powder or strength of choice
  7. Get 450 g (2 cups) green split peas
  8. Take 1920 ml (8 cups) vegetable stock
  9. Prepare 1 tsp low sodium salt
  10. Get 240 ml (1 cup) full fat coconut milk
  11. Make ready Freshly chopped coriander / cilantro to serve

Steps to make Vickys Curried Split Pea Soup, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Heat the oil in a large pan and fry the onion and carrot for around 5 minutes or until the onion is translucent
  2. Add the garlic and fry a further minute
  3. Add the tomato puree and curry powder, stir in and cook 2 minutes
  4. Add the split peas, stock and salt
  5. Bring to the boil then turn down and let simmer for 35 - 40 minutes or until the peas are softened
  6. Before serving stir through the coconut milk and season to taste
  7. Serve with some fresh coriander
