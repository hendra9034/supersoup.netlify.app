The ingredients needed to make Coconut Apricot bites:
  1. Prepare 150 g dessicated coconut
  2. Prepare 50 g granulated sugar
  3. Get 50 g dried apricots, chopped
  4. Prepare 1 medium egg

Instructions to make Coconut Apricot bites:
  1. Mix ingredients until covered in egg.
  2. Shape into 12 balls by pressing the coconut mixture together using your palms.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes in a preheated oven at 180C. Until toasted and golden.
  4. Cool and enjoy.
