The ingredients needed to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Take 15 onions
  2. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
  3. Take 4 tbsp salt
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp pepper
  5. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp ground sage
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp red chili flakes
  8. Get 10 qts veggie stock
  9. Make ready 1 cup sherry cooking wine
  10. Prepare 1 cup Blonde Roux
  11. Get 1 Stella bread
  12. Prepare 1 Slice Provolone Cheese

Instructions to make French Onion Soup:
  1. Clean and cut onions. The julienne cut all and put in seperate container.
  2. Sweat onions and caramelize, then deglaze with sherry cooking wine. Cook down until there is almost no wine left.
  3. Add all 5 tbsp butter and then add garlic cloves and seasoning cook for another three minutes.
  4. Add you veg stock and bring to boil for 20 minutes. Then let simmer for additional 20 minutes.
  5. Remove from burners and ice want to have proper heating and cooling temperatures.
  6. Cut three loafs of French baguette at a bias and you should get 8 slices per loaf. Season bread with salt, pepper, red flakes, and shredded provolone. And bake until crispy. This will go on top of soup before it's served, as a delicious garnish.
