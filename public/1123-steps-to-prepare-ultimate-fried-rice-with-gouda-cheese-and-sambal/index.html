The ingredients needed to make Fried Rice With Gouda Cheese And Sambal:
  1. Take 2 cup cooked white rice
  2. Prepare 5 slice Gouda cheese
  3. Get 5 eggs
  4. Prepare 5 shallot
  5. Take 2 spring onion
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp fish sauce
  7. Prepare Dash Sichuan pepper and salt
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp cooked Sambal Ikan bilis or any bbq sauce
  9. Prepare Filling Optian
  10. Get Shallot. Dried Anchovies, Prickle ginger, springonion, dried Chili pepper
Steps to make Fried Rice With Gouda Cheese And Sambal:
  1. With oil fry the shallot then add in the eggs, then add in the cooked white rice, flip once and let the egg set and TOP with seasoning
  2. Spread generously any cheese of your choice and sambal or (another your Favourite bbq sauce) cover lit to let it melt in Low heat
  3. Enjoy this quick and cheese meal
