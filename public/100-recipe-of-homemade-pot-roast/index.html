Instructions to make Pot roast 🥩 🥔 🍄:
  1. Place your roast in your slow cooker or crock pot. Wash everything, remove the ends from potatoes, and slice potatoes into 2 inch pieces.
  2. Place in a bowl of cold water and toss in the fridge til later.
  3. Drain water from potatoes and add them in the crock pot.
  4. Add in everything else, cover and cook on high for 4 hours.
  5. Cover and cook another 1-2 hours until roast is falling apart.
