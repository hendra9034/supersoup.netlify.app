The ingredients needed to make Chinese style beef stew:
  1. Prepare 500 g Beef
  2. Take 2 potatoes
  3. Make ready 2 carrots
  4. Make ready 6 slices ginger
  5. Prepare 6 garlic cloves
  6. Take 1 dried chilli pepper 🌶
  7. Make ready 1 g dried Sichuan pepper
  8. Make ready 4 tomatoes 🍅
  9. Prepare 1 spoon cooking wine
  10. Get 1 tsp soy sauce
  11. Prepare 1 spring onion
  12. Get 1 green pepper
  13. Make ready Half onion 🧅

Instructions to make Chinese style beef stew:
  1. Stir fry garlic ginger, dried chilli and Sichuan pepper first.
  2. Add beef in stir fry until it turns bit golden colour, then put onion in.
  3. Add cut tomatoes and cooking wine, spring onion,slow cook for 30 minutes until tomatoes are almost melt.
  4. Add soy sauce, salt then potatoes and carrots, together with a tsp of Italian mixed herbs. Slow cook for another 30 min.
  5. Finally add cut green pepper on top.Done ✅
