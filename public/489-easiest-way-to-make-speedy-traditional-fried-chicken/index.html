The ingredients needed to make Traditional Fried Chicken:
  1. Take 1/2 Chicken (or 1 Kg), cut small or medium
  2. Prepare 2 Tbsp desiccated coconut
  3. Make ready 3 bay leaves
  4. Prepare 400 ml water
  5. Make ready Oil for frying
  6. Take Spices need to be blend into paste form:
  7. Prepare 1/2 union
  8. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
  9. Take 7 cm turmeric or 1 tsp turmeric powder
  10. Take 2 cm ginger
  11. Take 3 cm galangal or dried galangal
  12. Make ready 1 Tbsp coriander powder
  13. Prepare 1 Tbsp salt
  14. Make ready 1/2 Tsp chicken stock powder (optional)
  15. Take 2 stalks lemongrass
  16. Take 1 Tbsp sugar

Steps to make Traditional Fried Chicken:
  1. Clean and cut the chicken as you want, then put it in the pot.
  2. Put in the blended spices, water, desiccated coconut, bay-leaves into the pot and mix well, then let it cook until the chicken cooked thoroughly.
  3. When it's cooked, then separate the chicken and the coconut, set aside.
  4. Heat the oil, and fry the chicken until it's golden colour. Once done with the chicken, fry the coconut into the oil until the colour changing. The coconut fried became so tasty because it's absorb all the spices.
