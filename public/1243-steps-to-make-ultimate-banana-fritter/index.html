The ingredients needed to make Banana fritter:
  1. Take 1 banana
  2. Make ready 2 spoons self raising flour
  3. Get 1 spoon corn flour

Steps to make Banana fritter:
  1. Mashed the banana with fork
  2. Add flour and a bit of cold water.
  3. Mix well, you can add more flour if too watery. It should looks like this.
  4. Fried at medium heat. You will get about 6 of this mini fritters.
