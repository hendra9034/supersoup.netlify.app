The ingredients needed to make 🍩🍥 Treacle Tart 🍥🍩:
  1. Get 300 g Golden Syrup
  2. Take 250 g pie pastry
  3. Take 4 eggs, well beaten
  4. Get 6 tblsp breadcrumbs
  5. Prepare 1 tblsp lemon juice
  6. Take Grated rind of 1 lemon
Steps to make 🍩🍥 Treacle Tart 🍥🍩:
  1. Line a flan tray/ pastry shell with the pastry and place in the ‘fridge for 30 minutes. - Pour the Golden Syrup into a bowl and place the bowl in a bigger bowl of hot water for 10 minutes.
  2. Add in the lemon juice and the lemon rind. Mix well. - Mix in the eggs, then mix in the breadcrumbs.
  3. Pour the mixture into the flan dish and bake in the oven at 180°C for 20-25 minutes. - Allow to cool completely before serving.
  4. #Tips: - Serve with ice cream or sour cream to complement the flavour of the tart.
  5. Although the addition of Black Treacle is optional, there really is no substitute for Golden Syrup. You can make a variations using molasses, corn syrup or pancake syrup but the taste just wont be the same!
