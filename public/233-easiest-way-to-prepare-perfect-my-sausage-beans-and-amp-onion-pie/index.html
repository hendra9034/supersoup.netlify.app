  1. Brown the sausages under the grill. Then set aside when cooled cut them in little slices
  2. In a pan add 1 tsp Butter heat up then add the onions fry until soft.
  3. Next add the boiling water to the onions sprinkle in the dried stock then stir in next add the beans and sweet peppers, tomato puree and black pepper mix in simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Next add the cornflour to thicken then add the sausage stir to mix.
  6. Using a slotted spoon, drain off some liquid when adding to a greased oven dish and hold some liquid for your gravy to have over the pie when serve.
  8. Boil the potatoes until soft. Mash them add butter, dijon mustard and cream.mash until smooth.
