The ingredients needed to make Japanese Miso Beef in Eggplant:
  1. Make ready 2 big eggplants
  2. Make ready 100 g minced beef
  3. Get 1/2 onion
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon Miso
  5. Take 1 tablespoon sugar
  6. Get 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  7. Get 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  8. Take Sesame seed
Steps to make Japanese Miso Beef in Eggplant:
  1. Cut eggplants half and hollow out.
  2. Chop contents of eggplant and onion. And mix all with seasonings.
  3. Flour eggplant and put beef mixture inside. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
