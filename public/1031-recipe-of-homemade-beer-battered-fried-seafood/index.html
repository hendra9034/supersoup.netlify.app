The ingredients needed to make Beer Battered Fried Seafood:
  1. Get 600 g Seafood Mix *Prawns, Fish, Squid, Scallops, Mussels, etc
  2. Prepare 2-3 tablespoons Rice Flour OR Plain Flour
  3. Take Oil for frying
  4. Take Beer Batter
  5. Take 3/4 cup Plain Flour
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup Rice Flour OR Potato Starch (OR Corn Starch)
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Fine Salt
  8. Get 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder *OR grated 1 clove Garlic
  9. Get 1 cup COLD Beer of your choice

Instructions to make Beer Battered Fried Seafood:
  1. Heat deep Oil to 180℃. Sprinkle Rice Flour (OR Plain Flour) over Seafood pieces, and coat them well.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine Plain Flour, Rice Flour (OR Potato Starch, Corn Starch), Fine Salt and Garlic Powder. Add COLD Beer and mix well.
  3. Dip the Seafood pieces in the batter and fry in the oil until nicely browned and crispy. Seafood can be cooked fairly quickly. Take them out and lay them on a rack to drain the oil.
  4. Sprinkle with extra Salt and enjoy while hot.
  5. *Note: If you have leftover batter, do not waste it. Add some sliced Onion and Carrot to the batter, and fry large tablespoon sized clumps until golden.
