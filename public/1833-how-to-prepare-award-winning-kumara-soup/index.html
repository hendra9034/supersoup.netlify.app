The ingredients needed to make Kumara Soup:
  1. Take 1 large Kumara diced
  2. Take 2 carrots, diced
  3. Take 2 stalks celery, diced
  4. Get 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. Take 1 tsp garlic
  6. Make ready 1/4-1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  7. Prepare 2 tsp ground Cumin
  8. Make ready 1 tsp ground coriander
  9. Take 1 tsp turmeric
  10. Prepare 4 cup stock (chicken, vegetable or beef)
  11. Take 3/4 cup red lentils

Instructions to make Kumara Soup:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan, add onion and cook until soft.
  2. Add garlic, chilli powder, Cumin, coriander, turmeric stock, red lentils and cook for 20 min
  3. Add carrots, celery and Kumara and cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Puree half the soup then combine and serve.
