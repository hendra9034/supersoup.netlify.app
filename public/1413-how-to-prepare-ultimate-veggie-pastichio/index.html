Instructions to make Veggie pastichio:
  1. Add the olive oil, onion and garlic to a large pan and sauté for 2 minutes.
  2. Add the carrots, zucchini, bell pepper and corn, and simmer for 8 minutes.
  3. Add the vegeta, black pepper, oregano and tomato sauce, and simmer for a further 10 minutes.
  4. Cook the pasta for 2 minutes less than what it says on the packet.
  5. Mix the pasta with the sauce and set aside.
  6. Prepare the bechamel: Melt the butter in a medium pan, add the flour and stir until smooth. Pour in hot milk, bit by bit, while constantly stirring. You can use an electric whisk. Season with salt, black pepper and nutmeg.
  7. Transfer the pasta into an ovenproof dish, cover with bechamel, and top with the cheeses.
  8. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180°C for 40 minutes.
