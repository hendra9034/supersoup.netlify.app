The ingredients needed to make Red lentil chick pea curry soup:
  1. Prepare soup ingredients
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  3. Prepare 1 onion, diced
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped celery
  5. Make ready 5 1/2 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  6. Get 5 tsp curry powder
  7. Get 1/4 tsp hot sauce
  8. Make ready 1 lb red lentils
  9. Take 1 bunch Swiss chard, middle stem removed, chopped leaves.
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 cup or 1 can drained chick peas (garbanzo beans)
  11. Get toppings/garnishes
  12. Get 1 cooked quinoa
  13. Get 1 plain yogurt

Instructions to make Red lentil chick pea curry soup:
  1. In a large stock pot, saute onion in olive oil til soft.
  2. Add vegetable broth, seasonings, Swiss chard, lentils, and chick peas. Simmer til peas are soft.
  3. Top with a dollop of yogurt and a spoonful of warm quinoa - yum!
