The ingredients needed to make HCG French Onion Soup:
  1. Get 1 each onion
  2. Get 2 cup beef broth
  3. Take 2 clove garlic
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp stevia
  5. Prepare 1 thyme
  6. Take 1 salt
  7. Get 1 salt
  8. Make ready 1 pepper
  9. Prepare 3 each melba toast
Instructions to make HCG French Onion Soup:
  1. slice onion into thin rings
  2. in a saucepan, medium heat, add onions and splash of beef broth. keep adding broth so it does not stick or burn onions
  3. after 10 minutes, add 1/2 teaspoon of stevia and 2 cups of beef broth
  4. add 2 garlic clove for the remainder of the cook time.
  5. on med-low heat, partially cover saucepan and cook for 20 more minutes
