The ingredients needed to make Beefy lentil and veggie soup:
  1. Take 1 lb. ground beef
  2. Take 1 cup dry green lentils, sorted and rinsed
  3. Make ready 1 small onion diced
  4. Make ready 4 garlic cloves minced
  5. Prepare 2 medium red potatoes diced
  6. Prepare 2 carrots peeled and diced
  7. Get 1 celery stalk diced
  8. Prepare 1 can petite diced tomatoes
  9. Take 2 8 oz. cans tomato sauce
  10. Take 1 beef boulion cube
  11. Take 1 tomato boulion cube
  12. Prepare 1 dash of worchesire sauce
  13. Get 1 dried oregano to taste
  14. Make ready 1 season salt to taste
  15. Prepare 1 ground black pepper to taste
  16. Prepare 6 cup water
  17. Prepare 2 dash sugar
  18. Take 1 olive oil

Steps to make Beefy lentil and veggie soup:
  1. Saute onion and garlic in a little olive oil just until onions are translucent. Add meat, worchesire, season salt, and pepper. Cook until meat is done then drain. Wipe excess oil from pot then put meat.mixture back in.
  2. Add lentils, potatoes, carrots, celery, tomatoes, tomato sauce, bullion cubes oregano, sugar, and water.
  3. Stir everything until mixed well and bring to a boil. Cut heat down, cover and simmer until all veggies are tender. Serve and enjoy!!
