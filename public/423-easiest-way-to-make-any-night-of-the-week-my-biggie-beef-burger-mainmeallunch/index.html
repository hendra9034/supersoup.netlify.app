The ingredients needed to make My Biggie Beef Burger ðŸ˜‹#Mainmeal#Lunch:
  1. Make ready 2 Quater pounder Beef Burgers
  2. Take 2 Brioch Buns toast the inside
  3. Get Handful Spinach on each
  4. Get 2 tbls sliced Onion
  5. Take 1 little sliced Tomato
  6. Prepare 2 Slices Hallumi cheese
  7. Take 4 slices Cucumber sliced thin
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Sweet Chilli sauce on each burger
  9. Prepare 1 tbls Sour Cream on each burger
  10. Get 1 little black cracked pepper on each
Steps to make My Biggie Beef Burger ðŸ˜‹#Mainmeal#Lunch:
  1. Toast the insides of the brioche buns.
  2. Add the burgers to a non stick frying pan and cook on medium
  3. When the Beef Burgers are nearly cooked add the slices of onion. Fry for 1 minute
  4. Next slice 2 slices of hallumi and add them to the frying pan and grill both sides.
  5. Add the spinach to the bottom bun. Then add the cucumber slices and tomatoes.
  6. Add the chilli sauce to the top burger and then add the sour cream.
  7. Add the Beef Burgers on top of the salad next the hallumi cheese Then the cooked onion also the black pepper.
  8. Add the top and serve hot on it's own or with chips. Enjoy
