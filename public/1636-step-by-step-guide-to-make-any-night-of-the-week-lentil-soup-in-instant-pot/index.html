The ingredients needed to make Lentil Soup In Instant Pot!:
  1. Prepare Half bag of lentils (I used brown. Only kind I had.)
  2. Prepare About 3 cups water
  3. Prepare About 4 cups chicken broth
  4. Prepare I large can of Whole San Marzano tomatoes-garlic&red pepper kind
  5. Take 3 stalks celery, chopped
  6. Make ready About teaspoon crushed or chopped garlic
  7. Take 1 medium onion, your choice, chopped or diced
  8. Get 2 bay leaves
  9. Make ready About teaspoon dried parsley or use fresh
  10. Take About 3 dashes of Paprika
  11. Prepare 3 Dashes onion powder
  12. Take 3 dashes garlic powder
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp Himalayan Pink salt or add to taste later as you like
  14. Get 1 light dash of Cardamom for luck

Steps to make Lentil Soup In Instant Pot!:
  1. Do not drain tomatoes. Crush with hands. Or use diced if you like. I prefer the San Marzano with garlic & red pepper. - I didnt soak the lentils but you can if you like. Wash and look through them. Take out any odd bits or shriveled lentils if you find them. Wash and rinse about 3 or 4 times. - Add lentils to Instant Pot. - Add ALL other ingredients. - Give it a stir. - Put on lid. Lock the lid. - Make sure valve is centered. - Set for MANUAL- 10 MINUTES - Press start.
  2. Use natural steam release or manual release after timer beeps. BE SAFE HERE. - Let soup rest at least 5 minutes. - SAFETY FIRST- after steam releases completely, use oven mitt and carefully open lid. - Stir and carefully remove bay leaves if you like. - Ladel soup into bowl.
