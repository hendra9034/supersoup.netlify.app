The ingredients needed to make Butternut Squash Soup with lemon and coconut:
  1. Take butternut squash, peeled and seeded (4 cups chopped)
  2. Take lemon or lime zested
  3. Get ginger or 1 teaspoon of powdered ginger
  4. Prepare Large onion sliced
  5. Prepare garlic clove, crushed
  6. Get Jalapeno chiles or cayenne pepper to taste.
  7. Prepare lemon juiced or 2 limes juiced
  8. Prepare coconut milk
  9. Take olive oil
  10. Make ready curry powder
Steps to make Butternut Squash Soup with lemon and coconut:
  1. Cook chopped squash, onion, garlic for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
  2. Add coconut milk, cook for 30 minutes on stove
  3. Puree and add lime juice.
  4. Serve with herbs.
