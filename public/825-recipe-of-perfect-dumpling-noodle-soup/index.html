The ingredients needed to make Dumpling Noodle Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 liter stock of your choice
  2. Take 8 dumpling /wanton skin
  3. Get 2 tbsp spring onion
  4. Take omelette
  5. Make ready 3 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 dash salt and pepper
  7. Take dumpling filling
  8. Prepare 1 dice carrot
  9. Get 2 dice celery
  10. Prepare 1 cup shreded cooked leftover chicken optional
  11. Make ready 1 tsp shao xing wine
  12. Prepare 1 dice onion
  13. Take 1 tbsp fish sauce
  14. Take NOODLE OPTION
  15. Prepare 4 combs of soy noodle
Instructions to make Dumpling Noodle Soup:
  1. Blend or chopped coarsely dumpling filling then fill up 1tbsp unto the duming /wanton skin ,using a dumpling mold or by hand then fasten all corner with wet finger tip
  2. make dumpling and set aside
  3. in pan with 1 tbsp of oil fry omelette then break it up the omelette into big pieces
  4. add spring onion and stock then bring it to a boil
  5. Add soy noodle and dumpling then lower down to a simmer for 3 minutes (so that the dumpling will not break up )then add seasoning and off heat and serve
