The ingredients needed to make Tawa Lamb Meat Balls:
  1. Take 2 lbs lamb mince (chicken or Quorn)
  2. Take 2.5 tsp Garlic ginger paste
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Green chilli paste or to taste
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder or to taste
  5. Prepare 1.5 tsp salt or to taste
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  7. Get 3 tsp coriander & cumin powder
  8. Take 1/2 Finely chopped onion
  9. Prepare To cook
  10. Take 3 tbsp oil
  11. Prepare 1/2 onion chopped
  12. Get 1 tomato finely chopped
  13. Get 1 tsp kasuri methi
  14. Get Coriander to garnish
  15. Take Lemon to garnish
  16. Make ready Chopped chilies to garnish
  17. Prepare 1 tsp Garam masala

Steps to make Tawa Lamb Meat Balls:
  1. Marinate the mince with top set of ingredients and cling film and put in fridge for 30 mins
  2. Add oil to pan- Wet hands and roll into balls and place on to a Tawa or a large griddle pan I think works best
  3. Allow to cook lamb natural will release a lot of moisture but if you cook at a medium to high heat the water will burn off
  4. Now add onion and the tomato and cover and cook for 6/7 mins on a medium heat add kasuri methi
  5. Add coriander and Garam masala to garnish top with lime wedges and chilies (optional)
