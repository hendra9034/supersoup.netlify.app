The ingredients needed to make Ballroom Tomato Soup:
  1. Take 5-6 tomatoes
  2. Take 2 cup chicken broth
  3. Make ready 1 Tsp Lemon Juice
  4. Get 1 Tbs Cayenne Pepper
  5. Get 1 Cup white vinegar
  6. Make ready 1-2 Hillshire Farm Beef Smoked Sausage
  7. Make ready 1 Tbs Sugar (Of your choice)
  8. Take 1 Tbs Garlic and Onion Powder
  9. Get Pinch of salt to taste
  10. Prepare 2 Tbs Basil
  11. Make ready 1 cup Half and Half
Instructions to make Ballroom Tomato Soup:
  1. Peel all the tomatoes and remove stems if have any. Then in a blender, Purée then for 10-30 seconds or until smooth.
  2. Preheat the oven at 350f on the oven and cook the sausages for an hour or more/less depending on your oven.
  3. While the sausages cook, prepare your soup by adding your tomato purée, chicken broth, Half and Half, lemon juice and white vinegar and let boil on medium temperature and stir for 2 minutes.
  4. As the soup boils, in a small bowl, mix cayenne pepper, salt, sugar, garlic powder, and onion powder and let sit for at least 1-2 minutes before adding them into the soup.
  5. After the sausages are cooked, take them from the oven and on to the counter. Then cut them into tiny pieces before adding them into the soup. Then stir for 20 seconds.
  6. Add basil to taste and let sit for 4 more minutes.
  7. After the soup is finished, serve in a bowl or cup with some grilled cheese and enjoy.
