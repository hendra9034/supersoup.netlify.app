The ingredients needed to make Vermicelli Soup With Napa Cabbage And Eggs:
  1. Prepare 5 large eggs /4 tbsp oil
  2. Get 1 tbsp each fine dice 4 shallot / 4 garlic
  3. Take napa cabbage
  4. Make ready 4 cup napa cabbage 2 carrot
  5. Get 1 liter any stock
  6. Prepare seasoning
  7. Take 1 dash Salt / light soy sauce
Instructions to make Vermicelli Soup With Napa Cabbage And Eggs:
  1. Sauté the carrot for 1 minute then add dice shallot / garlic then add the Napa cabbage for another minute
  2. Add stock and bring it to a boil then add in the vermicelli and eggs and cover lid in Low simmer for 5 minute and don't stir egg
  3. Season with a dash of light soy sauce and salt and stir only once without breaking the eggs, simmer for another minute then off heat and enjoy
