The ingredients needed to make Egyptian Red Lentil Chicken soup:
  1. Get 1 large bag of red lentils.
  2. Prepare 2 yellow onions. peeled and chopped finely.
  3. Take 8 large potatoes, peeled and finely cubed into small pieces.
  4. Make ready 1 medium size package of halal chicken drumsticks.
  5. Take 1 water
  6. Take 1 chicken broth
  7. Get 1 salt
  8. Take 1 black pepper
  9. Take 1 cilantro, finely chopped
  10. Take 1 ground cumin
  11. Prepare 1 minced garlic
  12. Take 1 paprika
  13. Make ready 1 olive oil
Instructions to make Egyptian Red Lentil Chicken soup:
  1. This recipe is not complicated, add the spices to your own preferences. Rinse the halal chicken drumsticks and set aside in a cold place until needed. In a deep pot, pour a few drops of olive oil into the bottom and place the onions to saute. It should only take a few minutes. Add the water, again as much or as little as you'd like. Add the chicken and broth and the rest of the spices and herbs. Add the lentils last.
