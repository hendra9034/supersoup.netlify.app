Steps to make chicken noodle soup:
  1. put uncooked chicken in pot, fill with water until chicken is covered
  2. cut up onion, add to pot
  3. boil until chicken is done, about 45 min
  4. remove chicken from pot and set in plate to cool
  5. break chicken in pieces and add back to pot
  6. cut veggies and add to pot along with noodles
  7. add about 6 bouillon cubes and boil until noodles are done and veggies are tender
