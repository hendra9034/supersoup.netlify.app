The ingredients needed to make Chicken Broccoli Cheese soup:
  1. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Prepare 2 onions, chopped
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp flour
  4. Get 28 oz chicken broth
  5. Make ready 5 cup broccoli, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 cup cooked chicken
  8. Make ready 1 cup half and half

Steps to make Chicken Broccoli Cheese soup:
  1. Heat oil over medium high heat. Add onions, cook until translucent (3 mins). Stir in flour, cook for 1 minute.
  2. Add broth, heat to boil. Stir until thickened.
  3. Add broccoli, cook until tender (5 minutes).
  4. Stir in cheese, chicken, half and half. Reduce heat and cook a minute or two until cheese is melted and incorporated.
