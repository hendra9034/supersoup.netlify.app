The ingredients needed to make broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. Take 2 cup chicken stock
  2. Get 2 cup half and half
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup flour
  6. Prepare 16 oz broccoli
  7. Get 3 medium carrots
  8. Get 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar
  10. Get 1 salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make broccoli and cheese soup:
  1. melt Tbs butter in small frying pan,chop onions add to pan and Sautee until its not quite clear,remove from heat,set to side.
  2. on medium low, melt the rest of butter in a big soup pot. whisking quickly and constantly ,add the flour and cook for 5minutes.
  3. add the half and half and the stock. bring to a simmer.
  4. while waiting for half and half And stock to simmer Peel and julienne your carrots and chop your broccoli. once mixture is simmering add the carrots and broccoli. salt and pepper to taste.
  5. stir occasionally for 25 min. 10 minutes into cook time add the sauteed onions.
  6. in the last 5minutes of cook time add your cheese, stir well, then add nutmeg,stir well.
  7. enjoy!
  8. if u like u can blend the soup in a blender for a few seconds, I like to leave It as is. also for a little kick serve it with a spoonful of sour cream!
