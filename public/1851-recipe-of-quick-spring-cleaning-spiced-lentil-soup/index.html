The ingredients needed to make Spring Cleaning Spiced Lentil Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tsp cumin seeds
  2. Get 2 tsp ground coriander
  3. Get 1 red onion finely diced
  4. Make ready 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  5. Make ready 1/8 tsp black pepper
  6. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Get 400 mls light coconut milk
  8. Take 600 mls veg stock
  9. Take Juice of 1/2 lemon
  10. Make ready 140 g red lentils
Steps to make Spring Cleaning Spiced Lentil Soup:
  1. Gently toast the cumin seeds in a dry saucepan for 2-3 minutes until aromatic then top into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Fry the onions in some oil for 5 mins then add the crushed garlic and continue to cook for a few more minutes.
  3. Stir in the toasted cumin seeds, coriander, salt and pepper. Add the lentils and stir well. 1. Then add the stick, lemon juice and coconut milk. It all looks like a milky puddle at this stage, don’t panic! Cover and leave to simmer for 30 mins,
