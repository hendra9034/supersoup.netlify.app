The ingredients needed to make Hearty Chickpea Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 mason jar dried chicken noodle soup mix
  2. Prepare 1 can chickpeas
  3. Make ready 1 can chicken broth
  4. Make ready 8 cups Water
  5. Take Salt & Pepper
  6. Take 2 small Green onion
  7. Get 2 medium carrots
  8. Make ready 1 can corn
  9. Take Chicken, Shredded or Diced
  10. Prepare Dried Soup Mix*
  11. Take 1 cup dried green split peas
  12. Prepare 1 cup dried yellow split peas
  13. Make ready 1 cup quinoa
  14. Prepare 1 cup white beans
  15. Take 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  16. Prepare 2 teaspoon onion powder
  17. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  18. Take 1 teaspoon ground sea salt
  19. Prepare 2 chicken bouillon cubes
  20. Take 2 cups pasta noodles
Steps to make Hearty Chickpea Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Set noodles aside. Place remaining soup mix, chickpeas, chicken broth, water, and chicken into large soup pot or crock pot. Cook on high for 2-4 hours or until beans are softened.
  2. Add green onion, carrot, corn, and salt & pepper. Simmer another 45-60 mins.
