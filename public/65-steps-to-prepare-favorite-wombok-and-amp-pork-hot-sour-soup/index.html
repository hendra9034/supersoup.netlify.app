The ingredients needed to make Wombok & Pork Hot Sour Soup:
  1. Make ready 1-2 teaspoons Sesame Oil
  2. Prepare 80 g thinly sliced Pork *cut into small pieces
  3. Take 3-4 thin slices Ginger *cut into fine strips
  4. Take Salt & White Pepper
  5. Make ready 2 cups Chicken Stock *OR 2 cups Water and 2 teaspoons Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  6. Take 1 tablespoon Sake (Rice Wine) *optional
  7. Prepare 150 g Wombok *cut 1cm in width
  8. Get 4-5 Dried Black Fungus *optional, soaked in water and sliced
  9. Get 1 tablespoon Potato Starch *mixed with 1 tablespoon Water
  10. Take 1 Egg *lightly whisked
  11. Make ready 1 Spring Onion *finely chopped
  12. Get Seasoning
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce) *OR Chilli as required
  14. Prepare 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  15. Get 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar
  16. Get *Note: Alter the amount to suit your taste. 1 teaspoon Toban Djan is quite mild

Instructions to make Wombok & Pork Hot Sour Soup:
  1. Soak Dried Black Fungus in cold/warm water until softened. Wash very well, drain and slice. Prepare all other ingredients.
  2. Heat Sesame Oil in a large saucepan over medium heat, cook Pork and Ginger, and lightly season with Salt & White Pepper.
  3. Add Chicken Stock, Sake, Wombok and Black Fungus, and bring to the boil. Cook until Wombok is soft. It won't take long. Season with Toban Djan (OR Chilli), Soy Sauce and Vinegar.
  4. Add Potato Starch mixture and stir. When the soup is slightly thickened, add lightly whisked Egg slowly in a circular motion into the boiling soup, and gently stir. When the Egg is cooked, remove from heat.
  5. Sprinkle with finely chopped Spring Onion and enjoy.
