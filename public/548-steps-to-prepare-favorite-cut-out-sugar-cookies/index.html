Steps to make Cut out sugar Cookies:
  1. Mix flour and sugar together with a whisk until well combined.
  2. Add in the cubed, cold butter. Mix using hand until it looks crumbly.
  3. Add in the vanilla extract and 1-2 tbsp of water to form into a dough.
  4. Cover in cling wrap and refrigerate for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough and cut into desired shapes. I recommend refrigerating the cut out shapes for another 10-20 minutes but it's optional.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes at 180 degrees celsius in a preheated oven.
  7. Wait until completely cooled if decorating.
