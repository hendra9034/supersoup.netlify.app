The ingredients needed to make My Homemade Chilli Beef Burgers üòÅ #Lunch#Mainmeal:
  1. Prepare 200 g Beef Mince
  2. Prepare 2 tbls Diced onion
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Chilli marinade
  4. Prepare 1/2 Tsp Garlic granuals
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  6. Take 1 cup grounded cheese flavoured Tortillas
  7. Take 1 Egg white
  8. Get 1/2 cup Flour and a little on a board to roll and shape
  9. Take Salt, to taste
  10. Prepare Black cracked pepper, to taste
Instructions to make My Homemade Chilli Beef Burgers üòÅ #Lunch#Mainmeal:
  1. Add the mince to a mixing bowl then add the diced onion
  2. Next add the chilli marinade then the egg white also the salt and pepper and Garlic granuals
  3. Add the tortilla crumbs they will give the burger a slight cheesey flavour instead of breadcrumbs or oats.
  4. Lastly the flour and the cayenne pepper. Then mix everything in squeezing with your hands until it all clumps together.
  5. On the flat surface add some flour and roll it and squeeze it into a firm ball. Cut in half and then half again you have 4 burgers
  6. Squeeze and roll each into a ball then with the palm of your hand flatten and push in the sides to a nice round firm shape. As thick as you want it.
  7. When all four are done add to a plate and add to the fridge for an hour or more this will chill and keep them firm for Grilling.
  8. When grilled and cooked through,add to a bun with some salad a sauce of your choice And cheese so much tastier than premade ones in shops.
