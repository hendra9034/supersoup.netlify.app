The ingredients needed to make Ford Fry Chicken Tortilla Soup:
  1. Prepare Broth
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 quarts chicken broth
  3. Take 1 small yellow onion, quartered
  4. Prepare 1 coarsely chopped carrot
  5. Prepare 1 coarsely chopped celery stalk
  6. Take 1 poblano pepper, seeded, coarsely chopped
  7. Get 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  8. Get 1/2 tsp oregano
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Take Soup
  11. Make ready 1 (15 oz) can hominy, drained, rinsed
  12. Prepare 1 small yellow onion, sliced
  13. Make ready 2 small carrots, sliced
  14. Get 1 small zucchini, sliced
  15. Make ready Chicken
  16. Take 1 pound chicken tenderloins seasoned with:
  17. Get 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  18. Prepare 1/2 tsp Chili powder
  19. Prepare 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  20. Prepare 1/2 tsp Cumin
  21. Prepare 1/2 tsp Paprika
  22. Get 1/2 tsp Brown Sugar
  23. Make ready 1/4 cup soy sauce
  24. Take Garnish
  25. Prepare 1 cup shredded Chihuahua or mild, soft white cheddar cheese
  26. Prepare 1 avocado, peeled, diced
  27. Prepare 1/2 cup grape tomatoes, quartered
  28. Get 1/2 cup tortilla strips
Steps to make Ford Fry Chicken Tortilla Soup:
  1. In a large pot, combine broth, onion, carrot, celery, poblano, cilantro, oregano, and salt, and bring to simmer over medium heat while stirring occasionally
  2. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, start chicken.
  3. While broth simmers, season chicken all over and cook in cast iron pan over medium high heat, 5 minutes per side or until it has a nice brown/ black fajita type crust but stays tender (155 F at the center!)
  4. Add your splash of soy sauce and a bit more brown sugar. Toss chicken 1 minute or until gooey caramelized. Should look kind of like this.
  5. Let chicken cool 5 minutes in a bowl, then either chop into bite sized chunks or shred.
  6. Strain broth when done, discarding solids. Add salt to taste.
  7. Return broth to pot over low heat. Add hominy, onion, carrots, zucchini and boil for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add chicken and bring back to boil.
  9. Divide soup into bowls. Garnish with cheese, avocado, tomato, and tortilla strips.
