The ingredients needed to make Chicken noodle soup in crockpot (creamy option):
  1. Make ready 1 large onion chopped
  2. Get 1/2 celery head chopped
  3. Take 6 large carrots chopped
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp minced garlic
  5. Prepare 4 cup chicken or veggie stock
  6. Take 2 cup egg or dumpling noodles
  7. Get 1 tbsp ground cumin
  8. Take 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  9. Get 2 bay leaf
  10. Take 1/2 tbsp dried basil
  11. Take 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  12. Make ready 2 chicken breasts cubed (cooked or uncooked)
  13. Make ready 1 can low fat condensed chicken soup ( for creamier soup)

Instructions to make Chicken noodle soup in crockpot (creamy option):
  1. Pour stock, spices and soup mix if using. mix in crockpot.
  2. Add chopped chicken and chopped veggies.
  3. Cook on high for 4 hours. Or low for 8. You can't overcook at this point but its ready when you are.
  4. 30 mins before you are ready to eat add pasta in, recover and cook for 20 Minutes or until tender. ENJOY.
