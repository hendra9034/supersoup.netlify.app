The ingredients needed to make Salmon with Broccoli and baby carrots in lemon sauce:
  1. Prepare 2 skinless salmon fillets
  2. Take Florets of 1 Broccoli
  3. Prepare 150-200 gr baby carrots or normal carrots cut in thick slices
  4. Make ready 1 cube vegetable stock
  5. Prepare 100 ml water
  6. Take Juice of half lemon
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil
  8. Prepare 80 ml whire wine
  9. Make ready 2 spoons finely chopped dill
  10. Prepare 1.5 spoon corn starch
  11. Prepare Salt and pepper

Steps to make Salmon with Broccoli and baby carrots in lemon sauce:
  1. Steam or boil the Broccoli for a few minutes (do not overcook it-they need to remain crispy) and then put the florets in a bowl with cold water and ice. At the same time, in a pan over high heat add the salmon fillets, season them and cook them for abt 1 min per side till it gets a golden colour. Add the wine and let it get the aromas for a few seconds. Take the salmon out of the pan and leave it aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the cube of veg stock, the water and the olive oil and keep them over medium heat. At the same time, in another bowl mix the starch with the lemon juice and then add same to the pan. Also, add the dill and keep stirring constantly till the sauce starts becoming a thicker (for abt 8-10mins)
  3. When the sauce is ready, add the salmon and the veggies and serve. You can sprinkle with some more fresh dill. Enjoy!
