The ingredients needed to make Italian Sausage lentil soup:
  1. Take 6 cup water
  2. Prepare 1 lb Italian Sausage
  3. Take 2 cup green lentil
  4. Get 3 cup carrots, chopped
  5. Prepare 3 stalk celery, chopped
  6. Get 1/2 medium onion, chopped
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup green peas
  8. Make ready 1 can tomato sauce
  9. Take 1 can tomatos, diced
  10. Take 2 tbsp beef boulion
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp basil
  12. Prepare 1 tsp sage
  13. Prepare chopped parsley
  14. Make ready 2 pinches thyme
Steps to make Italian Sausage lentil soup:
  1. Sort and rinse lentils, then add them to the 6 cups water. Bring to boil.
  2. Brown sausage over medium heat. Add drained meat to the lentils.
  3. Add chopped veggies to soup.
  4. Add seasonings and beef boulion.
  5. Simmer soup for 1 - 2 hours over med/low heat; until veggies are tender.
