The ingredients needed to make Loaded Baked Potato soup:
  1. Take 3 lbs potatoes
  2. Take 64 grams small diced onion
  3. Make ready 20 g menced Garlic
  4. Prepare 5 grams sea salt
  5. Get 4 Tbs butter
  6. Prepare 3 cups chicken broth
  7. Get 2 cups milk
  8. Prepare 2 tbs sour cream
  9. Take 1 lb bacon
  10. Prepare Green onion
  11. Prepare Grated cheese
  12. Prepare 2 tbs flour
Steps to make Loaded Baked Potato soup:
  1. Peel and Boil 2lbs of potatoes in water salted with 5grams sea salt until tender
  2. Poke holes throughout remaining 1lbs of potatoes wrap in foil and bake in over at 400F for 1 hour
  3. Fry bacon to preference
  4. Small Dice 64 grams of white onions and mence 20 grams of fresh garlic
  5. In a stock pot melt 4 Tbsp of butter add onion and garlic cook until tender
  6. Add 3 cups chicken broth, 2 cups milk, and 1 cup half and half to stock pot
  7. Add 2tbs of flour and 2tbs of sour cream whisk until smooth
  8. Mash the boiled potatoes and add them to the stock pot and bring to a simer
  9. Using an immersion blender or normal blender, blend soup base until smooth and return to stock pot
  10. Cube the baked potatoes into bite size pieces and cook for 5 min
  11. Add salt and pepper to taste
  12. Pour soup into bowl and top with cheese, bacon and green onions
  13. Hot sauce optional!
