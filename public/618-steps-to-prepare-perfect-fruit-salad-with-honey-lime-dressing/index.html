The ingredients needed to make 🍓🍇🍈🍍Fruit Salad with Honey Lime Dressing🍍🍈🍇🍓:
  1. Take Fruit Salad
  2. Prepare 1 melon or cantaloupe, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  3. Take 2 mangoes, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  4. Prepare 2 cups red grapes, sliced in half
  5. Take 5 kiwis, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  6. Make ready 400 g strawberries, cut into bite-sized pieces
  7. Prepare 1 pineapple, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  8. Prepare 1 cup fresh blueberries
  9. Make ready Honey Lime Dressing
  10. Take 1/2 tsp lime zest
  11. Prepare 3 tabls lime juice
  12. Make ready 1 tabls honey
Instructions to make 🍓🍇🍈🍍Fruit Salad with Honey Lime Dressing🍍🍈🍇🍓:
  1. Combine all the prepared fruit in a large glass bowl. - Combine lime zest, lime juice and honey in a small bowl or souted cup.
  2. Mix well - Pour the honey lime dressing over the fruit and gently stir to combine. Serve and Enjoy!
  3. #Notes: - - This fruit salad will last in the fridge for 3-5 days when stored in an airtight container. - - Use this recipe as a blueprint and sub in whatever fruits you have on hand. - - When possible, choose fruits that are local and in season for the best flavor.
