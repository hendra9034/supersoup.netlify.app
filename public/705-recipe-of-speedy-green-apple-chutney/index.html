The ingredients needed to make Green apple chutney:
  1. Get 1 green medium size apple
  2. Get 1 cup fresh coriander
  3. Get 6 fresh green chillies
  4. Get 1 inch ginger
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
  6. Take 1 tablespoon sugar
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon cumin
  8. Make ready 2 tablespoon olive oil
  9. Get Salt to taste
