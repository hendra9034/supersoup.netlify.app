The ingredients needed to make Giouvetsi:
  1. Take 500 g diced beef
  2. Make ready 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  3. Make ready 2 tsp garlic paste
  4. Take 2 carrots sliced
  5. Make ready 500 g passatta
  6. Prepare 250 ml water (more if required)
  7. Get 1 tbsp tomato puree
  8. Make ready 1 glass red wine (Alcohol Free optional)
  9. Make ready 1 cinnamon stick
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Dried marjoram
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Dried rosemary
  12. Make ready 5-6 whole cloves
  13. Get 2 tbs olive oil (Greek if Possible)
  14. Take 200 g orzo pasta
  15. Make ready Fresh Basil

Steps to make Giouvetsi:
  1. Mix the beef with seasoned flour. - Heat a pan with olive oil and brown off the beef, add the onions, garlic paste, carrots, Dried herbs, whole cloves, tomato purée and cinnamon stick and gently fry until the onions soften.
  2. Add the wine and cook until the wine has reduced right down to nothing. - Add the passata and water, bring to the boil, cover and simmer, checking regularly for an hour and half. If the sauce starts to become dry just add a drop more water.
  3. Heat a small sauté pan, add some olive oil and gently fry the orzo pasta until slightly brown. - After an hour and half tip the orzo pasta into the stew and cook slowly for another 45 mins. - Serve in a warmed bowl and stir in some fresh basil.
  4. TIP : The longer you can slow cook the stew the better, I continued to cook for a further three hours making sure you check regularly.
