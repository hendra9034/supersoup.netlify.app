The ingredients needed to make Sausage meat stuffed patty pans:
  1. Get 3 patty pans
  2. Get 400 grams sausage meat
  3. Get Nutmeg, sea salt and black pepper
  4. Take to taste Garlic
  5. Make ready Bechamel sauce to top(make it with butter, flour and milk)
  6. Prepare 1 Garlic clove

Arrange the sausage patties in a single layer on the pan. Cook for three to five minutes per side, or until the meat is browned and cooked through. Instructions to make Sausage meat stuffed patty pans:
  1. Bake the patty pans in the oven until tender for about 30 minutes at 160°C
  2. Cut top off the squashes and hollow them, I used both my pumpkin carving spoon and my melon baller
  3. Season patty pans inside, just a little pinch of salt and pepper will do.
  4. Sautee 1 garlic clove and before it turns gold add the chopped meat of the patty pans. Once they start to soften and colour add the seasoned sausage meat, mine is already seasoned with black pepper and nutmeg.
  5. Stuff the squashes carefully so that you don't break the skin. Top with a bit of bechamel sauce
  6. Bake in oven for further 20 minutes or until bechamel start to brown a bit
