The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Cauliflower Gnocchi Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 1 cup diced onion
  4. Prepare 1 cup diced celery
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  6. Make ready 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  7. Get 1 tsp dried thyme
  8. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup flour
  10. Get 2 cups chicken broth
  11. Make ready 2-3 cups almond milk or half and half
  12. Take 2 cups cooked chicken shredded or cubed
  13. Make ready 1 lb Gnocchi prepared, (I used cauliflower Gnocchi.)
  14. Get 2-3 cups baby spinach or chopped spinach
