The ingredients needed to make Honey salmon:
  1. Make ready 3 medium potatoes
  2. Make ready 3 medium carrots
  3. Make ready 2 fillets salmon
  4. Take 1/2 lemon (juice)
  5. Take 2 tblsp cooking cream
  6. Get 2 tblsp honey
  7. Prepare dried rosemary
  8. Get butter
  9. Prepare salt
  10. Take pepper
  11. Get olive oil
