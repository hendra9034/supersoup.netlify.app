The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken with Curry Courgette:
  1. Prepare 120 g rice
  2. Make ready 250 g diced chicken breast
  3. Make ready 2 courgettes
  4. Prepare 1 onion
  5. Prepare 5 cloves garlic
  6. Get 50 g breadcrumbs
  7. Take 10 g Curry sauce
  8. Get 15 g vegetables stocks paste
Instructions to make Fried Chicken with Curry Courgette:
  1. Prepare rice in a rice cooker or pan, cook for 25 minutes
  2. Take a big bowl, add breadcrumbs, drizzle of oil, season it with salt and pepper. Then add diced chicken breast, well mix them.
  3. Hot pan, 1 cm deep oil, and place chicken diced. Cook until golden brown for 10 minutes
  4. Chop onions, garlic and courgette. And them into the medium hot pan with drizzle of oil, cook for 5 minutes until they are bit soft. Then add curry sauce, vegetable stock paste and 100 ml water. Stir cook for 5 more minutes
  5. Serve it as below
