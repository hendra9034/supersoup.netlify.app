The ingredients needed to make CAZUELA DE POLLO A LA CHILENA. TIPICAL CHILEAN SOUP. JON STYLE:
  1. Make ready 6 chicken thighs, skin and fat removed
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp. Olive oil
  3. Get 2 garlic cloves chopped
  4. Prepare 1 medium size yellow onion, diced
  5. Make ready 2 large carrots, cut in chunks
  6. Prepare 6 chunks kabocha squash, cut in chunks (or butternut squash)
  7. Prepare 3 corns, cut in half
  8. Prepare 2 celery ribs, cut in chunks
  9. Prepare 1 red bell pepper, seeded and cut in chunks
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 cups string beans cut in pieces
  11. Get 6 Idaho potatoes peeled
  12. Make ready 1/4 cup brown rice
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp. Cumin
  14. Take 1 tbsp. Fresh thyme leaves
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp. Fresh chopped oregano leaves
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp. Chopped parsley
  17. Get 1 bay leave
  18. Get Water or chicken broth
  19. Prepare Salt and pepper to taste
  20. Prepare 4 pieces chicken bouillon cubes
  21. Take Chopped cilantro, for top decoration and flavor

Steps to make CAZUELA DE POLLO A LA CHILENA. TIPICAL CHILEAN SOUP. JON STYLE:
  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper. - Heat the oil in a skillet, brown the chicken on both sides and reserve.
  2. Heat a bit of oil in the same frying pan, cook garlic and onions together until just star browning. - Add carrots, celery, red bell pepper, spices, herbs and cook for a few minutes. - Add 1 cup of white wine and let it reduce.
  3. Divide all the vegetables, rice and chicken in 2 large cooking pots - Add enough water or chicken broth to the cooking pot to cover the ingredients. - Add chicken bouillon, salt and pepper to your own taste.
  4. Bring the liquid to a boil, lower the flame, cover and slowly cook until rice and potatoes are cooked. Serve immediately and sprinkle chopped cilantro on top. - https://youtu.be/8AsW229L85o
