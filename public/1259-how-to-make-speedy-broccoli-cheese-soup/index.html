The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Prepare 4 C chopped BROCCOLI florets
  2. Make ready 1 small diced ONION
  3. Get 3 diced CARROTS
  4. Make ready 1 C diced CELERY
  5. Prepare 3 C shredded CHEDDAR CHEESE
  6. Take 2 c heavy cream
  7. Get 2 t butter
  8. Get 5 cloves minced GARLIC
  9. Take SALT and PEPPER to taste
  10. Make ready 5 strips BACON
  11. Get Shredded CHEDDAR Cheese to taste

Instructions to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Place bacon in pot, saute until crispy. Drain.
  2. Add onions, carrots and celery to bacon drippings, cook until softened (10 minutes).
  3. Add broccoli, cream and 2 cups water. Cook on low pressure 10 minutes, quick release.
  4. Slowly add shredded cheese stirring until melted and incorporated.
  5. Add butter and garlic, stir until melted, simmer 10 minutes to thicken to desired consistency.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with cheese and bacon.
