The ingredients needed to make Yoghurt Mousse Jelly:
  1. Prepare 2 teaspoons Gelatine Powder
  2. Take 2-3 tablespoons Water
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk
  4. Take 1/4 cup Caster Sugar
  5. Take 1 cup Unsweetened Yoghurt
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  7. Take 1/2 cup Thickened Cream *whipped
  8. Get Topping of your choice *Today I used Strawberry Sauce and fresh Strawberries (see recipe)

Steps to make Yoghurt Mousse Jelly:
  1. Sprinkle Gelatine Powder into Water in a small bowl and soak for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Place Milk and Sugar in a saucepan, bring close to the boil, and remove from the heat. Add soaked Gelatine, mix well until Gelatine dissolves, then whisk in Yoghurt and Lemon Juice.
  3. Whip Cream until soft peaks form, add to the jelly mixture, and mix well. Pour into serving glasses. Place them in the fridge and leave to set.
  4. Before serve, add some fresh Fruit or Fruit Sauce on top. Today I used Strawberry Sauce and fresh Strawberries. Mango, Peaches, Melon, Passionfruit, or any fresh fruit that goes well with Yoghurt would be a good topping.
  5. *Note: For grown-ups, sweet Liqueur can be added to the topping.
