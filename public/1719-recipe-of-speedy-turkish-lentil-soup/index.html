The ingredients needed to make Turkish lentil soup:
  1. Prepare 1 medium onion
  2. Get 1 large potato
  3. Get 1 carrot
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp salcha
  6. Prepare 150 grams red lentils
  7. Prepare 1 beef stock cube
  8. Prepare 1 seasoning black pepper and salt to your taste
  9. Get 2 tbsp butter
  10. Make ready 1 pinch pepper flakes
  11. Take 1 enough hot water to cover
  12. Get 1 if you dont have salcha which is pepper paste just use tomato paste and add a small bit more chilli

Instructions to make Turkish lentil soup:
  1. in a pot place add the oil then chopped onion. at a medium heat sautee them and add salcha chopped carrots and chopped potatoes and cook for another 2 minutes. add the washed and drained red lentils, seasoning and hot water and stock cube. stir well. cook on a low heat for 30-45 minutes.
  2. at the end add pepper and chilli flakes and butter and mix in.
  3. take pot off the heat and blend with a soup blender until smooth.
  4. my husband squeezes lemon over it when eating i personally dont but add if its to your taste. ENJOY
