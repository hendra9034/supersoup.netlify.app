The ingredients needed to make Sesame Oil Chicken Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 pcs Chicken thighs
  2. Take 1 tbsp Ginger
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp Sesame Oil
  4. Take Rice wine water 600ml (or Rice wine 100ml + water 500ml)
  5. Take 1/4 tsp Salt
Instructions to make Sesame Oil Chicken Soup:
  1. Cut chicken into bite size. Cut ginger if you bought them fresh.
  2. Heat up the pot and add 1tbsp. of sesame oil, ginger then chicken, panfry until chicken is slightly cooked.
  3. Add rice wine water, then salt. Bring to boil, turn the heat down and continue to cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the remaining 2 tbsp. of sesame oil. Mix well.
