The ingredients needed to make Scain's comfort chicken noodle soup for crockpot:
  1. Get 1 lb chicken tenderloins
  2. Get 2 celery stalks, chopped
  3. Prepare 2 carrots, chopped
  4. Make ready 1 small onion, chopped
  5. Prepare 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  6. Make ready 4 tbsp butter, divided
  7. Prepare 4 cups chicken broth
  8. Take 1 chicken boullion cube
  9. Prepare Parmesan cheese rind
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp onion powder
  12. Take dash cayenne
  13. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  14. Get 8 oz egg noodles

Instructions to make Scain's comfort chicken noodle soup for crockpot:
  1. In a skillet, add 2 tbsp butter, then add veggies. Stir off and on for about 10 min. Don't let onions brown.
  2. In another skillet, add 2 tbsp butter, add chicken, garlic powder, onion powder and cayenne. Cook until chicken is no longer pink. I chopped my chicken the same size as my veggies
  3. Turn crockpot to high, add veggies and chicken
  4. Then add broth, boullion, bay leaf and cheese rind. Cover, and cook for 3-4 hrs until carrots and celery are tender.
  5. In a pot add water to boil noodles, cook per package instructions. Drain
  6. To serve, put noodles in a bowl then top with your soup. (My hubby doesn't like soggy noodles, so by doing it this way, your noodles keep their texture even the next day ;) )
