The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Get 2 Olive oil
  2. Get 1 Onion finely chopped
  3. Take 3 Flour
  4. Make ready 1 Garlic powder
  5. Prepare 1 Salt
  6. Make ready 3 Low sodium chicken broth
  7. Take 6 Broccoli florets
  8. Get 1 2% milk
  9. Prepare 1 Low fat sharp cheddar cheese
  10. Take 1 Black pepper to taste

Instructions to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over med heat. Add the onions and cook, stirring, until soft, bout 5 min. Stir in the flour, garlic powder, and salt and cook for 2 min
  2. Slowly whisk in the chicken broth and simmer the liquid until it thickens slightly. Add the broccoli and cook until tender, 7-10 min
  3. Remove the soup from the heat and purée using a blender, food processor, or immersion blender. If necessary, return the soup to the pot and heat over med heat. Whisk in the milk, 1 cp of the cheddar, and the pepper. Ladle the soup into serving bowls and sprinkle each with 1 tablespoon of the remaining cheese
