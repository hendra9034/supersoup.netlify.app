The ingredients needed to make Sukiyaki (Japanese Beef Pot):
  1. Take 200 g thin sliced good beef
  2. Get 3 spring onion
  3. Take 1 hard type tofu
  4. Prepare Enoki and Shiitake mushroom
  5. Make ready edible chrysanthemum(crown daisy)
  6. Get Udon noodles
  7. Get egg
  8. Take Soup
  9. Make ready 50 ml soy sauce
  10. Get 50 ml Mirin
  11. Prepare 100 ml Sake (Japanese wine)
  12. Take 1.5 tablespoon sugar

High quality thinly sliced beef is simmered in a sweet soy sauce based broth with tofu, vegetables and noodles. Steps to make Sukiyaki (Japanese Beef Pot):
  1. This is Shungiku (edible chrysanthemum, crown daisy). Tofu and vegetables.
  2. Combine all the soup ingredients.
  3. Put beef fat on pan and fry beef. Then put Sukiyaki soup on it.
  4. Add vegetables and Tofu. Beat egg.
  5. Dip beef and vegetable in egg. Put Shichimi chili powder to taste.
  6. Enjoy beef‼️
  7. Lastly put Udon noodles and cook with Sukiyaki soup.
