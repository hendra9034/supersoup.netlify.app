The ingredients needed to make Eggy Crumpets, Bacon & Maple Syrup:
  1. Prepare 4 crumpets
  2. Make ready 6 rashers smoked bacon
  3. Take 4 eggs
  4. Prepare Milk
  5. Make ready Maple syrup
  6. Prepare Black pepper
  7. Take Salt
  8. Take Oil
Instructions to make Eggy Crumpets, Bacon & Maple Syrup:
  1. Whisk 4 eggs in a bowl and add a splash of milk along with pepper and salt - to taste.
  2. In a large dish, tip the egg mixture in and soak the crumpets. Leaving them in there for a few minutes each side.
  3. Prepare a frying pan with a dash of oil to stop the crumpets sticking.
  4. Place the crumpets in the pan, bottom down and pour some of the egg mixture into the pockets of the crumpets.
  5. Cook for a few minutes either side. Everyone likes their eggs cooked differently.
  6. Serve on a plate with cooked smoked bacon, smothered in golden syrup.
