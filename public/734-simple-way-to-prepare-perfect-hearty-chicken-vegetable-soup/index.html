The ingredients needed to make Hearty Chicken Vegetable Soup:
  1. Prepare containers broth
  2. Get containers stock
  3. Make ready diced carrots
  4. Take rutabaga, diced
  5. Prepare small onions diced
  6. Make ready celery stalks diced
  7. Get pepper, seasoning salt, thyme, parsley, Mrs dash
  8. Make ready bay leaves
  9. Take cayenne pepper
  10. Get paprika, nutmeg
  11. Take sage, basil
  12. Prepare small hens or 7 breasts
  13. Get water
  14. Make ready pasta, i did egg noodles
  15. Get minced garlic
Instructions to make Hearty Chicken Vegetable Soup:
  1. Take a big stockpot and dump all liquid in
  2. Mince garlic, add to pot
  3. Dice carrots, onions, rutabaga, celery and put in pot
  4. Add chicken to pot
  5. Add everything else to the pot but pasta
  6. Cook on high til chicken is done (all white)
  7. Remove chicken, shred or dice and put back in the soup
  8. Add pasta and frozen peas if desired (i was out of peas)
  9. Cook on high til pastas cooked, vegetables tender about 20 to 25 min
  10. Serve and keep warm
