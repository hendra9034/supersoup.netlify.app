The ingredients needed to make Spicy Griddled lamb Chops:
  1. Take 12 lamb chops
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp Yogurt
  3. Get 2 tsp garlic ginger paste
  4. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Prepare 3 tsp coriander & cumin powder
  6. Take 1/4 grated nutmeg/ powder eq
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  8. Get 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  9. Prepare 3/4 tbsps Oil for cooking
  10. Take 1 tbsp Ghee
  11. Get Coriander for garnish
Instructions to make Spicy Griddled lamb Chops:
  1. Get your chops &. Pound them to tenderise
  2. Make your marinade & cover chops well
  3. Allow to sit in fridge for at least 30 mins
  4. Warm up a griddle pan add oil and place the chops and cook on a medium/ high heat for around 15/20 mins turning half way
  5. When you think the chops are done drizzle the ghee over them and garnish with coriander - Serve hot
