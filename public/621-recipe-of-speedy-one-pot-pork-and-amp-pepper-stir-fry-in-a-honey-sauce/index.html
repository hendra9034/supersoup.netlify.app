The ingredients needed to make One Pot Pork & Pepper Stir Fry in a Honey Sauce:
  1. Get 2 Ready to wok noodles for
  2. Take 400 g stir fry pork strips
  3. Make ready 2 bell peppers, sliced
  4. Take Yui’s Stir fry Sauce
  5. Get 3 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Take 2 tbsp honey
  7. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  8. Prepare 1 tsp sesame oil
  9. Take Pinch salt and pepper
  10. Get 1 tbsp cornflour
  11. Take Sesame seeds to garnish
Steps to make One Pot Pork & Pepper Stir Fry in a Honey Sauce:
  1. Whisk together the ingredients for the sauce and set to one side.
  2. In a little oil, stir fry the peppers in a frying pan or wok for 5 minutes or so until starting to soften up.
  3. Add the pork and continue to fry until the pork is just cooked through.
  4. Add the sauce and stir as it thickens in the pan.
  5. Stir in the noodles to coat everything in the sauce then serve topped with the sesame seeds.
