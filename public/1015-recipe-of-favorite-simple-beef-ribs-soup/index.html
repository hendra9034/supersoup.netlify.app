The ingredients needed to make Simple Beef Ribs Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Beef Ribs
  2. Make ready 1 lt of water
  3. Get Ingredients 2
  4. Take Carrot, peel the skin and cut
  5. Take 2 Celery stalk, thinly sliced
  6. Prepare Spring onion, thinly sliced
  7. Make ready 2 red tomatoes, roughly chopped
  8. Take To taste Fried shallot
  9. Prepare Seasoning
  10. Take 8 cloves garlic, finely mash
  11. Make ready White pepper to taste
  12. Get 1 nutmeg, finely mash
  13. Take 5 cloves
  14. Make ready 2 cm cinnamon
  15. Make ready To taste beef powder
  16. Prepare 2 tbs Cooking oil
Steps to make Simple Beef Ribs Soup:
  1. First, heat the water until it boils in a saucepan. After boiling, add beef ribs and boil for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove the beef ribs, drain and discard the boiled water from the pan.
  3. Then prepare the pan again, put back the ribs, cinnamon, cloves, and water. Cook on low heat until the ribs become tender, - - For me i cook in pressure cooker about 20 minutes.
  4. While waiting for the ribs, make the spices: blend the pepper, nutmeg, garlic, then saute until fragrant.
  5. When ribs are tender, add the sauteed spices and beef powder to the pan and mix well.
  6. Add carrot and cook until tender. Add spring onion, celery and tomato. Sprinkle fried shallot.
