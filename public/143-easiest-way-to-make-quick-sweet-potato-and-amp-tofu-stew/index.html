The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato & Tofu Stew:
  1. Take 180 g Onion (Sweet)
  2. Prepare 300 g Carrots
  3. Get 150 g Celery
  4. Prepare 150 g Asparagus
  5. Get 1 Clove Garlic
  6. Prepare 563 g Sweet Potatoes (2 Wh)
  7. Get 121 g Tomatos
  8. Make ready 215 g Corn
  9. Get 267 g Small White Beans
  10. Prepare 283 g Tofu
  11. Take 298 g Butternut Squash Soup
  12. Get 2 tbsp Canola Oil
  13. Take 8 oz Chicken Stock
  14. Take Spices
  15. Prepare Clove
  16. Take Smoked Paprika
  17. Make ready All spice
  18. Get Salt
  19. Take Pepper
  20. Take Tarragon
  21. Take Cayenne Pepper
Instructions to make Sweet Potato & Tofu Stew:
  1. Chop All Veggies, around 1 - 2 inch
  2. Saute Onions, Carrots, and Celery, once mixed broken down add Garlic.
  3. Remove from Pot/Pan, Put in Tofu and brown all 4 sides of each piece
  4. Once Tofu is Browned, add in chicken stock, soup, and the rest of the ingredients and mix well.
  5. Add desired spices, I prefer Clove, Paprika (Smoked), Pepper, Salt, Tarragon, and Cayenne Pepper, Mix well.
  6. Bring the burner to as low as possible and cook for 40 min - 2 hours depending on the sweet potatoes being soft.
