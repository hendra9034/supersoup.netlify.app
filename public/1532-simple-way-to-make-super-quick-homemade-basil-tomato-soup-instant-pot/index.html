The ingredients needed to make Basil Tomato Soup (Instant Pot):
  1. Get 1 tbspn butter
  2. Make ready 1 small onion
  3. Take 2 cloves minced garlic
  4. Prepare 1 (28 oz) can basil, garlic, & oregano diced tomatoes
  5. Take 2 cups chicken broth
  6. Take 1 cup water
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup basil sliced
  8. Make ready 1 tspn salt
  9. Make ready 1 tspn pepper
  10. Make ready 1/2 tspn oregano
  11. Get 2-4 dash Parmesan cheese grated
  12. Make ready 2 pinch red pepper flakes
  13. Get 1/2 cup heavy cream
