The ingredients needed to make Vickys Pear Pudding with a Cinnamon Sugar Crust, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Get 225 g caster sugar (superfine, 1 cup)
  2. Take 115 g gold foil-wrapped Stork block margarine (1 stick)
  3. Take 2 tbsp ground flaxseed mixed with 6 tbsp water / pear juice
  4. Make ready 120 ml light coconut milk (1/2 cup)
  5. Make ready 185 g gluten-free / plain flour (1.5 cups)
  6. Prepare 1/8 tsp xanthan gum if using GF flour
  7. Prepare 1.5 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 3 fresh, ripe pears
  9. Get Topping
  10. Prepare 15 g caster sugar (3 tbsp)
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
Steps to make Vickys Pear Pudding with a Cinnamon Sugar Crust, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Preheat the oven to gas 4 / 180C / 350F and grease / line a 9"× 13" baking tin
  2. Cream the sugar and butter together until light and fluffy
  3. Add the milk to the butter mixture and stir in - it'll look curdled but don't worry
  4. Mix the thickened flaxseed in - if left to stand the flax / water will turn gelatinous which is what you want (this mixture replaces 1 whole egg)
  5. Add the flour, xanthan gum if using and baking powder then mix until the batter is smooth
  6. Pour the batter into the cake tin and peel, core and slice the pears thinly
  7. Lay the pear slices across the top of the batter. Don't be too fussy as they'll sink in as the cake bakes
  8. Bake for 40 - 45 minutes until golden
  9. Mix the cinnamon and sugar topping together and sprinkle evenly over the top. Let melt a little bit
  10. Serve with custard or ice cream
