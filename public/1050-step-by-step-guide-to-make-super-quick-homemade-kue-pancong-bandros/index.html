Steps to make KUE PANCONG / BANDROS:
  1. Mixed together rice flour, coconut shredded, and salt.
  2. Add coconut milk little by little, mix well.
  3. Heat the cake mold over medium heat, grease with margarine or oil
  4. Pour the dough until it's full, cover and let it cook.
  5. Remove while hot and sprinkle with sugar on top.
