The ingredients needed to make Uzbekistan Buckwheat Beef Plov:
  1. Make ready 250 g buckwheat
  2. Get 150-200 g beef
  3. Get 1 carrot
  4. Take 2 green pepper
  5. Get 1 small potato
  6. Take 1 onion
  7. Make ready 50 ml vegetable oil
  8. Take 100 ml tomato juice
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt (to taste)
  10. Take 1/3 teaspoon coriander powder
  11. Take 1/3 teaspoon cumin powder
  12. Prepare Boiled Quail eggs
Steps to make Uzbekistan Buckwheat Beef Plov:
  1. Soak buckwheat in water for 30 minutes and drain with colander. Fry sliced onion with vegetable oil until good brown color.
  2. Add beef, carrot and other vegetables and fry. Put seasoning and tomato juice in it.
  3. Then add buckwheat and water. Pot water at the top of buckwheat. Check the taste of salt. Cook with low flame for about 30 minutes. Cover with lid.
  4. Mix well and serve.
