The ingredients needed to make Sensational Sides - Buttery Charred Sweetcorn:
  1. Prepare 2 sweet corn cobs cut in half
  2. Make ready 25 grams melted butter
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin
  4. Make ready Good pinch of salt and pepper
  5. Prepare Juice of a lemon or lime
  6. Make ready Handful fresh herbs chopped to serve
  7. Take Optional 20 grams of grated cheese
  8. Make ready Optional sprinkling of chilli flakes
Steps to make Sensational Sides - Buttery Charred Sweetcorn:
  1. In a pan melt the butter and add the seasoning and spices!
  2. Mix and pour into a bowl!
  3. Now on a griddle or a hot oven put in the sweetcorn cobs and coat them regularly with the butter mixture! Leave to char! Making sure the kernels are soft! They will take about 20 - 25 mins at 200 degrees turning the oven lower after 10 mins to 180 degrees!
  4. Meanwhile chop the parsley and slice the lemon.
  5. Optional grate the cheese!
  6. Drizzle over the lemon juice and sprinkle over the parsley and serve!
