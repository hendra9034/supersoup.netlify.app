The ingredients needed to make Soda Bread With Beetroot, Feta & Basil:
  1. Get 400 ml buttermilk,
  2. Take 240 g plain flour, extra for dusting,
  3. Get 240 g wholemeal flour,
  4. Prepare 3 pickled beetroots, chopped very finely, (around 2" wide),
  5. Make ready 100 g crumbled strong Greek feta cheese,
  6. Get 1 tsp garlic purée paste,
  7. Make ready 1 tsp bicarb soda,
  8. Prepare 1 tsp table salt,
  9. Take 2 generous handfuls fresh basil leaves, sliced
