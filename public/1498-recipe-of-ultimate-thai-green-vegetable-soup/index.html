The ingredients needed to make Thai Green Vegetable Soup:
  1. Take 2 cups cubed vegetables (broad beans, bottle gourd, green papaya, french beans, carrots, radish & onion)
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup green peas
  3. Take 1 tbsp. olive oil
  4. Make ready 1" galangal, sliced
  5. Prepare 1 fresh chilli, slit
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp. Thai green curry paste (store bought)
  7. Get 3 cups vegetable stock / water
  8. Get 8-10 kaffir lime leaves
  9. Take 1 lemongrass stalk, cut into 3 pieces
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  11. Prepare to taste salt
  12. Prepare 1/2 pepper powder or to taste
  13. Make ready as required coriander leaves, lime leaves & fresh chilies to garnish
Steps to make Thai Green Vegetable Soup:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and saute the Thai green curry paste on a low flame for a few seconds. Add all the chopped vegetables, lime leaves, lemon grass, galangal, green peas, fresh chilli, stock, salt and pepper powder.
  2. Pressure cook for 1 whistle. When cool, discard the galangal, lemongrass and lime leaves.
  3. Just before serving, add the coconut milk and heat through and serve in individual soup bowls, garnished with chopped coriander leaves, lime leaves and fresh chilies.
