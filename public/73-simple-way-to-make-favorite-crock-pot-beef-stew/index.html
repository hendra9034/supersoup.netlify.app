Steps to make Crock pot Beef stew:
  1. In a large plastic bag, combine dry onion soup mix and paprika. Add in meat and shake the bag to coat meat with the seasonings. You might have to do the step in batches for all the meat to fit. Once meat is coated spread out evenly into a greased 7 quart crock pot.
  2. Top meat evenly with diced potatoes, carrots,onions, and frozen peas.
  3. Mix the cream of celery soup and ketchup in a small bowl l. Pour mixture over the meat and vegetables.
  4. Cover crock pot with lid. Cook on low heat for 8 hours or until meat is cooked through and vegetables are tender. Stir to combine everything!
