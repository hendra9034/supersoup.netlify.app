The ingredients needed to make Carrot ginger soup:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup butter or coconut oil
  2. Make ready 1 cup onion, chopped
  3. Get 3 cup carrots (peeled and chopped)
  4. Make ready 1 large potato (peeled and cubed) or 1 cup cooked lentils
  5. Make ready 1 celery
  6. Make ready 3 cup chicken broth or vegetable broth
  7. Prepare 1 tsp ground or fresh ginger
  8. Get 1/2 cup milk or coconut milk
  9. Prepare 1 tsp curry powder
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  11. Get 1 black pepper (to taste)

Instructions to make Carrot ginger soup:
  1. Heat butter over low/medium heat, saute onions until translucent
  2. Add carrots, potato, celery, broth and ginger. Cover and bring to boil. Reduce to low heat, simmer for approx 30 min or until vegetables tender. Stir occasionally.
  3. Blend until smooth. Stir in milk, curry, salt and pepper. Simmer over low heat until heated through and reaches desires consistency (approx 10 min)
