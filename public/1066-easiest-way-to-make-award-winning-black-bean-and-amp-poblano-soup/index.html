The ingredients needed to make Black Bean & Poblano Soup:
  1. Take 13.4 oz Black Beans
  2. Make ready 1 Lime
  3. Get 1 Yellow Onion
  4. Get 4 tbsp Sour Cream
  5. Get 1 Large Poblano Pepper
  6. Get 1 Roma Tomato
  7. Get 1 tbsp Southwest Spice Blend
  8. Prepare 1.5 oz Tomato Paste
  9. Get 1 oz Tex Mex Paste
  10. Take 1.5 oz Vegetable Stock Concentrate
  11. Take 1/4 oz Cilantro
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup Monterey Jack Cheese
  13. Take 1.5 oz Blue Corn Tortilla Chips
  14. Get 1 tsp Vegetable Oil
  15. Take 1/2 cup Water
  16. Take Salt and Pepper, to season
Instructions to make Black Bean & Poblano Soup:
  1. Quarter the lime. Halve, peel, and diced onion. Core, DC, and finally diced Poblano. Diced tomato.
  2. In a small bowl, combine sour cream with a squeeze of lime juice to taste. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Heat a drizzle of oil in a medium pot over medium-high heat. Add Onion and Poblano; cook, stirring occasionally until browned and softened, 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Stir in Southwest spice blend, diced tomato, and tomato paste until combined. Cook until fragrant, one minute.
  5. Stir in black beans with their liquid, tex-mex paste, vegetable stock concentrate, and half cup of water into pot with veggies.
  6. Bring to a simmer, uncovered, then reduce heat to medium low. Cook, stirring often, until flavors meld and soup is slightly thickened, 8 to 10 minutes. Taste and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Meanwhile, pick cilantro leaves from stems. Divide soup between bowls with a dollop lime sour cream. Sprinkle with Monterey Jack and cilantro leaves. Serve with tortilla chips for dipping and any remaining lime wedges on the side.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
