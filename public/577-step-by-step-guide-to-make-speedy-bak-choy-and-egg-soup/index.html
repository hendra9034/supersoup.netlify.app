The ingredients needed to make Bak Choy And Egg Soup:
  1. Get eggs
  2. Make ready stock
  3. Make ready pounded garlic
  4. Make ready oil
  5. Make ready bak choy
  6. Prepare cherry plum tomato
Instructions to make Bak Choy And Egg Soup:
  1. in pan with oil saute garlic then add stock bring to a boil
  2. add eggs and break egg yolk and swirl 2 circle only then off heat
  3. once eggs set add bak choy and bring soup to a boil then off heat and serve immediately top tomato
