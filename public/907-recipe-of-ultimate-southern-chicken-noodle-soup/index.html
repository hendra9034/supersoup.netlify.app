The ingredients needed to make southern chicken noodle soup:
  1. Take 1/2 cup adobo
  2. Make ready 4 packages sazon
  3. Prepare 1 onion
  4. Make ready 3 carrots
  5. Get 3 celery stalks
  6. Make ready 1 packages no yolk egg noodles
  7. Take 1 large pot of water
  8. Prepare 1 whole chicken
  9. Prepare 2 bay leaves

Instructions to make southern chicken noodle soup:
  1. chop up all vegetables, set aside
  2. clean the chicken, and put inside the large pot of water, and set to boil
  3. add half of the chopped up vegetables
  4. add adobo and sazon and bay leaves
  5. leave to boil for 4 hours or until chicken is falling apart
  6. once chicken is done remove from pot and place into strainer
  7. add the rest off the vegetables and egg noodles
  8. tear the chicken to pieces placing the chicken back into the pot
  9. once noodles are ready, you can serve and enjoy :)
