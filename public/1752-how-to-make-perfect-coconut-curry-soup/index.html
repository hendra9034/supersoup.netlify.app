The ingredients needed to make Coconut curry soup:
  1. Make ready 2/3 cup orange lentil (dry)
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup green lentil (dry)
  3. Take 1/2 onion yellow
  4. Prepare 1 cup chopped broccoli

Instructions to make Coconut curry soup:
  1. Add oil, broccoli, cauliflower, peas, onion, & garlic to pot on med heat. Cook until veggies are el dante
  2. Add curry power make sure veggies get nicely coated
  3. Add water, vegetable bullion, lentils, and coconut milk. bring to a boil
  4. Add diced jalapēno
  5. Simmer for about 30mins until lentils are soft.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
