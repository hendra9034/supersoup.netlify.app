The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Red Lentil Soup (GF):
  1. Make ready 1 yellow onion, diced
  2. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Get 3 zucchini, diced
  4. Get 3 celery stalks, diced
  5. Take 2 large carrots, diced
  6. Take 2 cups water
  7. Prepare 9 cups chicken bone broth
  8. Get 2 cups red lentils, rinsed
  9. Make ready 2 cups GF rice pasta, elbows
  10. Prepare 1-1/2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  11. Make ready 2 tsp sea salt
  12. Make ready 2 tsp garlic powder
Instructions to make Instant Pot Red Lentil Soup (GF):
  1. Saute onions in olive oil in instant pot
  2. Add remaining ingredients and set instant pot to pressure cook for 6 minutes.
  3. Let instant pot release naturally and serve
