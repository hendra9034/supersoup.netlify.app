The ingredients needed to make Red Lentil Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  2. Prepare 1/2 Onion Finely Chopped
  3. Get 3 clove Garlic Minced
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Tomatoe Paste
  5. Get 1 large Tomatoe Diced
  6. Prepare 2 tsp Cumin Ground
  7. Make ready 4 cup Vegitable or Chicken Broth
  8. Get 1 cup Red Lentils
  9. Make ready 2 cup Water
  10. Make ready 1 pinch Chili Powder
  11. Make ready 1 Carrot Chopped
  12. Take 1 Salt
  13. Get 1 Pepper
  14. Prepare 1 Juice of 1 Lemon
  15. Prepare 1 pinch Red Pepper Flakes
  16. Take 3 tbsp Cilantro

Instructions to make Red Lentil Soup:
  1. Saute garlic and onion in olive. oil.
  2. In large pot add sauteed onion, diced tomatoes, sauteed garlic, tomatoe paste, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook on high just a few minutes before adding the other ingredients.
  4. Add broth, lentils, water, chili powder, and chopped carrot to pot while still on high heat.
  5. Stir, then reduce heat to simmer.
  6. While on simmer, cover pot with vented lid or put a wooden spoon between pot and lid to allow ventilation. Simmer 30 minutes or until lentils are soft.
  7. Stir in salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Puree half the soup in a food processor. Then add it back to the cooking soup.
  9. Add lemon juice and red pepper into simmering pot.
  10. Serve in bowls.
  11. Garnish with cilantro.
