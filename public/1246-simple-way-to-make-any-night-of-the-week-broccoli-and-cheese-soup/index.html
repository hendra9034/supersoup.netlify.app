The ingredients needed to make Broccoli and cheese soup.:
  1. Prepare 1 packages broccoli
  2. Prepare 2 shredded carrots
  3. Prepare 4 cup chicken broth
  4. Take 1/2 chopped onion
  5. Make ready 1 cup water
  6. Take 2 tsp butter
  7. Take 1/2 cup flour
  8. Take 16 oz package cheese (cut)
  9. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
