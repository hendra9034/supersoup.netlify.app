The ingredients needed to make Mini Meatballs:
  1. Prepare 300 gr minced meat (preferably beef)
  2. Make ready 1 egg
  3. Get 1 onion
  4. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  5. Prepare 50 gr parsley
  6. Prepare 3 spoonfuls oats
  7. Take 50 ml extra virgin olive oil
  8. Prepare splash vinegar
  9. Get sea salt to taste
  10. Take 1 pinch cumin
  11. Get 1 pinch ginger powder
  12. Get 1 pinch sweet paprika
  13. Take 4-5 leaves spearmint (fresh or dried)
  14. Take 150-200 ml sunflower or cornflower oil for frying (I use olive oil but it is not obligatory)
  15. Prepare all-purpose flour to cover the meatballs before frying

Instructions to make Mini Meatballs:
  1. Dice the onion and garlic. Add just a splash of olive oil to a frying pan, heat it over medium fire and add the onion and garlic. Cook until they are really nice and soft. Set aside to cool down.
  2. Grind the oats into flour. In a large bowl add minced meat, olive oil, vinegar, egg, onion, garlic, oats, parsley and spearmint finally chopped, and all the seasonings, to taste.
  3. Mix them well together. Cover and let it set in the fridge for at least an hour.
  4. Add the oil in a pot and heat over medium to high heat. For an electric stove that the heat goes from 1-9 heat over 7, a little bit over medium.
  5. Shape mini meatballs cover them with flour, shake off excess flour and add to the pot, in the hot oil. Fry them until golden brown. Place them in a plate covered with paper, this will absorb excess oil. Enjoy with cold beer.
