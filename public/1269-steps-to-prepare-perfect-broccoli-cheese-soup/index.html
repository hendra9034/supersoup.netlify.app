The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Make ready 4 tbsp Margarine
  2. Prepare 1 cup Chopped onion
  3. Get 4 tbsp Flour
  4. Take 3 cup Milk
  5. Take 2 cup Cooked broccoli
  6. Make ready 4 slice Velveeta cheese, cubed (approx 3/4 to 1 lb.)
  7. Take 1 tsp Salt
  8. Take 1 dash Pepper

Steps to make Broccoli Cheese Soup:
  1. Melt margarine in saucepan
  2. Add onion and cook until tender.
  3. Blend in flour until mixture is smooth.
  4. Add milk, broccoli, cheese and seasonings.
  5. Cook until mixture thickens and cheese is melted, stirring constantly.
