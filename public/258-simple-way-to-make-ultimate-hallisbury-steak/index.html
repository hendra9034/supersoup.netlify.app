The ingredients needed to make “Hall”isbury steak:
  1. Take 4 chop pork steaks
  2. Prepare 3 Tbs olive oil
  3. Get To taste Onion powder
  4. Make ready To taste Garlic powder
  5. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  6. Prepare 1 (10.75 oz) can of cream of bacon soup
  7. Get 1 (10.75 oz) cream of mushroom and garlic soup
  8. Make ready 6 oz chicken broth
  9. Make ready 1 onion
  10. Take 1/2 cup flour
  11. Take 1 (1 oz) dry onion soup mix
  12. Get 1 TVs Worcester sauce
  13. Take 1 tsp meat tenderizer
  14. Make ready to taste French fried onions

Steps to make “Hall”isbury steak:
  1. Heat oven to 350
  2. Sprinkle steaks with meat tenderizer, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper on both sides
  3. Dredge steaks through flour
  4. In a large pan that is usable on both stovetop and oven heat olive oil and add steaks browning on both sides (Aprox 4 min on each side mein/hai heat)
  5. In a bowl combine all soups, Worcester sauce and broth; pour over steaks
  6. Dice onion and lay overtop, cover with lid and bake at 350 for 90-120 minutes
  7. Top with French fried onions and serve with mashed potatoes
