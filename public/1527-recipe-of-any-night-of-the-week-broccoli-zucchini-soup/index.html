The ingredients needed to make Broccoli - Zucchini Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Broccoli
  2. Get 1/2 cup Zucchini
  3. Get 2 finely chopped onions
  4. Get 1 tsp Chopped garlic
  5. Make ready 2 cups Milk
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cornflour
  7. Take As required Salt to taste, Black pepper powder, Oregano, parsley
  8. Get As Required Roasted almonds
  9. Take 1 tbsp Butter

Steps to make Broccoli - Zucchini Soup:
  1. Heat butter in a pan. Saute chopped onion, garlic and then add broccoli & zucchini. Saute until soft.
  2. When the mix turns cold, churn the same by adding water to a puree.
  3. Add a mixture of cornflour and milk to the puree. Add salt, black pepper powder, oregano and parsley. Boil after mixing.
  4. Add chopped almonds to the soup and serve.
