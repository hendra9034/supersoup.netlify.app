The ingredients needed to make Charred corn, green chilli and lime:
  1. Make ready 6 long green chillies
  2. Take 2 tbsp grape seed oil plus extra for the corn
  3. Make ready 1 shallot, chopped
  4. Get 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Cayenne pepper
  6. Make ready 250 g unsalted butter, at room temperature, chopped
  7. Take 1/2 bunch coriander leaves picked and chopped
  8. Prepare 1/2 bunch flat leaf parsley leaves picked and chopped
  9. Make ready Sea salt
  10. Get Juice of 2 limes
  11. Get 8 corn cobs
  12. Make ready Smoke paprika to season
  13. Prepare Lime wedges to serve

Steps to make Charred corn, green chilli and lime:
  1. Preheat the barbecue grill plate over high heat.
  2. Toss 4 of the chillies in a little grape seed oil to coat.
  3. Cook until blackened, then place in a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave to steam and cool. 1. Heat the remaining oil in a small saucepan over medium heat and cook the shallot and garlic until soft but not coloured. Set aside to cool.
  4. Slip the blackened skins from the chillies and remove the seeds.
  5. Dice the flesh finely and place in a food processor with the shallot and garlic mixture and the cayenne pepper, butter, coriander and parsley.
