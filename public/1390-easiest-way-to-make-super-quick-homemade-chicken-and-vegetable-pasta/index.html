The ingredients needed to make Chicken and vegetable pasta:
  1. Prepare 500 g diced chicken breast
  2. Prepare 2-3 cups shell pasta
  3. Prepare 1 large onion sliced
  4. Take 1 crushed garlic clove
  5. Make ready 1 (160 g) tin of sweet-corn
  6. Make ready 1 handful broccoli cut into small pieces
  7. Prepare 1 (156 g) drain and sliced Mushrooms
  8. Take 1 chicken stock cube
  9. Take 2 tablespoons ketchup
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon seven seasoning
  12. Prepare Pinch salt
  13. Take 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps to make Chicken and vegetable pasta:
  1. Bring the pasta into a pan add water and start the cooking process takes up to 20 minutes on a medium heat
  2. In tray add vegetable oil heat and add the onion, crushed garlic and the sal, once sweat add the chicken and stir every now and them until all turns white
  3. Add the chicken stocking and the seasoning and cook for another minute
  4. Add all the vegetables to the chicken and stir and place in the over at 180c temperature for 5 minutes until sweat
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the tray
  6. Now add the ketchup and stir very well add about 2 tablespoons of hot water and place in the oven for another 3-5 minutes and its ready to be served
