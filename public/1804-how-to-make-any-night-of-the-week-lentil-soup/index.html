The ingredients needed to make lentil soup:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Prepare 2 carrots
  4. Get 2 celery stalks
  5. Make ready 2 garlic cloves
  6. Get 1 salt/pepper
  7. Get 1 can diced tomatoes
  8. Take 1 lb lentils
  9. Get 11 cup chicken broth
  10. Take 4 thyme sprigs
  11. Prepare 2/3 cup elbow pasta
  12. Make ready 1 cup shredded parmesan

Steps to make lentil soup:
  1. heat oil in large pot over medium heat. add onion carrots and celery. add garlic salt/pepper and saute until tender about 8-10 minutes. add tomato with juice simmer for about 8 minutes stirring occasionally. add lentils, mixing to coat, and broth stirring while doing so. add thyme and bring to boil. cover and simmer for about 30 minutes.
  2. stir in pasta and simmer until pasta is tender about 8 minutes. season with salt/pepper.
  3. garnish with parmesan and olive oil over top of individual servings.
