The ingredients needed to make Sago soup/ sabudana ka soup:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup sabudana or sago soaked overnight
  2. Prepare 1 tsp ghee
  3. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
  4. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper powder
  5. Take 3-4 Curry leaves
  6. Take 2 tbsp chopped potatoes
  7. Take 3-4 tbsp roasted peanuts crushed
  8. Prepare 2 tsp green chillies finely chopped
  9. Get 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  10. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice
  11. Make ready As per taste Sendha namak
  12. Make ready as required coriander leaves

Instructions to make Sago soup/ sabudana ka soup:
  1. Heat ghee in a deep pan, add the cumin seeds and curry leaves.
  2. When seeds crackle, add green chilies, potatoes, and grated ginger mix well and saute for 2 or 3 minutes.
  3. Add sago and peanuts mix well and saute for 3 mins. - Add enough water, mix well and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Add salt, black pepper powder, lemon juice and sugar, mix well.
  5. Serve hot garnished with coriander leaves.
