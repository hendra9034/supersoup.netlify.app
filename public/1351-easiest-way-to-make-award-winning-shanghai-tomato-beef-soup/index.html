Steps to make Shanghai tomato beef soup罗宋汤:
  1. Dice all veggies and meat.
  2. Sauté onion, carrot until caramelized. Add diced tomatoes and cook until red juice comes out.
  3. Add the rest of ingredients except cooked beef. Saute until all veggies are withered.
  4. Pour in a jar of beef stock and add 8-10 oz water until the liquid covers all ingredients.
  5. Add tomato paste and high pressure (low) for 1 minute with quick release.
  6. Switch to saute function and stir in beef, adjust seasoning to your desired sweet and sour taste. Cut in a Tsp of butter and dried herb. Serve with bread or rice.
