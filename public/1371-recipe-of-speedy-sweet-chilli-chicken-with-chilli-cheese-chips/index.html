Instructions to make Sweet Chilli Chicken with Chilli Cheese Chips:
  1. To the chicken add all ingredients from 1st section corn flour salt and pepper soy sauce oil g&g can be mixed ahead of cooking time or do it straight away and you can deep fry shallow fry but I find easiest & healthy way is to cook on high in the air fryer for 6/7 mins, will make it crispy not to brown
  2. While the chicken cooks make the sauce & put the Chips into the oven
  3. To make sauce follow the 2nd set of ingredients, add oil to the pan and cumin and stir fry the peppers and onions after a few minutes add the g&g and all the sauces
  4. In this time the chicken will have cooked add to sauce allow to cook - Into the sauce. Garnish with sesame seeds
  5. While the chicken warms through - Mix the sauces for the chips and grate the cheese ready. Once chips are cooked add sat & toss in the sauce finally add cheese
  6. Plate up chicken and chips - Enjoy
