The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Rice Pineapple Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 cup rice, I used minute rice
  2. Take 2 pounds chicken
  3. Get 2 teaspoon salt
  4. Prepare 2 cup chopped pineapple
  5. Get 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
  6. Take 1/2 cup butter
  7. Make ready 2-1/2 quarts water
  8. Take 3 teaspoon crushed chicken Bouillon cubes
  9. Get 1/4 cup water
  10. Take 2 tablespoons cornstarch
Steps to make Chicken and Rice Pineapple Soup:
  1. Boil the water with the chicken and salt
  2. When it has boiled for 25 minutes add the Bouillon cubes
  3. Chop the chicken add back to the broth.
  4. Add the pepper and chopped pineapple.
  5. Add the rice and cook till done.
  6. Mix the cornstarch and water and add to the boiling chicken broth
  7. Serve I hope you enjoy!
