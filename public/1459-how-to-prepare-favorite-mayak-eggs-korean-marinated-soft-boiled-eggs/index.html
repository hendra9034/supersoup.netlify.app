The ingredients needed to make Mayak Eggs (Korean Marinated soft boiled eggs):
  1. Get for boiling the eggs
  2. Get 6 medium large size eggs (room temperature)
  3. Get Enough water to cover the eggs completely
  4. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tbsp vinegar
  6. Take sauce
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup light soya sauce
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup light corn syrup/honey/sugar
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  10. Get 3 cloves garlic, minced
  11. Get Few bunches of spring onion, finely chopped
  12. Take 2 chilli padi, finely chopped
  13. Get 1 big green chilli, finely chopped
  14. Get 2 big red chilli, finely chopped
  15. Make ready 1 tbsp white sesame seeds
Instructions to make Mayak Eggs (Korean Marinated soft boiled eggs):
  1. Bring enough water to cover 6 eggs in a pot. Add salt and vinegar and bring it to boil and carefully add the eggs. Cook the eggs 6-7 minutes for runny yolk
  2. Once the cooking time is up, transfer the eggs to cold water to stop the eggs from cooking
  3. Meanwhile, prepare all ingredients. Combine soya sauce, water, corn syrup, minced garlic, chopped chilli, spring onions, sesame seeds. Adjust the chilli accordingly to suit your taste
  4. Peel the eggs carefully and soak the eggs in the sauce at least 6 hours or overnight before serve. Stay good in the fridge for 5-7 days
  5. Enjoy with a bowl of warm rice and a bit drizzle of sesame oil! (This photo is taken from internet)
  6. You can use it for your ramen/noodle topping too!
